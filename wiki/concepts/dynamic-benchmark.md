## Definition

**Dynamic Benchmark** (动态基准) — an evaluation benchmark that is periodically updated so the task instances, evidence, or scoring conditions evolve over time instead of remaining fixed.

## Key Points

- REALTIME QA publishes a new batch of questions every week rather than freezing a single static test set.
- The platform exposes both questions and baseline scores online, enabling direct comparison of participant systems against contemporaneous baselines.
- Past QA pairs are retained for development, but the main evaluation objective is performance under changing information conditions.
- The paper positions dynamic benchmarking as a better fit for real-world QA deployments than one-shot evaluation on static corpora.
