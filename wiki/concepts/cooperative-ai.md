## Definition

**Cooperative AI** (协作式人工智能) — AI systems designed to coordinate with humans or other agents toward shared goals rather than acting independently or competitively.

## Key Points

- CAMEL frames its assistant-user interactions as a concrete cooperative AI setting with shared task completion as the common objective.
- The paper argues that cooperation requires explicit handling of alignment, role stability, and communication protocol.
- Observed failures such as role flipping and flake replies show that cooperative behavior does not emerge reliably from base chat models alone.
- The framework is presented as a scalable way to study both the promise and fragility of cooperative multi-agent behavior.
