## Definition

**Emotional Intelligence** (情绪智力) — the capacity to perceive, interpret, regulate, and use emotion-related information in reasoning or behavior, here operationalized as a capability that LLM outputs may partially exhibit under prompting.

## Key Points

- The paper treats emotional intelligence as the ability to understand psychological emotional stimuli and translate them into better task performance.
- EmotionPrompt is designed as a probe for this capability rather than as a new model architecture or fine-tuning recipe.
- The authors argue that gains across automatic benchmarks, human evaluation, and TruthfulQA suggest LLMs can respond to emotion-related cues in a measurable way.
- The work explicitly connects this concept to psychology theories such as self-monitoring, social cognitive theory, and cognitive emotion regulation.
