## Definition

**Self-Improvement** (自我改进) — a process in which an agent modifies its future behavior using its own interaction history, learned artifacts, or policy updates rather than remaining fixed after deployment.

## Key Points

- ASG-SI argues that deployed self-improvement should be defined over promoted skill artifacts, not just changing model parameters.
- The paper treats successful trajectories as raw material for compiling reusable skills with explicit interfaces.
- Promotion decisions are gated by replayable evidence so that improvement can be audited and reproduced.
- The framework is motivated by security concerns including reward hacking, brittle specialization, and hard-to-trace behavioral drift.
