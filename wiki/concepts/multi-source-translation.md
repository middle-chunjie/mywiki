## Definition

**Multi-Source Translation** (多源翻译) — machine translation that uses multiple source-language sentences describing the same content to generate a target-language sentence.

## Key Points

- The survey positions multi-source translation as a distinct MNMT scenario that exploits source-side redundancy.
- It reviews both explicit multi-encoder architectures and simpler source-concatenation baselines.
- Linguistically similar source languages are reported to be especially helpful in low-resource settings.
- The paper also covers missing-source handling via dummy tokens or synthetic source sentences.
