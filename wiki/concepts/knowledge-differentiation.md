## Definition

**Knowledge Differentiation** (知识区分) — the ability of a model to separate newly provided knowledge from overlapping or conflicting internal knowledge it already stores.

## Key Points

- KD questions test whether models confuse a synthetic entity with its parent or other existing entities.
- The paper shows that higher property similarity and more similar names usually make KD harder for open models.
- Adding parent-entity context further degrades KD more than adding irrelevant entities for Vicuna and ChatGLM.
