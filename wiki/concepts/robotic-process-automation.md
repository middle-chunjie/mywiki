## Definition

**Robotic Process Automation** (机器人流程自动化) — an automation paradigm that executes repetitive tasks through manually specified workflows over application actions and interfaces.

## Key Points

- The paper uses RPA as the predecessor paradigm in which workflow execution is automated but workflow authoring still requires substantial manual effort.
- Apple Shortcuts is presented as a representative RPA-style environment with large, expert-built workflows.
- WorkflowLLM argues that the main limitation of RPA is not execution but the human cost of workflow construction and maintenance.
- The proposed APA setting inherits RPA's workflow substrate while shifting orchestration from humans to LLMs.
