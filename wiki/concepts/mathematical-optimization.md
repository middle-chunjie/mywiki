## Definition

**Mathematical Optimization** (数学优化) — the study of finding solutions that minimize or maximize an objective function under a specified problem structure.

## Key Points

- The paper uses mathematical optimization as a proof-of-concept domain before moving to prompt optimization.
- It evaluates OPRO on both a continuous problem, [[linear-regression]], and a discrete problem, [[traveling-salesman-problem]].
- The experiments are designed to test whether an LLM can infer improving search directions from prior solution-score pairs alone.
- The authors explicitly position the results as evidence of generality rather than as an attempt to beat specialized optimizers or solvers.
