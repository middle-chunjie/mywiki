## Definition

**Role-Playing** (角色扮演) — a prompting framework in which language-model agents are assigned complementary roles and interact under those roles to solve a shared task.

## Key Points

- CAMEL instantiates role-playing with one AI user and one AI assistant that share a task but have different responsibilities.
- A human only needs to provide an initial idea and role pair; a task-specifier agent expands that idea into a concrete task prompt.
- The role-playing prompts explicitly forbid role flipping and constrain each side's communication format.
- The paper treats role-playing both as a task-solving method and as a controlled environment for studying agent behavior.
