## Definition

**Optical character recognition** (光学字符识别) — the task of converting scanned or photographed textual content into machine-readable symbols.

## Key Points

- The paper positions classical OCR as a mature baseline family built mainly for well-scanned documents and simple textual layouts.
- It argues that OCR assumptions break down on structural images, where semantics depend on nested layout and object placement.
- STN is presented as a generalization beyond OCR, targeting transcription of music notation, formulas, and other grammar-rich images.
- The contrast with OCR motivates explicit modeling of both reading order and local content.
