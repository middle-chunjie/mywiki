## Definition

**Conversational Information Seeking** (对话式信息寻求) — an information access paradigm in which users and systems exchange natural-language turns to formulate needs, retrieve evidence, and refine the interaction over time.

## Key Points

- The paper frames ProCIS as infrastructure for conversational information seeking beyond simple query-response search.
- It emphasizes multi-party conversations rather than single-user chat, making context tracking and timing more central.
- Existing CIS benchmarks mostly evaluate reactive behavior, leaving proactive engagement underexplored.
- ProCIS uses Reddit conversations and Wikipedia grounding to operationalize open-domain CIS at large scale.
