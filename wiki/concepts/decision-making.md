## Definition

**Decision Making** (决策) — the process of selecting actions under uncertainty, information constraints, or competing objectives.

## Key Points

- The paper studies human decision making in two classic regimes: decisions from description and decisions from experience.
- It treats human choices, rather than reward maximization alone, as the target signal for model fitting and evaluation.
- The horizon-task analysis emphasizes that realistic decision making combines exploitation with structured exploration over time.
- The results suggest that pretrained language-model embeddings contain information rich enough to support behavioral prediction in multiple decision-making paradigms.
