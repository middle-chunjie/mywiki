## Definition

**Potential Competitor Recognition** (潜在竞争者识别) — a procedure for identifying firms whose patent portfolios indicate competitive pressure on a target company.

## Key Points

- PCR is one of the three core components of DTF.
- It scores company-company competition with three patent indicators: patent activity, technology share, and R&D emphasis.
- The paper computes competitive degree through a weighted Euclidean-distance form and keeps the top-`m` competitors per company per year.
- These competitor embeddings are injected into the target company's yearly internal-factor representation.
