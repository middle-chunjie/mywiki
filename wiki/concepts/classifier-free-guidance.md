## Definition

**Classifier-Free Guidance** (无分类器引导) — a conditional diffusion sampling strategy that amplifies conditioning signals by combining conditional and unconditional model predictions without a separate classifier.

## Key Points

- The survey introduces classifier-free guidance as one of the standard conditional-sampling mechanisms for diffusion models.
- It is discussed alongside classifier guidance in the paper's section on conditional generation.
- The paper treats CFG as relevant to text-conditioned and label-conditioned generation rather than only image synthesis.
- CFG appears in the survey as part of the practical toolkit that made conditional diffusion broadly useful across application domains.
