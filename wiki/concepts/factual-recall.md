## Definition

**Factual Recall** (事实回忆) — a model's ability to recover and output a correct stored fact when prompted with a query about the relevant entity or relation.

## Key Points

- On CounterFact, LASER improves GPT-J factual accuracy from `13.1%` to `24.0%` without any retraining.
- The recovered facts are disproportionately those that occur less frequently in the model's training data.
- Before intervention, failures often manifest as generic high-frequency tokens rather than semantically coherent answers.
- The results suggest that some facts are present in the model but are masked by noisy higher-order components.
