## Definition

**Agentic Process Automation** (智能体流程自动化) — a process-automation paradigm in which language-model-based agents generate or orchestrate workflows from user instructions rather than relying solely on manually authored scripts.

## Key Points

- The paper frames recent LLM-driven automation as a shift from robotic process automation toward agentic process automation.
- Existing APA systems are described as bottlenecked by limited action scale and weak handling of complex branching and looping logic.
- WorkflowLLM is proposed as a concrete method for strengthening APA by improving the data used to train workflow-generating models.
- The reported gains suggest that APA performance depends heavily on realistic workflow corpora rather than only on stronger base models.
