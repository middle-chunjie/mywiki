## Definition

**Persona-to-Persona** — a persona expansion method that derives additional personas by prompting for people in close social or functional relation to an existing persona.

## Key Points

- The paper uses this method to recover personas that may have low direct visibility on the web, such as children, beggars, or backstage workers.
- Expansion relies on interpersonal relations such as patient-caregiver, assistance, or co-worker links.
- The method is iterated `6` times per initial persona, loosely motivated by six degrees of separation.
- Persona-to-Persona complements Text-to-Persona rather than replacing it, extending coverage into harder-to-surface regions of the population.
