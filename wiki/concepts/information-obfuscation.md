## Definition

**Information Obfuscation** — the deliberate masking or vagueness of clues in a question so that the answer remains valid but cannot be recovered by a simple lookup.

## Key Points

- WebSailor obfuscates dates, names, and attributes when generating questions from web-derived graphs.
- This raises initial ambiguity and forces the agent to compare, search, and synthesize multiple evidence fragments.
- The paper uses obfuscation as one of the main mechanisms for creating Level-3 tasks that are difficult even for strong models.
- The resulting training data is intended to teach strategic exploration instead of shortcut pattern matching.
