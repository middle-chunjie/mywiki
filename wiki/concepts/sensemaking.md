## Definition

**Sensemaking** — the process of forming a coherent understanding of complex, distributed information in order to identify themes, relationships, and actionable interpretations.

## Key Points

- The paper targets global sensemaking queries that require understanding the entire corpus rather than retrieving a few supporting passages.
- Its adaptive benchmark generates personas, tasks, and questions intended to reflect realistic sensemaking use cases.
- Evaluation criteria emphasize comprehensiveness, diversity, and empowerment, all framed around supporting user understanding.
- GraphRAG is presented as an indexing and summarization method optimized for iterative corpus-scale sensemaking.
