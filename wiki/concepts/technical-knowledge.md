## Definition

**Technical Knowledge** (技术性知识) — domain knowledge about concrete techniques, materials, or interventions that can be applied to satisfy engineering requirements.

## Key Points

- The benchmark extracts technical knowledge separately from analytical knowledge so systems can be judged on both diagnosis and intervention quality.
- Technical knowledge is retrieved during SolutionRAG expansion to help draft or revise candidate solutions.
- Technical score evaluates whether a generated solution applies appropriate technologies to handle the requirement constraints.
- The paper merges duplicate technical knowledge items within the same domain to form a cleaner knowledge base.
