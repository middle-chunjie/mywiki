## Definition

**Scene text recognition** (场景文本识别) — the task of recognizing text strings embedded in natural images rather than in clean scanned documents.

## Key Points

- The paper reviews scene text recognition as a major encoder-decoder application with CNN encoders and RNN decoders.
- Prior scene-text models are treated as strong baselines but are designed for comparatively simple textual layouts.
- STN inherits the encoder-decoder setup while adding explicit spotlight control to better follow structural reading paths.
- The paper suggests future work may extend STN back to more ambitious scene text recognition settings.
