## Definition

**Social Bias** (社会偏见) — systematic differential judgments or associations about demographic groups expressed in model outputs or decisions.

## Key Points

- [[liu-nd-uncovering]] shows that code models can express social bias even when standard coding benchmarks appear human-irrelevant.
- The paper operationalizes bias as generated code that associates demographic groups with judgmental modifiers such as `disgusting`, `worse`, or `best`.
- Bias severity is measured at both overall and demographic-specific levels through CBS, UFS, and standard deviation over valid demographics.
