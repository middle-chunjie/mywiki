## Definition

**Welfare Analysis** (福利分析) — the evaluation of how a system choice affects the well-being or preference satisfaction of a target population or stakeholder subgroup.

## Key Points

- PRISM defines downstream welfare using mean rating and mean choice probability for a model over a stakeholder population.
- The paper studies how different feedback-sampling schemes induce different probabilities of selecting a given LLM, then measures the welfare imposed on other groups.
- Larger representative samples generally dominate smaller or demographically restricted samples in the induced welfare distributions.
- The analysis shows that optimizing average welfare for the whole population can still reduce welfare for minority sub-populations.
