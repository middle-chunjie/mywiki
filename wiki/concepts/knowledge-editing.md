## Definition

**Knowledge Editing** (知识编辑) — targeted modification of a model's stored knowledge without fully retraining the model from scratch.

## Key Points

- The survey presents knowledge editing as one route for reducing [[temporal-misalignment]] by updating stale parametric facts.
- It emphasizes that editing is efficient compared with full retraining but is not a magic bullet.
- Reported side effects include inconsistency, hallucination, and new forms of [[intra-memory-conflict]] after edits are applied.
- The paper therefore treats knowledge editing as both a mitigation tool and a possible source of new knowledge conflicts.
