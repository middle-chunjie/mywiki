## Definition

**Toxicity** (有害性) — the degree to which model outputs contain abusive, unsafe, or otherwise harmful language.

## Key Points

- The paper shows that imitation can transfer safety style even when it fails to transfer broad capabilities.
- On RealToxicityPrompts, more imitation data makes the student model less toxic by encouraging ChatGPT-like abstention behavior.
- This result motivates a narrower use case for imitation: inheriting safer response policies when the base LM is already strong.
- The authors treat toxicity reduction as a secondary benefit rather than evidence that holistic capability imitation has succeeded.
