## Definition

**AI Fairness** (人工智能公平性) — the study and practice of preventing unjust performance gaps, harms, or differential treatment across groups in AI systems.

## Key Points

- [[liu-nd-uncovering]] extends fairness evaluation from natural-language tasks to [[code-generation]], arguing that code assistants can amplify downstream social harms.
- The paper restricts fairness analysis to human-relevant scenarios and explicitly constructs prompts that surface demographic judgments.
- It treats fairness as both prevalence of biased outputs and disparity across demographic pairs, not just raw toxicity.
