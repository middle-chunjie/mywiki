## Definition

**Patent Mining** (专利挖掘) — the extraction of technological, competitive, or strategic signals from patent text, metadata, and classification structure.

## Key Points

- The paper uses USPTO patents as an open data source for tracing firms' R&D trajectories.
- It maps patents to technologies through Cooperative Patent Classification (CPC) codes and then aggregates them at CPC-group level.
- Patent text is encoded with a CNN so that content information influences company and technology representations.
- The framework uses patent-derived indicators both for competition among firms and collaboration among technologies.
