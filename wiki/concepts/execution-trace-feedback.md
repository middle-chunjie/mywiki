## Definition

**Execution-Trace Feedback** (执行轨迹反馈) — a feedback format where the model narrates step-by-step execution behavior to localize faults before revising code.

## Key Points

- [[chen-2023-teaching-2304-05128]] studies trace feedback for tasks with unit tests, especially code translation and MBPP.
- The traces are generated by the model itself rather than extracted from internal interpreter states.
- On MBPP, trace feedback gives Codex `70.8` and StarCoder `53.2`, outperforming their simpler feedback variants.
- The paper motivates trace feedback by analogy to human debugging through mental execution of a program.
