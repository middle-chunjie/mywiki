## Definition

**Knowledge Applying** (知识应用) — the ability of a model to use world knowledge to solve reasoning and problem-solving tasks rather than merely restating memorized facts.

## Key Points

- KoLA measures this level with six world-knowledge reasoning tasks, including HotpotQA, 2WikiMultiHopQA, MuSiQue, KQA Pro, KoRC, and the evolving ETA benchmark.
- The task suite emphasizes multi-hop reasoning over documents and knowledge graphs, including joint reasoning where neither the document nor background knowledge alone is sufficient.
- The paper finds that instruction tuning substantially increases the correlation between model size and this higher-level ability, with a reported Spearman jump from `0.02` to `0.53`.
