## Definition

**Benchmark Dataset** (基准数据集) — a curated collection of standardized evaluation tasks, labels, and protocols used to measure and compare model or agent capabilities.

## Key Points

- InfoMosaic-Bench is positioned as the first benchmark focused on multi-source information seeking for tool-augmented agents.
- Its items include both final answers and condition-level supervision, enabling more granular analysis than a single end score.
- The paper uses the benchmark to compare `14` agents under web-only and domain-tool settings.
