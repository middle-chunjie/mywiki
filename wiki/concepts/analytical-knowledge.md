## Definition

**Analytical Knowledge** (分析性知识) — domain knowledge used to interpret the constraints and causal structure of a problem before selecting a technical solution.

## Key Points

- In SolutionBench, analytical knowledge is one of the five extracted fields from each engineering report.
- The paper distinguishes analytical knowledge from technical knowledge by assigning it to requirement interpretation rather than direct intervention.
- Analytical score measures whether a system uses the right analytical knowledge to reason about complex constraints.
- The benchmark stores analytical knowledge items in each domain-specific knowledge base alongside technical knowledge.
