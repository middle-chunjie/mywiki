## Definition

**Uncertainty Reduction** — the process of narrowing a large ambiguous hypothesis space through targeted evidence gathering, branch pruning, and synthesis.

## Key Points

- WebSailor frames difficult web search as uncertainty reduction rather than ordinary multi-hop reasoning.
- The paper claims that proprietary deep-research systems succeed because they can systematically reduce extreme uncertainty over many tool interactions.
- Its synthetic training data is explicitly designed to maximize both initial uncertainty and the difficulty of reducing it.
- The resulting policy learns to plan exploration paths instead of relying on direct recall or shallow query reformulation.
