## Definition

**Preference Optimization** (偏好优化) — a post-training approach that aligns model behavior to preferred outputs using preference data, reward models, or direct preference objectives.

## Key Points

- The paper identifies preference optimization as a standard alignment stage after LLM pretraining.
- SORRY-Bench evaluates the downstream refusal behavior of models shaped by such post-training pipelines.
- The benchmark shows that preference-optimized models can still differ widely in refusal policy across categories and prompt forms.
