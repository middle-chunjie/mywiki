## Definition

**Text-to-Persona** — a persona induction method that infers likely readers, writers, or interested individuals from a text passage and uses those inferred descriptions as reusable personas.

## Key Points

- The input can be arbitrary web text, including plain or structured text, making the method highly scalable.
- Prompts explicitly ask who is likely to read, write, like, or dislike the text, and request specific rather than coarse persona descriptions.
- Fine-grained source text tends to yield correspondingly fine-grained persona descriptions.
- The method is the primary mechanism used to bootstrap Persona Hub from massive public web corpora such as RedPajama v2.
