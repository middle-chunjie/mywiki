## Definition

**Mathematical Reasoning** (数学推理) — the capability to analyze, transform, and solve mathematical problems through multi-step symbolic, numerical, or logical inference.

## Key Points

- [[gou-2024-tora-2309-17452]] studies mathematical reasoning as a setting where pure language reasoning and pure program synthesis each have clear weaknesses.
- The paper frames mathematical reasoning as benefiting from interleaving natural-language planning with executable tool use.
- ToRA is evaluated on 10 mathematical reasoning datasets ranging from GSM8k to the competition-level MATH benchmark.
- The paper's analysis shows that mathematical reasoning failures still come largely from incorrect reasoning, diagram understanding, and brittle tool use.
