## Definition

**Entity Popularity** (实体流行度) — a measure of how frequently an entity is discussed, approximated here by Wikipedia page-view statistics and used to predict factual memorization.

## Key Points

- The paper uses monthly Wikipedia page views as a cheap proxy for how often an entity appears on the web.
- Accuracy rises with entity popularity across most model families and many relationship types.
- Entity popularity is predictive enough that it can drive the paper's Adaptive Retrieval decision rule.
- The authors note that popularity is time-dependent and only an approximation of pretraining exposure.
