## Definition

**Benchmark** (基准测试) — a standardized collection of tasks, datasets, and metrics used to compare models on a target capability.

## Key Points

- BLANCA is presented as a benchmark for textual coding artifacts, positioned as an analogue of GLUE for code-related language understanding.
- The suite spans ranking, classification, and regression tasks rather than a single metric or dataset.
- Its value lies both in evaluation and in serving as supervision for fine-tuning better representations.
- The paper emphasizes benchmark coverage of manuals, documentation, and forum posts instead of source code alone.
