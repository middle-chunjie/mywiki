## Definition

**Long-Tail Knowledge** (长尾知识) — knowledge about infrequently discussed entities or relations that lies outside the high-frequency head of the data distribution.

## Key Points

- POPQA is designed specifically to expose long-tail factual knowledge that popular QA benchmarks underrepresent.
- The paper shows that larger models still perform poorly on the least popular `4,000` questions, indicating limited memorization in the tail.
- Retrieval augmentation provides much larger gains in the tail than on popular questions.
- The work argues that long-tail QA is where selective use of external memory becomes most valuable.
