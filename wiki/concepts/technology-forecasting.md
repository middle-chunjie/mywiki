## Definition

**Technology Forecasting** (技术预测) — the task of estimating future technological developments or emphases from historical technical, market, or innovation signals.

## Key Points

- The paper treats technology forecasting as a company-specific ranking problem rather than a market-wide trend estimation problem.
- Its target is each firm's next-year technology distribution over CPC groups, not just a binary prediction for one field.
- The proposed DTF framework combines internal patent content, external technology evolution, competitor relations, and temporal dynamics.
- The evaluation is performed on patent histories from USPTO data and measured with `NDCG@K`.
