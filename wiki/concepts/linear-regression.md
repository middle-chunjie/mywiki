## Definition

**Linear Regression** (线性回归) — a modeling problem that fits linear coefficients to explain a target variable from one or more input variables.

## Key Points

- The paper uses a one-dimensional linear-regression task with parameters `w` and `b` as its continuous optimization case study.
- Synthetic data are generated by `y = w_true x + b_true + ε` over `50` points with Gaussian noise.
- OPRO starts from `5` random `(w, b)` pairs and asks the optimizer LLM to propose lower-value candidates from the best `20` historical pairs.
- Results show LLMs can often infer local descent directions from the trajectory even without access to the explicit formula.
