## Definition

**Docstring Generation** (文档字符串生成) — the automatic production of documentation strings that describe the semantics, inputs, outputs, and usage of program units such as functions.

## Key Points

- [[cui-2022-codeexp-2211-15395]] focuses on long-form explanatory docstrings rather than one-line summaries.
- The paper shows that existing corpora contain many docstrings that are short, stale, or semantically incomplete.
- The best-performing models generate structured sections for arguments, returns, and exceptions, approaching human-written docstrings on the evaluation set.
- The work uses Python function-docstring pairs as the primary setting for studying this generation problem.
