## Definition

**Information Seeking** — the process of locating, disambiguating, and synthesizing evidence from external information sources to answer a query or complete a task.

## Key Points

- The paper organizes information-seeking tasks into three levels based on initial uncertainty and how hard that uncertainty is to reduce.
- BrowseComp-en and BrowseComp-zh are presented as high-uncertainty Level-3 tasks that require adaptive exploration rather than fixed multi-hop lookup.
- WebSailor is trained specifically for open-web information seeking where the solution path is not predefined.
- The authors report that training on hard information-seeking tasks remains compatible with simpler tasks such as SimpleQA.
