## Definition

**Benchmarking** (基准评测) — the design and use of standardized evaluation tasks, metrics, and protocols to compare systems under controlled conditions.

## Key Points

- SkillsBench benchmarks the efficacy of an augmentation artifact, not just raw model capability, by pairing each task with no-skills and with-skills conditions.
- The paper uses `84` evaluated tasks, `11` domains, `7` model-harness configurations, and `7,308` valid trajectories to support cross-condition comparison.
- Its construction pipeline combines community task submission, automated validation, leakage checks, and human review to improve benchmark quality.
- The authors argue that paired evaluation should become standard practice for studying skills and similar augmentation methods.
