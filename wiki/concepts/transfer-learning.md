## Definition

**Transfer Learning** (迁移学习) — improving a low-resource task by initializing or jointly training it with parameters learned from a higher-resource source task.

## Key Points

- The survey identifies transfer learning as a core strategy for low-resource MNMT.
- A standard recipe is to train a parent model on a high-resource pair and fine-tune a child model on the low-resource pair.
- Language relatedness strongly affects transfer gains, especially for low-resource settings.
- The paper also covers alternatives such as meta-learning, joint training, lexical transfer, and syntactic transfer.
