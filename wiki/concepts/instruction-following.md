## Definition

**Instruction Following** (指令遵循) — the ability of a language model to stay on task and produce outputs that conform to the user's requested format, constraints, and intent.

## Key Points

- The imitation models improve substantially over raw GPT-2 and LLaMA baselines in perceived instruction following according to both crowd workers and GPT-4 judges.
- Much of the apparent success of ChatGPT imitation comes from learning to reproduce high-level answer structure such as overview paragraphs, lists, and authoritative tone.
- Human preference ratings saturate quickly as more imitation data is added, suggesting that instruction following is easier to copy than deeper reasoning or factual knowledge.
- The paper treats improved instruction following as real but insufficient evidence that model capabilities have transferred.
