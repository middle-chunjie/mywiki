## Definition

**Political Stance Control** (政治立场控制) — steering generation so wording and framing reflect a target political orientation or ideological perspective.

## Key Points

- The paper studies pro-Russia versus anti-Russia generation using `744` manually labeled English tweets.
- A second case study learns contrasting media perspectives from one article per source on `5` controversial news events.
- The examples show stance-dependent wording changes, such as framing Crimea as an "invasion" versus a "geopolitical earthquake."
- The authors present this as evidence that LM-Switch can capture narrative and agenda differences beyond simple sentiment polarity.
