## Definition

**Knowledge Association** (知识关联) — the ability of a model to connect newly provided knowledge with pre-existing knowledge through relational reasoning chains.

## Key Points

- KA questions are built from multi-hop chains that start at an artificial entity and end in an existing entity or fact.
- This is the hardest ALCUNA capability for all evaluated models, including ChatGPT.
- Providing chain entities in the context substantially improves KA, indicating that the main bottleneck is linking new and internal knowledge rather than generic reasoning alone.
