## Definition

**Persona** (用户画像) — an abstract user profile representing a cohort with shared preferences, goals, or interests that can be used to guide generation or evaluation.

## Key Points

- The paper models latent preferences with personas rather than with individual user histories.
- E-commerce personas include price, quality, brand reputation, features and functionality, and ethical considerations.
- General-knowledge personas emphasize discussion, history, events, people, or locations.
- The click simulator samples personas uniformly during interaction simulation, making personas the main unit of population heterogeneity.
