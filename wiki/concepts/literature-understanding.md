## Definition

**Literature Understanding** (文献理解) — the ability of an agent to retrieve, read, synthesize, and answer questions about scientific papers with evidence-backed outputs.

## Key Points

- In AstaBench this category includes search, multiple-choice QA, long-form QA, and literature-review table generation.
- ScholarQA-CS2 measures answer quality with citation recall, citation precision, answer relevance, and answer coverage.
- ArxivDIGESTables-Clean evaluates end-to-end table generation by unrolling tables into statements and checking entailment against a reference table.
- Literature tasks are the strongest area for current science agents, with top long-form QA systems scoring around the mid-to-high `80s`.
