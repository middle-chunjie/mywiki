## Definition

**Document Reasoning** (文档推理) — reasoning over visually structured documents by jointly reading text, layout, tables, figures, and page-level context to answer questions or reconstruct content.

## Key Points

- GPT-4V is shown reading floor plans, posters, exam papers, charts, tables, and multi-page technical reports from images.
- The paper reports that the model can reconstruct tables in Markdown, answer chart questions, and summarize multi-page technical content.
- Performance remains incomplete on detail-heavy inputs, with missed specifics such as the correct `1861` example count and `HOG` features in one technical report.
- Visual referring prompts are proposed as a way to highlight lines or regions inside documents and charts when finer-grained grounding is needed.
