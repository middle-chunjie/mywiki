## Definition

**Complex Engineering Solution Design** (复杂工程方案设计) — a task in which a system must generate a complete, feasible engineering plan that satisfies multiple interacting real-world constraints rather than merely answering a factual question.

## Key Points

- The paper defines this as a gap left by prior RAG work, which mostly studies multi-hop QA and long-form QA.
- Inputs are engineering requirements with several coupled constraints, such as rainfall, soil, and seismic conditions.
- Outputs are expert-style end-to-end solutions, not short entity answers or loosely integrated summaries.
- The authors argue the task requires both broad domain knowledge and iterative reliability checking during generation.
- SolutionBench is introduced specifically to evaluate this capability under realistic engineering scenarios.
