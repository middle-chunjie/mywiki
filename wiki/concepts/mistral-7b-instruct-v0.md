## Definition

**Mistral-7B-Instruct-v0** — The instruction-tuned variants of the Mistral 7B language model (versions v0.1 and v0.2), fine-tuned for instruction following using supervised fine-tuning and RLHF.

## Key Points

- 7B parameter open-weight model from Mistral AI with strong instruction-following capability.
- v0.1 and v0.2 differ in training data and alignment quality; v0.2 shows improved instruction adherence.
- Commonly used as a baseline in retrieval and reranking benchmarks.
