## Definition

**Planning** (规划) — the process of decomposing goals, sequencing actions, and satisfying constraints to produce an executable strategy.

## Key Points

- Planning is one of the real-world agent skills the paper explicitly targets, especially in TravelPlanner and ScienceWorld.
- EvoAgent improves planning quality by generating specialists with different emphases and then integrating their candidate solutions.
- The paper argues that prompt-only methods such as CoT or Self-Refine are insufficient for complex planning under multiple constraints.
- In TravelPlanner, the generated specialists help cover diverse requirements such as transportation, food, and commonsense travel constraints.
