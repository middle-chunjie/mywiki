## Definition

**Participatory AI** (参与式人工智能) — an approach to AI development that actively includes affected or relevant people in data collection, evaluation, governance, or design decisions rather than treating them as passive data sources.

## Key Points

- PRISM frames broader human participation in alignment as a design goal, recruiting `1,500` people from diverse geographies instead of relying only on narrow technical communities.
- Participants contribute not just labels but also self-descriptions, free-text constitutions, conversations, and explanations of their preferences.
- The paper treats participatory data collection as useful for surfacing disagreement on controversial and value-laden topics.
- The authors also note a limit: participation remains thin because contributors still do not directly control deployed model behavior.
