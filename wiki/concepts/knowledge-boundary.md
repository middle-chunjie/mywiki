## Definition

**Knowledge Boundary** (知识边界) — the effective limit of what a model can answer reliably from its internal parameters before it should defer, retrieve, or ask for external information.

## Key Points

- SMART frames strategic tool use as a knowledge-boundary recognition problem rather than a generic tool-availability problem.
- The dataset is built to expose this boundary by mixing substeps that are easy for the model with substeps that genuinely require tools.
- The method seeks alignment between the model's subjective confidence and its actual knowledge boundary so it can decide when tools are necessary.
- The paper links poor knowledge-boundary awareness to both wasted computation and degraded downstream accuracy.
