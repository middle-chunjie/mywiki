## Definition

**Pivot Translation** (śěĘŤĹīÁŅĽŤĮĎ) ‚ÄĒ translating between a source and target language by routing through an intermediate pivot language that has better parallel resources.

## Key Points

- The survey treats pivoting as one of the three main low/zero-resource MNMT strategies.
- It distinguishes run-time cascades from pre-training approaches that create pseudo-parallel corpora for direct source-target fine-tuning.
- Joint training and teacher-student learning are presented as ways to reduce pivot error propagation.
- The paper notes that even a small clean source-target corpus can rival much larger pseudo-parallel data for pivot-based adaptation.
