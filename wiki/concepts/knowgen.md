## Definition

**KnowGen** — a method for constructing novel but plausible entity knowledge by recombining structured attributes and relations from related existing entities.

## Key Points

- KnowGen starts from ontology-style triplets over entities, attributes, and relations rather than free-form text.
- The method combines heredity, variation, dropout, and extension so an artificial entity stays class-consistent while remaining distinct from existing entities.
- Numeric attributes are perturbed with Gaussian noise, while non-numeric values and relation targets are replaced using sibling entities.
- The method is designed to generate long-lasting new knowledge that remains novel to all evaluated models.
