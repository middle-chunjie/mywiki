## Definition

**Hallucination** (幻觉) — the generation of unsupported or false claims that are not warranted by the evidence available to the model.

## Key Points

- The paper motivates citation-grounded generation as a direct response to hallucination in LLM-based information seeking.
- ALCE separates correctness from citation quality so that a fluent answer can still be penalized if its claims are unsupported by cited evidence.
- On ELI5, even the strongest evaluated systems leave roughly half of outputs without complete citation support, indicating persistent hallucination risk.
- The study argues that requiring citations can improve both answer verifiability and pressure models to stay faithful to retrieved passages.
