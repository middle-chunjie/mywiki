## Definition

**Technology Tracing** (技术追踪) — the task of following and predicting how a specific actor's technology emphasis evolves over time.

## Key Points

- The paper defines technology tracing for high-tech companies as forecasting next-year technology shares from prior patent histories.
- It explicitly models dynamic interactions between companies and technologies across multiple years.
- Competitive relations among companies and collaborative relations among technologies are treated as first-class signals rather than side information.
- The method outputs a ranked technology distribution for each company instead of a single best technology.
