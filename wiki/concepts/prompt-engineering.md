## Definition

**Prompt Engineering** (提示工程) — the practice of designing or rewriting natural-language inputs so a language model produces more useful, accurate, or safe outputs.

## Key Points

- The paper positions BPO as an automated, preference-aware form of prompt engineering rather than manual prompt crafting.
- Unlike task-specific search methods such as OPRO, BPO performs sample-specific prompt rewriting conditioned on preference data.
- The optimized prompts often add explanations, clarifications, hints, or safety constraints that make the user's intent easier for the model to act on.
- The paper argues prompt engineering becomes especially valuable when target models are closed APIs and cannot be further fine-tuned.
