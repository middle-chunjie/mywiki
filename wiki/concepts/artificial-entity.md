## Definition

**Artificial Entity** (人工实体) — a newly constructed entity inserted into an existing knowledge system with plausible but previously unseen properties and relations.

## Key Points

- ALCUNA creates artificial biological organisms by modifying properties of real taxa from the same class.
- Each artificial entity is designed to preserve class commonality while differing from its parent through changed, dropped, and extended properties.
- The benchmark uses artificial entities to avoid leakage from model pretraining corpora while still supporting realistic reasoning tasks.
