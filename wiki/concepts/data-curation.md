## Definition

**Data Curation** (数据整理) — the selection, cleaning, filtering, and structuring of source material to maximize training value and reduce noise, corruption, or harmful content.

## Key Points

- Phi-4's report frames careful curation of both synthetic seeds and organic corpora as the central reason a `14B` model can compete with much larger systems.
- The team builds custom pipelines for HTML, TeX, PDF, Word, ePub, code, and multilingual web sources to preserve math, tables, code blocks, and forum structure.
- The authors argue that even small seed errors can cascade into severe degradations in downstream synthetic data quality.
