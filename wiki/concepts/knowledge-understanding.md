## Definition

**Knowledge Understanding** (知识理解) — the ability of a model to read newly provided knowledge and answer direct questions about it correctly.

## Key Points

- In ALCUNA, KU questions are generated directly from one-hop property triplets of artificial entities.
- KU isolates whether the model can use the provided new knowledge without needing conflict resolution or cross-knowledge reasoning.
- ChatGPT performs best on KU, but open models remain substantially weaker across zero-shot and few-shot settings.
