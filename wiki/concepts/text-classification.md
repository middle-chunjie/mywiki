## Definition

**Text classification** (文本分类) — the task of assigning one or more predefined labels to a text sequence based on its semantic content.

## Key Points

- [[unknown-nd-improving-2401-02993]] focuses on non-knowledge-intensive text understanding tasks such as sentiment analysis, opinion polarity, and question-type classification.
- The paper treats text classification as a prompt-based masked-word prediction problem rather than a direct classifier over pooled features.
- Retrieval augmentation is used to improve classification accuracy without concatenating long textual contexts into the prompt.
- Benchmarks such as SST-2, SST-5, MR, CR, MPQA, SUBJ, TREC, and CoLA are used as representative text classification tasks.
