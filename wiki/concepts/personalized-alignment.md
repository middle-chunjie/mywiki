## Definition

**Personalized Alignment** (个性化对齐) — the alignment objective of adapting model behavior to the preferences, values, or needs of specific individuals or sub-populations instead of optimizing for a single universal target.

## Key Points

- PRISM links each conversational rating to a participant profile, making individual-level alignment analysis possible.
- The paper explicitly contrasts abstract stated preferences with contextual in-situ preferences, showing that the two can diverge.
- Individualized annotations let researchers study whether different users consistently prefer different model behaviors or model families.
- The dataset supports personalized-alignment research without actually deploying personalized models, so it is primarily an observational resource.
