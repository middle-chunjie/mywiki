## Definition

**Knowledge Transfer** (知识迁移) — the reuse of representations or parameters learned from one language pair, language, or resource condition to improve performance on another.

## Key Points

- The survey frames knowledge transfer as the main reason multilingual training can help low-resource translation.
- Benefits are strongest when related languages share lexical or syntactic structure.
- Transfer can happen through shared encoders, common subword vocabularies, aligned embeddings, or parent-to-child parameter initialization.
- The paper argues that compact multilingual models generalize better because they are exposed to diverse languages.
