## Definition

**BM25** — a bag-of-words ranking function that estimates document relevance from term frequency, document length, and inverse document frequency.

## Key Points

- [[li-2023-skcoder-2302-06144]] uses BM25 over natural-language descriptions to retrieve a similar code snippet before sketch extraction.
- The paper implements BM25 with [[lucene]] because it offers a practical balance between retrieval quality and low computational cost.
- Retrieval is performed against the training split for HearthStone and Magic and against mined GitHub projects for AixBench-L.
- SKCoder uses `Top-5` BM25 results to construct training triples and `Top-1` retrieval during inference.
