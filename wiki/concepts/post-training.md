## Definition

**Post-Training** — the stage after base pretraining where a model is adapted with supervision, preference optimization, or reinforcement learning for a target behavior.

## Key Points

- WebSailor is presented as a complete post-training methodology for web-agent reasoning rather than a new pretraining recipe.
- Its pipeline combines synthetic data generation, reasoning reconstruction, rejection-sampling fine-tuning, and reinforcement learning.
- The paper argues that targeted post-training can close much of the gap between open-source agents and proprietary systems on difficult browsing tasks.
- The strongest gains come from aligning training data and objectives with high-uncertainty information-seeking behavior.
