## Definition

**Diversity Metric** (多样性度量) — a quantitative measure of how varied a set of samples is with respect to some chosen representation or notion of similarity.

## Key Points

- The paper argues that many ML diversity metrics are too specialized because they rely on references, labels, or domain-specific features.
- It positions Vendi Score as a general diversity metric that can be instantiated with different similarity functions across molecules, images, text, and datasets.
- The paper emphasizes that a useful diversity metric should have interpretable scale and should not collapse when correlations or duplicates are present.
