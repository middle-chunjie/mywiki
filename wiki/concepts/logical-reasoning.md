## Definition

**Logical Reasoning** (逻辑推理) — the ability to derive valid conclusions from stated conditions, temporal relations, or other formally constrained premises.

## Key Points

- [[weng-2023-large-2212-09561]] evaluates logical reasoning with the Date Understanding benchmark.
- In this paper, logical reasoning uses True-False Item Verification rather than Condition Mask Verification.
- Self-verification improves Date Understanding on `code-davinci-001` from `38.72` to `39.03` and on `code-davinci-002` from `65.43` to `66.57`.
- The reported gains are smaller than on arithmetic tasks, suggesting that verification targets are harder to specify in logical reasoning.
