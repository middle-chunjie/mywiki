## Definition

**Data Standardization** (数据标准化) — the process of mapping heterogeneous raw data into a shared representation that supports consistent analysis, exchange, and downstream processing.

## Key Points

- The paper frames lack of standardization, rather than lack of raw data, as the main bottleneck for large-scale agent supervised fine-tuning.
- ADP standardizes mismatched action spaces, observation formats, and metadata conventions across coding, tool-use, and web datasets.
- Once standardized, datasets can be compared with common statistics such as trajectory length, action mix, and function-thought coverage.
- The standardization layer also improves reproducibility because agent-specific training converters consume the same intermediate format.
