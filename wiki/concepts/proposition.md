## Definition

**Proposition** (命题) — a minimal, self-contained textual unit that expresses one atomic fact and can be interpreted independently of its source passage.

## Key Points

- The paper defines propositions by three criteria: distinct meaning, minimality, and stand-alone contextualization.
- Proposition units are designed to avoid the extra distractor content often bundled into passages and compound sentences.
- In FACTOIDWIKI, propositions average `11.2` words, much shorter than sentences (`21.0`) and passages (`58.5`).
- The authors use propositions as retrieval units rather than only as analysis objects, showing direct gains in retrieval and QA.
- Proposition quality is produced automatically through a distilled generator and is mostly faithful, minimal, and self-contained.
