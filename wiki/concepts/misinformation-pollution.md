## Definition

**Misinformation Pollution** (错误信息污染) — the contamination of contextual evidence with false or misleading information that can push a model away from truthful answers.

## Key Points

- The survey treats misinformation pollution as a major cause of both [[context-memory-conflict]] and [[inter-context-conflict]].
- It covers poisoned retrieved corpora, fabricated documents, misleading user messages, and prompt-based attacks.
- The paper connects this phenomenon to related behaviors such as [[sycophancy]], where models follow user opinions instead of evidence.
- Reviewed defenses include vigilant prompting, query augmentation, and discriminator-based filtering of unreliable evidence.
