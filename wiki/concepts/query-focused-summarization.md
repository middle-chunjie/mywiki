## Definition

**Query-Focused Summarization** (面向查询的摘要) — the task of producing a summary tailored to a specific information need rather than summarizing a document or corpus generically.

## Key Points

- The paper argues that global corpus questions in RAG systems are fundamentally QFS problems rather than passage retrieval problems.
- GraphRAG answers QFS queries by generating partial answers from community summaries and then reducing them into a final global answer.
- The evaluation questions are designed to require whole-corpus understanding instead of retrieval of isolated low-level facts.
- GraphRAG improves comprehensiveness and diversity over vector RAG on these query-focused summarization tasks.
