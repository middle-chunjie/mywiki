## Definition

**Time-Sensitive Question** (时间敏感问题) — a question whose correct answer can change as the world state changes over time.

## Key Points

- The paper argues time-sensitive questions should almost always trigger retrieval because a static model may hold outdated answers.
- TAQA and FreshQA are used as representative datasets for this scenario.
- UAR separates time-sensitive questions from generic factual questions instead of assuming all factual tasks behave the same.
- Strong retrieval rates on time-sensitive datasets are a central reason for UAR's downstream gains.
