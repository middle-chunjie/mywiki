## Definition

**Contrastive Evaluation** (对比式评测) — an evaluation design that scores a model through carefully defined contrasts rather than isolated absolute metrics.

## Key Points

- KoLA's contrastive evaluation system combines standardized relative scores across heterogeneous tasks with a separate self-contrast protocol for knowledge creation.
- The benchmark uses contrasts both across models on the same task and within a single model's paired generations to reduce biases from incomparable raw metrics or writing style.
- This design is central to KoLA's claim that benchmark results should be numerically comparable, diagnostically interpretable, and more faithful to the targeted knowledge ability.
