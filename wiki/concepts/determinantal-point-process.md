## Definition

**Determinantal Point Process** (行列式点过程) — a probabilistic model over subsets that favors diversity by assigning probability according to determinants of kernel submatrices.

## Key Points

- The paper discusses DPPs as a related kernel-based object for diverse subset selection.
- It contrasts the determinant objective of DPPs with the entropy-based Vendi Score, arguing that the latter is easier to interpret as an effective number of distinct items.
- The discussion also notes a practical limitation of determinant-based diversity measures: exact duplicates force the determinant to zero.
