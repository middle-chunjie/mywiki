## Definition

**Compositional Task** (组合式任务) — a user request that bundles multiple simple requirements into one natural-language instruction, forcing an agent to decompose and coordinate several operations.

## Key Points

- WildToolBench treats compositionality as a common property of real user behavior rather than an artificially difficult corner case.
- In the benchmark, compositional tasks are the main source of pressure on tool orchestration quality.
- The paper shows that performance degrades sharply when compositional tasks require both sequential and parallel tool dependencies.
- The authors argue that benchmark realism depends more on such natural composition than on synthetic complexity alone.
