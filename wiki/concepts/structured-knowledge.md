## Definition

**Structured Knowledge** (结构化知识) — knowledge represented as explicit entities, attributes, values, and relations instead of only natural-language descriptions.

## Key Points

- The paper models each entity with attribute triplets `(e, a, v)` and relation triplets `(e, r, e')`.
- Structured input lets the benchmark generate both direct and relational question types systematically.
- The authors report that JSON-form structured knowledge is easier for tested LLMs to use than equivalent natural-language descriptions.
