## Definition

**Contextual Suggestion** (上下文建议) — the act of retrieving resources that add useful background or related information to an interaction without necessarily answering an explicit query.

## Key Points

- ProCIS treats document retrieval as contextual suggestion for ongoing human conversations.
- Suggested documents are graded by how much useful context they contribute, not only by literal topical overlap.
- The benchmark distinguishes between useful context, partial relevance, and irrelevance for each candidate document.
- Both the reactive and proactive tasks are framed around contextual suggestion rather than direct question answering.
