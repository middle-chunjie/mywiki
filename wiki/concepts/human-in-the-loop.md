## Definition

**Human-in-the-Loop** (人在回路中) — a workflow in which automated model outputs are iteratively reviewed, corrected, or extended by humans as part of the core process.

## Key Points

- The taxonomy refinement loop uses GPT-4 for provisional mapping and human authors for adjudicating uncovered or ambiguous examples.
- Human authors also create missing prompts to balance low-resource safety categories.
- The paper releases a large human judgment dataset because automated judges need human labels for both evaluation and supervised tuning.
- This design treats human review as essential infrastructure rather than a one-off validation step.
