## Definition

**Knowledge-Rich Text Synthesis** (知识密集文本合成) — the generation of informative, topic-dense textual content by conditioning an LLM on prompts that elicit specific expertise or perspectives.

## Key Points

- The paper uses personas to induce Quora-style articles and other informative passages from relevant viewpoints.
- Persona conditioning is intended to surface both topical knowledge and perspective-specific framing from the underlying model.
- The resulting texts are proposed as useful synthetic corpora for pretraining or post-training.
- The method is presented as broadly scalable because Persona Hub spans many professions, interests, and cultural backgrounds.
