## Definition

**Multi-Agent System** (多智能体系统) — a system in which multiple agents interact, coordinate, or compete to achieve outcomes that depend on their joint behavior.

## Key Points

- CAMEL studies a simple but extensible two-agent assistant-user architecture as its base multi-agent system.
- The paper formalizes the system through message passing over a shared conversation state `M_t`.
- It argues that the same framework can be extended beyond two agents by using message-passing graphs.
- The paper emphasizes that system-level behaviors, including loops and coordination failures, cannot be understood from single-agent prompting alone.
