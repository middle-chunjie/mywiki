**Finding**: at the sampled depth, hub concepts in a research wiki built from recent papers rarely contain explicit contradictions — sources generally build on each other. Real contradictions are more likely in *narrower* methodological concepts where different papers propose incompatible approaches. L1 is most valuable on user-targeted specific concepts, not on hub sweeps.

**Recommendation**: invoke L1 manually on specific concepts of interest, e.g., `lint phase 2 L1 concept=<slug>`.
