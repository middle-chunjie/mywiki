## Description

Zhoujun Cheng is a researcher at [[shanghai-jiao-tong-university]] and the first author of [[cheng-2023-batch-2301-08721]]. In this paper, he helps introduce batch prompting as an efficiency-oriented prompting method for LLM APIs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the batch prompting framework and its theoretical token-cost analysis.
- Led experimental validation across 10 datasets and multiple OpenAI model families.
