## Description

Huazhong Yang is a co-author of the Skeleton-of-Thought paper and part of the [[tsinghua-university]] author team listed on the manuscript. The paper associates Yang with the project introducing SoT for efficient LLM generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the SoT research paper and its system-level motivation.
- Contributed to the framing of answer-structure planning as an efficiency technique.
