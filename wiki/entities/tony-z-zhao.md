## Description

Tony Z. Zhao is a co-first author (equal contribution with Eric Wallace) of [[zhao-2021-calibrate-2102-09690]], affiliated with UC Berkeley. He is known for research on improving reliability of few-shot in-context learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the contextual calibration procedure for correcting output-distribution biases in few-shot language model inference.
- Contributed the empirical analysis of majority label bias, recency bias, and common token bias across multiple prompt configurations.
- Released the accompanying codebase for reproducing few-shot calibration experiments.
