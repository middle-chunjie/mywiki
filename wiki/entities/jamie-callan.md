## Description

Jamie Callan is a researcher at [[carnegie-mellon-university]] and a co-author of [[gao-2021-scaling-2101-06983]]. In this paper Callan contributes to a retrieval-oriented method for scaling contrastive learning under constrained GPU memory.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the gradient-cache approach for large-batch contrastive optimization.
- Participated in the DPR-based evaluation showing single-GPU reproduction of large-batch dense retrieval training.
