## Description

Xiao Liu is a researcher affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]] and [[zhipu-ai]], and a co-first author of [[cheng-2024-blackbox-2311-04155]]. He is listed with the KEG group at Tsinghua University in the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the BPO method for black-box alignment of large language models.
- Helped position BPO as a practical alternative and complement to RLHF and DPO.
