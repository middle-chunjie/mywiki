## Description

Donald Metzler is a Google Research co-author of [[pradeep-2023-how]] and a prominent contributor to generative retrieval research. In this paper he helps frame and evaluate the scaling limits of the paradigm on realistic passage-retrieval corpora.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the first large-scale study of generative retrieval on the full MS MARCO passage ranking task.
- Helps position the results against dense dual-encoder baselines and earlier DSI-style systems.
