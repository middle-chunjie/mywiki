## Description

Aidan M. Swope is a co-author of [[yang-nd-leandojo]] and is affiliated with [[nvidia]] in this paper. His role is tied to the open-source theorem proving system and the evaluation of retrieval-augmented proof search.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the LeanDojo release that makes Lean data extraction and interaction reproducible.
- Contributes to the ReProver evaluation showing gains over no-retrieval and GPT-4 baselines.
