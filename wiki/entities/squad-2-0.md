## Description

SQuAD 2.0 is the single-turn reading comprehension dataset that [[li-2023-sm-2312-16511]] converts into synthetic multi-turn conversations.

## Key Contributions

- Provides contexts together with existing single-turn QA pairs that seed S2M generation.
- Supplies the raw material from which the paper creates `19K` synthetic conversations and `342K` questions.
