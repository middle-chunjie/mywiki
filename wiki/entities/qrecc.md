## Description

QReCC is a large-scale open-domain conversational QA and retrieval dataset used as a main training and evaluation benchmark in this paper. ConvAug is trained on QReCC and also transferred from it to zero-shot CAsT benchmarks.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the primary benchmark for normal evaluation of ConvAug.
- Provides the source-domain training data for the paper's zero-shot transfer experiments.
