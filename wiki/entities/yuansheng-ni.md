## Description

Yuansheng Ni is a researcher at the University of Waterloo and a co-author of MMLU-Pro. The paper associates Ni with the construction and analysis of a harder multi-discipline LLM benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MMLU-Pro and its two-stage expert-review pipeline.
- Helped evaluate how harder distractors and reasoning-focused questions change model rankings.
