## Description

Gao Huang is the corresponding author of [[jiang-2022-crossmodal-2211-09623]] and was affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]] and BNRist. He is the senior author overseeing the paper's Cross-Modal Adapter work.

## Key Contributions

- Supervised the parameter-efficient CLIP adaptation project for text-video retrieval.
- Co-authored the paper's architectural design and large-scale benchmark evaluation.
