## Description

Benjamin Chang is a researcher affiliated with Johns Hopkins University and a co-author of [[weller-2024-followir-2403-15246]]. He contributes to the paper's study of long natural-language instructions in retrieval evaluation and training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the construction and analysis of the FollowIR benchmark.
- Helps evaluate how retrieval models respond to altered relevance instructions rather than query-only matching.
