## Description

VATEX is a multilingual video-language dataset used as a text-video retrieval benchmark in [[jiang-2022-crossmodal-2211-09623]]. The paper evaluates on the validation split following prior work.

## Key Contributions

- Shows that Cross-Modal Adapter matches CLIP4Clip on text-to-video `R@1 = 59.3` with under `1M` trainable parameters.
- Provides evidence that the shared-adapter design transfers beyond smaller datasets such as MSVD and MSR-VTT.
