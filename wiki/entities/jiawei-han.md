## Description

Jiawei Han is a researcher at [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and a co-author of [[gao-2021-scaling-2101-06983]]. In this source Han is part of the collaboration that formalizes and evaluates exact large-batch contrastive training with cached representation gradients.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the mathematical factorization underlying gradient cache.
- Helped validate the method on [[dpr]] retrieval with large in-batch negative sets.
