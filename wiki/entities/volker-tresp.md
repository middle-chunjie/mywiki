## Description

Volker Tresp is a co-author of [[yan-2026-memoryr-2508-19828]] affiliated with Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich and the Munich Center for Machine Learning. In this paper, he contributes to the design of long-term memory for LLM agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Memory-R1 framework for learned memory management and utilization.
- Helped evaluate the approach across multiple long-context dialogue benchmarks.
