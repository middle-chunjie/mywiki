## Description

Chao Huang is a researcher at [[university-of-hong-kong]], the corresponding author of [[ren-2024-representation]], and one of the main designers of the RLMRec framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the proposal of mutual-information-based semantic alignment for recommendation representation learning.
- Oversaw the empirical validation of RLMRec across multiple datasets, backbones, and robustness settings.
