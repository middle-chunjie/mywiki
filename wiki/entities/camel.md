## Description

CAMEL is the open-source agent framework and dataset-generation library introduced in [[li-2023-camel-2303-17760]] for studying cooperative interactions among language-model agents.

## Key Contributions

- Implements the role-playing and inception-prompting workflow used throughout the paper.
- Produces the AI Society, Code, Math, and Science datasets used for analysis and fine-tuning.
- Serves as a research platform for probing agent coordination failures, alignment issues, and controllability extensions.
