## Description

Marcel Binz is a researcher at the MPRG Computational Principles of Intelligence, [[max-planck-institute-for-biological-cybernetics]], and a co-author of this paper on adapting large language models into cognitive models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the CENTaUR framing that turns frozen LLaMA embeddings into predictors of human choice.
- Contributes the empirical analysis showing strong fit on choices13k, the horizon task, and a held-out transfer task.
