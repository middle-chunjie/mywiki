## Description

Chenyan Xiong is a researcher at Microsoft Research and a co-author of [[li-2023-structureaware-2305-19912]]. His role in the paper centers on dense retrieval and structure-aware representation learning for structured data.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed SANTA's retrieval-oriented pretraining framework for structured documents.
- Helped validate that alignment-based pretraining materially improves zero-shot and supervised retrieval effectiveness.
