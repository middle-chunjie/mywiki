## Description

Alessandro Cappelli is a co-author of [[penedo-2023-refinedweb-2306-01116]] and a member of the Falcon LLM team. In this paper, he is listed under affiliation marker `^1` in the author block.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the RefinedWeb dataset paper and its large-scale web-data refinement study.
- Helped document the relation between filtering, deduplication, and zero-shot performance.
