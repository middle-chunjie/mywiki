## Description

Proof-Pile is an open-source dataset of mathematical and scientific documents (primarily arXiv papers) maintained by the Hoskinson Center, used as a long-context language modeling benchmark to evaluate perplexity on technical text.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a long-context evaluation corpus of arXiv papers longer than 32K tokens for perplexity measurement.
- Used alongside [[pg19]] and [[codeparrot]] to benchmark sliding-window perplexity of context extension methods in the Activation Beacon paper.
