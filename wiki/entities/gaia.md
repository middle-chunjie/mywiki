## Description

GAIA is the agent benchmark used in [[wu-2025-agentic-2502-04644]] to test reasoning, browsing, and tool-use ability across tasks of different difficulty levels. It is also the main benchmark used in the paper's memory and agent-design ablations.

## Key Contributions

- Evaluates integrated reasoning plus tool-use rather than pure closed-book QA.
- Shows the paper's Mind-Map and agentic tool design improving average performance to `66.13%`.
