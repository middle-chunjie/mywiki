## Description

Bowen Jin is a co-author of [[jin-2025-llm-2502-03699]] affiliated with [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and [[google-cloud-ai-research]]. In this paper Jin helps formulate LLM alignment as an IR problem and introduces LARPO.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the retriever-reranker framing that maps policy LLMs to retrievers and reward models to rerankers.
- Helped propose the iterative LARPO training pipeline with ranking losses, hard negatives, and candidate-list memorization.
- Contributed to the benchmark evaluation on AlpacaEval 2 and MixEval.
