## Description

Max Tegmark is the senior author of [[liu-2024-kan-2404-19756]] and is affiliated in the paper with Massachusetts Institute of Technology and the NSF Institute for Artificial Intelligence and Fundamental Interactions. In this source he is associated with the paper's overall thesis that KANs can serve as interpretable function-based models for AI + Science.

## Key Contributions

- Senior co-author of the paper introducing KANs.
- Helped frame KANs as a bridge between neural approximation, interpretability, and scientific discovery.
