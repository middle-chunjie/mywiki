## Description

Srivathsan Koundinyan is a researcher at Microsoft Research India and a co-author of [[java-2026-frugalrag-2507-07634]]. The paper associates him with the design and evaluation of a retrieval-efficient multi-hop QA pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the two-stage FrugalRAG training recipe.
- Contributed to experiments comparing retrieval quality, answer quality, and search latency across three benchmarks.
