## Description

Jane Dwivedi-Yu is a coauthor of [[jiang-2023-active-2305-06983]] and is listed with [[meta-ai]] in this paper. She contributes to the evaluation of forward-looking retrieval on open-ended factual generation tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Helped assess FLARE on long-form QA and open-domain summarization benchmarks.
- Contributed to the comparison between implicit masked queries and explicit generated-question queries.
- Coauthored the broader argument that retrieval should track future generation intent rather than past context alone.
