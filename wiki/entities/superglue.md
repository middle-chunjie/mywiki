## Description

SuperGLUE is a suite of challenging natural language understanding benchmarks. [[chowdhery-2022-palm-2204-02311]] evaluates both few-shot and finetuned PaLM on SuperGLUE to compare decoder-only scaling against strong encoder-decoder baselines.

## Key Contributions

- Acts as the paper's main finetuning benchmark for general NLU.
- Quantifies the remaining gap between few-shot prompting and supervised adaptation.
