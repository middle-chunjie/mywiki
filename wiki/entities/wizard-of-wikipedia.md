## Description

Wizard of Wikipedia is the dialogue-generation benchmark used in [[jin-2024-bider-2402-12174]] to test whether refined retrieval evidence helps knowledge-grounded conversation. In this paper, it is one of the settings where BIDER shows the largest relative gain over the original prompt.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the dialogue-generation evaluation setting for BIDER.
- Shows an improvement from `0.086` to `0.122` F1 while reducing input length from `710` to `69` tokens.
