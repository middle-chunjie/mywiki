## Description

text-bison is the instruction-tuned scorer model used in [[yang-2024-large-2309-03409]] for Q_begin and Q_end prompt-optimization experiments on GSM8K and BBH.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the scorer for the BBH experiments where OPRO improves over baseline instructions on most tasks.
- Is also evaluated as an optimizer model in mathematical optimization and prompt optimization comparisons.
