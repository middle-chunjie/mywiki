## Description

Lun Du is a co-author of [[shi-2023-cocosoda-2204-03293]] and was affiliated with [[microsoft-research]] in this paper. Du contributes to the design and evaluation of the CoCoSoDa retrieval model.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the multimodal contrastive objective used for aligning code and queries.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison against UniXcoder, CodeBERT, and GraphCodeBERT.
