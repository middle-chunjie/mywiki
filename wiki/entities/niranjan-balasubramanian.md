## Description

Niranjan Balasubramanian is a researcher at [[stony-brook-university]] and a co-author of [[trivedi-2023-interleaving-2212-10509]]. His affiliation in this paper connects him to few-shot retrieval, reasoning, and open-domain QA research.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the IRCoT method for alternating retrieval and reasoning steps.
- Contributed to the few-shot experimental setup and evaluation across four multi-hop QA benchmarks.
