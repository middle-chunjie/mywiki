## Description

Petros Maniatis is a [[google-brain]] researcher and co-author of [[vasic-2019-neural-1904-01720]]. In this paper he contributes to a neural approach that jointly detects, localizes, and repairs variable-misuse bugs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the multi-headed pointer model for joint bug localization and repair.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison against enumerative and graph-based baselines.
