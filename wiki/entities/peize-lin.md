## Description

Peize Lin is a co-author of [[lu-2019-molecular]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China's Key Laboratory of Quantum Information. The paper places him among the contributors connecting the model design to quantum-chemistry motivation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the multilevel interaction formulation used by MGCN.
- Helped position the method against DFT-based molecular property prediction.
