## Description

Ming Tan is a researcher affiliated with [[aws-ai-labs]] and a co-author of [[ding-nd-crosscodeeval]]. In this paper he is part of the team constructing and evaluating a benchmark for cross-file code completion.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the construction of CROSSCODEEVAL from recent, permissively licensed repositories.
- Contributed to the benchmark design that ties code completion quality to cross-file retrieval quality.
