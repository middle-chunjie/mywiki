## Description

Lingyong Yan is a researcher at [[baidu]] and a co-author of [[sun-2023-chatgpt-2304-09542]], which studies large language models as passage re-ranking agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the paper's evaluation of ChatGPT and GPT-4 on TREC, BEIR, and multilingual ranking benchmarks.
- Contributes to the framing of permutation generation as a practical ranking interface for chat LLMs.
