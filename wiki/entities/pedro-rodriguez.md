## Description

Pedro Rodriguez is a researcher affiliated with the University of Maryland and Facebook Reality Labs at the time of this paper, and a lead author of [[rodriguez-2021-evaluation]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the DAD leaderboard formulation that applies Bayesian IRT to NLP evaluation.
- Contributes the empirical SQuAD 2.0 analysis linking item parameters to ranking reliability and annotation quality.
