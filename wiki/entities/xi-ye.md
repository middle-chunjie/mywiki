## Description

Xi Ye is the lead author of [[ye-2025-longproc-2501-05414]]. The provided paper markdown lists the author block under Princeton Language and Intelligence and The University of Texas at Austin, but it does not cleanly preserve Xi Ye's per-author affiliation marker.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the introduction of LONGPROC as a benchmark for long procedural generation.
- Helped define the six-task benchmark structure and its deterministic evaluation setup.
- Contributed to the empirical analysis of 23 long-context language models across `0.5K`, `2K`, and `8K` output regimes.
