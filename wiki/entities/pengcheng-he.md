## Description

Pengcheng He is a co-author of [[ma-nd-query-2305-14283]] affiliated with [[microsoft-azure-ai]]. In this paper, he contributes to the design and evaluation of query rewriting for black-box retrieval-augmented LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the Rewrite-Retrieve-Read method and its trainable variant.
- Contributed to the experimental setup using ChatGPT and Vicuna-13B readers.
