## Description

Howard Chen is a co-author of the C-STS paper; in this source, he is listed with the Princeton University author group.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the C-STS benchmark paper and its experimental framing.
- Contributed to the study of condition-specific sentence similarity.
- Helped report empirical gaps between classic STS systems and conditional evaluation.
