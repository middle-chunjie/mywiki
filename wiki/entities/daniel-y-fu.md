## Description

Daniel Y. Fu is a Stanford University researcher and co-first author of [[saad-falcon-2024-benchmarking-2402-07440]]. In this paper he helps adapt Monarch Mixer into a long-context retrieval encoder.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designs the M2-BERT encoder built on Monarch Mixer for sequences up to `32k` tokens.
- Contributes to the pretraining and fine-tuning recipe used to make state-space retrieval practical at long context.
