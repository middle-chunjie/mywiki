## Description

Alex Mallen is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and the first author of this paper. His contribution here centers on analyzing when language models can rely on memorized factual knowledge versus external retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced the POPQA benchmark for probing long-tail factual memorization.
- Co-developed the Adaptive Retrieval heuristic that selectively triggers retrieval by popularity and relation type.
