## Description

Mark Steyvers is a researcher in Cognitive Sciences at [[university-of-california-irvine]] and a co-author of [[wang-2024-large-2301-11916]]. His affiliation grounds the paper's Bayesian and cognitive-science flavored interpretation of latent concepts in prompting.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the Bayesian interpretation of in-context learning as posterior inference over task concepts.
- Co-authored the empirical study connecting theory to real-world LLM behavior.
