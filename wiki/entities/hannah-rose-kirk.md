## Description

Hannah Rose Kirk is a researcher at the University of Oxford and the lead author of the PRISM dataset paper on pluralistic human feedback for LLM alignment.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRISM dataset paper.
- Helped introduce a dataset linking participant profiles, stated preferences, and live conversational ratings.
- Contributed to empirical analysis of topic diversity, model preference variation, and welfare effects in alignment data.
