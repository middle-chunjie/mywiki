## Description

Yubo Ma is the first author of [[ma-2023-large-2303-08559]] and a researcher at [[nanyang-technological-university]]. He leads the paper's empirical comparison between direct LLM prompting and supervised SLMs for few-shot IE.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designs the unified benchmark across four IE tasks and nine datasets.
- Co-proposes the adaptive [[filter-then-rerank]] pipeline for hard-sample reranking.
