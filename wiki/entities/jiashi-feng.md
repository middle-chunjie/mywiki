## Description

Jiashi Feng is a ByteDance-affiliated author of [[jin-2024-mvadapter-2301-07868]]. Feng is listed among the researchers behind the proposed multimodal transfer architecture.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MV-Adapter and its PE-VTR problem formulation.
- Participated in the empirical validation of CLIP-based adapter transfer across five benchmarks.
