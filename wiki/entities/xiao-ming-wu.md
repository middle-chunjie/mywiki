## Description

Xiao-Ming Wu is a faculty member at the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), and a co-author of the MTP-CLNN framework for new intent discovery.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MTP-CLNN method for unsupervised and semi-supervised new intent discovery.
