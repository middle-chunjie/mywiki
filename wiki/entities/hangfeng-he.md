## Description

Hangfeng He is the first author of [[he-2022-rethinking-2301-00303]] and is affiliated with the University of Rochester in this paper. The footnote notes that part of the work was completed while he was at the University of Pennsylvania.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed Rethinking with Retrieval as a post-processing method for evidence-grounded LLM inference.
- Helped design the decomposition-based retrieval pipeline across commonsense, temporal, and tabular reasoning tasks.
- Contributed to the paper's analysis of explanation faithfulness and retrieval-based error correction.
