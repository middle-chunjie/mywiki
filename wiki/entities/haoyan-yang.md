## Description

Haoyan Yang is the first author of [[yang-2023-prca]], affiliated with [[ping-an-technology-shenzhen]] and [[new-york-university]]. The paper studies PRCA for fitting black-box LLMs in retrieval question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRCA method for adapting frozen black-box generators in retrieval QA.
- Helped evaluate the method across multiple retriever-generator combinations and datasets.
