## Description

ARC-Challenge is the commonsense science-question benchmark used in [[unknown-nd-bstar]] to test whether B-STaR improves self-improvement beyond math and coding tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the paper's commonsense reasoning evaluation benchmark.
- Shows B-STaR improving Pass@1 to `73.0`, above online RFT's `71.2`.
