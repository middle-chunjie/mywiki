## Description

L1 is the paper's family of length-controllable reasoning models, built by applying [[length-controlled-policy-optimization]] to [[deepscaler-1.5b-preview]]. It is designed to satisfy exact or maximum token budgets while preserving reasoning accuracy.

## Key Contributions

- Demonstrates strong length adherence across requested budgets from `512` to `3600` tokens.
- Outperforms [[s1]] on fixed-budget math reasoning and generalizes to OOD tasks.
- Shows that a long-CoT reasoning model can also operate as a [[short-reasoning-model]].
