## Description

Andrei Ungureanu is a researcher affiliated with Jina AI GmbH in Berlin and a co-author of [[g-nther-2025-jinaembeddingsv-2506-18902]]. He is listed among the authors of the paper introducing `jina-embeddings-v4` and the Jina-VDR benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the multimodal multilingual embedding model and its evaluation.
- Contributed to the paper's benchmarking claims on visually rich retrieval and multilingual search.
