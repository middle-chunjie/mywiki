## Description

Zhijing Wu is a co-author of [[su-2024-dragin-2403-10081]] affiliated with the School of Computer Science and Technology at [[beijing-institute-of-technology]]. In this paper, Zhijing Wu is part of the collaboration that studies attention-guided dynamic retrieval for LLM generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the DRAGIN framework, including its evaluation on multihop and reading-comprehension benchmarks.
- Helped demonstrate that attention-informed query formulation can outperform sentence-local retrieval heuristics.
