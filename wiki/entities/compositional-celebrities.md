## Description

Compositional Celebrities is the paper's automatically generated benchmark of celebrity-centered 2-hop questions designed to measure compositional reasoning in language models.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `8.6k` questions across `17` templates that combine individually common facts in unlikely ways.
- Enables direct estimation of the compositionality gap by pairing each 2-hop question with decomposed sub-questions.
