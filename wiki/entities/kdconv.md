## Description

KdConv is a Chinese multi-domain [[knowledge-grounded-dialogue]] dataset covering music, travel, and film. [[wang-2024-improving-2401-04361]] uses it as one of the main benchmarks for evaluating EnCo.

## Key Contributions

- Provides about `85K` KGD samples from `4.5K` dialogues and `86K` utterances.
- Includes `13.1K` entities, `9.1K` relations, and `157.0K` knowledge triples.
- Serves as the benchmark where EnCo reports best BLEU across all three domains.
