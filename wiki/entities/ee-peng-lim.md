## Description

Ee-Peng Lim is a researcher at Singapore Management University and a co-author of [[hu-2023-llmadapters-2304-01933]], focused on PEFT-based adaptation of LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LLM-Adapters framework paper and its evaluation protocol over fourteen reasoning datasets.
- Helped show that smaller adapted LLMs can approach or exceed larger closed models on selected tasks.
