## Description

text-davinci-003 is the large proprietary language model baseline evaluated in the paper through the Azure API, both with and without monitor-guided decoding.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the largest-model comparison point in the study.
- Its monitored variant improves from `CR 62.66` to `74.26` and from `NIM 86.18` to `91.19`.
