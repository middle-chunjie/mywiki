## Description

Ibrahim Abdelaziz is a researcher at IBM Research, T.J. Watson Research Center and the lead author of the BLANCA benchmark paper on language models for code-related text understanding.

## Key Contributions

- Co-created BLANCA for evaluating textual artifacts about code.
- Co-authored the use of Graph4Code-linked data for benchmark construction.
- Evaluated fine-tuning and multi-task training for code-understanding embeddings.
