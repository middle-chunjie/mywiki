## Description

Sikuan Yan is a co-author of [[yan-2026-memoryr-2508-19828]] affiliated with Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich and the Munich Center for Machine Learning. In this paper, he contributes to the RL-based memory-agent framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Memory-R1, which learns memory operations instead of scripting them heuristically.
- Helped evaluate the framework on long-horizon dialogue benchmarks including LoCoMo.
