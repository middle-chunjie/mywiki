## Description

The University of Newcastle is the Australian institutional affiliation of [[hongyu-zhang]] in [[gu-2018-deep]]. In this paper it is one of the collaborating institutions behind the CDCS code-search study.

## Key Contributions

- Hosted part of the collaboration that developed cross-domain deep code search with few-shot meta learning.
- Contributed institutional support for the empirical evaluation on domain-specific programming languages.
