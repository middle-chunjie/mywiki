## Description

Gemini 1.0 Pro is the Google DeepMind base model fine-tuned for the coding experiments in [[kumar-2024-training-2409-12917]].

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the SCoRe backbone for MBPP training and HumanEval evaluation.
- Demonstrates strong gains from multi-turn RL over prompting and Pair-SFT baselines.
