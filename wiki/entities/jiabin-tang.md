## Description

Jiabin Tang is a researcher affiliated with [[musketeers-foundation-institute-of-data-science]] and a co-author of [[tang-2024-graphgpt-2310-13023]], which proposes GraphGPT for graph-oriented instruction tuning of LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the design of the GraphGPT framework and its dual-stage graph instruction tuning pipeline.
- Participated in the evaluation of zero-shot and supervised performance on citation-graph benchmarks.
