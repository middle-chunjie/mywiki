## Description

Sichuan University (四川大学) is a comprehensive research university located in Chengdu, China. Its College of Computer Science has active research groups in NLP and information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Affiliated institution for Hang Zhang and Jiancheng Lv on the AR2 dense retrieval paper.
