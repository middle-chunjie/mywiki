## Description

Qi Gou is a researcher at the State Key Laboratory for Novel Software Technology, Nanjing University, and the first author of [[gou-2023-diversify]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed RAST for retrieval-augmented style transfer in question generation.
- Contributed to the RL training setup that jointly optimizes retrieval and generation for diversity-aware QG.
