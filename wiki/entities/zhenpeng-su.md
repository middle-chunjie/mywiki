## Description

Zhenpeng Su is a researcher affiliated with the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. He co-authored [[ma-2026-lightretriever-2505-12260]] on efficient LLM-based retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the query-lightweight architecture that keeps full document-side encoding.
- Contributed to the training setup and large-scale retrieval evaluation reported in the paper.
