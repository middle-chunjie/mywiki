## Description

Xi Victoria Lin is a researcher at [[meta-ai]] and a co-author of [[ma-2025-drama-2502-18460]]. She works on multilingual dense retrieval and on training small retrievers from LLM teachers.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduces DRAMA, which combines diverse LLM augmentation with pruned Llama backbones for smaller dense retrievers.
- Helps evaluate the method across BEIR, MIRACL, MTEB retrieval subsets, and long-context retrieval benchmarks.
- Contributes to the multilingual training recipe that mixes English web, multilingual web, and multilingual Wikipedia augmentation data.
