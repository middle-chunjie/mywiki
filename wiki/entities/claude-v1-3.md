## Description

Claude-v1.3 is a target black-box LLM in [[jiang-2023-llmlingua-2310-05736]], used to test whether prompt compression transfers beyond the GPT family.

## Key Contributions

- Confirms that LLMLingua generalizes across closed-model APIs, not just GPT-3.5-Turbo-0301.
- Under GSM8K, the paper reports `83.51` EM at `5x` compression and `82.61` EM at `14x` compression with Claude-v1.3.
