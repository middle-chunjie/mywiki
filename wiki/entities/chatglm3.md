## Description

ChatGLM3 is one of the six large language models sampled to construct the real-world dataset in [[xu-2024-facerag-2407-01080]].

## Key Contributions

- Provides `200` generated RAG answers for the real-world benchmark.
- Has a reported average answer length of `158.0` characters and an error rate of `36.5%`.
