## Description

Changle Qu is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and the first author of [[qu-2024-completenessoriented-2405-16089]] on completeness-oriented tool retrieval for LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed COLT, a two-stage retrieval framework that combines semantic matching with graph-based collaborative learning.
- Helped introduce the COMP@K metric and the ToolLens benchmark for multi-tool retrieval.
