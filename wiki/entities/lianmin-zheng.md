## Description

Lianmin Zheng is a PhD student at UC Berkeley and co-author of H2O. He is a primary contributor to the FlexGen and vLLM inference systems, and to the MT-Bench / Chatbot Arena evaluation framework.

## Key Contributions

- Brought FlexGen integration expertise for the H2O throughput evaluation pipeline.
- Co-authored the latency and throughput benchmarking results on T4 and A100 GPUs.
