## Description

SemEval-2014 Task 4 is the benchmark dataset for aspect-based sentiment analysis, providing Restaurant and Laptop review corpora annotated with aspect terms and three-class sentiment polarity labels (positive, negative, neutral). It is the most widely used ABSA evaluation benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the Restaurant split (2164 train / 728 test positive examples) and Laptop split (994 train / 341 test) used in [[zhang-2022-incorporating]] and numerous prior ABSA works.
- Establishes the three-class sentiment polarity evaluation protocol that standardizes comparison across ATAE-LSTM, BERT-based, and graph-based ABSA methods.
