## Description

Keshav Santhanam is a researcher at [[stanford-university]] and a co-author of the DSP paper. The paper connects his retrieval work to prompt-based, knowledge-intensive NLP pipelines built from frozen modules.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the DSP framework and its retrieval-heavy implementations for open-domain, multi-hop, and conversational QA.
- Helps evaluate DSP with CoLBERTv2-based search over aligned Wikipedia corpora.
