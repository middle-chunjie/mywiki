## Description

Allan Hanbury is a TU Wien researcher and co-author of [[hofst-tter-2021-efficiently-2104-06967]]. In this paper he contributes to the design and analysis of efficient dense retrieval training under constrained compute budgets.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes the TAS-Balanced training strategy for resource-efficient dense retrieval.
- Contributes to the paper's robustness and systems-level analysis.
