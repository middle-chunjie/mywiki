## Description

Xilun Chen is a co-author of [[lin-2023-how-2302-07452]] affiliated with Meta AI. He helps position DRAGON as a BERT-base dense retriever that improves both in-domain and zero-shot effectiveness through better augmentation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the final DRAGON recipe mixing cropped sentences, synthetic queries, and progressive label augmentation.
- Contributes to the analysis showing diverse teachers outperform a single cross-encoder teacher.
