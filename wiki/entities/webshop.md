## Description

WebShop is the web-interaction benchmark used in [[tu-2026-dynamic-2603-28716]] to evaluate D2Skill on long-horizon, language-mediated decision making.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the two main evaluation environments for D2Skill.
- Shows that D2Skill improves both average score and success rate over GRPO and SkillRL under Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct.
