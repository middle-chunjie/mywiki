## Description

William Yang Wang is a faculty researcher in the Department of Computer Science at [[university-of-california-santa-barbara]] and the senior author of [[wang-2024-large-2301-11916]]. The paper proposes a transferable demonstration selection method derived from a latent-variable account of in-context learning.

## Key Contributions

- Led the work linking latent concept learning with practical in-context example selection.
- Oversaw the multi-model evaluation showing transfer from small selection models to larger LLMs.
