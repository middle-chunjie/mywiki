## Description

Howard Yen is a co-author of [[gao-2024-how-2410-02660]] and is affiliated with Princeton Language and Intelligence at [[princeton-university]]. The paper also cites his prior work on HELMET and long-context language modeling as part of its evaluation and training context.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the experimental study of long-context data mixtures, scaling, and SFT choices.
- Helped connect ProLong's development to HELMET-based long-context evaluation.
- Contributed to the analysis of realistic downstream tasks beyond synthetic recall tests.
