## Description

ProCIS is the benchmark dataset introduced in [[samarinas-2024-procis]] for proactive and reactive document retrieval in multi-party conversations. It aligns Reddit threads with Wikipedia documents and provides a densely judged test set with supporting evidence annotations.

## Key Contributions

- Provides over 2.8 million weakly labeled conversations for training and development.
- Supplies a test collection with graded relevance labels and ideal evidence positions for proactive evaluation.
- Serves as the benchmark on which lexical, sparse, dense, multi-vector, and LLM-based retrieval methods are compared.
