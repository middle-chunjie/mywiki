## Description

Yuxiang Wei is a researcher at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and the first author of [[wei-2023-magicoder-2312-02120]]. In this paper he helps formulate OSS-INSTRUCT and the Magicoder training pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed OSS-INSTRUCT for synthesizing code instruction data from open-source snippets.
- Helped train and evaluate Magicoder and MagicoderS across Python, multilingual, and data-science benchmarks.
