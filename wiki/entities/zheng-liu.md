## Description

Zheng Liu is a researcher affiliated with the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence and Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He is listed as a corresponding author on the Retro* paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Retro* and its BRIGHT evaluation.
- Helped shape the paper's reasoning-intensive retrieval framing.
- Served as a corresponding author for the work.
