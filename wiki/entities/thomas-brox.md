## Description

Thomas Brox is a professor at the University of Freiburg leading the Computer Vision Group, with broad expertise in optical flow, dense correspondence, and self-supervised representation learning.

## Key Contributions

- Senior author of CrossCLR; leads the University of Freiburg computer vision group.
- Known for contributions to optical flow estimation (FlowNet) and self-supervised feature learning.
