## Description

AI21 Labs is the institutional affiliation of all authors on [[ratner-2023-parallel-2212-10947]] and the organization behind the [[jurassic-1]] model family used heavily in the paper's experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Introduced [[parallel-context-windows]] as a training-free long-context inference method for decoder-only LLMs.
- Evaluated PCW on Jurassic-1, LLaMA, and GPT-2 across classification, information extraction, retrieval QA, and multi-hop QA.
