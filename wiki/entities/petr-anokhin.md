## Description

Petr Anokhin is a researcher affiliated with Learnable Intelligence Lab in Moscow. He is a co-author of [[sorokin-2026-qrag-2511-07328]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored a retriever fine-tuning approach that avoids updating the downstream LLM.
- Helped position Q-RAG as a lower-cost alternative to LLM-centric multi-step retrieval training.
