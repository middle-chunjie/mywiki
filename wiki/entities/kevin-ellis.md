## Description

Kevin Ellis is a researcher affiliated in this paper with Cornell University. He is a co-author of [[fijalkow-2022-scaling]] on scalable symbolic search for neural program synthesis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's synthesis benchmark and the evaluation on DreamCoder-style machine-learned PCFGs.
- Helped connect the search algorithms to modern neural program synthesis pipelines in [[fijalkow-2022-scaling]].
