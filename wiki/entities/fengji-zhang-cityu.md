## Description

Fengji Zhang is a researcher at the City University of Hong Kong (CityU). Disambiguated from [[fengji-zhang]] by affiliation: City University of Hong Kong (vs. Microsoft Research in the earlier source). In this paper, Zhang is a lead author of RepoCoder.

## Key Contributions

- Lead author of RepoCoder, proposing the iterative retrieval-generation framework for repository-level code completion.
- Co-designed the RepoEval benchmark covering line, API invocation, and function body completion.
