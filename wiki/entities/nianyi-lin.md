## Description

Nianyi Lin is a coauthor of [[yu-2024-kola-2306-09296]] affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]]. The paper credits the full team with building the KoLA benchmark for world-knowledge evaluation of large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the KoLA benchmark spanning 19 tasks across four knowledge-related ability levels.
- Contributed to KoLA's benchmark construction, evolving-data setup, and evaluation study over open-source and commercial LLMs.
