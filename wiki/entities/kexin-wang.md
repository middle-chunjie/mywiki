## Description

Kexin Wang is the lead author of [[wang-2022-gpl-2112-07577]] and is affiliated with [[technical-university-of-darmstadt]] in this paper. The paper places Wang in work on dense retrieval and unsupervised domain adaptation for information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed GPL, which combines synthetic query generation, hard-negative mining, and cross-encoder pseudo labeling for domain-adapted dense retrieval.
- Led the paper's empirical study over six [[beir]] retrieval datasets.
- Analyzed how training steps, corpus size, and query-generation settings affect GPL performance.
