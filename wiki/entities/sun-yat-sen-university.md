## Description

Sun Yat-sen University is the affiliation of Yipeng Gao and Wei-Shi Zheng in [[gao-2024-sculpting-2311-01734]]. In this source it represents one of the institutions behind MixCon3D.

## Key Contributions

- Provides part of the research team that proposes MixCon3D for language-image-3D contrastive pre-training.
- Contributes authors involved in both the method design and empirical evaluation.
