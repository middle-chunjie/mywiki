## Description

Linjun Zhang is a researcher at Rutgers University and a co-author of [[xu-2026-alternating-2602-01511]]. The Rubric-ARM paper associates Zhang with rubric-guided reward modeling for open-ended LLM evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the method that treats rubrics as latent actions in reward modeling.
- Contributed to experiments on reward benchmarks and out-of-distribution writing evaluation.
