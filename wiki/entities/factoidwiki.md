## Description

FACTOIDWIKI is the processed English Wikipedia corpus introduced in the paper, segmented into passages, sentences, and propositions for controlled retrieval experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a single Wikipedia source indexed at three granularities for fair comparison.
- Contains `256,885,003` proposition units in addition to passage and sentence segmentations.
- Serves as the retrieval corpus for all passage-retrieval and QA evaluations in the paper.
