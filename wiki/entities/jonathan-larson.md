## Description

Jonathan Larson is a Microsoft Research author of [[edge-2024-local-2404-16130]]. Larson is part of the team that introduces GraphRAG and its evaluation for global sensemaking tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the GraphRAG workflow for knowledge-graph-based retrieval and summarization.
- Contributed to the paper's adaptive question generation and evaluation methodology.
