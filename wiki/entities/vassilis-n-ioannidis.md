Vassilis N. Ioannidis is a researcher at Amazon and a co-senior author of [[wu-2024-stark-2404-13207]]. The paper presents him as one of the senior contributors behind the STaRK benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-senior authored the STaRK benchmark paper on semi-structured retrieval.
