## Description

Kun Luo is a researcher affiliated with the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence and the Chinese Academy of Sciences. He is an author of [[li-2024-making-2409-15700]] and studies dense retrieval and LLM-based embedding systems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the `bge-en-icl` paper and its MTEB and AIR-Bench evaluation.
- Contributed to the paper's framing of LLMs as adaptable retrieval encoders.
