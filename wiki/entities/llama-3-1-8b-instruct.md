## Description

Llama3.1-8B-Instruct is the secondary backbone evaluated in the paper. The authors report both direct inference and a weaker Tool-Light variant on this model, highlighting sensitivity to the base model.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the paper's counterpoint showing that Tool-Light does not transfer equally well across backbones.
- The Llama-based Tool-Light variant reaches `36.8` average performance, far below the Qwen-based system.
