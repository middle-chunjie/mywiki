## Description

Wanrong Zhu is a researcher in the Department of Computer Science at [[university-of-california-santa-barbara]] and a co-author of [[wang-2024-large-2301-11916]]. The work connects Bayesian latent-variable reasoning to demonstration selection for in-context learning.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the theoretical framing of LLM prompting as latent-variable inference.
- Contributed to the empirical validation across multiple text-classification datasets and GSM8K.
