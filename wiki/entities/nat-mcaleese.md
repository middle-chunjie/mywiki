## Description

Nat McAleese is a researcher at [[deepmind]] and a co-author of [[menick-2022-teaching-2203-11147]]. He co-designed GopherCite, proposed Inline Evidence with [[vladimir-mikulik]], and supervised the project.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the training scheme and evaluations for GopherCite.
- Proposed the Inline Evidence approach with [[vladimir-mikulik]].
- Supervised the project and contributed to RL infrastructure and paper writing.
