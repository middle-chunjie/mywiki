## Description

Vincent Ng is a researcher at the Human Language Technology Research Institute, [[university-of-texas-at-dallas]], and a co-author of [[niu-2022-deep-2205-11739]]. In this survey, he helps connect source-code pre-training to broader NLP transfer-learning ideas.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the survey's framing of CodePTMs as an extension of the NLP pre-training paradigm.
- Helps articulate why code-specific structure and natural-language signals matter for transfer.
