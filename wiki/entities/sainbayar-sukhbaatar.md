## Description

Sainbayar Sukhbaatar is a Meta AI researcher and co-author of [[weston-2023-system-2311-11829]], where he helps introduce System 2 Attention as a context-regeneration method for LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the S2A method that rewrites context before final answer generation.
- Studies how irrelevant context and user opinions affect factuality, objectivity, and math reasoning robustness.
