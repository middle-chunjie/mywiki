## Description

Zeyu Wang is a researcher at UC Santa Cruz and a co-author of [[gao-2024-sculpting-2311-01734]]. The paper places him on the team that develops MixCon3D for multimodal 3D representation learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the joint image-3D alignment and multi-view training strategy used in MixCon3D.
- Helps establish the experimental evaluation across three zero-shot 3D benchmarks.
