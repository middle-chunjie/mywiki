## Description

Zihan Dong is a researcher at Rutgers University and a co-author of [[xu-2026-alternating-2602-01511]]. This work places Dong in research on rubric-conditioned judging and LLM post-training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Rubric-ARM, which optimizes rubric generation as a latent action for preference prediction.
- Contributed to experiments spanning reward-model evaluation and downstream policy optimization.
