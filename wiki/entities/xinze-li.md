## Description

Xinze Li is a researcher at Northeastern University, China, and the first author of [[li-2023-structureaware-2305-19912]]. He works on structure-aware retrieval and representation learning for structured data.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed SANTA, which combines [[structured-data-alignment]] and [[masked-entity-prediction]] for [[dense-retrieval]] on structured data.
- Helped demonstrate strong zero-shot and fine-tuned gains on code search and product search.
