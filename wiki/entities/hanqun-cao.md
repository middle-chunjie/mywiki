## Description

Hanqun Cao is a researcher affiliated in this paper with The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Zhejiang Lab, and the AI Lab of Westlake University. He is a co-author of the survey that synthesizes the theory, algorithms, and applications of generative diffusion models.

## Key Contributions

- process design, likelihood optimization, and distribution bridging. Co-authored a broad taxonomy of diffusion-model improvements spanning acceleration
- score-SDE, and probability-flow-ODE formulations. Helped curate the survey's mathematical overview of DDPM
