## Description

SlimPajama is the large-scale pretraining corpus used for the xLSTM language-modeling experiments. The paper trains matched models on both `15B` and `300B` tokens from SlimPajama.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the main training corpus for the paper's matched perplexity and scaling comparisons.
- Enables the `2048`-context training and `16384`-context extrapolation study reported in the large-model section.
