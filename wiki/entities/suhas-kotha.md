## Description

Suhas Kotha is a co-author of [[springer-2024-repetition-2402-15449]] affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]]. He is part of the team showing that repeated-input prompting can improve decoder-only embedding quality without architectural changes.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the empirical study of echo embeddings across zero-shot and supervised settings.
- Helped compare repeated-input embeddings against classical causal and bidirectional baselines on MTEB.
