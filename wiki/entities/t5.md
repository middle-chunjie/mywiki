## Description

T5 is the pretrained text-to-text Transformer family used to initialize the encoder and define the retrieval model scales in [[du-2024-bottleneckminimal-2405-10974]]. The paper evaluates BMI with T5-mini, T5-small, and T5-base checkpoints inside the NCI architecture.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies pretrained encoder weights for the sequence-to-sequence retrieval model used in the experiments.
- Makes it possible to test how index quality interacts with model capacity, with BMI showing its largest gains on smaller T5 variants.
