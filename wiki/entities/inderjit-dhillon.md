## Description

Inderjit Dhillon is a co-author of FLOATER and is affiliated with both UT Austin and Amazon Inc. in this paper. The paper positions FLOATER as a parameter-efficient and inductive alternative to fixed or learned positional encodings.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the continuous dynamical formulation for positional encoding in Transformer models.
- Helped frame the method around parameter efficiency, compatibility, and length extrapolation.
