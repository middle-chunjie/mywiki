## Description

Xueqing Deng is a ByteDance-affiliated author of [[jin-2024-mvadapter-2301-07868]]. Deng is part of the author group introducing efficient multimodal transfer for retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MV-Adapter method for PE-VTR.
- Helped evaluate adapter-based retrieval on multiple public datasets.
