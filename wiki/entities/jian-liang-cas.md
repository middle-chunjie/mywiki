## Description

Jian Liang is a researcher at the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), affiliated also with the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS). His research interests include domain adaptation and domain generalization.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Environment Label Smoothing work for Domain Adversarial Training.
