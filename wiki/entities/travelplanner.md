## Description

TravelPlanner is a real-world planning benchmark used in [[yuan-2024-evoagent-2406-14228]] to evaluate whether generated specialists can satisfy user preferences, commonsense constraints, and hard constraints in travel itineraries.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the paper's main real-world planning testbed with multiple explicit and implicit constraints.
- Shows EvoAgent improving GPT-4 final pass rate from `2.2` to `7.2` while also improving commonsense and hard-constraint satisfaction.
