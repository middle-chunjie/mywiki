## Description

Ronak Pradeep is a University of Waterloo and Google Research-affiliated researcher, marked in the paper as having completed the work while a student researcher at Google. He is the first-listed co-author of [[pradeep-2023-how]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the large-scale empirical study of generative retrieval on MS MARCO.
- Helps analyze how synthetic queries, identifier design, and model scaling interact at corpus sizes up to `8.8M` passages.
