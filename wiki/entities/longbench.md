## Description

LongBench is a long-context benchmark suite from which [[li-2024-retrieval-2407-16833]] selects seven English, real, query-based datasets for evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies most of the paper's real-task evaluation workload.
- Contributes datasets such as NarrativeQA, Qasper, HotpotQA, MuSiQue, and QMSum to analyze RAG failure modes and routing behavior.
