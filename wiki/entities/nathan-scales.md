## Description

Nathan Scales is a researcher at Google Research / Brain Team and a co-author of the least-to-most prompting paper. He has also co-authored work on compositional generalization (CFQ benchmark, Keysers et al. 2020).

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the least-to-most prompting strategy for enabling complex reasoning in LLMs.
- Co-authored work measuring compositional generalization on realistic data (ICLR 2020).
