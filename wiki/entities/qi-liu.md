## Description

Qi Liu is an author of [[fang-2021-guided]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China. The paper positions him as a contributor to the guided-attention model and its evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored GACEN, which fuses topic-aware and position-aware attention before CRF decoding.
- Contributed to the analysis showing strong gains over prior concept-extraction baselines.
