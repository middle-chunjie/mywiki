## Description

Luke Zettlemoyer is a University of Washington researcher and QLoRA co-author associated with efficient adaptation methods for large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the QLoRA method and its evaluation at scales up to LLaMA 65B.
- Contributed to the study's framing of data quality, benchmark validity, and efficient LLM fine-tuning.
