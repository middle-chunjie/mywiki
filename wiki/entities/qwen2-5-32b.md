## Description

Qwen2.5-32B is the base language model that DAPO trains into a stronger reasoning model through large-scale reinforcement learning.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the pretrained starting point for the DAPO experiments.
- Reaches `50` avg@32 on [[aime-2024]] after DAPO training according to the paper.
