## Description

Haode Zhang is a researcher at the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). He is a co-author of MTP-CLNN for new intent discovery and has worked on pre-training methods for few-shot intent classification.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the multi-task pre-training (MTP) strategy for new intent discovery.
- Prior work on effectiveness of pre-training for few-shot intent classification (Zhang et al., 2021d).
