## Description

C4 is one of the main evaluation corpora used in [[borgeaud-2022-improving-2112-04426]] for measuring language-model bits-per-byte under different model scales, retrieval database sizes, and neighbor counts.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as a central benchmark showing RETRO's gains over non-retrieval baselines, including `0.78 -> 0.66` bpb at `7.5B`.
- Supports the paper's ablation studies on retrieval design choices and cross-attention placement.
