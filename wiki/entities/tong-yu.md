## Description

Tong Yu is a co-author of [[wu-2024-coral-2403-06447]] and is affiliated with [[adobe-research]]. In this paper he contributes to the integration of collaborative retrieval and LLM prompting for recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CoRAL framework that augments LLM prompts with retrieved collaborative information.
- Helped position the method against both collaborative-filtering and LLM-based recommendation baselines.
