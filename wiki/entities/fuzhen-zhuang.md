## Description

Fuzhen Zhuang is a researcher affiliated with the Institute of Artificial Intelligence and SKLSDE, School of Computer Science, Beihang University, and a co-author of [[qin-2023-metaoptimized]]. This paper associates Fuzhen Zhuang with the meta-optimized contrastive-learning framework proposed for sequential recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the two-stage optimization strategy for training encoder and augmenters.
- Helped position MCLRec against prior self-supervised sequential recommendation baselines.
