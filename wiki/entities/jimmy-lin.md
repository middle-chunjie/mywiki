## Description

Jimmy Lin is a University of Waterloo researcher and co-author of [[hofst-tter-2021-efficiently-2104-06967]]. The paper builds on his prior dense retrieval and TREC-DL work while extending efficient first-stage retrieval training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the paper's evaluation framework over TREC-DL and MSMARCO Passage Ranking.
- Helps position TAS-Balanced within practical low-latency and reranking pipelines.
