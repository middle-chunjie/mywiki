## Description

Shu-tao Xia is a co-author of [[guo-2024-refir-2410-05601]] and is affiliated with Tsinghua University and Peng Cheng Laboratory in the paper. He appears on the study proposing ReFIR for grounding LRMs with retrieved visual references.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored ReFIR and its training-free mechanism for injecting retrieved textures into diffusion-based restoration models.
