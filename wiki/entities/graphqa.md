## Description

GraphQA is the benchmark introduced in [[he-2024-gretriever-2402-07630]] for question answering over real-world textual graphs. It standardizes multiple graph QA datasets into a common graph-plus-question format spanning commonsense, scene, and knowledge-graph settings.

## Key Contributions

- Unifies [[explagraphs]], [[scenegraphs]], and [[webquestionssp]] under a single evaluation interface.
- Provides the empirical test bed used to compare G-Retriever with inference-only, prompt tuning, and LoRA baselines.
