## Description

Sebastian Hofstatter is a TU Wien researcher and the lead author of [[hofst-tter-2021-efficiently-2104-06967]]. In this paper he studies efficient dense retrieval training through topic-aware batch construction and teacher-guided supervision.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes TAS-Balanced for training dense retrievers without large-batch infrastructure.
- Co-designs the dual-teacher supervision recipe that combines `BERT_CAT` and ColBERT signals.
