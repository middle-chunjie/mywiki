## Description

Josh Susskind is a coauthor of [[malach-2026-infinity-2510-14826]], listed with Apple affiliation in the extracted paper. In this work he is part of the team studying efficient tool-using SSMs for long-output reasoning and coding tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the paper's study of interactive tool use as a mechanism for overcoming bounded internal memory.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison between SSMs, recurrent models, and Transformer baselines.
- Helped support the coding-task analysis based on filtered SWE-agent trajectories.
