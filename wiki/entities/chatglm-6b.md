## Description

ChatGLM-6B is a bilingual open LLM baseline evaluated in [[chen-2023-benchmarking-2309-01431]] for English and Chinese RAG robustness.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a `6B`-scale open-model comparison point for RGB across all four evaluation abilities.
- Shows moderate noise robustness but poor negative rejection, reaching only `9.00%` exact rejection in English and `6.33%` in Chinese.
