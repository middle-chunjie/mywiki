## Description

Zhuyun Dai is a researcher affiliated with [[google-research]] and a co-author of [[dai-2022-promptagator-2209-11755]]. The paper's author-contribution appendix credits Dai with helping originate the few-shot retrieval idea and building early infrastructure.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed the few-shot retrieval idea behind PROMPTAGATOR.
- Performed early validation experiments and contributed retrieval pipeline infrastructure.
- Participated in the PROMPTAGATOR experiments.
