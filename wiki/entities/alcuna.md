## Description

ALCUNA is the benchmark introduced in [[yin-2023-alcuna-2310-14820]] for evaluating how LLMs handle genuinely new knowledge. It is built from artificial biological entities generated by KnowGen and organized into direct and reasoning-based QA subsets.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `84,351` questions over `3,554` artificial entities derived from structured biological data.
- Separates evaluation into knowledge understanding, knowledge differentiation, and knowledge association.
- Enables model-agnostic testing of new-knowledge behavior without depending on temporal data collection.
