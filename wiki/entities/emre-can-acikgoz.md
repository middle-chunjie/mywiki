## Description

Emre Can Acikgoz is a researcher at [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and a co-author of [[qian-2025-smart-2502-11435]]. He contributes to the paper's metacognition-inspired formulation of strategic tool use.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the SMARTAgent training setup for tool-vs-reasoning decisions.
- Contributed to the construction of SMART-ER across math, time, and intention domains.
- Helped evaluate efficiency gains from reduced tool usage.
