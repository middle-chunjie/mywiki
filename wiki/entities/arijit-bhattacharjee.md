## Description

Arijit Bhattacharjee is a researcher at Iowa State University and a coauthor of [[tehranijamsaz-2024-coderosetta-2410-20527]]. His work in this paper centers on unsupervised translation between `C++`, CUDA, and Fortran with structure-aware pretraining.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the design and evaluation of CodeRosetta across `C++ ↔ CUDA` and `Fortran ↔ C++` translation tasks.
