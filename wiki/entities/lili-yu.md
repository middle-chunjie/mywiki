## Description

Lili Yu is a Meta AI researcher and corresponding author of [[yu-2023-megabyte-2305-07185]]. She is an equal-contribution author on the paper and is affiliated with the team that proposes MEGABYTE for million-byte autoregressive modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MEGABYTE multiscale decoder architecture for tokenization-free byte modeling.
- Helped position the method across text, image, and audio experiments under compute-matched comparisons.
