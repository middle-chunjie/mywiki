## Description

Tim Dettmers is a University of Washington researcher and lead author of the QLoRA paper on low-memory fine-tuning of quantized large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored QLoRA and its memory-saving recipe built from NF4, double quantization, and paged optimizers.
- Helped demonstrate 4-bit fine-tuning results across academic benchmarks and chatbot evaluation.
