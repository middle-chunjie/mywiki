## Description

Zhongxiang Sun is a researcher affiliated with the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence at Renmin University of China. In [[sun-2025-rearter-2501-07861]], he is the first author of ReARTeR on trustworthy process rewarding for retrieval-augmented reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- First author of [[sun-2025-rearter-2501-07861]], which introduces ReARTeR for trustworthy retrieval-augmented reasoning.
- Part of the collaboration reporting evaluation on five public multi-hop QA benchmarks.
