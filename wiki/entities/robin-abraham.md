## Description

Robin Abraham is a Microsoft-affiliated researcher and co-author of [[lin-2023-batchprompt-2309-00384]], which reports competitive accuracy with far fewer calls than SinglePrompt.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the overall BatchPrompt framework and its efficiency-oriented evaluation.
- Contributed to benchmarking the method against SinglePrompt on BoolQ, QQP, and RTE.
