## Description

Tony Yu is a researcher at Georgia Institute of Technology and a co-author of [[xu-2026-alternating-2602-01511]]. He is part of the team that studies rubric-based rewards for non-verifiable LLM alignment.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the alternating RL training procedure linking rubric generation with judgment accuracy.
- Helped validate the method on instruction-following, preference, and creative-writing evaluations.
