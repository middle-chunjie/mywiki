## Description

The University of Waterloo is the institutional affiliation of Sheng-Chieh Lin, Jheng-Hong Yang, and Jimmy Lin in [[hofst-tter-2021-efficiently-2104-06967]]. It is the second academic site behind the paper's TAS-Balanced dense retrieval study.

- Provides the affiliation context for three of the paper's five authors.
- Supports the paper's empirical work on dense retrieval, distillation, and evaluation pipelines.
