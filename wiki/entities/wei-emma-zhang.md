## Description

Wei Emma Zhang is a researcher at Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia, working on adversarial robustness of deep learning models in natural language processing and related areas of machine learning.

## Key Contributions

- First author of the first comprehensive survey on adversarial attacks targeting NLP deep learning models (arXiv 1901.06796).
- Developed taxonomy of white-box and black-box textual adversarial attack methods and defense strategies.
