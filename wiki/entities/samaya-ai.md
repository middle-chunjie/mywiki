## Description

Samaya AI is the industry affiliation for Ashwin Paranjape, Yuhao Zhang, and Jack Hessel in [[weller-2024-promptriever-2409-11136]]. In this source it anchors the collaboration behind Promptriever's instruction-aware retrieval experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Provides part of the author team that builds Promptriever and its synthetic instruction corpus.
- Serves as the industry side of the JHU-Samaya collaboration reported in the paper.
