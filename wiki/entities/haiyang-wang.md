## Description

Haiyang Wang is a co-author of the TokenFormer paper and is affiliated with Max Planck Institute for Informatics and Peking University in this work.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the TokenFormer architecture that replaces linear projections with attention over parameter tokens.
- Contributed to the progressive parameter-scaling experiments from `124M` to `1.4B` parameters.
