## Description

Xiaoxin He is the first author of [[he-2024-gretriever-2402-07630]] and is affiliated with National University of Singapore in the paper. He works on graph learning and graph-aware language-model methods for textual graph understanding.

## Key Contributions

- Led the paper's introduction of the [[graphqa]] benchmark for textual graph question answering.
- Co-developed G-Retriever, combining retrieval, PCST-based subgraph construction, and graph-conditioned generation.
