## Description

Ji-Rong Wen is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and co-author of [[dong-2024-progressive-2412-14835]] on AR-MCTS for multimodal reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the AR-MCTS framework combining active retrieval, MCTS, and progressive PRM alignment.
- Contributed to positioning the method as a plug-and-play verifier-centric approach rather than full multimodal backbone retraining.
