## Description

Peiyi Wang is the lead author of [[wang-2024-mathshepherd-2312-08935]] and is affiliated with Peking University, with a footnote noting contribution during an internship at DeepSeek-AI. In this paper he helps propose the automatic process-annotation framework behind Math-Shepherd.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the automatic process-annotation pipeline for training a [[process-reward-model]].
- Evaluates the method in both verification and [[step-by-step-ppo]] settings.
- Reports large-scale experiments on math reasoning benchmarks with open-source LLMs.
