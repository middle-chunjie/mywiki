## Description

Peng Xu is a co-author of [[liu-2024-chatqa-2401-10225]]. Based on the same recurring author team around [[wei-ping]], [[mohammad-shoeybi]], and [[bryan-catanzaro]], this page treats him as affiliated with the [[nvidia]] research group for this paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ChatQA system that combines stage-wise instruction tuning with conversational retriever adaptation.
- Contributed to the paper's benchmark study over `10` conversational QA datasets and its analysis of unanswerable cases.
