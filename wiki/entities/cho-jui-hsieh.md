## Description

Cho-Jui Hsieh is a co-author of FLOATER and is affiliated with UCLA in this paper. The work uses continuous-time positional dynamics to improve Transformer models without giving up checkpoint compatibility.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored FLOATER and its position-aware bias reformulation for `Q`, `K`, and `V`.
- Contributed to the warm-start training recipe used for both translation and RoBERTa-based downstream tasks.
