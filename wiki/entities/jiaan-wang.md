## Description

Jiaan Wang is the first author of [[wang-2024-improving-2401-04361]] and belongs to the Soochow University-centered author team behind the EnCo framework for robust [[knowledge-grounded-dialogue]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed EnCo, the entity-based contrastive learning framework proposed in the paper.
- Helped design the entity-guided paraphrasing and negative augmentation pipeline.
- Contributed to the evaluation on KdConv, DuConv, and DuRecDial.
