## Description

Chunting Zhou is a [[meta-ai]] researcher and co-author of [[shi-2024-incontext-2310-10638]]. In this paper, she is part of the team studying how to pretrain language models on sequences of related documents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the In-Context Pretraining method for building related-document LM training contexts.
- Contributed to the paper's large-scale evaluation across in-context learning, reading comprehension, and long-context reasoning.
