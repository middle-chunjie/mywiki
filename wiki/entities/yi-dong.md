## Description

Yi Dong is a co-author of [[wang-2023-shall]] affiliated with [[nvidia]]. In this paper he is part of the team reproducing and evaluating large-scale retrieval-augmented decoder-only language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the RETRO reproduction recipe spanning `148M` to `9.5B` parameters over a `330B`-token corpus.
- Contributed to the study showing retrieval pretraining helps knowledge-intensive evaluation and open-domain QA relative to matched GPT baselines.
