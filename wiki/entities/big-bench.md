## Description

BIG-bench is a broad benchmark suite of 150+ difficult language tasks designed to probe generalization and emergent capabilities. [[chowdhery-2022-palm-2204-02311]] uses it to show that PaLM 540B exceeds average human performance in aggregate 5-shot evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a large and diverse stress test for few-shot reasoning and language understanding.
- Supports the paper's analysis of discontinuous capability gains with scale.
