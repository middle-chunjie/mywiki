## Description

Haotao Wang is an author of [[wang-2023-graph-2304-02806]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-texas-at-austin]] in this paper. He co-authors the proposal of Graph Mixture of Experts for large-scale graph learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the GMoE architecture that routes nodes to hop-specific graph experts.
- Helps validate GMoE across OGB graph, node, and link prediction benchmarks.
