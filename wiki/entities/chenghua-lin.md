## Description

Chenghua Lin is a researcher at the [[university-of-manchester]] and corresponding author of [[li-2023-compressing]]. The paper positions Selective Context as a model-agnostic way to reduce long-context inference cost.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the formulation of context pruning via self-information and percentile-based filtering.
- Helped frame the method as complementary to architectural efficiency work such as sparse attention.
