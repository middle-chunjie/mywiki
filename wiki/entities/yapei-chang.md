## Description

Yapei Chang is a researcher at [[university-of-massachusetts-amherst]] and the first author of the BOOOOKSCORE paper on book-length summarization evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed a contamination-aware evaluation setup for summarizing recently published books.
- Co-authored the BOOOOKSCORE metric and the associated coherence error taxonomy.
