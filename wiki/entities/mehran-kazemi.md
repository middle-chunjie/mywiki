## Description

Mehran Kazemi is a Google DeepMind researcher and co-author of [[kazemi-2025-bigbench-2502-19187]], which introduces [[bbeh]] as a harder successor to [[bbh]] for broad LLM reasoning evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the design of `23` harder benchmark tasks spanning logical, temporal, spatial, commonsense, and algorithmic reasoning.
- Helped analyze aggregate and task-specific failure modes for general-purpose and reasoning-specialized models.
