## Description

The University of Massachusetts Amherst is the affiliation listed for both authors of [[samarinas-2024-procis]]. In this source, it is the institutional home from which the ProCIS benchmark is introduced.

## Key Contributions

- Affiliation of Chris Samarinas and Hamed Zamani in the ProCIS paper.
