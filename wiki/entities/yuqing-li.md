## Description

Yuqing Li is a co-author of [[wang-2025-jinarerankerv-2509-25085]] affiliated with Jina AI GmbH and the University of Pittsburgh. The paper credits this author with work on multilingual listwise reranking and its empirical evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored jina-reranker-v3 and its last-but-not-late interaction design.
- Helped evaluate the model on English, multilingual, and code-retrieval benchmarks.
