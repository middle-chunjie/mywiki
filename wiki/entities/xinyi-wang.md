## Description

Xinyi Wang is a researcher in the Department of Computer Science at [[university-of-california-santa-barbara]] and the first author of [[wang-2024-large-2301-11916]]. The paper studies latent-variable explanations of in-context learning and uses them to derive a practical demonstration selection algorithm.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the latent concept interpretation of in-context learning for real-world LLMs.
- Helped design the two-stage pipeline of concept-token learning and posterior-based demonstration selection.
