## Description

Jiacheng Ye is a co-author of [[wu-2023-selfadaptive]] and is affiliated in this paper with [[university-of-hong-kong]]. He contributes to the paper's formulation and evaluation of self-adaptive ICL.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the select-then-rank framework for adaptive demonstration organization.
- Contributes to the analysis of how example labels and prompt ordering affect in-context learning.
- Helps position the method as a route for narrowing the gap between ICL and finetuning.
