Udari Madhushani Sehwag is a Stanford University researcher and co-author of SORRY-Bench. In this source, she contributes to the benchmark's data curation and large-scale safety analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Helped support the paper's empirical evaluation across open and proprietary LLMs.
