## Description

Dale Schuurmans is a Google Research, Brain Team researcher and a co-author of [[wang-2023-selfconsistency-2203-11171]], which introduces self-consistency decoding for chain-of-thought reasoning in large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the self-consistency method that samples multiple reasoning paths and aggregates them by answer agreement.
- Helped demonstrate training-free reasoning gains across arithmetic, commonsense, symbolic, and standard NLP benchmarks.
