## Description

Mathilde Caron is a researcher affiliated in this paper with Meta AI Research, Inria, and Universite Grenoble Alpes. She co-authored the contrastive pre-training approach behind Contriever.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the unsupervised contrastive learning setup used to train Contriever.
- Contributed to the multilingual retrieval results reported for mContriever.
