## Description

PalmTree is the main binary-code Transformer baseline compared against SymC in [[pei-2024-exploiting-2308-03312]]. The paper uses it to contrast pre-training and absolute positional embeddings with architecture-level symmetry preservation.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the primary binary-analysis baseline for function similarity, signature, and memory-region prediction.
- Anchors the paper's efficiency comparison, where SymC reaches comparable performance with dramatically less compute.
