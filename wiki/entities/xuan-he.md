## Description

Xuan He is a researcher at the University of Waterloo and a co-author of MMLU-Pro. The paper associates He with constructing and validating a benchmark for higher-level LLM reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MMLU-Pro and its prompt-robust evaluation design.
- Helped support the benchmark's emphasis on harder reasoning and cleaner annotation.
