## Description

Yupeng Hou is a co-author of [[wu-2024-coral-2403-06447]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-california-san-diego]]. In this source he is part of the collaboration that learns minimal-sufficient collaborative evidence for recommendation prompts.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CoRAL retrieval policy for selecting informative user-item evidence.
- Helped analyze exploration-versus-exploitation behavior across retrieval rounds.
