## Description

James Zou is a researcher affiliated with Stanford University and a co-senior author of AgentFlow. The paper presents him as one of the senior contributors behind the framework's design and evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the AgentFlow project on trainable multi-turn planning and tool use.
- Helped position in-the-flow planner optimization as an alternative to monolithic tool-using policies.
