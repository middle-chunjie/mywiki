## Description

Llama 3 8B is one of the open-source LLM backbones evaluated by [[yeh-2026-lumina-2509-21875]] for Lumina, mainly on RAGTruth and the proxy-detector study.

## Key Contributions

- Reaches `0.7446` AUROC and `0.7874` AUPRC with Lumina on RAGTruth.
- Shows that proxy-detector performance can remain strong even when the detector and generator backbones differ.
