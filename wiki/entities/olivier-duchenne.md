## Description

Olivier Duchenne is a co-author of [[wei-2025-swerl-2502-18449]] and is listed among the Meta-affiliated authors on the paper. He contributes to the dataset and training pipeline behind SWE-RL.

## Key Contributions

- Helps formulate the large-scale pull-request curation pipeline used to build SWE-RL training seeds.
- Contributes to the reinforcement-learning setup that trains Llama3-SWE-RL-70B from open software-evolution data.
