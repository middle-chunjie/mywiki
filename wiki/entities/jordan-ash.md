## Description

Jordan T. Ash is a co-author of the LASER paper and is affiliated with Microsoft Research, New York City. The paper marks him as an equal advisor on the project.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LASER method and its cross-model evaluation on language understanding benchmarks.
- Contributed to the interpretation of higher-order singular components as potential sources of noisy outputs.
