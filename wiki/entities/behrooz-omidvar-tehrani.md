## Description

Behrooz Omidvar-Tehrani is a researcher affiliated with [[amazon-science]] and a co-author of [[guinet-2024-automated-2405-13622]]. In this paper he is associated with the use of hierarchical IRT and task-specific synthetic exams for RAG evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the methodology for corpus-grounded exam generation and filtering.
- Helped develop the interpretability layer that decomposes RAG performance into LLM, retrieval, and ICL components.
- Contributed to the experimental analysis across ArXiv, StackExchange, DevOps, and SEC tasks.
