## Description

Yuqi Zhou is a researcher at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, [[renmin-university-of-china]], and co-first author (equal contribution) of [[zhou-2026-retrieve-2604-04949]], which proposes the LRAT framework for training retrievers from agent trajectories.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the LRAT framework that mines retrieval supervision from deep research agent interaction traces.
- Co-designed the reasoning-aware positive filtering and relevance intensity estimation components of LRAT.
