## Description

Defa Zhu is a researcher on the Seed-Foundation-Model Team at [[bytedance]] and the lead author of [[zhu-2025-hyperconnections-2409-19606]], proposing hyper-connections as a replacement for residual connections in large language model pre-training.

## Key Contributions

- Lead author and primary architect of the [[hyper-connections]] method.
- Designed both static and dynamic HC variants and the initialization strategy that ensures equivalence to Pre-Norm at the start of training.
