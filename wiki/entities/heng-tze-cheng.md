## Description

Heng-Tze Cheng is a researcher affiliated with [[google-deepmind]] and a co-author of [[zheng-2024-take-2310-06117]]. In this work, Cheng contributes to the development and evaluation of Step-Back Prompting across STEM, Knowledge QA, and Multi-Hop Reasoning tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored [[step-back-prompting]] as a prompting technique for improving LLM reasoning via abstraction.
- Co-contributed to the experimental design and benchmarking on MMLU, TimeQA, and MuSiQue.
