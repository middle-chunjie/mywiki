## Description

Amit Sharma is a researcher at Microsoft Research India and a co-author of [[java-2026-frugalrag-2507-07634]]. In this paper he appears as part of the team proposing FrugalRAG for efficient retrieval and reasoning in multi-hop QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored FrugalRAG and its latency-aware framing of retrieval-augmented QA.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison against ReAct, LeReT, Search-R1, and CoRAG-style baselines.
