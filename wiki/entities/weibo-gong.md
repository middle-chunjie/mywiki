## Description

Weibo Gong is a ByteDance-affiliated author of [[jin-2024-mvadapter-2301-07868]]. The paper credits Gong as part of the team proposing a parameter-efficient CLIP transfer method for video-text retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the multimodal adapter design used in the paper.
- Co-authored the empirical study across five video-text retrieval benchmarks.
