## Description

Mu Li is a researcher at Amazon Web Services, known for contributions to distributed machine learning systems and deep learning. He is a co-author of the Auto-CoT paper and co-creator of the MXNet framework and the Dive into Deep Learning textbook.

Disambiguated from other researchers named Mu Li by affiliation: Amazon Web Services.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Auto-CoT, the automatic chain-of-thought prompting method based on diversity-driven question clustering.
- Co-developed the MXNet deep learning framework and the D2L open textbook.
