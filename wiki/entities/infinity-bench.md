## Description

InfinityBench is the very-long-context benchmark suite used in [[li-2024-retrieval-2407-16833]] for the En.QA and En.MC evaluations.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the paper's longest contexts, averaging over `140k` tokens in the selected subsets.
- Reveals cases where limited-context models such as [[gpt-3-5-turbo]] can benefit more from retrieval than from full-context prompting.
