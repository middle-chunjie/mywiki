## Description

FEVER is the fact-verification benchmark used in [[jin-2024-bider-2402-12174]] to measure whether evidence refinement helps text-based verification. The paper reports smaller gains here than on open-domain QA and dialogue.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the fact-verification setting in the five-dataset evaluation suite.
- Shows BIDER improving accuracy from `0.517` to `0.524` while shrinking input from `805` to `93` tokens.
