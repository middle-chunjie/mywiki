## Description

Qiancheng Xu is a researcher in the Department of Computing at [[hong-kong-polytechnic-university]] and the first author of [[xu-2024-enhancing-2406-17465]] on iterative feedback for tool retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed the iterative LLM-feedback framework for improving dense tool retrieval.
- Co-built TR-bench for in-domain and out-of-domain evaluation of tool retrievers.
