## Description

Rupesh Prajapati is a researcher at Pennsylvania State University and a co-author of [[li-2022-alphaprog]]. He is listed among the authors of the ALPHAPROG compiler-fuzzing study.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the end-to-end reinforcement-learning framework for generating valid programs for compiler fuzzing.
- Helped evaluate ALPHAPROG against AFL and analyze discovered BFC bugs.
