## Description

Enhong Chen is an author of [[hao-2020-asgn]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China. In this paper he appears among the senior contributors to the ASGN method and its empirical study.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored ASGN, integrating graph representation learning, pseudo labeling, and active learning.
- Contributed to the experimental validation showing strong gains over supervised and semi-supervised baselines.
