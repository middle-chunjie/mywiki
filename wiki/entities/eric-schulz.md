## Description

Eric Schulz is a researcher at the MPRG Computational Principles of Intelligence, [[max-planck-institute-for-biological-cybernetics]], and co-author of this paper on LLM-based cognitive modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designs the behavioral modeling setup that aligns LLaMA embeddings with human decision data.
- Helps position cross-task transfer and individual-difference modeling as central tests of cognitive adequacy.
