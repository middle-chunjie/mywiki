## Description

Dawei Yin is a researcher at [[baidu]] and co-author of [[qu-2024-completenessoriented-2405-16089]], which proposes a completeness-oriented retriever for LLM tool use.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to framing completeness as a first-class objective in tool retrieval.
- Helped introduce evaluation protocols linking retrieval quality to downstream answer quality.
