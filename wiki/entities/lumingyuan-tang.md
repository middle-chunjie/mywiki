## Description

Lumingyuan Tang is a co-author of [[sun-2024-thinkongraph-2307-07697]] affiliated with the University of Southern California and IDEA Research. The paper lists him among the contributors who develop and evaluate Think-on-Graph.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the triple-path and relation-path formulations used in ToG and ToG-R.
- Contributed to experiments spanning nine reasoning benchmarks.
