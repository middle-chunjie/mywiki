## Description

George-Eduard Zaharia is a researcher at the University Politehnica of Bucharest (Faculty of Automatic Control and Computers), working on NLP topics including complex word identification, cross-lingual transfer, and text simplification.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed a domain-adaptation framework with gradient reversal for complex word identification (CWI) in both monolingual multi-domain and cross-lingual settings.
- Explored character-level BiLSTM combined with Transformer encoders (RoBERTa / XLM-RoBERTa) for lexical complexity prediction, achieving state-of-the-art MAE on CompLex LCP 2021 and CWI 2018.
