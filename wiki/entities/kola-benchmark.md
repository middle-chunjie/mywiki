## Description

KoLA Benchmark is a world-knowledge evaluation benchmark for LLMs introduced in [[yu-2024-kola-2306-09296]]. It organizes 19 tasks across memorization, understanding, applying, and creating, and refreshes evolving test sets every three months.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces a four-level taxonomy for diagnosing knowledge-related LLM abilities.
- Combines Wikipedia-based known data with evolving recent corpora for fairer evaluation.
- Adds standardized cross-task scores and a self-contrast metric for knowledge creation.
