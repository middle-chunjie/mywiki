## Description

Yifeng Ding is a University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign researcher and co-author of [[wei-2023-magicoder-2312-02120]]. In this paper he is part of the group proposing OSS-INSTRUCT and the Magicoder model family.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the training recipe that fine-tunes code LLM backbones on OSS-INSTRUCT.
- Helped run ablations on language mix and direct open-source finetuning.
