## Description

Zhan Su is a researcher affiliated with the University of Montreal and the University of Copenhagen, and a co-author of [[mo-2024-historyaware-2401-16659]]. The paper notes that this work was done while he was at the University of Montreal.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the use of historical turns as pseudo positives and historical hard negatives.
- Helped study retrieval behavior in long conversational sessions with topic shifts.
