## Description

Kentaro Inui is a researcher affiliated with Tohoku University and RIKEN, and he is a co-author of REALTIME QA. In this paper he is part of the group that defines the benchmark's annotation and evaluation methodology.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the REALTIME QA benchmark and its first-year analysis.
- Helped establish the task as a bridge between temporal QA, retrieval, and real-time NLP evaluation.
