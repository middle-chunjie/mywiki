## Description

DR Tulu-8B is the paper's open `8B` deep-research model, trained to produce long-form, citation-grounded answers with tool use. It is the main system evaluated throughout the study.

## Key Contributions

- Demonstrates direct RL training for open-ended long-form deep research.
- Achieves the strongest average results among open deep-research models in the paper's benchmark suite.
- Provides citation-capable, multi-tool inference at low per-query cost.
