## Description

Ladder Networks is the weakly supervised learning model used in [[sener-2018-active-1708-00489]] when the authors allow training to access both labeled and unlabeled data.

## Key Contributions

- Improves feature quality in the weakly supervised setting, which strengthens the paper's geometry-based acquisition method.
- Provides the semi-supervised training regime under which the paper reports especially large gains.
