## Description

University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign is the institutional affiliation of Yunyi Zhang and Jiawei Han in [[gao-2021-scaling-2101-06983]]. In this source it is one of the two academic sites behind the proposed gradient-cache method.

## Key Contributions

- Hosts co-authors who derive and evaluate gradient cache for memory-limited contrastive training.
- Serves as the institutional context for the paper's retrieval and optimization analysis.
