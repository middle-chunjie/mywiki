## Description

Peijie Yu is the first author of WildToolBench and is affiliated with Tencent HY according to the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored WildToolBench, a benchmark with `256` scenarios and `1024` tasks for in-the-wild LLM tool-use evaluation.
- Helped frame evaluation around tool orchestration, hidden intention, and instruction transition.
