## Description

Yoichi Takahashi is a researcher affiliated with Tohoku University and a co-author of REALTIME QA. The paper credits him as one of the authors behind the ongoing benchmark construction and analysis effort.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the dynamic QA benchmark paper and its first-year experimental analysis.
- Contributed to the weekly data collection and evaluation setup described in the benchmark workflow.
