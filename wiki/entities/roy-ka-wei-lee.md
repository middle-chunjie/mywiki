## Description

Roy Ka-Wei Lee is a researcher at Singapore University of Technology and Design and a co-author of [[hu-2023-llmadapters-2304-01933]], which introduces the LLM-Adapters framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the unified adapter framework and the accompanying empirical study on PEFT design choices.
- Helped establish the paper's findings on placement, configuration, and reasoning-task transfer.
