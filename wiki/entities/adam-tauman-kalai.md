## Description

Adam Tauman Kalai is a researcher at Microsoft Research and a co-author of *Textbooks Are All You Need*. The paper lists him on the team that develops and analyzes phi-1's data-centric training strategy.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the phi-1 paper on textbook-quality pretraining data, exercise finetuning, and contamination analysis.
