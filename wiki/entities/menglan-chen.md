## Description

Menglan Chen is a researcher affiliated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University and a co-author of [[du-2026-infomosaicbench-2510-02271]]. The paper places Chen in the collaboration that introduced the InfoMosaic-Bench benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored InfoMosaic-Bench.
- Contributed to the benchmark's multi-domain evaluation of information-seeking agents.
