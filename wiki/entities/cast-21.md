## Description

CAsT-21 is a TREC Conversational Assistance Track benchmark used for zero-shot evaluation in this paper. It complements CAsT-20 with another held-out conversational search testbed.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as a second target benchmark for zero-shot generalization.
- Shows ConvAug reaching `MRR 54.8` and `NDCG@3 36.8`, again exceeding the compared baselines.
