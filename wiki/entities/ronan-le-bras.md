## Description

Ronan Le Bras is a researcher affiliated with the Allen Institute for AI and a co-author of REALTIME QA. The paper places him on the team building and maintaining the benchmark's real-time baselines.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the release of REALTIME QA as an online benchmark for current-world question answering.
- Helped evaluate the open-book and closed-book baseline systems reported in the paper.
