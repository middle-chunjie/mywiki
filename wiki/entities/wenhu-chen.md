## Description

Wenhu Chen is a researcher at the University of Waterloo and a co-author of MMLU-Pro. The paper presents Chen as one of the core contributors to a benchmark aimed at more discriminative LLM evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MMLU-Pro and its two-round expert review process.
- Helped frame the benchmark around saturation, reasoning demand, and prompt robustness in LLM evaluation.
