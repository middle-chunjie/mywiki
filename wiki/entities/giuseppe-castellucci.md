## Description

Giuseppe Castellucci is an author of [[senel-2024-generative-2406-05255]] affiliated with Amazon.com, Inc., Seattle. He co-authors the paper's prompt-based optimization approach for improving generated recommendations from implicit feedback.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the method that combines exploration and exploitation in an LLM optimizer.
- Helped validate the method through CTR-based offline experiments and human evaluation.
