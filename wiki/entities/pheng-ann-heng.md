## Description

Pheng-Ann Heng is a researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. He is a senior co-author of the survey on generative diffusion models.

## Key Contributions

- algorithmic improvements, and applications. Co-authored a broad survey of diffusion-model theory
- Helped position diffusion models within a wider landscape of deep generative modeling approaches.
