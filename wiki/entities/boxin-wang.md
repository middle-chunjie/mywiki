## Description

Boxin Wang is the first author of [[unknown-nd-instructretroinstruction-2310-07713]]. In this paper he appears in the same recurring [[nvidia]] retrieval-augmented LLM author team as [[wei-ping]], [[peng-xu]], [[mohammad-shoeybi]], and [[bryan-catanzaro]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the scale-up from GPT `43B` to Retro `48B` through continued retrieval-augmented pretraining on an extra `100B` tokens.
- Co-authored the instruction-tuning study showing that retrieval-augmented pretraining improves zero-shot QA and summarization relative to a matched GPT baseline.
