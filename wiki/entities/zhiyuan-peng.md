## Description

Zhiyuan Peng is a researcher at Santa Clara University and the first-listed author of [[peng-2024-soft-2307-08303]], which introduces SPTAR for LLM-assisted dense retrieval augmentation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the SPTAR pipeline that combines soft prompt tuning, prompt-example filtering, and weak-data filtering.
- Evaluates SPTAR on MS MARCO and FiQA-2018 with DPR, ColBERT, and BM25CE.
