## Description

Yile Wang is the first author of [[wang-2023-selfknowledge-2310-05002]] and was affiliated with the Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), [[tsinghua-university]], at the time of the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed SKR, which adaptively decides whether retrieval should be invoked for an LLM question.
- Helped evaluate SKR across five QA benchmarks with InstructGPT and ChatGPT.
