## Description

Xin Luna Dong is a co-author of [[yang-2024-crag-2406-04744]] and is listed on the paper under the Meta Reality Labs / FAIR, Meta / HKUST / HKUST(GZ) collaboration. She contributes to CRAG's benchmark design and the broader framing of trustworthy retrieval-augmented QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the CRAG benchmark and its realism-centered task formulation.
- Helps analyze the gap between current RAG systems and trustworthy QA.
