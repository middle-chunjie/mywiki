## Description

ComplexWebQuestions is a benchmark for multi-hop complex question answering over [[freebase]], and it is the harder of the two main datasets used in [[ding-2024-enhancing-2402-02175]]. The paper reports `27,639` train, `3,519` validation, and `3,531` test questions.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the main benchmark where EPR sets a new state of the art among IR-based methods.
- Exposes the runtime and combinatorial trade-offs of retrieving more atomic patterns.
