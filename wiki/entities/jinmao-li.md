## Description

Jinmao Li is a researcher at [[nanjing-university]] and a co-author of [[ding-2024-enhancing-2402-02175]]. His work in this paper centers on evidence-aware retrieval for complex KGQA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the formulation of evidence patterns as explicit structural evidence for answers.
- Contributed to the experimental validation of EPR on [[complexwebquestions]] and [[webquestionssp]].
