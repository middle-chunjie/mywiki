## Description

Haoran Huang is a researcher at [[bytedance-ai-lab]] and a corresponding author of [[wu-2024-daco-2403-02528]]. In this work, he co-leads the study of benchmark construction and RL alignment for code-grounded data analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the DACO dataset and the Daco-RL algorithm.
- Helped design the reward-model stack that combines answer, contribution, and regularization signals.
