## Description

Abhimanyu Dubey is a researcher affiliated with Meta AI in [[ray-nd-cola]] and a co-author of the paper. In this work he contributes to studying how pretrained vision-language representations can be adapted for better attribute-object binding.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the comparison of six adaptation strategies on CLIP and FLAVA.
- Helped establish the paper's multimodal-adaptation baseline for compositional retrieval.
