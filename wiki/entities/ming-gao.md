## Description

Ming Gao is a faculty member at the School of Data Science and Engineering, [[east-china-normal-university]], Shanghai, and a corresponding author of the PGNN-EK paper on program understanding inspired by human behaviors.

## Key Contributions

- Corresponding author of PGNN-EK; supervised the research on graph neural network-based code understanding.
- Co-supervised the design of S-AST and the BCB-F benchmark dataset release.
