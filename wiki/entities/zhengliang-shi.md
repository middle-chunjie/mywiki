## Description

Zhengliang Shi is a researcher at [[shandong-university]] and the first author of [[shi-2024-generatethenground]], which introduces GenGround for multi-hop QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes the generate-then-ground framework that alternates answer deduction and evidence-based revision.
- Co-develops the instructional grounding distillation pipeline for adapting the method to smaller models.
