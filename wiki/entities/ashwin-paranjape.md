## Description

Ashwin Paranjape is a researcher affiliated with [[samaya-ai]] and a co-author of [[weller-2024-promptriever-2409-11136]]. In this paper he is part of the team building a promptable dense retriever and its synthetic instruction-training corpus.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the Promptriever training recipe that combines MS MARCO retrieval data with synthetic per-instance instructions.
- Contributes to the paper's evidence that promptable retrieval can improve instruction following without sacrificing standard retrieval quality.
