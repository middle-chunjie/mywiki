## Description

WebWalkerQA is the bilingual benchmark introduced by [[wu-2025-webwalker-2501-07572]] to evaluate web traversal for information-seeking question answering on official websites.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `680` QA pairs over `1373` webpages across conference, organization, education, and game domains.
- Covers both single-source and multi-source questions with explicit depth-based difficulty labels.
- Exposes the weakness of shallow search-based RAG on deeply nested website content.
