## Description

Prince Zizhuang Wang is a researcher at [[carnegie-mellon-university]] and the first author of SLEA-RL. In this paper he contributes to the design of step-level experience retrieval and step-level credit assignment for multi-turn LLM-agent training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors [[wang-2026-slearl-2603-18079]], which introduces SLEA-RL for multi-turn agentic reinforcement learning.
- Develops the paper's core combination of experience-library evolution, observation clustering, and step-level policy optimization.
