## Description

AutoAgents is an automatic agent-generation framework used as a baseline in [[yuan-2024-evoagent-2406-14228]]. EvoAgent compares against it to argue that evolutionary generation yields stronger and more flexible multi-agent specialization than a fixed human-designed architecture.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a baseline for automatic multi-agent construction on NLP reasoning and knowledge-intensive tasks.
- Motivates the paper's claim that generation pipelines tied to a predefined architecture remain less general than EvoAgent.
