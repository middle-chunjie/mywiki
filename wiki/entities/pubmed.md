## Description

PubMed is a citation-network dataset used in [[gong-2022-magcl-2212-07035]] for benchmark [[node-classification]] evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a larger citation benchmark on which MA-GCL reaches `83.5 +/- 0.4` on public splits and `85.7 +/- 0.3` on random splits.
- Contributes evidence that the proposed model augmentations transfer beyond the smallest citation graphs.
