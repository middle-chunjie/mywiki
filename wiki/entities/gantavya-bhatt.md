## Description

Researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and a co-author of [[kusupati-2024-matryoshka-2205-13147]]. He contributed to the study of nested embeddings for efficient large-scale classification and retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MRL paper and helped validate adaptive deployment with shared embeddings across dimensions.
