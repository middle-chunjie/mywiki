## Description

Llama2 is the `7B` large language model used in the paper to test whether LASER generalizes beyond GPT-J and RoBERTa. It serves as the strongest of the three evaluated text models on several of the reported tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Shows LASER improving CounterFact accuracy from `35.6%` to `37.6%`.
- Shows strong gains on FEVER `59.3% -> 64.5%` and BigBench-Epistemic Reasoning `44.8% -> 63.4%`.
- Demonstrates that the intervention also helps TruthfulQA, improving `50.5% -> 56.2%`.
