## Description

Maximilian Beck is a co-author of the xLSTM paper and is listed with the ELLIS Unit / LIT AI Lab at Johannes Kepler University Linz, NXAI Lab, and NXAI GmbH. The paper marks him as an equal-contribution author.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the xLSTM architecture that modernizes LSTMs with sLSTM and mLSTM cells.
- Helped position xLSTM as a large-scale language-modeling alternative to Transformers and state-space models.
