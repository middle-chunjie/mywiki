## Description

Bertie Vidgen is a researcher affiliated with the University of Oxford and Contextual AI and a co-author of the PRISM dataset paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRISM dataset paper.
- Helped position PRISM as a resource for studying disagreement in alignment data rather than collapsing it away.
- Contributed to analyses of model ranking, topic diversity, and welfare trade-offs.
