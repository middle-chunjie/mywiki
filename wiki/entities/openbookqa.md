## Description

OpenBookQA (OBQA) is a multiple-choice commonsense reasoning benchmark with 5,957 questions (4 answer choices each), requiring reasoning over fundamental scientific facts and their applications (Mihaylov et al., 2018). It maintains a public official leaderboard at AllenAI.

## Key Contributions

- Primary benchmark in KnowGPT evaluation; KnowGPT achieves 92.4% test accuracy and 91.6% on the official leaderboard, approaching human performance (91.7%).
- Used to demonstrate that black-box KG injection can surpass both ChatGPT (+32.4%) and GPT-4 (+1.4%) on commonsense reasoning requiring scientific background knowledge.
