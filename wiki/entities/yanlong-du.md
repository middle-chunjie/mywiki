## Description

Yanlong Du is a researcher at [[alibaba-group]] and a co-author of [[ouyang-2023-contrastive]], a SIGIR 2023 paper on contrastive CVR prediction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the full CL4CVR framework combining supervised ESMM training with contrastive objectives.
- Helped establish that EM, FNE, and SPI provide complementary gains on industrial and public advertising datasets.
