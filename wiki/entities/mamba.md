## Description

Mamba is a state-space-model baseline used throughout the xLSTM paper for matched comparisons in language modeling, long-context extrapolation, and downstream evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Acts as one of the strongest non-Transformer baselines in the paper's 15B- and 300B-token experiments.
- Highlights that xLSTM can beat a strong modern SSM baseline on validation perplexity at multiple scales.
