## Description

Yu Zhang is a researcher affiliated with Texas A&M University and a co-author of AgentFlow. The paper places him on the team proposing the multi-module framework and its reinforcement-learning optimization.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored AgentFlow and the Flow-GRPO training method.
- Contributed to the study of how planner optimization changes tool selection and task performance.
