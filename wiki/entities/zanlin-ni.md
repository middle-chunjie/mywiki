## Description

Zanlin Ni is a co-author of [[jiang-2022-crossmodal-2211-09623]] and was affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]] and BNRist. This paper credits Zanlin Ni as part of the team developing parameter-efficient text-video retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Cross-Modal Adapter model and its ablation studies.
- Helped evaluate efficiency and overfitting behavior against full fine-tuning.
