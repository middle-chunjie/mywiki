## Description

Researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and a co-author of [[kusupati-2024-matryoshka-2205-13147]]. He helped study multifidelity embeddings for efficient downstream use.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MRL paper, which shows that nested prefixes can preserve accuracy while reducing retrieval and classification cost.
