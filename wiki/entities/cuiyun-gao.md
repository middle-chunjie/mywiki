## Description

Cuiyun Gao is a researcher at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen and a co-author of the HiTYPER paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the hybrid static-plus-neural type inference approach in HiTYPER.
- Contributed to the evaluation against Typilus, Type4Py, Pytype, and Pyre Infer.
