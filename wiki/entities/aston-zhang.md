## Description

Aston Zhang is a researcher affiliated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University and Amazon Web Services, working on deep learning, NLP, and large language model applications. He is a co-first author (with Zhuosheng Zhang) of the Auto-CoT paper and co-author of the Dive into Deep Learning (D2L) textbook.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed Auto-CoT, showing that diversity-based automatic demonstration construction matches human-crafted chain-of-thought prompting.
- Co-authored the widely used open-source deep learning textbook Dive into Deep Learning.
