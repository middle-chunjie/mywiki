## Description

Huiqiang Jiang is a Microsoft Corporation researcher and the first-listed author of [[jiang-2023-llmlingua-2310-05736]], which proposes LLMLingua for prompt compression on black-box LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the coarse-to-fine prompt-compression framework in [[jiang-2023-llmlingua-2310-05736]].
- Contributed to the empirical evaluation across reasoning, conversation, and summarization benchmarks.
