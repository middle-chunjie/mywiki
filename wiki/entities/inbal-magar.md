## Description

Inbal Magar is a researcher at [[ai21-labs]] and a co-author of [[ratner-2023-parallel-2212-10947]] on parallel long-context prompting for LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the study showing that PCW can improve information extraction and multi-class in-context learning.
- Helped analyze when parallel windows help versus when cross-window dependence becomes a bottleneck.
