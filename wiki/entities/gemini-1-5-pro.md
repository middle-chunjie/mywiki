## Description

Gemini-1.5-Pro is the strongest long-context model evaluated in [[li-2024-retrieval-2407-16833]], with support for contexts up to 1 million tokens.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the paper's highest-context LC system in the RAG-versus-LC comparison.
- Achieves an `81.74%` Self-Route answerable rate and uses only `38.6%` of LC tokens under routing.
