## Description

Zaid Alibadi is a researcher affiliated with Lowe's Companies, Inc. (email: zalibadi@email.sc.edu, affiliated with University of South Carolina at time of publication). He is a co-author of the UIA unified information access framework presented at SIGIR 2023.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the UIA dense retrieval framework for unified information access.
- Contributed to experimental evaluation on large-scale e-commerce datasets.
