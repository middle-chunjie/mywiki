## Description

Lingwei Chen is a researcher at Wright State University and a co-author of [[li-2022-alphaprog]]. The paper credits him with work on the reinforcement-learning-based generator and its evaluation on compiler fuzzing.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ALPHAPROG framework for program generation under compiler-derived rewards.
- Contributed to the empirical analysis of validity, coverage, and synthesized-program complexity.
