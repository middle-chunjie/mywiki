## Description

Boyi Wei is a Princeton University researcher and co-author of SORRY-Bench. In this work, Boyi Wei is part of the team that measures refusal behavior across models and prompt mutations.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the benchmark evaluation over `56` LLMs.
- Helped analyze how linguistic changes shift fulfillment rates.
