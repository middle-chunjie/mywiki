## Description

BLIP-2 is the vision-language model used in [[levy-2023-chatting-2305-20062]] as an automatic answer provider that imitates the user during large-scale evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Answers generated questions in place of a human so different questioners can be compared at scale.
- Serves as a reasonable but imperfect proxy for human answers, with lower final performance than human answerers.
- Also motivates the paper's discussion of domain gap between model-generated and human answers.
