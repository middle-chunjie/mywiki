## Description

Tao Dai is the corresponding author of [[guo-2024-refir-2410-05601]] and is affiliated with Tsinghua University and Shenzhen University in the paper. He is listed on the work introducing ReFIR for grounding large restoration models with retrieved references.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the design of ReFIR as a training-free retrieval-augmented framework for LRMs.
