## Description

TU Wien is the institutional affiliation of Sebastian Hofstatter and Allan Hanbury in [[hofst-tter-2021-efficiently-2104-06967]]. In this paper it is one of the two academic homes behind the proposed TAS-Balanced dense retrieval method.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the affiliation context for two of the paper's five authors.
- Participates in the work on efficient dense retrieval and teacher-guided training.
