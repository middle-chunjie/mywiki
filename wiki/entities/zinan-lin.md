## Description

Zinan Lin is a first co-author of the Skeleton-of-Thought paper and is affiliated with [[microsoft-research]]. The paper credits Lin as part of the team developing and evaluating SoT and SoT-R.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the SoT prompting pipeline and its routed extension.
- Helped evaluate efficiency and answer quality on API-based and open-source models.
