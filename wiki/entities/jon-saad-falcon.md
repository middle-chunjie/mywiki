## Description

Jon Saad-Falcon is a Stanford University researcher and co-first author of [[saad-falcon-2024-benchmarking-2402-07440]], which introduces LoCoV1 and the M2-BERT long-context retrieval encoder.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the LoCoV1 benchmark for long-context retrieval.
- Co-authors the M2-BERT training and evaluation framework spanning pretraining, fine-tuning, and efficiency analysis.
