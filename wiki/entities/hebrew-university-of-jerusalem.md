## Description

The Hebrew University of Jerusalem is the academic affiliation of [[matan-levy]] and [[dani-lischinski]] in [[levy-nd-chatting]]. It is one of the institutions behind the ChatIR research project.

## Key Contributions

- Hosts part of the research team proposing chat-based image retrieval.
- Contributes the academic setting for the paper's method design and empirical evaluation.
