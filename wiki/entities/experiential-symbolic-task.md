## Description

The experiential-symbolic task is the paper's unseen hold-out benchmark, where one option is described symbolically and the other is represented through experienced outcomes.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the strongest transfer test in the paper because CENTaUR is evaluated on it without task-specific finetuning data.
- Reveals whether the model captures the human tendency to overweight symbolic descriptions relative to experiential evidence.
