## Description

Revanth Gangi Reddy is a researcher affiliated with [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and a co-author of [[reddy-2024-agrame-2405-15028]]. In this paper he helps introduce AGRaME for fine-grained ranking with multi-vector embeddings.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduces AGRaME, which reuses coarse-grained encodings for finer-grained ranking.
- Helps formulate the multi-granular contrastive training objective for sentence supervision.
- Contributes to the evaluation spanning open-domain QA, proposition retrieval, and citation addition.
