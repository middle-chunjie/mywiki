## Description

Junda Wu is the first author of [[wu-2024-coral-2403-06447]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-california-san-diego]]. In this paper he contributes to the design of collaborative retrieval-augmented prompting for long-tail recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CoRAL framework for injecting collaborative evidence into LLM-based recommendation.
- Helped formulate retrieval as a reinforcement-learning problem over user-item evidence.
