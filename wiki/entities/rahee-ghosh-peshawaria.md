## Description

Rahee Ghosh Peshawaria is a researcher at Microsoft Research and a co-author of the KITAB paper. In this work she contributes to dataset construction and evaluation for constrained information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the KITAB benchmark study.
- Contributed to the benchmark's data-collection and evaluation pipeline.
