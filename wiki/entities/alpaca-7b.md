## Description

Alpaca-7B is the stronger small language model used as `M_s` in [[jiang-2023-llmlingua-2310-05736]] for perplexity-based compression and distribution alignment.

## Key Contributions

- Computes demonstration and token perplexities for the budget controller and ITPC modules.
- Provides better compression quality than GPT2-Alpaca across the reported GSM8K compression settings.
