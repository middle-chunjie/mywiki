## Description

Xiaoting Li is a researcher at Pennsylvania State University and a co-first author of [[li-2022-alphaprog]]. She is listed on the paper as contributing to the reinforcement-learning-based compiler fuzzing framework ALPHAPROG.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored ALPHAPROG, which learns to generate valid Brainfuck programs for compiler fuzzing from compiler feedback.
- Helped evaluate reward designs that trade off validity, coverage, and control-flow complexity.
