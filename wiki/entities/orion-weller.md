## Description

Orion Weller is a researcher affiliated with Johns Hopkins University and the lead author of [[weller-2024-followir-2403-15246]]. In this paper he helps frame instruction following as an explicit evaluation target for information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designs the FollowIR benchmark built from altered TREC annotator narratives.
- Co-develops the `p-MRR` pairwise evaluation protocol for ranking changes under modified instructions.
- Co-authors the FollowIR-7B training pipeline for teaching retrieval models to follow long instructions.
