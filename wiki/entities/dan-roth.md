## Description

Dan Roth is a coauthor of RETOMATON and is listed with an Amazon affiliation in this paper. In this source he appears as part of the team developing a neuro-symbolic retrieval mechanism for language modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored RETOMATON and its empirical analysis of search-saving tradeoffs.
- Helped position the method as a neuro-symbolic alternative to purely neural retrieval control.
