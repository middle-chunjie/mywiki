## Description

LaMDA is Google's dialog-oriented language model family; the paper measures speculative-decoding agreement rates on a `137B` LaMDA target with `100M`, `2B`, and `8B` approximation models.

## Key Contributions

- Extends the empirical `α` analysis beyond T5 to large decoder-style dialog modeling.
- Shows that approximation models much smaller than the target can still reach `α` values around `0.57-0.75`.
