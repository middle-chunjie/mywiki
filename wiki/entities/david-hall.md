## Description

David Hall is a researcher at [[stanford-university]] and a co-author of the DSP paper. The work credits him with helping build task-aware LM-RM pipelines for knowledge-intensive QA settings.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the DSP formulation of explicit transformations over examples, demonstrations, queries, passages, and answers.
- Contributes to the empirical evaluation across Open-SQuAD, HotPotQA, and QReCC.
