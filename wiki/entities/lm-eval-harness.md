## Description

lm-eval-harness is an open-source framework for evaluating language models on a standardized suite of few-shot NLP benchmarks, developed by EleutherAI (Gao et al., 2021).

## Key Contributions

- Used in H2O as one of two evaluation frameworks (alongside HELM) to assess generation quality across 8 downstream tasks with varying KV-cache budgets.
