## Description

Gautier Izacard is a researcher affiliated in this paper with Meta AI Research, Ecole normale superieure, PSL University, and Inria. He is the first author of Contriever, an unsupervised dense retrieval method trained with contrastive learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Contriever, which trains a dense retriever without labeled query-document pairs.
- Helped extend the retrieval framework to multilingual and cross-lingual evaluation settings.
