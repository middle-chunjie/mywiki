## Description

Tianci Liu is a researcher at Purdue University and a co-author of [[xu-2026-alternating-2602-01511]]. He works on rubric-based reward modeling and LLM alignment in the Rubric-ARM paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the joint rubric-generation and judging framework optimized with alternating reinforcement learning.
- Helped evaluate Rubric-ARM on reward-model, instruction-following, and policy-alignment benchmarks.
