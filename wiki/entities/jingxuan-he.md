## Description

Jingxuan He is an ETH Zurich-affiliated co-author of the SWT-Bench paper. In this source the author contributes to the benchmark curation and analysis of automated test generation methods.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the dataset construction and metric definitions for SWT-Bench.
- Helped analyze how generated tests correlate with coverage and code-repair success.
