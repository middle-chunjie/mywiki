## Description

NVIDIA is the sole institutional affiliation of the authors of [[ficek-2025-scoring-2502-13820]] and the organization behind the paper's released Scoring-Verifiers benchmark assets.

## Key Contributions

- Hosted the research effort that introduced HE-R, HE-R+, MBPP-R, and MBPP-R+ for evaluating synthetic verifiers.
- Released code and dataset artifacts accompanying the benchmark proposal.
