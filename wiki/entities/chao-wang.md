## Description

Chao Wang is a co-author of [[lu-2019-molecular]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China. The paper places him on the team proposing MGCN for multilevel quantum interaction modeling in molecules.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MGCN architecture for molecular property prediction.
- Contributed to the empirical validation on QM9 and ANI-1.
