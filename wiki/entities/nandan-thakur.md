## Description

Nandan Thakur is a researcher affiliated with [[university-of-waterloo]] and, for this paper, a student researcher at [[google-research]]. He is the lead author of [[thakur-2024-leveraging-2311-05800]], which introduces SWIM-IR and SAP for multilingual dense retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the SWIM-IR synthetic multilingual retrieval dataset covering `33` languages.
- Co-introduced SAP to improve multilingual query generation quality for retrieval training.
- Led empirical evaluation of synthetic-only multilingual dense retrieval across XOR-Retrieve, MIRACL, and XTREME-UP.
