## Description

Tiejun Huang is a researcher at [[peking-university]] and co-author of [[qian-2024-memorag-2409-05591]]. In this paper, he is part of the team proposing MemoRAG for long-context processing via memory-guided retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MemoRAG's dual-system design combining global memory formation with retrieval-augmented generation.
- Contributed to the paper's evaluation across long-context QA, summarization, and domain-specific benchmarks.
