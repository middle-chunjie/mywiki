## Description

Simran Arora is a Stanford University researcher and co-first author of [[saad-falcon-2024-benchmarking-2402-07440]], contributing to the long-context benchmark and M2-BERT retriever study.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the LoCoV1 benchmark construction across multiple long-document domains.
- Contributes to the empirical study comparing M2-BERT against strong retrieval baselines.
