## Description

Yoav Levine is a researcher at [[ai21-labs]] and a co-author of [[ratner-2023-parallel-2212-10947]] on long-context inference for decoder-only language models.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the PCW method for exposing pretrained LLMs to more demonstrations and documents without retraining.
- Co-authored the paper's evaluation across Jurassic-1, LLaMA, and GPT-2 models.
