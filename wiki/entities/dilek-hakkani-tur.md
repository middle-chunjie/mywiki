## Description

Dilek Hakkani-Tür is a researcher at [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and a co-author of [[qian-2025-smart-2502-11435]]. She contributes to the paper's agent-oriented framing of calibrated reasoning and tool use.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the SMARTAgent formulation for strategic tool use.
- Helped position tool-overuse mitigation as an agent efficiency and usability problem.
- Contributed to the evaluation across intention and time-sensitive tasks.
