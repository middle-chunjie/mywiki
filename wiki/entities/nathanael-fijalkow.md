## Description

Nathanaël Fijalkow is a researcher affiliated in this paper with CNRS, LaBRI, Université de Bordeaux, and The Alan Turing Institute. He is the lead author of [[fijalkow-2022-scaling]] on scalable search methods for neural program synthesis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced the distribution-based search framework for analyzing search loss under a program distribution.
- Co-developed Heap Search, SQRT Sampling, and the grammar splitter evaluated in [[fijalkow-2022-scaling]].
