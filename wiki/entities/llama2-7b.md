## Description

Llama 2 7B is one of the instruction-tuned open-source LLMs used as both generator and detector backbone in [[yeh-2026-lumina-2509-21875]].

## Key Contributions

- Achieves Lumina scores of `0.7646` AUROC on RAGTruth and `0.9153` AUROC on HalluRAG.
- Is also used in the paper's proxy-detector experiments that test cross-model generalization.
