## Description

ScienceWorld is an interactive scientific problem-solving environment used in [[yuan-2024-evoagent-2406-14228]] to test whether EvoAgent helps agents act in open-world settings with long-horizon decisions. The benchmark stresses memory, sub-task decomposition, and scientific commonsense.

## Key Contributions

- Evaluates EvoAgent in an interactive environment rather than only on static QA or generation tasks.
- Shows GPT-4 improving from `27.97` to `30.42` average score and GPT-3.5 from `17.12` to `19.02` under EvoAgent.
