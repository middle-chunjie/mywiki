## Description

Haoyu Wang is a researcher at the University at Albany and a co-author of [[xu-2026-alternating-2602-01511]]. He is part of the Rubric-ARM work on scalable rubric-based supervision for non-verifiable LLM post-training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Rubric-ARM and its evaluation across reward modeling, policy alignment, and creative-writing tasks.
- Contributed to evidence that rubric-conditioned rewards can improve both offline and online post-training.
