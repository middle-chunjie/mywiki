## Description

Haohan Lin is the first author of [[unknown-nd-leanstar]]. In this paper he is listed with [[tsinghua-university]], with the work noted as having been done during a visit to [[carnegie-mellon-university]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced Lean-STaR, a framework for interleaving thoughts and tactics in Lean theorem proving.
- Contributed to the synthetic thought-generation pipeline based on retrospective rationale annotation.
- Helped develop and evaluate expert-iteration training for improved formal proving performance.
