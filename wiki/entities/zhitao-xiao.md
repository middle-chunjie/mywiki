## Description

Zhitao Xiao is a coauthor of the paper Multi-Scale Subgraph Contrastive Learning and is listed as affiliated with Tiangong University.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MSSGCL and the associated local-view regressor design.
- Contributed to evaluating the method in both unsupervised and semi-supervised settings.
