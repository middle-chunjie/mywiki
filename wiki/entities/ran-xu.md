## Description

Ran Xu is a researcher at Emory University and a co-author of [[xu-2026-alternating-2602-01511]]. In this paper, Xu helps introduce Rubric-ARM for rubric-based reward modeling in non-verifiable LLM post-training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Rubric-ARM framework that jointly trains a rubric generator and judge with alternating RL.
- Contributed to the variance-based justification for judge-first optimization and the large multi-benchmark evaluation suite.
