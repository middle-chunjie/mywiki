## Description

Li Li is a co-author of [[guo-2022-modeling]] and was affiliated with Monash University at the time of publication. In this paper Li contributes to the study of source code summarization with structurally informed AST representations.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CODESCRIBE method and experimental comparison on Java and Python benchmarks.
- Helped validate that triplet-position-aware AST encoding improves over prior Transformer and GNN baselines.
