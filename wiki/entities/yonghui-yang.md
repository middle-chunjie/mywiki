## Description

Yonghui Yang is the first author of [[yang-2023-generative-2307-05100]]. The paper lists Yang with Hefei University of Technology and notes that this work was completed while Yang was an intern at [[ant-group]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored VGCL, which generates recommendation contrastive views by sampling from learned node distributions.
- Helped frame graph contrastive learning as a combination of variational reconstruction and cluster-aware self-supervision.
