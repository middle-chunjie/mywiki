## Description

The Pile is a large multi-domain language-model benchmark used in [[borgeaud-2022-improving-2112-04426]] to compare a `7.5B` RETRO model against much larger systems such as GPT-3, Jurassic-1, and Gopher.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the paper's headline comparison showing that RETRO can be competitive with models over an order of magnitude larger.
- Reveals domains where retrieval helps strongly, such as `github`, and harder cases such as `dm mathematics` and `ubuntu_irc`.
