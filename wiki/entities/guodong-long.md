## Description

Guodong Long is a co-author of [[shen-2023-large-2304-14233]] affiliated with [[university-of-technology-sydney]]. In this paper he is part of the team arguing that transparent lexical retrieval can better exploit LLM-generated augmentations than weak dense retrievers.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the LameR method that combines candidate-prompted answer generation with BM25.
- Contributes to the paper's zero-shot retrieval evaluation and analysis of retriever bottlenecks.
