## Description

Xinhao Li is a UC San Diego researcher and co-author of [[sun-2024-learn-2310-13807]], where he contributes to the MTTT framework linking test-time learning with attention-style computation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's bilevel formulation for learning the self-supervised task used in test-time adaptation.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison between MTTT variants, linear attention, and self-attention on ImageNet.
