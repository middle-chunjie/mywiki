## Description

Shijue Huang is a researcher affiliated with ByteDance Seed and a co-author of ReTool. The paper associates Huang with RL-based tool-use learning for code-interpreter-assisted mathematical reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the cold-start plus PPO training pipeline used in ReTool.
- Contributed to the empirical study of strategic code use and execution-feedback-driven reasoning.
