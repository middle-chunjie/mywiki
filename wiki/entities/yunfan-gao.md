## Description

Yunfan Gao is a researcher affiliated with the Shanghai Research Institute for Intelligent Autonomous Systems at Tongji University. In this paper, Gao is a co-author of a broad survey on retrieval-augmented generation for large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the 2024 survey that organizes RAG into Naive, Advanced, and Modular paradigms.
- Helped synthesize retrieval, generation, augmentation, and evaluation dimensions of RAG systems.
- Contributed to the paper's discussion of future directions such as robustness, long-context interaction, and production-ready RAG.
