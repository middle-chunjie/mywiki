## Description

Tatsunori Hashimoto is a Stanford University researcher, equal advising author, and co-author of [[sun-2024-learn-2310-13807]], which proposes Meta Test-Time Training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the bilevel optimization formulation used to learn self-supervised test-time objectives.
- Helped position the method as both a theoretical reinterpretation of attention and a practical long-sequence architecture.
