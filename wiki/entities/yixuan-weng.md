## Description

Yixuan Weng is a co-first author of [[weng-2023-large-2212-09561]] and was affiliated with the Laboratory of Cognition and Decision Intelligence for Complex Systems, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed the self-verification framework that reranks chain-of-thought candidate answers by backward verification.
- Helped evaluate the method across arithmetic, commonsense, and logical reasoning benchmarks using GPT-3 and InstructGPT.
