## Description

DuConv is a Chinese film-and-entertainment [[knowledge-grounded-dialogue]] dataset used in [[wang-2024-improving-2401-04361]] for main evaluation, robustness testing, ablations, few-shot study, and human study.

## Key Contributions

- Contains about `180K` samples from `29K` dialogues and `270K` utterances.
- Provides a large-scale knowledge source with about `3.6M` triples.
- Acts as the main benchmark for the paper's robustness study under eight noisy conditions.
