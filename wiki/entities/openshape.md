## Description

OpenShape is the strongest baseline compared against [[gao-2024-sculpting-2311-01734]] for language-image-3D contrastive pre-training. The paper also reuses OpenShape design choices such as the scaled PointBERT setup and cross-modal downstream protocols.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the baseline that MixCon3D improves through a better training recipe and joint image-3D alignment.
- Provides prior practice for evaluating text-to-3D retrieval and point-cloud captioning from learned 3D embeddings.
