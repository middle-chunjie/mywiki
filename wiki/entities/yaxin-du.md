## Description

Yaxin Du is a researcher affiliated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University and a co-author of [[du-2026-infomosaicbench-2510-02271]]. This paper places Du in the collaboration that introduced InfoMosaic-Bench for evaluating multi-source information seeking in tool-augmented agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored InfoMosaic-Bench, a benchmark for multi-source information-seeking tasks.
- Contributed to the study of how LLM agents use web search and domain-specific tools.
