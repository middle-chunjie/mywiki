## Description

Zeyu Zheng is a co-author of [[xia-2026-skillrl-2602-08234]] and is listed with UC Berkeley on the paper's title page. In this work, he contributes to the design and evaluation of recursively evolving skill libraries for LLM agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the structured skill retrieval and update pipeline used in SkillRL.
- Contributes to the empirical study across ALFWorld, WebShop, and search-augmented QA tasks.
