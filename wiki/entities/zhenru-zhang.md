## Description

Zhenru Zhang is a researcher at the Qwen Team, Alibaba Group, and the lead author of [[zhang-2025-lessons-2501-07301]], which investigates the limitations of Monte Carlo estimation and Best-of-N evaluation for Process Reward Models in mathematical reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Led the empirical study diagnosing MC estimation's inferiority compared to LLM-as-a-judge and human annotation for PRM data construction.
- Co-developed the consensus filtering mechanism that combines MC estimation and LLM-as-a-judge for efficient PRM training.
- Co-authored the PROCESSBENCH benchmark (with Chujie Zheng et al.) for step-level process error evaluation.
