## Description

YouCook2 is a large-scale video dataset containing 2000 YouTube cooking videos covering 89 recipe types, with ~9.6K training clips and ~3.2K validation clips, each annotated with manually written textual descriptions; a standard benchmark for video-text retrieval and video captioning.

## Key Contributions

- Benchmark for CrossCLR: Text→Video R@1 = 19.5, R@5 = 45.9, R@10 = 58.3 (new SOTA at publication).
- Captioning benchmark: CrossCLR achieves CIDEr-D = 58.65 (vs COOT 54.07).
