## Description

Yu Su is a researcher at The Ohio State University and the senior co-author of [[guti-rrez-2024-hipporag-2405-14831]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed HippoRAG as a retrieval-centric long-term memory architecture for large language models.
- Helped position the method against iterative retrieval systems, emphasizing lower serving cost and latency with strong multi-hop QA performance.
