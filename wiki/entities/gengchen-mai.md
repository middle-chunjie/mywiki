## Description

Gengchen Mai is a faculty researcher at the University of Georgia (corresponding author) and co-author of the Img2Loc paper, with research focused on geospatial representation learning and spatial machine intelligence.

## Key Contributions

- Served as corresponding author for the Img2Loc project.
- Earlier work includes Sphere2Vec (spherical position encoding for large-scale geospatial prediction), CSP (contrastive spatial pre-training for geospatial-visual representations), and Img2Loc.
