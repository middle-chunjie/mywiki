## Description

Weihang Su is a co-author of [[fang-2024-scaling-2403-18684]] affiliated with Tsinghua University and Zhongguancun Laboratory. In this paper, Weihang Su is part of the team studying how dense-retrieval performance scales with more parameters and annotations.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the standardized training and evaluation pipeline used for the scaling-law analysis.
- Helped analyze the retrieval trade-offs between annotation cost and model capacity.
