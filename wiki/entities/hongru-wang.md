## Description

Hongru Wang is a researcher at [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and a co-author of [[qian-2025-smart-2502-11435]]. In this work he contributes to the dataset, training, and evaluation of self-aware tool-using agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-built SMART-ER's compositional reasoning trajectories and tool annotations.
- Co-evaluated SMARTAgent on in-domain and OOD benchmarks.
- Helped analyze tool overuse, confidence, and efficiency trade-offs in agent systems.
