## Description

NCI1 is a molecular graph classification dataset from TUDataset used by the paper Multi-Scale Subgraph Contrastive Learning in both diagnostic analysis and benchmark evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the datasets for the paper's semantic-similarity study over sampled subgraphs.
- MSSGCL reaches `81.45 +- 0.48` in the unsupervised setting and `74.77 +- 0.31` with `10%` labels in the semi-supervised setting.
