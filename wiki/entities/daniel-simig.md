## Description

Dániel Simig is a Meta AI researcher and equal-contribution author of [[yu-2023-megabyte-2305-07185]]. In this paper he is part of the team developing a multiscale Transformer for long byte sequences.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MEGABYTE architecture that separates global patch modeling from local byte prediction.
- Contributed to the compute-controlled evaluation of byte-level modeling across multiple modalities.
