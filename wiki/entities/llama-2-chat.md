## Description

Llama 2-Chat is the augmentation LLM used by ConvAug, specifically the `7B` chat variant. It generates paraphrases, intent-shifted conversations, entity substitutions, and dependency-aware conversational analyses under the paper's prompting scheme.

## Key Contributions

- Produces the augmented conversational training data used by ConvAug.
- Supports the three-step cognition-aware prompting process introduced in the paper.
