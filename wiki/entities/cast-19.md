## Description

CAsT-19 is the 2019 TREC Conversational Assistance Track benchmark used in [[mao-2022-convtrans]] to evaluate conversational dense retrieval. The paper reports 50 conversations and 479 turns, with NDCG@3 as the primary evaluation metric.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the two main evaluation benchmarks for ConvTrans.
- Shows that ConvTrans reaches `MRR = 0.732` and `NDCG@3 = 0.453`, the best results among compared baselines.
