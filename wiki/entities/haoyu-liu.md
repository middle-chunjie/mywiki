## Description

Haoyu Liu is a researcher at [[microsoft]] and the first author of [[liu-2025-gear-2501-02772]], which proposes GeAR for joint global and local retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed GeAR's combination of a bi-encoder, fusion encoder, and lightweight decoder.
- Helped frame retrieval as a joint document-level and fine-grained information retrieval problem.
