## Description

Tao Shen is the first author of [[shen-2023-large-2304-14233]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-technology-sydney]]. He helps formulate LameR as a zero-shot retrieval pipeline built from LLM-based answer generation and BM25.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes LameR for candidate-grounded LLM query augmentation in zero-shot retrieval.
- Helps design the empirical study across TREC DL19/20 and low-resource BEIR datasets.
