## Description

Siyu Yuan is a researcher affiliated with [[fudan-university]] and the lead author of [[yuan-2024-evoagent-2406-14228]]. In this paper, he contributes to framing automatic multi-agent generation as an evolutionary search problem for LLM-based agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors EvoAgent, which evolves agent roles, skills, and prompts into a collaborative multi-agent system.
- Helps demonstrate the method across NLP reasoning, multimodal QA, interactive science, and travel planning tasks.
