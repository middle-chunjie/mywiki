## Description

Yutao Zhu is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and co-author of [[dong-2024-progressive-2412-14835]] on active retrieval for multimodal reasoning verification.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the AR-MCTS method for dynamically retrieving step-specific evidence during multimodal reasoning.
- Helped validate the method on mathematical and general multimodal reasoning benchmarks.
