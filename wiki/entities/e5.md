## Description

E5 is the pre-trained text encoder family used as the default base retriever in [[kim-2025-syntriever-2502-03824]], where Syntriever starts from an approximately `125M`-parameter checkpoint.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the initialization point for Syntriever's distillation and alignment stages.
- Provides a strong retrieval baseline that the paper then improves substantially across supervised and zero-shot settings.
