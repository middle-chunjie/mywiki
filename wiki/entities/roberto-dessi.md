## Description

Roberto Dessi is a coauthor of [[schick-2023-toolformer-2302-04761]] and is marked in the author list as affiliated with Universitat Pompeu Fabra. In this paper he contributes to the design and evaluation of self-supervised tool-use training for language models.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the loss-filtered API-call generation pipeline used to construct Toolformer's finetuning data.
- Helped evaluate the model across factual completion, arithmetic, QA, multilingual QA, and temporal benchmarks.
- Contributed to the paper's analysis of when retrieved or computed information actually reduces LM loss.
