## Description

Rich James is a co-author of [[shi-2023-replug-2301-12652]] and contributes to the paper's retrieval-augmented black-box LM framework. The locally available `paper.md` does not preserve the author-affiliation block, so this page records his role in the work without a firm affiliation claim.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the RePlug and RePlug LSR framework for retrieval-augmenting frozen or API-only language models.
- Contributed to experiments spanning language modeling, MMLU, and open-domain QA.
