## Description

Chunyan Miao is a researcher at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, and the senior author of [[li-2023-rethinking-2310-08069]]. The paper associates Chunyan Miao with research on improving neural code retrieval through weighted contrastive objectives.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the Soft-InfoNCE method and its justification via KL-style distribution matching and importance sampling.
- Oversees experimental validation on CodeBERT, GraphCodeBERT, and UniXCoder.
