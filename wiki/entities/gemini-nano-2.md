## Description

Gemini Nano 2 is a smaller Google DeepMind model evaluated in [[allamanis-2024-unsupervised-2402-08699]] alongside Gemini Pro on RTC-based code tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Achieves `18.9% RTC_pass` on HumanEval and `7.7%` on ARCADE in the paper.
- Records average cross-domain forward lift `7.0%` and EDITINGRTC exact-match RTC `5.2%`.
