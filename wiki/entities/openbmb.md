## Description

OpenBMB is an open-source AI research initiative affiliated with Tsinghua University and ModelBest Inc., focused on large language models and related tooling. It maintains the GitHub organization at https://github.com/OpenBMB.

## Key Contributions

- Released RAGEval framework and DragonBall dataset at https://github.com/OpenBMB/RAGEval.
- Developed MiniCPM series of efficient small language models (MiniCPM-2B, MiniCPM3-4B).
- Maintains open-source toolchains for LLM training, fine-tuning, and evaluation.
