## Description

Ashwin Kalyan is a co-author of the C-STS paper and is affiliated with the Allen Institute for AI in this source.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the C-STS benchmark and its evaluation framing.
- Contributed to the comparison between traditional STS models and condition-aware evaluation.
- Helped characterize the challenge posed by explicit natural-language conditions.
