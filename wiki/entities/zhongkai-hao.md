## Description

Zhongkai Hao is an author of [[hao-2020-asgn]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China. He contributes to the ASGN framework for semi-supervised and active molecular property prediction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored ASGN, which combines a teacher-student design with active learning for molecular property prediction.
- Helped develop the paper's semi-supervised training objective over molecular graphs.
