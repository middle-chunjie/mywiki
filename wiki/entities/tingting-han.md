## Description

Tingting Han is a researcher at Birkbeck, University of London, UK, working on formal methods, software verification, and adversarial robustness of deep neural networks applied to software engineering tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ACCENT framework for adversarial robustness of code comment generation models, contributing to threat modeling and experimental evaluation.
