## Description

Michael Saxon is a researcher in the Department of Computer Science at [[university-of-california-santa-barbara]] and a co-author of [[wang-2024-large-2301-11916]]. He is part of the team that analyzes how task latents can explain transferable demonstration quality.

## Key Contributions

- Helped formulate the theoretical conditions under which in-context learning approaches a Bayes-optimal classifier.
- Helped evaluate whether demonstrations selected by a small LM transfer to larger LLMs.
