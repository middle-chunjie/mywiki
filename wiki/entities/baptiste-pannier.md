## Description

Baptiste Pannier is a co-author of [[penedo-2023-refinedweb-2306-01116]] and a member of the Falcon LLM team. In this paper, he is listed under affiliation marker `^1` in the author block.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the RefinedWeb paper and its presentation of a `5T`-token web-only corpus for LLM pretraining.
- Helped document how aggressive filtering and deduplication improve downstream zero-shot accuracy.
