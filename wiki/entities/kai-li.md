## Description

Kai Li is a Princeton University researcher and co-author of SORRY-Bench. In this work, Kai Li contributes to the benchmark and its analysis of model refusal variation across tasks and versions.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the large-scale evaluation over `56` language models.
- Contributed to the study of category-level and version-level safety differences.
