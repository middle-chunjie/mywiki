## Description

Junlong Li is the first author of [[li-2025-codeio-2502-07316]] and is affiliated with [[deepseek-ai]], [[shanghai-jiao-tong-university]], and [[hkust]]. The paper notes that this work was done during an internship at DeepSeek-AI.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed the CodeI/O and CodeI/O++ pipeline for code input-output prediction in natural-language CoT form.
- Helped scale the corpus to `454.9K` executable functions and `3.5M` training instances.
- Co-led evaluation across `14` reasoning benchmarks and four base-model families.
