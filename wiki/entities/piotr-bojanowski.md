## Description

Piotr Bojanowski is a researcher affiliated in this paper with Meta AI Research. He co-authored the paper on contrastive pre-training for dense retrieval and its multilingual extension.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Contriever and the associated contrastive pre-training design.
- Contributed to multilingual retrieval experiments with mContriever.
