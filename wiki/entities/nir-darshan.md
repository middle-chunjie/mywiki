## Description

Nir Darshan is a co-author of [[levy-nd-chatting]] and is affiliated with [[originai]]. He is part of the group proposing ChatIR as a proactive retrieval interface for image search.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the retriever and evaluation pipeline used for chat-based image retrieval.
- Participates in the empirical study comparing LLM questioners, BLIP2 answer simulation, and human-in-the-loop retrieval.
