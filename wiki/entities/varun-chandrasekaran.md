## Description

Varun Chandrasekaran is a co-author of the KITAB paper and is affiliated with the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in the author list. He contributes to the benchmark's evaluation of constraint satisfaction for LLM-based retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the KITAB benchmark study.
- Helped analyze model behavior under lexical, temporal, and entity constraints.
