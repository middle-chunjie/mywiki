## Description

Shengda Fan is a researcher affiliated with Renmin University of China and the first author of [[unknown-nd-workflowllm]]. He co-authored the WorkflowLLM framework for improving LLM workflow orchestration.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored WorkflowLLM and the WorkflowBench dataset construction pipeline.
- Helped frame workflow orchestration as a data-centric training problem for LLM agents.
