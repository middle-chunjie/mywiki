## Description

Baichuan2 is one of the six large language models used to generate answers for the real-world Face4RAG benchmark in [[xu-2024-facerag-2407-01080]].

## Key Contributions

- Supplies `200` model-generated answers for the benchmark's real-world evaluation slice.
- Shows an average answer length of `320.6` characters and an error rate of `40.5%` in the reported statistics.
