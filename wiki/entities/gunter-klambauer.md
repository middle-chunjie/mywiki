## Description

Günter Klambauer is a co-author of the xLSTM paper and is explicitly listed with Johannes Kepler University Linz, NXAI Lab, and NXAI GmbH affiliations.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the xLSTM study on scalable recurrent language models.
- Helped connect the architecture design to modern large-scale training and evaluation practice.
