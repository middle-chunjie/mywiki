## Description

Scott Martens is a researcher affiliated with Jina AI GmbH in Berlin and a co-author of [[g-nther-2025-jinaembeddingsv-2506-18902]]. He appears on the paper introducing `jina-embeddings-v4` for multimodal and multilingual retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the presentation of the unified multimodal embedding architecture.
- Contributed to the reported evaluation on visually rich document retrieval benchmarks.
