## Description

Dawn Song is a co-author of [[gudibande-2023-false-2305-15717]] and is affiliated with [[uc-berkeley]]. The paper lists her among the researchers arguing that broad ChatGPT imitation is a false promise under current methods.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's evaluation of imitation-model failure modes.
- Helped frame the mismatch between stylistic imitation and factual or reasoning competence.
- Contributed to the paper's discussion of technical and societal implications of model imitation.
