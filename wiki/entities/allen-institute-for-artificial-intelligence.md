## Description

The Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence is the industry research affiliation shared by Yejin Choi and Hannaneh Hajishirzi in this paper. It is part of the collaboration that develops and analyzes infini-gram at web-corpus scale.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed senior authorship and institutional support for the infini-gram project.
- Anchored the paper's connection to large-scale language modeling and corpus analysis research.
