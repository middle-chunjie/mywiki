## Description

Alexander Wettig is a co-author of [[friedman-2023-transformer-2306-01128]] and is affiliated with Princeton University in this source. He contributes to the model design, experiments, and interpretability analysis around learned Transformer Programs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the discrete architectural constraints and optimization scheme used to learn Transformer Programs.
- Helped analyze learned programs by converting them into executable Python and tracing their circuits.
