## Description

Re-DocRED is a revised version of DocRED that supplements positive relation instances missed in the original dataset. In [[sun-2024-consistency-2401-13598]], it serves as a second benchmark for evaluating zero-shot document-level relation and triplet extraction.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a harder and cleaner evaluation target than the original DocRED by addressing missed positives.
- Is used to report the paper's strongest ZeroDocRE and ZeroDocRTE test gains over baseline models.
