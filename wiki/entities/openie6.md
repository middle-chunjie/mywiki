## Description

OpenIE6 is the open information extraction model used in [[li-2023-sm-2312-16511]] to extract triples from contexts and QA pairs for graph construction and matching.

## Key Contributions

- Extracts the triples that become the nodes of the paper's knowledge graph.
- Supports matching generated and existing QA pairs to graph nodes before graph traversal.
