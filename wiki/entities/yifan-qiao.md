## Description

Yifan Qiao is a researcher at Tsinghua University and the first author of [[qiao-2019-understanding-1904-07531]]. In this paper, he studies how BERT behaves under different ranking formulations for passage reranking and ad hoc retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed and evaluated four BERT-based ranking variants spanning representation-based and interaction-based formulations.
- Helped analyze attention allocation and influential-token behavior in BERT rerankers on MS MARCO.
