## Description

Yuandong Tian is a Meta AI author on the paper. In this source he contributes to the framing of StreamingLLM as a deployment-friendly approach for finite-window LLMs operating on effectively infinite streams.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the cache-local positional treatment used for RoPE and ALiBi models.
- Helped validate StreamingLLM on multiple open LLM families and streaming QA settings.
