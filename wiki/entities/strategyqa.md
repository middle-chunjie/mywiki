## Description

StrategyQA is the commonsense reasoning benchmark used in [[he-2022-rethinking-2301-00303]] to evaluate whether RR improves answer accuracy and explanation faithfulness on implicit multi-step questions.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies `2,290` training examples and a `229`-example development split for the paper's commonsense experiments.
- Provides decompositions, evidence paragraphs, and supporting facts that make it possible to analyze both retrieval quality and explanation faithfulness.
