## Description

Gail Kaiser is a Columbia University researcher and co-author of [[ding-2024-semcoder-2406-01006]]. In this work, Kaiser is part of the team connecting program understanding, debugging, and code-model training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored SEMCODER and its monologue-based semantic training recipe.
- Supported the paper's framing of debugging and self-refinement as reasoning-heavy code tasks.
