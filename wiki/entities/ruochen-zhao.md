## Description

Ruochen Zhao is a co-author of [[li-2024-can-2410-01428]] affiliated with DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group and Hupan Lab. The paper credits Zhao on the design and empirical study of critic-guided planning for knowledge-intensive reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the framework that jointly plans reasoning, query generation, and retrieval.
- Helped evaluate CR-Planner on competitive programming, math, and domain retrieval benchmarks.
