## Description

Xin Du is a co-author of [[du-2024-bottleneckminimal-2405-10974]]. The extracted manuscript text available in this repository does not preserve the author affiliation block, so this page records authorship while leaving the affiliation unspecified.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the bottleneck-minimal indexing formulation for generative document retrieval.
- Helped introduce the query-aware hierarchical `k`-means indexing method evaluated on NQ320K and MARCO Lite.
