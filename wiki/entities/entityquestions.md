## Description

EntityQuestions is an open-domain QA dataset with a long-tail entity distribution used as a secondary evaluation benchmark in this paper. The authors use the subset whose subject mention can be uniquely linked to a Wikidata entity.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as an external benchmark validating that tail-focused findings are not limited to POPQA.
- Shows that Adaptive Retrieval can still reduce cost on a harder distribution dominated by less popular entities.
