## Description

Abhishek Srivastava is a coauthor of the BEIR paper and is affiliated with [[technical-university-of-darmstadt]] through UKP-TUDA in this source. He is part of the team that frames BEIR as a benchmark for robust zero-shot retrieval evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the benchmark construction and software standardization around `(corpus, queries, qrels)`.
- Helped evaluate zero-shot retrieval behavior across lexical, sparse, dense, late-interaction, and re-ranking models.
