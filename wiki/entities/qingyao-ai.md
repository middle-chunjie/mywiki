## Description

Qingyao Ai is a co-author of [[fang-2024-scaling-2403-18684]] affiliated with Quan Cheng Laboratory and the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University. The paper associates Qingyao Ai with the retrieval-focused research collaboration behind the scaling study.

- Helped frame the implications of annotation quality and resource allocation for retrieval research.
