## Description

Le Chen is a researcher at Iowa State University and a coauthor of [[tehranijamsaz-2024-coderosetta-2410-20527]]. The paper places him on the team developing CodeRosetta for unsupervised translation of parallel code.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the study showing that a compact `~0.8B` encoder-decoder model can outperform stronger baselines on `C++ → CUDA`.
