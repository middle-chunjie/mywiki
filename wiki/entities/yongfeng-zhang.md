## Description

Yongfeng Zhang is the corresponding author of [[wang-2026-ragrouterbench-2602-00296]] and is affiliated with [[rutgers-university]]. In this source Zhang leads a benchmark study of adaptive RAG routing.

## Key Contributions

- Led the design of [[ragrouter-bench]] as a benchmark for routing across RAG paradigms.
- Framed routing as a utility-maximizing decision over query-corpus pairs.
- Oversaw the empirical analysis of quality-cost trade-offs across multiple corpora and models.
