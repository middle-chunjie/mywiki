## Description

Hanyang Wang is a co-first author of [[xia-2026-skillrl-2602-08234]] with affiliations to [[unc-chapel-hill]] and the University of Chicago as shown on the PDF title page. He is part of the author group developing SkillRL for long-horizon LLM agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the training pipeline that combines cold-start SFT with GRPO.
- Contributes to the benchmark study showing large gains on ALFWorld and WebShop.
