## Description

Yunqi Xu is a researcher at [[ant-group]] and the first author of [[xu-2024-facerag-2407-01080]], which introduces the Face4RAG benchmark and the L-Face4RAG evaluation pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the Face4RAG benchmark for Chinese RAG factual consistency evaluation.
- Co-authored the L-Face4RAG method for logic-aware factuality checking.
