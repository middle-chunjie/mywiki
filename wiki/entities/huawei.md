## Description

Huawei is the industrial affiliation for [[zheng-liu-huawei]] and [[zhao-cao]] in [[xiao-2022-retromae-2205-12035]]. In this paper it is part of the academia-industry collaboration that develops RetroMAE for dense retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the Huawei-based co-authorship behind RetroMAE's retrieval-oriented pre-training study.
- Contributes to the paper's empirical work on BEIR, MS MARCO, and Natural Questions retrieval.
