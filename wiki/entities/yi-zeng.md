## Description

Yi Zeng is a Virginia Tech researcher and co-author of SORRY-Bench. The paper credits this author team with building the human-judgment-backed evaluation recipe for safety refusal benchmarking.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the human annotation dataset and the meta-evaluation of automated judges.
- Helped analyze how evaluator choice changes reported refusal results.
