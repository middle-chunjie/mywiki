## Description

Lixin He is a co-author of [[lu-2019-molecular]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China's Key Laboratory of Quantum Information. The paper places him on the team introducing MGCN as a scalable learned alternative to expensive quantum-chemistry computation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MGCN framework for multilevel molecular interaction modeling.
- Contributed to the framing of speed and transferability advantages over traditional baselines.
