## Description

RAGAS is the framework introduced in [[es-2023-ragas-2309-15217]] for automated, reference-free evaluation of retrieval-augmented generation pipelines.

## Key Contributions

- Defines metric procedures for [[faithfulness]], [[answer-relevance]], and [[context-relevance]].
- Supports evaluation without gold answers by combining LLM prompting and embedding-based similarity.
- Integrates with commonly used RAG toolchains such as llama-index and LangChain.
