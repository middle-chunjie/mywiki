## Description

Chaofeng Guo is a researcher at [[alibaba-group]] and a co-author of [[ouyang-2023-contrastive]], which augments ESMM with contrastive learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the integration of the supervised ESMM objective with a contrastive loss.
- Contributed to the industrial-scale evaluation on sparse-conversion advertising logs.
