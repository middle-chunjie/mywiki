## Description

Korbinian Pöppel is a co-author of the xLSTM paper and is listed with the Johannes Kepler University Linz / NXAI affiliation cluster in Linz, Austria. The paper marks him as an equal-contribution author.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the xLSTM paper's recurrent redesign based on exponential gating and new memory cells.
- SSM, and RNN baselines. Contributed to the empirical study comparing xLSTM against Transformer
