## Description

Ying Sheng is a Stanford University researcher and co-author of SORRY-Bench. The paper includes Ying Sheng in the author group behind the dataset, judge study, and benchmark analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the benchmark's empirical study of refusal patterns across models and categories.
- Contributed to the data and evaluation pipeline supporting SORRY-Bench.
