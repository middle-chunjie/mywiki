## Description

DeepMind Lab is the 3D reinforcement-learning environment suite used in [[oord-2019-representation-1807-03748]] to test CPC as an auxiliary objective on top of a batched A2C agent.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the five RL tasks used to measure whether CPC improves control learning.
- Shows that CPC helps on `4` of `5` tasks after `1` billion training frames.
