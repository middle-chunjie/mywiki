## Description

Taylor Berg-Kirkpatrick is a coauthor of Efficient Nearest Neighbor Language Models and is listed with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California, San Diego. In this paper, he contributes to the empirical study of efficient non-parametric language modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the analysis of adaptive retrieval, datastore pruning, and PCA compression for `kNN-LM`.
- Helped evaluate the speed-perplexity tradeoff on WikiText-103 and Law-MT.
