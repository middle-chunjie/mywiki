## Description

Xingxuan Li is the first author of [[li-2024-can-2410-01428]] and is affiliated with DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group, Hupan Lab, and Nanyang Technological University through a joint PhD program. In this paper, Li leads the proposal of CR-Planner for reasoning-heavy tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced CR-Planner for critic-guided reasoning and retrieval planning.
- Helped formulate MCTS-based trajectory collection for training the critic models.
