## Description

Tuan Dinh is a coauthor of [[dinh-2023-large-2306-03438]] affiliated with the [[university-of-wisconsin-madison]]. In this paper he contributes to the formulation and evaluation of [[buggy-code-completion]].

## Key Contributions

- Coauthors the introduction of [[buggy-code-completion]] and the definition of [[potential-bug]].
- Helps build the buggy-HumanEval and buggy-FixEval benchmarks used to evaluate code LLM robustness.
