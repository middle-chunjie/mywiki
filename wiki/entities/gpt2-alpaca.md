## Description

GPT2-Alpaca is a smaller aligned compressor model evaluated in [[jiang-2023-llmlingua-2310-05736]] as a weaker alternative to Alpaca-7B.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as a small-LM baseline for testing how compressor strength affects prompt-compression quality.
- The paper reports GSM8K EM of `77.02`, `76.42`, and `76.27` at `5x`, `14x`, and `18x` compression respectively.
