## Description

Federico Tombari is a co-author of the TokenFormer paper and is affiliated with Google in this work.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the benchmarking of TokenFormer on language and vision tasks.
- Helped position TokenFormer as a scalable alternative to scratch-trained Transformers.
