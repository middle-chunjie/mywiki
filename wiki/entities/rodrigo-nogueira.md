## Description

Rodrigo Nogueira is a researcher at the University of Campinas (UNICAMP), Brazil (rfn@unicamp.br), known for foundational work on neural and dense retrieval. He is a co-author of the UIA unified information access framework.

## Key Contributions

- Key contributor to dense passage retrieval research (MonoBERT, duoBERT re-ranking pipelines).
- Co-authored the UIA framework for personalized dense retrieval across multiple information access tasks.
