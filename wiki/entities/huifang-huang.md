## Description

Huifang Huang is a co-author of [[gui-2022-crosslanguage-2201-07420]] and is affiliated with the School of Mathematics and Statistics at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. The paper includes Huang among the collaborators developing and evaluating XLIR.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the cross-language evaluation study for binary-source and source-source matching.
- Co-authored the empirical analysis demonstrating gains from IR-based modeling.
