## Description

LeanDojo is the open-source Lean playground introduced in [[yang-nd-leandojo]] for extracting proof data and interacting with Lean programmatically. In the paper it names the broader released ecosystem of toolkit, benchmark, models, and documentation.

## Key Contributions

- Exports proof trees, proof states, tactic traces, and premise provenance from Lean.
- Provides a reliable interaction interface that sharply reduces proof-checking errors relative to prior tooling.
- Enables the construction of the LeanDojo Benchmark and the training of ReProver.
