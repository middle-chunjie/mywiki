## Description

Yuhao Zhang is a researcher affiliated with [[samaya-ai]] and a co-author of [[weller-2024-promptriever-2409-11136]]. The paper places him on the team introducing instruction-aware prompting as a control interface for dense retrievers.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the synthetic data pipeline for instruction generation and instruction-negative mining.
- Helps validate the claim that prompt-based control can transfer from language models to retrieval models.
