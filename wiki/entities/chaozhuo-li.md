## Description

Chaozhuo Li is a researcher at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications and a co-author of [[liu-2024-lighter-2409-15699]]. The paper lists Li as part of the team developing and evaluating FlexRAG.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the FlexRAG method for compressive context adaptation in RAG.
- Contributed to the experimental study on compression ratios, retriever variation, and ablations.
