## Description

Jurassic-1 is a large autoregressive language model used in [[borgeaud-2022-improving-2112-04426]] as an external comparison on the Pile. RETRO is positioned as competitive with Jurassic-1 despite having many fewer parameters.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the strongest contemporaneous baselines in the paper's large-scale comparison.
- Highlights that retrieval can narrow the gap between `7.5B` models and much larger closed-book systems.
