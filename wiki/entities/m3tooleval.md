## Description

M3ToolEval is the `82`-task benchmark introduced in [[wang-2024-executable-2402-01030]] to measure complex multi-tool, multi-turn agent behavior. It covers domains such as web browsing, finance, travel planning, science, and information processing.

## Key Contributions

- Evaluates whether action formats can support multi-tool composition and iterative correction.
- Reports both success rate and average interaction turns.
- Exposes a large gap between open-source and closed-source LLM agents on complex tasks.
