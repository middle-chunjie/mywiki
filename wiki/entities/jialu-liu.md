## Description

Jialu Liu is a researcher at [[google-research]] and a co-author of [[qin-2024-large-2306-17563]]. In this paper Liu contributes to the broader effort on LLM-based comparative ranking.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRP method and its benchmark study on passage reranking.
- Supported comparisons between open LLM rankers and strong supervised baselines.
