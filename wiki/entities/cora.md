## Description

Cora is a citation-network dataset used in [[gong-2022-magcl-2212-07035]] as a benchmark for unsupervised [[node-classification]].

## Key Contributions

- One of the six datasets used to compare MA-GCL with prior graph contrastive baselines.
- Used in both the public-split and random-split evaluations reported in the paper.
