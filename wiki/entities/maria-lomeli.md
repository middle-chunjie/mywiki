## Description

Maria Lomeli is a Meta AI Research coauthor of [[schick-2023-toolformer-2302-04761]]. She contributes to the paper's tool integrations, especially the use of external QA and retrieval components inside a language-model training loop.

## Key Contributions

- Helped build and evaluate Toolformer's external-tool stack across QA, search, calculator, MT, and calendar APIs.
- Coauthored experiments showing that tool use can outperform much larger frozen baselines on several zero-shot tasks.
- Contributed to the analysis of temporal and multilingual settings where tool utility varies by task.
