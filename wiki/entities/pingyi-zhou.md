## Description

Pingyi Zhou is a co-author of [[guo-2022-modeling]] and was affiliated with Noah's Ark Lab, Huawei, at the time of publication. In this paper Zhou contributes to the design and evaluation of CODESCRIBE for code summary generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the model that combines AST structure, token sequence modeling, and multi-source copying.
- Helped validate the method's gains over CopyTrans and mAST+GCN on both benchmarks.
