## Description

Sinead Williamson is a coauthor of [[malach-2026-infinity-2510-14826]], listed with Apple affiliation in the extracted paper. In this paper she contributes to the study of how interactive tools change the generalization behavior of recurrent and state-space language models.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the paper's formalization of long-form generation and the GSSM analysis.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison between Mamba, recurrent baselines, and Transformer baselines.
- Helped connect external-memory interaction to improved out-of-distribution generalization.
