## Description

`jina-embeddings-v4` is the flagship embedding model used throughout [[xiao-2026-embedding-2602-00079]] for detailed baseline tables, entropy analysis, and multimodal compression experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the headline `2048d` benchmark where the method improves compression from `1.20×` to `1.58×`.
- Provides the empirical exponent-distribution example showing strong angular concentration around exponent `127`.
- Appears in both single-vector and multi-vector evaluation settings.
