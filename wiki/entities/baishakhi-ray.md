## Description

Baishakhi Ray is a Columbia University researcher and co-author of [[ahmad-2021-unified]]. Her work in this paper centers on broad-transfer pre-training for software-engineering tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored PLBART and its multi-task evaluation across generation and understanding tasks.
- clone detection, and vulnerability detection. Supports the paper's claims about transfer to repair
