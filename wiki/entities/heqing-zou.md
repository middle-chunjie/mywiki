## Description

Heqing Zou is the first and corresponding author of UniS-MMC, affiliated with [[nanyang-technological-university]] (Singapore). His research focuses on multimodal learning, audio/speech processing, and cross-modal representation learning.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed UniS-MMC, the unimodality-supervised multimodal contrastive learning method for image-text classification.
- Earlier work on speech emotion recognition with co-attention-based multi-level acoustic features (ICASSP 2022).
