## Description

Lola Le Breton is a researcher affiliated with Chandar Research Lab, Mila, and Polytechnique Montreal, and a lead author of [[breton-2025-neobert-2502-19587]]. Her role in the paper centers on developing and validating NeoBERT as a modern encoder backbone.

## Key Contributions

- pretraining recipe, and controlled ablation study. Co-authored the NeoBERT architecture
- Helped position NeoBERT as an open encoder optimized for long-context embedding and classification workloads.
