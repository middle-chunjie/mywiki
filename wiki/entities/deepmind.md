## Description

DeepMind is the research lab affiliation listed for all authors of [[borgeaud-2022-improving-2112-04426]]. In this paper it provides the organizational context for scaling RETRO to trillion-token retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Hosted the team that introduced RETRO as a retrieval-enhanced large language model.
- Combined large-scale training infrastructure, retrieval indexing, and evaluation needed for the MassiveText experiments.
