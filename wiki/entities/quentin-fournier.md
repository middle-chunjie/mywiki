## Description

Quentin Fournier is a researcher at Mila and a co-author of [[breton-2025-neobert-2502-19587]]. In this paper he contributes to the modernization of BERT-style pretraining and evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the NeoBERT pretraining pipeline over RefinedWeb and the long-context extension stage.
- Contributed to the GLUE and MTEB evaluation setup used to isolate backbone quality.
