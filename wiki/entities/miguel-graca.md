## Description

Miguel Graça is a coauthor of LumberChunker and is affiliated with INESC-ID. The paper places him among the authors building and analyzing LLM-assisted segmentation for retrieval over narrative books.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the [[dynamic-chunking]] procedure used by LumberChunker.
- Contributes to the retrieval experiments on [[gutenqa]].
- Helps analyze chunking effects in [[retrieval-augmented-generation]] pipelines.
