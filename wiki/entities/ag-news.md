## Description

AG News is a topic-classification dataset used in [[wang-2023-label]] to test the anchor hypothesis and Hiddenanchor compression under longer demonstrations. It is the benchmark with the largest reported acceleration ratio.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as a four-way classification benchmark for saliency and intervention studies.
- Shows `81.94` accuracy with anchor re-weighting and up to `2.9x` compression speedup on GPT-J.
