## Description

Nesreen K. Ahmed is a researcher at Cisco Outshift and a coauthor of [[tehranijamsaz-2024-coderosetta-2410-20527]]. In this source she contributes to a model for unsupervised translation between programming languages and their HPC-oriented extensions.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the introduction of AST Entity Recognition and code-specific denoising objectives for CodeRosetta.
