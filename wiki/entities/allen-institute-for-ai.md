## Description

The Allen Institute for AI is a nonprofit AI research institute co-affiliated with SELF-RAG authors Yizhong Wang and Hannaneh Hajishirzi. The institute is associated with knowledge-intensive NLP and evidence-grounded language-model research.

## Key Contributions

- Co-affiliated senior contributors to the SELF-RAG project.
- Connects the paper to broader work on retrieval, QA, and grounded generation.
