## Description

OpenEA is the benchmark suite used in [[liu-2023-rhgn]] for evaluating entity alignment across cross-lingual and monolingual knowledge graph pairs. The paper uses its V1 splits with 15K reference alignments and no gold relation alignment labels.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the EN-FR, EN-DE, DBpedia-Wikidata, and DBpedia-YAGO evaluation settings used by RHGN.
- Provides standardized train, validation, and test splits that enable comparison against prior entity alignment methods.
