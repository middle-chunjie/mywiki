## Description

Daniel Khashabi is a researcher affiliated with Johns Hopkins University and a coauthor of this paper. His role in this work is tied to knowledge-intensive QA and the evaluation of when retrieval helps or harms factual answering.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the retrieval-augmentation analysis showing both large gains and harmful failure cases.
- Helped characterize efficiency and cost tradeoffs for selective retrieval.
