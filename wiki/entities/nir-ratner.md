## Description

Nir Ratner is a researcher at [[ai21-labs]] and the first author of [[ratner-2023-parallel-2212-10947]], which introduces [[parallel-context-windows]] for extending LLM context at inference time.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the PCW formulation based on positional-embedding reuse and window-local attention masking.
- Helped demonstrate training-free gains on in-context learning, retrieval QA, and multi-hop QA settings.
