## Description

Adji Bousso Dieng is the senior co-author of [[friedman-2023-vendi-2210-02410]], listed with affiliations to [[princeton-university]] and [[vertaix]]. The paper credits her with the same core contribution of formulating and validating the Vendi Score.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed [[vendi-score]] and its theoretical properties.
- Led experiments showing how the metric reveals [[mode-collapse]] and duplicate-heavy generations missed by baseline metrics.
