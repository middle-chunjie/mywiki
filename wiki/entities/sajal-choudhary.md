## Description

Sajal Choudhary is a co-author of [[yang-2024-crag-2406-04744]] and is listed on the paper under the Meta Reality Labs / FAIR, Meta / HKUST / HKUST(GZ) collaboration. He is part of the team developing CRAG as a realistic RAG benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the CRAG benchmark and its three-task formulation.
- Contributes to empirical evaluation of baseline and industry RAG systems.
