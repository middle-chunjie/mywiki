## Description

CREPE is the contemporary compositional benchmark used in [[ray-nd-cola]] for comparison against `COLA`. The paper treats it as an easier inverse image-to-text task where simpler adaptation methods already perform competitively.

## Key Contributions

- Provides an external benchmark for comparing `COLA` against prior compositional evaluation settings.
- Helps show that `COLA` is harder than CREPE because multimodal adaptation is more necessary on `COLA`.
