## Description

Sheng-Chieh Lin is a University of Waterloo researcher and co-author of [[hofst-tter-2021-efficiently-2104-06967]]. The paper credits him with work on dense retrieval training, in-batch supervision, and evaluation on TREC-style benchmarks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the dual-teacher dense retriever training recipe evaluated in the paper.
- Contributes to the empirical analysis on TREC-DL and MSMARCO Passage Ranking.
