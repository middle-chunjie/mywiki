## Description

Ye Liu is an author of [[fang-2021-guided]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China. He contributed to the work on guided attention for concept extraction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the model that uses clue words and title/topic signals as extra supervision.
- Helped validate the approach on three benchmark datasets.
