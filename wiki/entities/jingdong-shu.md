## Description

Jingdong Shu is a co-first author of [[wan-2019-multimodal-1909-13516]] and is affiliated with Zhejiang University. The paper credits Shu as part of the team developing MMAN for semantic source code retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the tri-modal MMAN architecture and its attention-based fusion mechanism.
- Contributed to the dataset construction and automatic retrieval evaluation on commented C methods.
