## Description

Zifu Wang is a co-author of the Skeleton-of-Thought paper and is affiliated with [[ku-leuven]]. The paper includes Wang in the collaboration that studies inference-speed improvements through answer-structure planning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the SoT method for parallel point expansion.
- Contributed to the paper's evaluation and analysis of latency-speed trade-offs.
