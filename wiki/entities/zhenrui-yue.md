## Description

Zhenrui Yue is a researcher affiliated with [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and a lead author of [[unknown-nd-inference]]. In this paper, Yue helps formulate inference scaling for long-context RAG and evaluate DRAG and IterDRAG across benchmark QA tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the DRAG and IterDRAG framework for scaling RAG at test time.
- Helps define effective context length as the paper's compute budget variable.
- Contributes to the empirical study of scaling laws and computation allocation for RAG.
