## Description

Gemini Nano is the on-device branch of the Gemini family in [[unknown-nd-geminia-2312-11805]], designed for memory-constrained deployment through small distilled and quantized model variants.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `1.8B` and `3.25B` parameter variants for low- and high-memory devices.
- Uses distillation from larger Gemini models plus `4-bit` quantization for deployment efficiency.
- Delivers strong summarized performance on factuality, reasoning, coding, and multilingual tasks relative to size.
