## Description

Longtriever is the dense retrieval model introduced in [[yang-2023-longtriever]] for encoding long documents with alternating inter-block and intra-block Transformer modules.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a tightly coupled hierarchical encoder for long-document dense retrieval.
- Introduces LMAE-based continuous pretraining for document-level semantic modeling.
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on MS MARCO Document Ranking and TREC 2019 Doc in the paper.
