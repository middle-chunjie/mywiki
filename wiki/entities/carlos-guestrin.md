## Description

Carlos Guestrin is a Stanford University researcher and co-author of [[sun-2024-learn-2310-13807]], contributing to the paper's meta-learning view of attention replacement.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the theoretical framing that makes linear attention and self-attention interpretable as special cases of TTT.
- Supported the paper's large-scale empirical comparison on ImageNet.
