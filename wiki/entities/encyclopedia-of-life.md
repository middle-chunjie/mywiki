## Description

Encyclopedia of Life is the structured biological database used in [[yin-2023-alcuna-2310-14820]] as the source knowledge base for constructing artificial entities. The paper relies on its taxonomic tree and rich attribute-relation metadata.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies `2,404,790` entities and `13,625,612` properties across `669` property types as the raw source for ALCUNA.
- Provides taxonomic classes that make KnowGen's parent-sibling construction procedure possible.
