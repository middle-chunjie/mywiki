## Description

Yue Yu is a researcher affiliated with the National University of Defense Technology and Peng Cheng Laboratory. In this paper, he co-authors the investigation into code-augmented training for LLM reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the construction and analysis of code-mixed pre-training and instruction-tuning settings.
- Helped study the effects of CoT prompting and dynamic code/text mixing.
