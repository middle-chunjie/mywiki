## Description

Zheyuan Hu is an author of [[hao-2020-asgn]] from the University of Science and Technology of China. He is part of the collaboration proposing ASGN for semi-supervised molecular graph learning and property prediction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ASGN framework for exploiting unlabeled molecules in property prediction.
- Helped support the model's evaluation under limited-label and active-learning settings.
