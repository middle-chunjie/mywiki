## Description

Mingyue Cheng is a researcher at the University of Science and Technology of China (Anhui Province Key Lab. of Big Data Analysis and Application), co-author of GAST for cross-domain sentiment classification.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the GAST model integrating POS-Transformer and Hybrid Graph Attention for cross-domain sentiment classification.
- Contributed to the design and empirical evaluation of the Integrated Adaptive Strategy.
