## Description

Adam D. Lelkes is a Google Research co-author of [[pradeep-2023-how]]. He is part of the team evaluating large-scale generative retrieval across multiple corpus sizes and model scales.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the experimental comparison of DSI variants, PAWA, and constrained decoding.
- Helps extend the analysis to model scales up to `11B` parameters.
