## Description

Luu Anh Tuan is a researcher at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, and a co-author of [[li-2023-rethinking-2310-08069]]. In this paper, Luu Anh Tuan contributes to the theoretical and empirical study of weighted contrastive learning for code retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the formulation and analysis of Soft-InfoNCE for non-uniform negative handling.
- Helps validate the method on CodeSearchNet across multiple pretrained code models.
