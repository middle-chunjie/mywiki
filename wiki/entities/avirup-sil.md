## Description

Avirup Sil is a researcher at IBM Research AI and a co-author of SELF-RAG. In this work he contributes to a retrieval-grounded LLM framework that critiques its own generations.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored SELF-RAG's retrieval and critique formulation.
- Contributed to the paper's empirical evaluation on factuality and citation-focused tasks.
