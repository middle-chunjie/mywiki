## Description

IRCoT is the iterative retrieval baseline compared against and combined with HippoRAG in [[guti-rrez-2024-hipporag-2405-14831]].

## Key Contributions

- Represents a strong multi-step retrieval approach for knowledge-intensive multi-hop QA.
- In the paper, combining IRCoT with HippoRAG yields the best overall retrieval and QA results across the evaluated datasets.
