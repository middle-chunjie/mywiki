## Description

Hritik Bansal is a Google DeepMind and [[ucla]] researcher and co-author of [[kazemi-2025-bigbench-2502-19187]], which presents [[bbeh]] for evaluating broad LLM reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored a benchmark that raises difficulty through longer contexts, more distractors, and more compositional reasoning demands.
- Helped compare frontier reasoning-specialized models against general-purpose models across `23` reasoning tasks.
