## Description

Frank F. Xu is a coauthor of RETOMATON and is listed with the Language Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon University in this paper. He contributes to retrieval-based language modeling research.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the RETOMATON method and its evaluation on WIKIText-103 and Law-MT.
- Helped study how datastore structure can be reused to reduce repeated retrieval.
