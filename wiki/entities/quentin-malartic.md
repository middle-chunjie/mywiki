## Description

Quentin Malartic is a co-author of [[penedo-2023-refinedweb-2306-01116]] and a member of the Falcon LLM team. In this paper, he is listed under affiliation marker `^2` in the author block.

- Contributed to the paper's claim that stringent refinement can make web data competitive with curated corpora.
