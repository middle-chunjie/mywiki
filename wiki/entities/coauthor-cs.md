## Description

Coauthor-CS is a co-authorship graph benchmark used in [[gong-2022-magcl-2212-07035]] for [[node-classification]] evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Extends the evaluation beyond citation graphs to a co-author network setting.
- MA-GCL reaches `94.19 +/- 0.1` accuracy on this benchmark in the paper.
