## Description

Yuchong Sun is a researcher from Renmin University of China and a co-first author of [[sun-2024-parrot-2310-07301]]. The paper notes that this work was carried out while he was visiting Kuaishou as an intern.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the Parrot framework for collecting higher-quality multi-turn instruction data.
- Contributed to training the question-asking model Parrot-Ask and the downstream Parrot-Chat system.
- Co-authored the construction and evaluation of Parrot-40K and MT-Bench++.
