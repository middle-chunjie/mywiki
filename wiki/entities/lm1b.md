## Description

LM1B is the One Billion Word Benchmark used for the paper's GPT-like unconditional language-generation experiments comparing a `97M` target model with smaller draft models.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the decoder-only language-model setting where a `6M` draft achieves `α ≈ 0.88-0.89` against a `97M` target.
- Provides evidence that speculative decoding is not limited to encoder-decoder translation tasks.
