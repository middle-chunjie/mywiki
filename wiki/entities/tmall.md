## Description

Tmall is a public e-commerce recommendation dataset used in [[ren-2023-disentangled]] to evaluate DCCF on purchase behavior with implicit feedback.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a third benchmark to test generalization beyond Gowalla and Amazon-book.
- Contains `47,939` users, `41,390` items, and `2,357,450` interactions with density `1.2e-3`.
