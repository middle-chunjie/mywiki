## Description

Lütfi Kerem Senel is an author of [[senel-2024-generative-2406-05255]] affiliated with the Center for Information and Language Processing at LMU Munich and the Munich Center for Machine Learning. In this paper he is part of the team proposing training-free optimization for generative recommenders.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper introducing CTR-guided Explore-Exploit optimization for generative question recommendation.
- Contributed to the paper's offline evaluation across e-commerce and Wikipedia question-suggestion settings.
