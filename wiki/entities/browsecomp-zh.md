## Description

BrowseComp-zh is the Chinese counterpart of BrowseComp-en and a main benchmark in [[li-2025-websailor-2507-02592]] for evaluating difficult browsing-based reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Measures whether a web agent can solve high-uncertainty information-seeking tasks in Chinese.
- Shows `30.1` accuracy for `WebSailor-72B`, close to the proprietary Doubao result reported in the paper.
- Supports the paper's claim that the training pipeline transfers across languages.
