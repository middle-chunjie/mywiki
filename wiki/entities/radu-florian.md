## Description

Radu Florian is a researcher at [[ibm-research-ai]] and a co-author of [[rosenthal-2025-clapnq]]. In this paper he contributes to the design and evaluation of a grounded LFQA benchmark for full RAG systems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CLAPnq benchmark and its framing across retrieval, generation, and full RAG evaluation.
- Helped analyze how current models trade off faithfulness, conciseness, and abstention on long-form answers.
