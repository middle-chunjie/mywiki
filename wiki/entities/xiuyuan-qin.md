## Description

Xiuyuan Qin is a researcher affiliated with Soochow University and the first author of [[qin-2023-metaoptimized]]. This paper presents Xiuyuan Qin as a contributor to meta-optimized contrastive learning for sequential recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed MCLRec, which combines stochastic data augmentation with learnable model augmentation.
- Contributed to the empirical validation of MCLRec on Sports, Beauty, and Yelp benchmarks.
