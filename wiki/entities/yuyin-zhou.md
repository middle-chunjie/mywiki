## Description

Yuyin Zhou is a researcher at UC Santa Cruz and a co-author of [[gao-2024-sculpting-2311-01734]]. The paper credits her on MixCon3D, a framework for holistic language-image-3D contrastive learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the method that combines improved optimization, fused image-point representations, and multi-view image features.
- Contributes to the empirical study showing better zero-shot 3D recognition on long-tailed and real-scanned benchmarks.
