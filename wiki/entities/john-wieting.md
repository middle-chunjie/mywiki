## Description

John Wieting is a researcher affiliated with [[google-deepmind]] and a co-author of [[thakur-2024-leveraging-2311-05800]]. In this work he contributes to the multilingual retrieval study built around synthetic query generation and SWIM-X training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the SWIM-IR dataset and multilingual retrieval evaluation pipeline.
- Helped position synthetic training against supervised baselines on XTREME-UP and MIRACL.
- Contributed to the paper's analysis of multilingual transfer and low-resource retrieval behavior.
