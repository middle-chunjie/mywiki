## Description

SceneGraphs is the scene-graph question answering dataset used in [[he-2024-gretriever-2402-07630]] as part of [[graphqa]]. The paper samples scene graphs from GQA and textualizes objects, attributes, and relations into a unified graph representation.

## Key Contributions

- Provides open-ended scene understanding questions evaluated by accuracy.
- Serves as the dataset on which the paper reports large efficiency gains after retrieval and runs its hallucination analysis.
