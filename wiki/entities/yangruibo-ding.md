## Description

Yangruibo Ding is a researcher affiliated with Columbia University and the lead author of [[ding-2022-dissimilarity-2110-03868]]. The paper notes that part of this work was done during his internship at [[ibm-research]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed DISCO, a code representation model trained to capture functional similarity and dissimilarity.
- Designed structure-guided augmentations that generate semantic clones and bug-injected hard negatives for pretraining.
