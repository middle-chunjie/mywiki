## Description

Md Arafat Sultan is a researcher at [[ibm-research-ai]] and a co-author of [[saad-falcon-2023-udapdr-2303-00807]]. In this paper he contributes to the collaboration on synthetic-query generation, reranker training, and distillation for zero-shot retrieval adaptation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the UDAPDR pipeline for adapting retrieval models to new domains without labeled in-domain queries.
- Helps evaluate the method across LoTTE, BEIR, Natural Questions, and SQuAD.
