## Description

Rajarshi Das is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and a coauthor of this paper. In this work he is part of the team studying factual memorization limits and retrieval-based mitigation strategies for language models.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the analysis of parametric versus non-parametric memory in factual QA.
- Helped frame Adaptive Retrieval as a simple system-level policy for selective evidence use.
