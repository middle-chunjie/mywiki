## Description

MPT-7B-Instruct is the strongest-performing evaluation model in [[merth-2024-superposition-2404-06910]], used to demonstrate the paper's main RAG efficiency and accuracy gains.

## Key Contributions

- Produces the headline NaturalQuestions-Open result: `0.465` accuracy with `93.7x` theoretical speedup versus naive RAG `0.026`.
- On MuSiQue, the best reported superposition setting reaches `F1 = 0.120` and `EM = 0.040`, outperforming the naive baseline.
