## Description

Daochen Zha is a researcher at [[rice-university]] and co-author of KnowGPT. He works on data-centric AI, reinforcement learning, and their intersection with NLP.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of KnowGPT; also lead author of a data-centric AI survey (Zha et al., 2023, arXiv:2303.10158).
- Contributed expertise in reinforcement learning to the path extraction module design.
