## Description

Guangxuan Xiao is the first author of the paper and is affiliated with the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He leads the empirical analysis of attention sinks and the proposal of StreamingLLM for long-stream decoding.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced the attention-sink explanation for why pure window attention collapses.
- Co-developed StreamingLLM, which keeps a small sink cache plus recent-token KV states.
- Helped evaluate the method across Llama-2, MPT, Falcon, and Pythia.
