## Description

Ziwei Liu is a faculty member at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, known for research on large vision models, generative models, and multimodal learning.

## Key Contributions

- Senior / corresponding author of [[zhang-nd-what]], directing the investigation into visual in-context learning and prompt retrieval.
- Known for foundational contributions to face recognition (DeepFashion, CelebA) and recent work on large-scale vision models.
