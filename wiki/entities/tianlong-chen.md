## Description

Tianlong Chen is a researcher at the University of Texas at Austin and co-author of H2O. His work spans model pruning, sparsity, and efficient training and inference of large neural networks.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the empirical analysis of attention sparsity and heavy-hitter token identification.
- Co-authored the theoretical formalization and ablation studies in the H2O paper.
