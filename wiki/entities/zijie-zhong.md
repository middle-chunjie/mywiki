## Description

Zijie Zhong is a researcher at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (PJ Lab), working on retrieval-augmented generation and information retrieval. He is the first author of the Mix-of-Granularity paper.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed the Mix-of-Granularity (MoG) and MoGG frameworks for adaptive chunk-granularity selection in RAG systems.
- Designed the soft-label training objective to circumvent non-differentiable top-k selection during router training.
