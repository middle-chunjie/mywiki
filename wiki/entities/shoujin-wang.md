## Description

Shoujin Wang is a researcher at the [[university-of-technology-sydney]] and a co-author of [[huang-2023-dual]]. His work in this paper centers on hierarchical sequential recommendation and temporal relation modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the hierarchical preference formulation used by HPM.
- Contributed to the empirical evaluation against strong SIGIR-style sequential recommendation baselines.
