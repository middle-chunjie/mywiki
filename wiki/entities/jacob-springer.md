## Description

Jacob Mitchell Springer is the first author of [[springer-2024-repetition-2402-15449]] and is affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]]. In this paper he helps introduce echo embeddings as a way to obtain stronger text embeddings from decoder-only language models.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the echo embeddings method for zero-shot and fine-tuned text embedding extraction.
- Helped design the synthetic causal-attention failure analysis and the MTEB evaluation protocol.
