## Description

Sachin Vaidya is a co-author of [[liu-2024-kan-2404-19756]] and is affiliated in the paper with Massachusetts Institute of Technology. In this source he is part of the team presenting KANs for small-scale AI-for-science problems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the KAN paper.
- Helped present the empirical case that KANs can improve accuracy and interpretability relative to MLPs on structured tasks.
