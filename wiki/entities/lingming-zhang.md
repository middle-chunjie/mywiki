## Description

Lingming Zhang is a University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign faculty researcher and the senior author of [[wei-2023-magicoder-2312-02120]]. He leads the work introducing OSS-INSTRUCT and Magicoder for open-source code LLM alignment.

## Key Contributions

- Directed the overall method design and empirical evaluation of Magicoder.
- Positioned open-source-code-inspired instruction tuning as an alternative to narrow synthetic-task bootstrapping.
