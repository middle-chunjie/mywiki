## Description

Evangelos Kanoulas is a researcher at the University of Amsterdam and a co-author of [[askari-2024-generative-2408-02152]], which introduces Few-Shot GR.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the method that uses pseudo queries and prompting to build a retrieval-time docid bank.
- Helps demonstrate strong benchmark performance on [[natural-questions]] and [[ms-marco]].
