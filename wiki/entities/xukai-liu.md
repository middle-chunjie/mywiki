## Description

Xukai Liu is an author of [[liu-2023-rhgn]] affiliated with the [[university-of-science-and-technology-of-china]]. In this paper he contributes to the RHGN method for entity alignment in knowledge graphs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored RHGN, combining relation-gated convolution, cross-graph embedding exchange, and soft relation alignment.
- Helped validate the model on four OpenEA benchmark datasets.
