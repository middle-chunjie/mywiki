## Description

Paul Röttger is a researcher at Bocconi University and a co-author of the PRISM dataset paper on multicultural and subjective alignment evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRISM dataset paper.
- Helped frame alignment evaluation as sensitive to participant diversity and subjective disagreement.
- Contributed to a dataset focused on controversial and value-laden LLM interactions.
