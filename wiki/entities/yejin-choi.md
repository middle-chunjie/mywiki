## Description

Yejin Choi is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and the Allen Institute for AI, and she is a co-author of REALTIME QA. The paper positions her as part of the team developing the benchmark and its language-model baselines.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper proposing REALTIME QA as a dynamic benchmark for present-time question answering.
- Helped frame the empirical comparison between retrieval-augmented and closed-book language-model baselines.
