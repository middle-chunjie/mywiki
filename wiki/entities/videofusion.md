## Description

VideoFusion is the text-to-video model used to test whether indirect anchors and mitigation strategies transfer from image generation to video generation.

## Key Contributions

- Extends the paper's evaluation beyond static images to `16`-frame video generation.
- Shows that co-occurrence-ranked keywords and combined mitigations remain relevant in video settings.
