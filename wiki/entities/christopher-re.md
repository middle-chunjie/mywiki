## Description

Christopher Ré is a Stanford University professor and senior author of [[saad-falcon-2024-benchmarking-2402-07440]], overseeing the LoCoV1 benchmark and the M2-BERT state-space retrieval encoder.

## Key Contributions

- Co-leads the paper's design of a long-context retrieval benchmark and retriever.
- Supports the use of Monarch Mixer and batch-size-independent fine-tuning for efficient long-document retrieval.
