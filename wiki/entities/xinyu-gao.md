## Description

Xinyu Gao is a researcher affiliated with the Shanghai Key Laboratory of Data Science, School of Computer Science, Fudan University. In this paper, Gao is a co-author of a survey on RAG for large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the 2024 RAG survey.
- Helped synthesize design choices spanning retrieval source, granularity, and post-retrieval processing.
- Contributed to the survey's framing of evaluation targets and future research directions.
