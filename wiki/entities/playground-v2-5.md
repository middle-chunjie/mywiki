## Description

Playground v2.5 is the paper's primary open-source text-to-image model for evaluating both copyrighted-character generation and mitigation effectiveness.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the main analysis platform for measuring `DETECT` and `CONS`.
- Shows strong vulnerability to both character-name and indirect anchoring.
- Demonstrates large risk reduction when prompt rewriting is combined with negative prompts.
