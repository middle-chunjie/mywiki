## Description

Xiaolong Wang is a UC San Diego researcher and co-author of [[sun-2024-learn-2310-13807]], where he helps study MTTT as a scalable alternative to attention on long sequences.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the raw-pixel ImageNet experiments showing that MTTT-MLP remains practical when standard self-attention is infeasible.
- Helped connect the paper's method design to transformer-style sequence modeling.
