## Description

Heung-Yeung Shum is a co-author of [[sun-2024-thinkongraph-2307-07697]] affiliated with IDEA Research and the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. The paper credits him as part of the collaboration developing Think-on-Graph.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's `LLM ⊗ KG` paradigm for deep reasoning on knowledge graphs.
- Helped position ToG as a plug-and-play alternative to training-heavy KG-enhanced LLM methods.
