## Description

Sudipta Sengupta is a coauthor of RETOMATON and is listed with an Amazon affiliation in this paper. He contributes to the paper's retrieval-efficient language modeling experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the RETOMATON framework for automaton-augmented retrieval.
- Helped evaluate the method in both in-domain and domain-adaptation settings.
