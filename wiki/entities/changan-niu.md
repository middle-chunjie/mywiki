## Description

Changan Niu is a researcher at the State Key Laboratory for Novel Software Technology, [[nanjing-university]], and the first author of [[niu-2022-deep-2205-11739]]. In this paper, he helps synthesize the design space and empirical behavior of pre-trained models for source code.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors a survey that organizes `20` CodePTMs across architecture, modality, pre-training tasks, and programming-language coverage.
- Summarizes evidence across `18` downstream software-engineering tasks and benchmark datasets.
