## Description

Omri Abend is a researcher at [[ai21-labs]] and a co-author of [[ratner-2023-parallel-2212-10947]], which proposes PCW as a minimal inference-time modification to pretrained LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the formulation that combines reused positional embeddings with modified attention masks.
- Contributed to the paper's analysis of bridge versus comparison multi-hop reasoning under PCW.
