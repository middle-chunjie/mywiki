## Description

ToolBench is the second main evaluation benchmark in [[unknown-nd-tool-2508-05152]]. The paper focuses on the `I1` level and builds category-level dependency graphs over its API collections.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the larger-scale benchmark for evaluating TGR.
- Provides the structured tool documents used by ToolBench-IR and TGR.
- Reveals that denser dependency graphs lead to larger retrieval gains.
