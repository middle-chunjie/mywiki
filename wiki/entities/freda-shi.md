## Description

Freda Shi is the first author of [[shi-2022-natural-2204-11454]], affiliated with [[meta-ai]] and Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago. Her role in this paper is to frame natural-language-to-code generation as a semantics-aware program selection problem.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduces `MBR-EXEC`, which reranks sampled programs by execution-result agreement.
- Helps design the cross-dataset evaluation on MBPP, Spider, and NL2Bash.
