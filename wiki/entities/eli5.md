## Description

ELI5 is a long-form explanation-seeking question answering dataset used in [[menick-2022-teaching-2203-11147]] to evaluate supported answers with inline evidence.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the longer-form evaluation setting for GopherCite.
- Shows a best reported score of `66.9 ± 7.0` S&P under `RL - top@16`.
- Motivates evaluation of explanatory answers rather than only short extractive facts.
