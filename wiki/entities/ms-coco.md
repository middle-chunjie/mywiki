## Description

MS COCO is the image-captioning dataset used in [[friedman-2023-vendi-2210-02410]] to evaluate diversity in text decoding. The paper compares human captions, beam search, and diverse beam search on this benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the caption groups used to compare [[vendi-score]] with [[n-gram-diversity]].
- Supports the paper's demonstration that stronger diversity penalties trade off against BLEU quality.
