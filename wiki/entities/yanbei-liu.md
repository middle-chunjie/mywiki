## Description

Yanbei Liu is a coauthor of the paper Multi-Scale Subgraph Contrastive Learning and is affiliated with Tiangong University in the author list.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MSSGCL, a multi-scale subgraph contrastive learning framework for graph representation learning.
- Contributed to the empirical analysis showing that semantic similarity varies systematically with subgraph scale.
