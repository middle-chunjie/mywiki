## Description

Nabeel Seedat is a co-author of LLAMBO and is affiliated in this paper with DAMTP, University of Cambridge. In this work Seedat is part of the team studying LLM-guided search in hyperparameter optimization.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the formulation of LLAMBO as a natural-language interface for Bayesian optimization.
- Participated in the end-to-end evaluation of the method on public, private, and synthetic HPO tasks.
