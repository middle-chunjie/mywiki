## Description

Hunter Lightman is an OpenAI researcher and co-author of [[lightman-2023-lets-2305-20050]]. The paper lists OpenAI as his institutional affiliation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the study showing that [[process-supervision]] can outperform [[outcome-supervision]] for ranking mathematical reasoning traces.
- Helped introduce the PRM800K data release used to train large-scale [[reward-model]] systems.
