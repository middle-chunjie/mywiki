## Description

Ninglu Shao is a researcher affiliated with the Gaoling School of AI at Renmin University of China and a co-author of [[liu-2024-lighter-2409-15699]]. The paper credits Shao as part of the FlexRAG research team.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the FlexRAG architecture for context compression and RAG adaptation.
- Helped validate the method on both long multi-document QA and open-domain QA benchmarks.
