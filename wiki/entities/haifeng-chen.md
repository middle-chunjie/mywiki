## Description

Haifeng Chen is a co-author of [[xia-2026-skillrl-2602-08234]] and is affiliated with NEC Labs America in the paper. He is part of the collaboration proposing SkillRL's skill abstraction and RL training loop.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the overall SkillRL method and its benchmark-driven validation.
- Helps establish that structured skills outperform direct raw-memory retrieval in agent training.
