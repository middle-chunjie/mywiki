## Description

Nagarajan Natarajan is a researcher at Microsoft Research India and a co-author of [[java-2026-frugalrag-2507-07634]]. In this paper he is part of the team developing the retrieval policy, reward shaping, and benchmark analysis for FrugalRAG.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the RL stage that learns when to stop retrieving under a fixed budget.
- Helped evaluate the method on HotPotQA, 2WikiMultiHopQA, and MuSiQue.
