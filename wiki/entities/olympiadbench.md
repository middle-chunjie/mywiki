## Description

OlympiadBench is a bilingual (Chinese/English) multimodal benchmark of Olympiad-level scientific problems designed to promote research toward AGI-level reasoning. In [[zhang-2024-accessing-2406-07394]] the pure-text subset (1275 problems) is used.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a high-difficulty evaluation setting for mathematical reasoning; MCTSr improves LLaMA-3 8B from 1.25% to 7.76% on the pure-text subset.
- Covers competition problems across mathematics and science at international Olympiad level.
