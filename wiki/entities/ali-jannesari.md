## Description

Ali Jannesari is a researcher at Iowa State University and a coauthor of [[tehranijamsaz-2024-coderosetta-2410-20527]]. He appears on the paper introducing CodeRosetta for unsupervised translation between conventional and parallel programming languages.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the end-to-end training recipe combining pretraining, denoising, back translation, and optional synthetic-data fine-tuning.
