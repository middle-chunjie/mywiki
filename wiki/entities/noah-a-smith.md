## Description

Noah A. Smith is a researcher affiliated with the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence and the University of Washington. The paper identifies him as one of the leaders of the OLMo project.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the OLMo technical report and helped lead the OLMo project.
- Helped frame OLMo as a fully open release spanning models, data, training, evaluation, and adaptation artifacts.
