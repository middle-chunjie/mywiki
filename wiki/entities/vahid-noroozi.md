## Description

Vahid Noroozi is a researcher at [[nvidia]] and a co-author of [[ficek-2025-scoring-2502-13820]], a paper on benchmark-based evaluation of synthetic verification methods.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the solution-generation, filtering, and ranking procedure used to build the new scoring benchmarks.
- Helped evaluate how generated tests and reward models track oracle correctness scores.
