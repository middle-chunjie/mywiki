## Description

Jin Liu is the corresponding author of [[guo-2022-modeling]] and was affiliated with Wuhan University at the time of publication. In this paper Liu co-leads the proposal of CODESCRIBE for hierarchical-syntax-aware code summarization.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the overall architecture that fuses Transformer code encoding with GraphSAGE AST encoding.
- Helped frame the empirical study comparing CODESCRIBE against eight prior baselines.
