## Description

LoCoV1 is the long-context retrieval benchmark introduced in [[saad-falcon-2024-benchmarking-2402-07440]]. It covers `12` tasks with long, evidence-dense documents drawn from multiple high-stakes domains.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the paper's main evaluation target for long-context retrieval quality.
- Aggregates tasks from Tau Scrolls, LongBench, QASPER, CourtListener, Australian Legal Case Reports, and StackOverflow.
- Exposes the weakness of truncation and chunk-averaging baselines on long documents.
