## Description

Guangyuan Ma is a researcher affiliated with the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. He is the first author of [[ma-2026-lightretriever-2505-12260]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed LightRetriever's asymmetric query/document retrieval design.
- Helped formulate the cached dense query embedding and count-based sparse query pipeline.
