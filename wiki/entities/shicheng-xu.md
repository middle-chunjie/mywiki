## Description

Shicheng Xu is a researcher affiliated with [[chinese-academy-of-sciences]] through the Institute of Computing Technology and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the first author of [[xu-2024-listaware-2402-02764]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed GenRT, a joint model for reranking and truncation in list-aware retrieval.
- Contributed experiments spanning web-search benchmarks and retrieval-augmented QA settings.
