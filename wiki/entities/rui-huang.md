## Description

Rui Huang is a co-author of [[jiang-2022-crossmodal-2211-09623]] and was affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]] and BNRist. In this paper Rui Huang contributes to the Cross-Modal Adapter approach for text-video retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the adapter-based retrieval method and its benchmark evaluation.
- Helped validate that shared adapter weights improve multimodal retrieval over uni-modal adapters.
