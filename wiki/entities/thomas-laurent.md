## Description

Thomas Laurent is a co-author of [[he-2024-gretriever-2402-07630]] and is affiliated with Loyola Marymount University in the paper. He is part of the team designing a retrieval-aware architecture for graph question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the G-Retriever framework that couples retrieved subgraphs with a frozen LLM.
- Helped evaluate the effects of retrieval and architecture ablations on WebQSP and related datasets.
