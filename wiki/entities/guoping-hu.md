## Description

Guoping Hu is a co-author of [[yin-2018-transcribing]] affiliated with FLYTEK Research. The extracted paper text also appears to render his name as "Guoping Hui", but the BibTeX metadata gives "Guoping Hu".

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper introducing the Spotlighted Transcribing Network.
- Participated in the empirical validation of STN on structural image transcription benchmarks.
