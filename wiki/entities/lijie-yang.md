## Description

Lijie Yang is a researcher at [[carnegie-mellon-university]] School of Computer Science and co-author of RaLMSpec, contributing to the design and evaluation of speculative retrieval for iterative RAG serving.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the RaLMSpec framework for low-latency iterative RAG serving.
- Contributes to the ablation study comparing prefetching, OS3, and asynchronous verification strategies.
