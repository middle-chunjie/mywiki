## Description

Somshubra Majumdar is a researcher at [[nvidia]] and a co-author of [[ficek-2025-scoring-2502-13820]], contributing to the evaluation of synthetic verifiers for code quality ranking.

## Key Contributions

- Helped design and analyze HE-R, HE-R+, MBPP-R, and MBPP-R+ as verifier-oriented benchmarks.
- Contributed to the comparison of reasoning-based test generation against reward-model scoring.
