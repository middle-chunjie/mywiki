## Description

Mike Lewis is a Meta AI researcher and co-author of this paper. Here he contributes to the study of why few-shot demonstrations help large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the analysis of direct and channel prompting across models from `774M` to `175B`.
- Helped evaluate how input distribution and label-space cues affect in-context learning performance.
