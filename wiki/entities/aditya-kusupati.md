## Description

Researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and Google Research, and a co-author of [[kusupati-2024-matryoshka-2205-13147]]. He helped introduce Matryoshka Representation Learning for nested, flexible embeddings.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MRL paper, which trains coarse-to-fine prefixes of a single embedding for adaptive classification and retrieval.
