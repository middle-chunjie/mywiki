## Description

Armand Joulin is a researcher affiliated in this paper with Meta AI Research. He co-authored the Contriever work on unsupervised and multilingual dense retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the unsupervised contrastive retrieval method behind Contriever.
- Contributed to the multilingual and cross-lingual retrieval results in the paper.
