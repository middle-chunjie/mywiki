## Description

Qiaoyu Tang is an author of [[tang-2024-selfretrieval-2403-00801]] affiliated with the Chinese Information Processing Laboratory, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed Self-Retrieval as a single-LLM architecture that unifies indexing, retrieval, and reranking.
- Helped formulate corpus internalization through self-supervised sentence-to-passage learning.
- Contributed to the constrained-decoding retrieval design and the empirical evaluation on NQ, TriviaQA, and MS MARCO.
