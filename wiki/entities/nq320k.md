## Description

NQ320K is the Natural Questions-derived retrieval benchmark used in [[wu-2024-generative-2404-00684]] for both reranking and end-to-end comparisons between GR and MVDR variants.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `109,739` documents, `7,830` test queries, and `307,373` training pairs in the reported setup.
- Serves as the main dataset for showing the reranking gap between query-to-document and document-to-query alignment.
- Supports the evaluation of PAWA and NP-decoding as document-encoding improvements for GR.
