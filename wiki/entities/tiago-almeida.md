## Description

Tiago Almeida is a researcher affiliated with the University of Aveiro, Portugal, and the lead author of [[almeida-2024-exploring]]. In this paper he studies efficient zero-shot synthetic dataset generation for information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed the NI-based document selection strategy used before question generation.
- Co-designed the benchmark comparing small language models and decoding strategies for synthetic IR data generation.
