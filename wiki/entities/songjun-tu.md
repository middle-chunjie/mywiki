## Description

Songjun Tu is a researcher affiliated with the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences and Pengcheng Laboratory. In this source, he is the first author of D2Skill.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes D2Skill for dynamic dual-granularity skill reuse in agentic RL.
- Contributes the empirical study covering ALFWORLD and WEBSHOP.
