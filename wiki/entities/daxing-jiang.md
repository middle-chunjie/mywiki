## Description

Daxing Jiang is a Microsoft Corporation researcher and co-author of [[yang-2021-xmoco]]. He contributes to the paper's cross-momentum training design and retrieval experiments for open-domain question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the xMoCo retrieval method for asymmetric question-passage matching.
- Helps validate the method on multi-dataset and single-dataset open-domain QA retrieval settings.
