## Description

Jason Choi is an author of [[senel-2024-generative-2406-05255]] affiliated with Amazon.com, Inc., Seattle. He co-authors the paper on training-free generative recommendation optimization using LLM prompts and simulated CTR feedback.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the feedback loop that replaces low-CTR questions with newly generated candidates.
- Helped evaluate Explore-Exploit against full-ctr and other ablations.
