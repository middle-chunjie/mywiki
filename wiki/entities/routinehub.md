## Description

RoutineHub is the shortcut-sharing community used in [[unknown-nd-workflowllm]] as a major source of expert-authored Apple Shortcuts workflows. It provides titles, descriptions, and linked iCloud shortcuts that the authors crawl and filter.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the primary source for the `14,771` high-quality collected shortcuts in the seed dataset.
- Anchors the paper's claim that real workflows are longer and more structurally complex than prior agent benchmarks.
