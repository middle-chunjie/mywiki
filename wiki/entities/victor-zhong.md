## Description

Victor Zhong is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and a coauthor of this paper. He contributes to the study's analysis of factual QA, retrieval augmentation, and long-tail knowledge behavior.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the empirical study of how entity popularity predicts factual memorization.
- Helped evaluate retrieval-augmented QA systems across POPQA and EntityQuestions.
