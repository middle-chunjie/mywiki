Yifan Luo is a researcher at Tsinghua University and an equal-contribution co-first-author of [[zhu-2024-rageval-2408-01262]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the RAGEval schema-based pipeline for generating scenario-specific RAG evaluation datasets.
- Co-introduced the DragonBall benchmark covering finance, law, and medical domains in Chinese and English.
