## Description

Karthik Venkat Ramanan is a researcher affiliated with the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and a co-author of [[samuel-2023-can-2309-12426]]. In this source he is part of the team evaluating GPT-4-generated synthetic data for extractive MRC.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the setup that generates contexts first and QA pairs second with few-shot prompting.
- Helped evaluate filtered and unfiltered synthetic data for downstream RoBERTa training.
