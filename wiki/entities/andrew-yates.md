## Description

Andrew Yates is a researcher affiliated with [[johns-hopkins-university]] and a co-author of [[weller-2025-rank-2502-18418]]. In this paper he is part of the team introducing a reasoning-trace-based reranker for search.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the distillation setup that transfers R1 reasoning behavior into smaller rerankers.
- Contributes to the paper's evaluation on hard retrieval benchmarks and manual analysis of DL19 labels.
