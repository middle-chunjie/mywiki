## Description

Shiguang Guo is a researcher at the University of Waterloo and a co-author of MMLU-Pro. The paper associates Guo with benchmark construction for evaluating advanced LLM reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MMLU-Pro, including the data-review process that removes noisy and ambiguous items.
- Helped analyze how the benchmark separates frontier models more clearly than MMLU.
