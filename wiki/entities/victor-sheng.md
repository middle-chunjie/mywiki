## Description

Victor Sheng is a researcher affiliated with Texas Tech University and a co-author of [[qin-2023-metaoptimized]]. The paper lists Victor Sheng as part of the team proposing MCLRec for sequential recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MCLRec framework and its benchmark study.
- Contributed to positioning the method against strong self-supervised and contrastive recommendation baselines.
