## Description

Stella Biderman is a researcher at EleutherAI and the lead author of the Pythia paper. In this work, she conceived, organized, and led the project and designed major case studies.

## Key Contributions

- Led the overall Pythia project and paper.
- Designed the memorization and pretraining-frequency case studies.
- Helped frame Pythia as a controlled scientific suite for LLM analysis.
