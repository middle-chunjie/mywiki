## Description

Rong Tong is a researcher at Singapore Institute of Technology and a co-author of [[sun-2024-consistency-2401-13598]], which proposes GenRDK for zero-shot document-level relation triplet extraction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the chain-of-retrieval prompting and denoising pipeline described in GenRDK.
- Helped validate the framework across both [[docred]] and [[re-docred]].
