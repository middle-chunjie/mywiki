## Description

Zhongliang Zhou is a PhD student at the University of Georgia and co-first author (equal contribution) of the Img2Loc paper on multimodal image geolocation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the Img2Loc retrieval-augmented generation framework for training-free image geolocation.
- Co-led experiments on Im2GPS3k and YFCC4k benchmarks.
