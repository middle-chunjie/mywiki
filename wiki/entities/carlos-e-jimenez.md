## Description

Carlos E. Jimenez is a co-first author of the C-STS paper and is affiliated with Princeton University in this source.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced the C-STS task formulation for conditioned sentence similarity.
- Contributed to benchmark construction and the empirical comparison across encoder architectures.
- Helped analyze how C-STS differs from traditional STS evaluation.
