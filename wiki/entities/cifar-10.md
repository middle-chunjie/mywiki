## Description

CIFAR-10 is one of the image classification benchmarks used in [[sener-2018-active-1708-00489]] to evaluate active learning for CNNs.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the coarse-grained `10`-class evaluation setting in the experiments.
- Helps demonstrate that geometric core-set selection is stronger when the class count is smaller.
