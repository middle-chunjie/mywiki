## Description

Zhou Yang is a researcher at [[singapore-management-university]] and the first author of [[yang-2022-natural-2201-08698]]. The paper presents Zhou Yang as a co-developer of ALERT, a naturalness-aware black-box attack on pre-trained models of code.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored ALERT, which combines natural substitute generation with greedy and genetic search for identifier renaming attacks.
- Helped evaluate robustness on [[codebert]] and [[graphcodebert]] across vulnerability detection, clone detection, and authorship attribution.
