## Description

Xinchao Wang is a paper author affiliated with the [[national-university-of-singapore]]. In this work, he co-argues that visual Mamba should be justified by task characteristics rather than adopted as a default backbone choice.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the hypothesis that Mamba is unnecessary for ImageNet classification but may still help dense long-sequence vision tasks.
- Co-designs the MambaOut family and its controlled ablation of SSM.
- Helps position MambaOut as a baseline for future visual Mamba research rather than as a universal replacement.
