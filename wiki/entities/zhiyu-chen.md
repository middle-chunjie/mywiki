## Description

Zhiyu Chen is an author of [[senel-2024-generative-2406-05255]] affiliated with Amazon.com, Inc., Seattle. The paper credits him as part of the team building a feedback-driven generative recommender for question suggestion.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper introducing Explore-Exploit prompting over historical question/CTR pairs.
- Contributed to the e-commerce and general-knowledge evaluation setup.
