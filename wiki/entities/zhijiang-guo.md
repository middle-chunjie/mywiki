## Description

Zhijiang Guo is a researcher at the University of Cambridge and a co-author of [[huang-2024-effilearner-2405-15189]]. He contributes to the study of efficiency-aware post-generation refinement for code LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the method and analysis showing profiler feedback is stronger than naive self-refinement.
- Helped position EFFI-LEARNER as a model-agnostic efficiency optimization framework.
