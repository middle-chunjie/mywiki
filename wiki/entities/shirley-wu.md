## Description

Shirley Wu is a researcher at Stanford University and a co-first author of [[wu-2024-stark-2404-13207]]. The paper presents her as part of the core team introducing the STaRK benchmark for semi-structured retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored STaRK, a benchmark for retrieval over textual and relational knowledge bases.
- Helped deliver the benchmark's dataset construction and empirical evaluation pipeline as part of the author team.
