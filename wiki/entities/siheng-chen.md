## Description

Siheng Chen is a researcher affiliated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University and MemTensor (Shanghai) Technology Co., Ltd, and the corresponding author of [[du-2026-infomosaicbench-2510-02271]]. This paper associates Chen with the benchmark design and empirical analysis of tool-augmented multi-source information seeking.

## Key Contributions

- Corresponding author of the InfoMosaic-Bench paper.
- Co-authored the benchmark and its analysis of tool-use failures and gains.
