## Description

MS MARCO Passage Ranking is the English dense-retrieval benchmark used in [[fang-2024-scaling-2403-18684]]. The paper uses it for model-size scaling, data-size scaling, annotation-quality experiments, and the joint budget-allocation fit.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies a corpus of `8.8M` passages and about `0.5M` training queries for the paper's main English experiments.
- Provides about `7,000` validation queries for monitoring contrastive perplexity and fitting the proposed scaling laws.
- Serves as the dataset on which the paper studies ICT, docT5query, and ChatGLM3-generated annotations.
