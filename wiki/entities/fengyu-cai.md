## Description

Fengyu Cai is the first author of the REVELA paper and is affiliated with the Technical University of Darmstadt in this source. He is one of the researchers proposing dense retriever learning via language modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored REVELA and its in-batch-attention training framework for dense retrieval.
- BRIGHT, and BEIR. Contributed to the empirical study spanning CoIR
