## Description

Haojun Jiang is the first author of [[jiang-2022-crossmodal-2211-09623]] and was affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]] and BNRist at the time of publication. He leads the paper's proposal and evaluation of Cross-Modal Adapter for text-video retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the Cross-Modal Adapter architecture for parameter-efficient CLIP adaptation.
- Helped design the retrieval experiments across five text-video benchmarks.
