## Description

PG19 is the long-book language-modeling benchmark used throughout the paper to evaluate perplexity under long streaming contexts. The authors use both the first `65K`-token book and the concatenated `100`-book test set.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the main perplexity benchmark for comparing dense attention, window attention, sliding-window recomputation, and StreamingLLM.
- Supports the paper's claim that StreamingLLM remains stable beyond `4 million` tokens.
