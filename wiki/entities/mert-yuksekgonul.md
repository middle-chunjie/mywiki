## Description

Mert Yuksekgonul is a co-author of the KITAB paper and is affiliated with Stanford University in the paper. The work builds directly on his earlier constraint-satisfaction framing for analyzing factual errors in language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored KITAB and extended prior constraint-satisfaction analysis into a benchmark dataset.
- Helped connect model failures to popularity and constrainedness.
