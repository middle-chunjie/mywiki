## Description

Qwen is one of the six large language models used to generate the real-world evaluation set in [[xu-2024-facerag-2407-01080]].

## Key Contributions

- Contributes `200` generated answers to the benchmark's real-world split.
- Records a `29.0%` error rate in the reported dataset statistics and is highlighted as having a relatively higher logical-fallacy proportion than GPT-4.
