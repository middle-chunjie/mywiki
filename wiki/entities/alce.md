## Description

ALCE is the citation-grounded answer-generation benchmark used in [[yen-2024-helmet-2410-02694]] for the generation-with-citations category. The paper evaluates its ASQA and QAMPARI subsets to test both answer quality and citation quality.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies HELMET's core benchmark instances for citation-aware long-form answer generation.
- Helps distinguish factual answering ability from citation-following ability in long contexts.
