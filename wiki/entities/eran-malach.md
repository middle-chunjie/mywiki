## Description

Eran Malach is a coauthor of [[malach-2026-infinity-2510-14826]], listed with Apple affiliation in the extracted paper. In this work he contributes to the theoretical and empirical study of interactive tool use for [[state-space-model]] length generalization.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the proof that CoT-only or single-turn GSSMs fail on sufficiently complex [[long-form-generation]] tasks.
- Coauthored the constructive interactive-tool result showing how external memory can recover [[length-extrapolation]].
- Contributed to the paper's arithmetic, reasoning, and coding experiments with tool-augmented models.
