## Description

Qinggang Zhang is a researcher at [[hong-kong-polytechnic-university]], co-first-author (equal contribution) of KnowGPT with Junnan Dong. His work focuses on knowledge graph integration with large language models and graph-based question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the KnowGPT black-box knowledge injection framework combining deep RL path extraction with MAB prompt construction.
- Co-author of HamQA (hyperbolic graph attention for multi-hop QA) referenced in the KnowGPT baselines.
