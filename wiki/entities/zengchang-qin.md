## Description

Zengchang Qin is a professor at Beihang University and co-founder/CTO of theSight Technology, serving as a corresponding author on the Mix-of-Granularity paper. His research spans machine learning and intelligent information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-supervised the Mix-of-Granularity project on adaptive chunking for RAG systems.
