## Description

Yihuai Lan is a researcher at Singapore Management University and a co-author of [[hu-2023-llmadapters-2304-01933]] on PEFT methods for reasoning with LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LLM-Adapters study on adapter placement, bottleneck size, and LoRA rank selection.
- Contributed to the evaluation across fourteen reasoning datasets.
