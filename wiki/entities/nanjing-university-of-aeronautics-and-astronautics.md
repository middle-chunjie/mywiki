## Description

Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA) is a Chinese research university in Nanjing, Jiangsu Province, known for engineering and computer science research. It is the home institution of Yu Zhou, Xiaoqing Zhang, and Juanjuan Shen in the ACCENT adversarial robustness work.

## Key Contributions

- Host institution for the ACCENT adversarial attack and masked training research on code comment generation.
