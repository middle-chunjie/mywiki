## Description

Xianyu Chen is a researcher at [[shanghai-jiao-tong-university]] and a co-author of [[long-2022-multiview]]. Chen contributed to the paper's program-graph formulation and empirical validation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the construction of semantic program views used by MVG.
- Contributed to the benchmark evaluation on algorithm classification.
- Helped compare MVG against NCC, TBCNN, LRPG, and Devign.
