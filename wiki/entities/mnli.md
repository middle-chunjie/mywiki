## Description

MNLI is a multi-genre natural language inference dataset used by supervised SimCSE alongside SNLI. In this paper it expands the coverage of entailment and contradiction pairs used for contrastive fine-tuning.

## Key Contributions

- Broadens supervised training beyond the domain of SNLI with additional NLI examples.
- Provides entailment and contradiction pairs that are integrated directly into the SimCSE loss.
- Helps make NLI supervision the strongest labeled-data option among the datasets compared in the paper.
