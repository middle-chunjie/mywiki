## Description

Sérgio Matos is a researcher at the University of Aveiro, Portugal, and co-author of [[almeida-2024-exploring]]. The paper positions his work at the intersection of synthetic data generation and neural information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the paper's retrieval-oriented filtering framework for synthetic questions.
- Co-authored the downstream evaluation showing that cheap synthetic data can train useful rerankers.
