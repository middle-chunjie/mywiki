## Description

GPT-3.5 is OpenAI's chat-oriented language model family evaluated in [[cheng-2023-batch-2301-08721]] to test whether batch prompting transfers beyond Codex. The paper reports that GPT-3.5 often retains or improves accuracy under batched prompting.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as evidence that batch prompting is compatible with chat-based API models.
- Shows notable gains on CSQA, GSM8K, AddSub, and RTE in the paper's comparison table.
