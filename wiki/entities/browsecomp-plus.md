## Description

BrowseComp-Plus is the complex reasoning benchmark used in the paper to evaluate deep-research agents. The reported setup uses `830` filtered problems together with a fixed `100K`-page web corpus.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the main benchmark for the paper's deep-research evaluation.
- Reveals a large performance gap between InfoSeek-trained models and models trained only on NQ plus HotpotQA.
