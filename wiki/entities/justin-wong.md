## Description

Justin Wong is a UC Berkeley co-author of this paper on SPEAC and UCLID5 text-to-code generation. He is part of the collaboration that studies how LLMs can be guided through intermediate languages and repair.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the SPEAC method and the UCLID5 case study.
- Contributes to the empirical analysis of LLM behavior on VLPL tasks.
