## Description

Davis Yoshida is an author of [[senel-2024-generative-2406-05255]] affiliated with Amazon.com, Inc., Seattle. He is part of the group developing an LLM-based optimizer for generative recommendation without fine-tuning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's iterative item-pool refinement method based on observed CTR.
- Helped evaluate the approach on simulated user populations across two domains.
