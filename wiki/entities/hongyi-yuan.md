## Description

Hongyi Yuan is a co-author of [[yuan-2023-rrhf-2304-05302]] with affiliations to both [[alibaba-damo-academy]] and [[tsinghua-university]] in this paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed RRHF and contributed to its empirical study on preference alignment for LLMs.
- Helped demonstrate that offline ranking over sampled responses can match PPO-style alignment quality.
