## Description

Neel Guha is a Stanford University researcher and co-author of [[saad-falcon-2024-benchmarking-2402-07440]], which studies long-context retrieval in domains such as law, science, and medicine.

## Key Contributions

- Helps assemble and analyze domain-specific long-context retrieval tasks within LoCoV1.
- Co-authors the study showing standard chunking and truncation strategies underperform on long documents.
