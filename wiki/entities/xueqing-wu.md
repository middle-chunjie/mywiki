## Description

Xueqing Wu is a researcher at [[ucla]] and a corresponding author of [[wu-2024-daco-2403-02528]]. In this work, she helps define application-driven data analysis, build the DACO resource, and study RL-based improvement for code-grounded analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the DACO dataset and benchmarking setup for stakeholder-oriented tabular analysis.
- Helped develop the multi-turn code-generation annotation pipeline and Daco-RL training framework.
