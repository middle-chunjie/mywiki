## Description

Sentence-BERT is the embedding model used in [[anand-2024-understanding-2408-17103]] to cluster passages and generated intents by cosine similarity.

## Key Contributions

- Produces embeddings for passage clustering with similarity threshold `0.8`.
- Produces embeddings for intent clustering with similarity threshold `0.9`.
