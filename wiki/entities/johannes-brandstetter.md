## Description

Johannes Brandstetter is a co-author of the xLSTM paper and is explicitly listed with the Johannes Kepler University Linz, NXAI Lab, and NXAI GmbH affiliations.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the xLSTM architecture and its scaling evaluation.
- Contributed to the framing of xLSTM as a recurrent alternative to Transformer and SSM language models.
