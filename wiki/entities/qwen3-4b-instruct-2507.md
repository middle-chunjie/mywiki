## Description

Qwen3-4B-Instruct-2507 is one of the backbone models used in [[tu-2026-dynamic-2603-28716]] for D2Skill training and evaluation. The paper studies both direct instruct initialization and an SFT-initialized variant on top of this model.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the smaller open backbone used for the main ALFWORLD ablation study.
- Shows that D2Skill can raise ALFWORLD success from `53.9` with GRPO to as high as `72.7`.
- Supports additional experiments on teacher-initialized RL training.
