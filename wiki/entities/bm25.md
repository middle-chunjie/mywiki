## Description

BM25 is the lexical retrieval model used throughout [[almeida-2024-exploring]] as both a baseline retriever and the preferred filter for synthetic question quality. The paper argues that BM25 better captures retrieval suitability than a pure relevance scorer while remaining computationally cheap.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the unsupervised baseline on all five downstream datasets.
- Acts as the main quality filter by checking whether a generated question retrieves its paired document within cutoff `k`.
- Provides candidate lists that are reused for negative sampling in reranker training.
