## Description

Jheng-Hong Yang is a University of Waterloo researcher and co-author of [[hofst-tter-2021-efficiently-2104-06967]]. In this work he is part of the team that develops and evaluates the TAS-Balanced dense retrieval method.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the topic-aware and balanced-margin sampling strategy for dense retriever training.
- Helps evaluate low-latency retrieval quality on TREC-DL and MSMARCO benchmarks.
