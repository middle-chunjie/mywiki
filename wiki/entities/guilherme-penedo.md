## Description

Guilherme Penedo is a co-author of [[penedo-2023-refinedweb-2306-01116]] and a member of the Falcon LLM team. In this paper, he is listed under affiliation marker `^1` in the author block.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored [[penedo-2023-refinedweb-2306-01116]], which introduced [[refinedweb]] and the MDR web-data refinement pipeline.
- Helped establish a web-only large-scale pretraining baseline that outperformed The Pile in the paper's zero-shot evaluations.
