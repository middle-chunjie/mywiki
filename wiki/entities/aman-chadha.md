## Description

Aman Chadha is a researcher affiliated with Stanford University and Amazon AI and a co-author of [[samuel-2023-can-2309-12426]]. In this paper he contributes to the analysis of LLM-based synthetic data augmentation for low-resource QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the study of GPT-4-generated contexts and QA pairs for specialized low-resource domains.
- Helped report the paper's opportunity-versus-challenge framing across the three benchmark datasets.
