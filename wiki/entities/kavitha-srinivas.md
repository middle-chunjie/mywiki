## Description

Kavitha Srinivas is a researcher at IBM Research, T.J. Watson Research Center and a co-author of the BLANCA benchmark paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the BLANCA benchmark for code-related text understanding.
- Helped analyze the effect of fine-tuning and multi-task training on embedding quality.
