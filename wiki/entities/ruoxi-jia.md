## Description

Ruoxi Jia is a Virginia Tech researcher and co-author of SORRY-Bench. In this paper, Ruoxi Jia helps develop the human-annotation-backed recipe for automated refusal evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the human judgment dataset used for judge training and meta-evaluation.
- Contributed to the comparison of off-the-shelf, few-shot, and fine-tuned safety evaluators.
