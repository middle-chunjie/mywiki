## Description

MuSiQue is one of the main multi-hop question answering benchmarks used in [[guti-rrez-2024-hipporag-2405-14831]] to evaluate retrieval and downstream QA quality.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a challenging answerable multi-hop QA setting with a retrieval corpus of `11,656` passages in the paper's `1,000`-question development setup.
- Shows consistent HippoRAG gains over single-step baselines and further gains when HippoRAG is used inside IRCoT.
