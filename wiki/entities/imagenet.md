## Description

ImageNet is the vision benchmark used in [[oord-2019-representation-1807-03748]] for linear evaluation of CPC image representations learned from overlapping image crops.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the transfer benchmark for CPC's visual representation quality.
- CPC reaches `48.7` top-1 and `73.6` top-5 accuracy on ImageNet linear evaluation in the paper.
