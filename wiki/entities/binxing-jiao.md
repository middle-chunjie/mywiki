## Description

Binxing Jiao is a Microsoft Corporation researcher and co-author of [[yang-2021-xmoco]]. In this paper, he contributes to adapting momentum contrastive learning to dense passage retrieval for open-domain QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the xMoCo formulation with separate fast/slow encoders and question/passage queues.
- Supports the empirical evaluation of xMoCo against DPR and BM25 across five QA datasets.
