## Description

Qi Ye is a researcher at [[huawei-poisson-lab]] and co-author of [[li-2024-retrollm-2412-11919]]. The paper ties Ye to the practical realization of retrieval-in-generation over large external corpora.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed RetroLLM's constrained evidence generation pipeline and experimental evaluation.
- Contributed to the study of efficient fine-grained retrieval for open-domain QA.
