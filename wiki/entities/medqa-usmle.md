## Description

MedQA-USMLE is a biomedical multiple-choice QA benchmark with 12,723 questions (4 options each) sourced from United States Medical Licensing Examination (USMLE) preparatory materials, requiring clinical and biomedical knowledge (Jin et al., 2021).

## Key Contributions

- Domain-specific benchmark in KnowGPT evaluation; KnowGPT achieves 78.1% test accuracy using the USMLE biomedical KG, outperforming ChatGPT by 29.4% and GPT-4 by 1.8%.
- Demonstrates that black-box KG injection is particularly impactful on specialized domains where LLMs lack coverage of professional knowledge.
