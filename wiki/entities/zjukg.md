## Description

ZJUKG is the Knowledge Graph research group at Zhejiang University, led by Professor Huajun Chen. The lab focuses on knowledge graph construction, embedding, reasoning, and KG-enhanced AI systems including LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Produced KnowPAT for domain-specific KG-enhanced LLM preference alignment.
- Maintains an active open-source presence at https://github.com/zjukg.
