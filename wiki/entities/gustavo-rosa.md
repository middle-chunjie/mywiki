## Description

Gustavo Rosa is a researcher at Microsoft Research and a co-author of *Textbooks Are All You Need*. The paper includes him in the team behind phi-1's small-model code-generation recipe.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the phi-1 paper on textbook-quality data and compact code LLM training.
