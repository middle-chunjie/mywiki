## Description

Yubo Wang is a researcher at the University of Waterloo and a co-author of MMLU-Pro. The paper places Wang on the benchmark construction and evaluation effort for more discriminative LLM assessment.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MMLU-Pro, a `12,032`-question benchmark spanning `14` disciplines.
- Helped study benchmark saturation, prompt robustness, and reasoning-oriented evaluation for frontier LLMs.
