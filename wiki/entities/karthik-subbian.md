## Description

Karthik Subbian is a researcher at Amazon and a co-senior author of [[wu-2024-stark-2404-13207]]. The paper lists him among the senior contributors to the STaRK benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-senior authored the STaRK paper on retrieval over semi-structured knowledge bases.
- Helped shape the benchmark's scope and evaluation as part of the senior author team.
