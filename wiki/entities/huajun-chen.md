## Description

Huajun Chen is a full professor at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, and the head of the ZJUKG lab. His research covers knowledge graphs, semantic web, and KG-enhanced AI systems.

## Key Contributions

- Corresponding/senior author of KnowPAT and multiple ZJUKG publications on KG-enhanced LLMs.
- Led the development of ZJUKG's research agenda connecting knowledge graphs to large language models.
