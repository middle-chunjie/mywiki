## Description

SciDocs is the scientific-document retrieval dataset included in [[almeida-2024-exploring]] to test synthetic-data generation on scholarly content. The paper reports that SciDocs is among the harder collections for question generation quality estimation.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a domain-shifted scientific retrieval benchmark in the five-dataset suite.
- Shows downstream improvement over BM25 for both highlighted synthetic-data configurations.
