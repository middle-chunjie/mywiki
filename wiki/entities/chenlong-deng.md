## Description

Chenlong Deng is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and co-author of the LLM4IR survey examining how large language models transform IR system components.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LLM4IR survey providing systematic coverage of LLM applications across IR pipeline stages.
- Contributed to the search agent and future direction sections of the survey.
