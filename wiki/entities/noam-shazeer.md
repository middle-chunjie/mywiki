## Description

Researcher at Google Brain at the time of the Transformer paper; equal-contribution co-author of [[vaswani-2017-attention-1706-03762]]. Also co-author of the sparsely-gated mixture-of-experts (MoE) paper cited as reference [32] of the Transformer work.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the Transformer architecture.
- Prior work on conditional computation and MoE informed the scaling philosophy later inherited by LLMs.
