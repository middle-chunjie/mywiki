## Description

Ziyi Ye is a co-author of [[su-2025-parametric-2501-15915]] affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]]. In this work, Ziyi Ye contributes to the empirical study of Parametric RAG on retrieval-augmented QA benchmarks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the experimental evaluation of Parametric RAG across multiple model sizes.
- Helped establish that parametric and in-context knowledge injection can be combined.
