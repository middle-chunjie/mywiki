## Description

Mohammadreza Zolfaghari is a computer vision researcher affiliated with the University of Freiburg; work on CrossCLR was done during an internship at Amazon Tübingen.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed CrossCLR, a cross-modal contrastive loss that adds intra-modal alignment and influential-sample-based negative pruning for multi-modal video representations.
