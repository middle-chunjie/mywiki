## Description

Yonatan Belinkov is a researcher at [[ai21-labs]] and a co-author of [[ratner-2023-parallel-2212-10947]], which studies inference-time methods for long-context LLM use.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the empirical analysis showing that PCW is strongest on tasks with many output labels.
- Helped position PCW relative to other long-context and prompting methods.
