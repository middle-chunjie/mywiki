## Description

Sara Rosenthal is a researcher at [[ibm-research]] and a co-author of [[katsis-2025-mtrag-2501-03468]]. She is listed as an equal-contribution author on the mtRAG paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the mtRAG benchmark and its human annotation workflow.
- Helped evaluate both retrieval and generation quality in multi-turn RAG settings.
