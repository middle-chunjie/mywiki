## Description

Hyunji Lee is the first author of RouterRetriever and appears in the paper as one of the `1`-affiliated authors together with Minjoon Seo. In this work, Lee helps frame routing over domain-specialized embedding experts as an alternative to a single general-purpose dense retriever.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored RouterRetriever, which routes each query to a domain-specialized LoRA expert.
- Contributed to the pilot-embedding routing design and the BEIR evaluation showing gains over single-model baselines.
