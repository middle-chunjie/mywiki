## Description

Taolue Chen is a professor at Birkbeck, University of London, UK, with research interests in formal verification, program analysis, and neural network robustness for software engineering. He is the corresponding author of the ACCENT paper.

## Key Contributions

- Led the ACCENT research effort on adversarial robustness of deep code comment generation as corresponding author.
- Contributed to formal problem framing of adversarial attacks under syntactic and semantic constraints for source code.
