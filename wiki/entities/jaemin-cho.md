## Description

Jaemin Cho is a researcher at [[unc-chapel-hill]] and the first author of [[cho-2023-visual-2305-15328]]. In this paper, he is associated with the design of VPGen and VPEval for interpretable text-to-image generation and evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the visual-programming formulation of both generation and evaluation in the paper.
- Helped develop the layout-aware LM setup and the modular T2I evaluation protocol.
