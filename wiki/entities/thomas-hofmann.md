## Description

Thomas Hofmann is a paper author and researcher affiliated with the Department of Computer Science at ETH Zurich. In [[he-2023-simplifying-2311-01906]], he co-authors the analysis showing that several standard Transformer-block components can be removed without sacrificing training speed.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the empirical and theoretical study of SAS / SAS-P simplified Transformer blocks.
- Frames architectural simplification as both an efficiency improvement and a way to align practical Transformer design with signal-propagation theory.
