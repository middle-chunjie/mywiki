## Description

Zhonghua Li is a researcher at [[huawei-poisson-lab]] and co-author of [[li-2024-retrollm-2412-11919]]. In this work, Li is part of the collaboration that studies constrained evidence retrieval inside autoregressive generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the RetroLLM method that replaces separate retrieval with clue-conditioned evidence generation.
- Helped evaluate how hierarchical constraints and forward-looking relevance affect QA performance and efficiency.
