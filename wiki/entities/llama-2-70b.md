## Description

Llama-2-70B is the larger prompted baseline compared against the trained `13B` robust model in [[unknown-nd-making-2310-01558]].

## Key Contributions

- Provides a scale-up baseline for testing whether more parametric knowledge alone improves robustness to irrelevant retrieval.
- Reaches `42.4` on NQ with top-1 Google retrieval, still below the trained `13B` SA-RetRobust model.
