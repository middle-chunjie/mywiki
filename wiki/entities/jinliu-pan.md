## Description

Jinliu Pan is a researcher affiliated with the Shanghai Key Laboratory of Data Science, School of Computer Science, Fudan University. Pan co-authored the survey paper reviewing retrieval-augmented generation for large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the survey of RAG paradigms and component designs.
- Helped summarize query rewriting, query expansion, and retriever adaptation methods.
- Contributed to the paper's discussion of robustness and deployment challenges.
