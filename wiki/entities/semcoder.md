## Description

SEMCODER is the semantic-aware code language model introduced in [[ding-2024-semcoder-2406-01006]], initialized from DeepSeekCoder-6.7B and trained to reason over multiple views of program semantics.

## Key Contributions

- Combines NL-to-code training with forward and backward monologue reasoning.
- Achieves strong code-generation, execution-reasoning, and self-refinement results at `6.7B` parameters.
