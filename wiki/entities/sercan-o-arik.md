## Description

Sercan O. Arik is a co-author of [[jin-2025-llm-2502-03699]] affiliated with [[google-cloud-ai-research]]. In this paper Arik is part of the team proposing LARPO for direct LLM alignment.

## Key Contributions

- Helped formulate LARPO as iterative retriever preference optimization.
- Contributed to the ranking-loss variants evaluated in the paper.
- Supported the empirical comparison against strong offline and online alignment baselines.
