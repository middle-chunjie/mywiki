## Description

Yuxi Bi is a researcher affiliated with the College of Design and Innovation at Tongji University. In this paper, Bi is a co-author of a survey that maps the modern RAG landscape for LLM systems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the 2024 survey on RAG architectures and workflows.
- Helped summarize evaluation dimensions such as faithfulness, answer relevance, and information integration.
- Contributed to the survey's outlook on multimodal and production-oriented RAG.
