## Description

Raj Dabre is the first author of this survey and is affiliated with NICT in Kyoto, Japan. In this paper he contributes to the synthesis of multilingual neural machine translation literature across architectures, datasets, and resource settings.

## Key Contributions

- low/zero-resource, and multi-source settings. Co-authored a structured taxonomy of MNMT covering multiway
- transfer learning, pivoting, and zero-shot translation. Summarized design trade-offs around parameter sharing
- Highlighted open research problems such as language-agnostic representations and multilingual evaluation gaps.
