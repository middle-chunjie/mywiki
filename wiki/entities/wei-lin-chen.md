## Description

Wei-Lin Chen is a co-author of [[unknown-nd-instructrag-2406-13629]] from [[university-of-virginia]]. In this paper Chen contributes to the rationale-supervision design that supports both in-context learning and supervised fine-tuning for noisy RAG.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the rationale-generation and rationale-learning formulation of InstructRAG.
- Helped compare the method against vanilla RAG, Self-RAG, and RetRobust baselines.
- Supported the ablation and generalization studies reported in the paper.
