## Description

MultiSpanQA is the closed-book question answering benchmark used in [[dhuliawala-2023-chainofverification-2309-11495]] to test CoVe beyond list generation.

## Key Contributions

- Contributes a filtered test set of `418` factoid questions with short multi-span answers.
- Shows CoVe improving F1 from `0.39` to `0.48` over the few-shot Llama 65B baseline.
