## Description

Zhiqing Sun is a coauthor of [[jiang-2023-active-2305-06983]] and is listed with [[carnegie-mellon-university]] in this paper. He contributes to the design and evaluation of FLARE for retrieval-grounded long-form generation.

## Key Contributions

- Helped formulate confidence-triggered retrieval based on low-probability tokens.
- Contributed to comparing implicit masking and explicit question generation for future-aware query formulation.
- Participated in evaluation across multi-hop QA, commonsense reasoning, long-form QA, and summarization.
