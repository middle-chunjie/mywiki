## Description

Ranjay Krishna is a researcher affiliated with Meta AI and the University of Washington in [[ray-nd-cola]] and a co-author of the paper. His contributions here center on compositional benchmarking and vision-language evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the `COLA` benchmark for measuring fine-grained attribute-object composition.
- Helped position text-to-image retrieval over hard distractors as a stricter benchmark setting than inverse image-to-text tasks.
