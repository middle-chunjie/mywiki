## Description

Mihaela van der Schaar is a co-author of LLAMBO and is affiliated in this paper with DAMTP, University of Cambridge. She is the senior collaborator on the work introducing an LLM-augmented Bayesian optimization pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the proposal of [[llambo]] as a modular framework for LLM-enhanced Bayesian optimization.
- Oversaw the study's evaluation across multiple hyperparameter optimization benchmarks and datasets.
