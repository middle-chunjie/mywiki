## Description

Ziyue Li is a co-author of [[li-2024-your-2410-10814]] from the Department of Computer Science at [[university-of-maryland-college-park]]. In this paper, Li studies how MoE router activations can be repurposed as sentence embeddings without extra finetuning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes MoEE, which combines routing weights and hidden states for training-free embedding.
- Helps analyze the complementary clustering and prompt-robustness behavior of routing weights versus hidden states.
