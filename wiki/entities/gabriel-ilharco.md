## Description

Gabriel Ilharco is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and the Allen Institute for AI. In this paper, he is a co-author on the study of transfer structure and task selection for multi-task NLP.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the empirical analysis of pairwise task transfer across architectures and adaptation methods.
- Co-authored the evaluation of TASKSHOP in both single-task and multi-task settings.
