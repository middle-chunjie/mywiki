## Description

BEAST is a domain-specific cognitive baseline for risky choice that the paper uses as the main non-LLM comparison on choices13k.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the strongest hand-crafted baseline on the decisions-from-description benchmark.
- Establishes that CENTaUR's gains are not only over raw LLaMA but also over a dedicated cognitive model.
