## Description

Eshaan Nichani is a co-author of [[malladi-2024-finetuning-2305-17333]] and is affiliated with [[princeton-university]]. In this paper, he contributes to the empirical evaluation of forward-only language-model fine-tuning across model scales and tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the experimental study showing MeZO can tune models from RoBERTa-large up to OPT-66B.
- Helped validate MeZO across classification, multiple-choice, and generation settings.
- Contributed to the paper's analysis of memory, storage, and wall-clock tradeoffs against full fine-tuning.
