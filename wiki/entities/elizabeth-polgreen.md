## Description

Elizabeth Polgreen is a University of Edinburgh co-author of this paper on SPEAC for UCLID5. She contributes formal-methods expertise to the study of text-to-code generation in very low-resource languages.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the SPEAC method for VLPL code generation.
- Helps connect the UCLID5 case study to formal verification practice.
