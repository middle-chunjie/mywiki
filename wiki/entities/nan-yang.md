## Description

Nan Yang is a researcher at [[microsoft-research]] and a co-author of [[ma-2023-finetuning-2310-08319]]. His work in this paper focuses on LLM-based retrieval and reranking design.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the RepLLaMA and RankLLaMA formulations for dense retrieval and pointwise reranking.
- Helps position the system against prompt-based reranking and prior dense retrieval baselines.
