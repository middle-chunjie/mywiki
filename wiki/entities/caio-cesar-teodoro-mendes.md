## Description

Caio César Teodoro Mendes is a researcher at Microsoft Research and a co-author of *Textbooks Are All You Need*. In this paper he is part of the team introducing phi-1's textbook-quality data recipe for code generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the phi-1 paper on filtered web code, synthetic textbooks, and CodeExercises finetuning.
