## Description

Nicola Tonellotto is an information retrieval researcher at the University of Pisa and co-author of [[macavaney-2024-reproducibility]]. The paper positions him as a contributor to the empirical study of efficient late-interaction retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the reproducibility analysis of PLAID and the comparison against BM25 reranking and LADR.
- Helps extend evaluation from sparse MS MARCO labels to the denser TREC DL 2019 setting.
