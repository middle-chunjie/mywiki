## Description

The University of Maryland is the primary institutional home for four authors of [[rodriguez-2021-evaluation]] and the main site where the DAD leaderboard work was conducted.

## Key Contributions

- Hosts the largest author cluster behind the paper: Pedro Rodriguez, Joe Barrow, Alexander Hoyle, and Jordan Boyd-Graber.
- Serves as the institutional base for developing and analyzing IRT-based leaderboard methodology.
