## Description

Shiguang Wu is a researcher at [[shandong-university]] and the first author of [[wu-2024-generative-2404-00684]], which connects generative retrieval to multi-vector dense retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-derives the reformulation of generative retrieval logits into the MVDR-style `sum(E_d^T Q ⊙ A)` framework.
- Contributes to the alignment analysis comparing GR's document-to-query direction with MVDR's query-to-document direction.
