## Description

Jianing Wang is a co-author of [[wu-2024-coral-2403-06447]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-california-san-diego]]. In this paper he contributes to the study of how collaborative evidence can better align LLM reasoning in recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the collaborative prompting formulation used by CoRAL.
- Contributed to evaluating long-tail recommendation performance under multiple backbone initializations.
