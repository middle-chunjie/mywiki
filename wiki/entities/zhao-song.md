## Description

Zhao Song is a researcher at Adobe Research and co-author of H2O. He specializes in theoretical aspects of attention mechanisms and submodular optimization applied to transformer models.

## Key Contributions

- Provided the theoretical analysis formalizing H2O as a dynamic submodular maximization problem.
- Co-authored the proof of the near-optimal approximation guarantee (Theorem 4.4).
