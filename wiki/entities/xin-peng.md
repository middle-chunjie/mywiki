## Description

Xin Peng is a researcher at Fudan University and a co-author of [[du-2023-classeval-2308-01861]], which benchmarks LLMs on class-level code generation.

## Key Contributions

- Helped define the benchmark construction methodology and evaluation scope.
- Contributed to comparing class-level results against function-level benchmarks such as HumanEval and MBPP.
