## Description

Xinbei Ma is the first author of [[ma-nd-query-2305-14283]] and is affiliated with [[shanghai-jiao-tong-university]]. The paper credits Ma with introducing the Rewrite-Retrieve-Read framework and the trainable query rewriter.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the Rewrite-Retrieve-Read pipeline for retrieval-augmented LLMs.
- Helped design the trainable T5-based rewriter optimized with PPO.
- Reported experiments on HotpotQA, AmbigNQ, PopQA, and MMLU.
