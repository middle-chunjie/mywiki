## Description

Nan Wang is a researcher affiliated with Jina AI GmbH in Berlin and a co-author of [[g-nther-2025-jinaembeddingsv-2506-18902]]. The paper positions `jina-embeddings-v4` as a single model for text, image, visually rich document, and code retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's multimodal multilingual retrieval model.
- Helped report results across retrieval, semantic similarity, and code-search evaluations.
