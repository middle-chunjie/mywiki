## Description

Abhinav Java is a researcher at Microsoft Research India and the first author of [[java-2026-frugalrag-2507-07634]]. In this paper he works on efficient multi-hop QA with adaptive retrieval and stop-policy learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored FrugalRAG, a two-stage framework that separates evidence exploration from retrieval stopping.
- Helped frame multi-hop RAG efficiency as a first-class objective alongside answer quality and recall.
