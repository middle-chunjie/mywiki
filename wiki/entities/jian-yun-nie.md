## Description

Jian-Yun Nie is a researcher affiliated with the University of Montreal and a co-author of [[mo-2024-historyaware-2401-16659]]. He is one of the senior authors behind the paper's history-aware conversational retrieval formulation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the overall HAConvDR framework that combines PRJ, denoised reformulation, and contrastive supervision mining.
- Helped frame the method around conversational search with topic shifts and historical feedback signals.
