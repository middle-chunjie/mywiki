## Description

Lewis Tunstall is a coauthor of the SETFIT paper and is affiliated with [[hugging-face]] in the author block. In this source he is part of the team proposing prompt-free few-shot text classification with Sentence Transformers.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the SETFIT method and its English, multilingual, and efficiency evaluations.
- Helped position sentence-transformer fine-tuning as a practical alternative to prompt-based few-shot methods.
