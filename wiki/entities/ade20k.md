## Description

ADE20K is the semantic segmentation benchmark used in [[yu-2024-mambaout-2405-07992]] to evaluate whether MambaOut can match visual Mamba backbones on long-sequence dense prediction.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the validation benchmark for the paper's semantic segmentation experiments with UperNet.
- Helps test the claim that SSM may still matter for long-sequence visual workloads even when it is unnecessary for ImageNet classification.
