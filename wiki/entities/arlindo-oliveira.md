## Description

Arlindo Oliveira is the senior author of LumberChunker and is affiliated with INESC-ID. In this source he is part of the team proposing LLM-guided segmentation for retrieval and QA over long-form books.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes LumberChunker for [[document-segmentation]] in narrative corpora.
- Co-authors the GutenQA [[benchmark]] used to evaluate retrieval quality.
- Helps connect chunking quality to downstream [[retrieval-augmented-generation]] performance.
