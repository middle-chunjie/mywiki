## Description

Harsh Trivedi is a researcher affiliated with [[stony-brook-university]] and the first author of [[trivedi-2023-interleaving-2212-10509]]. In this paper he is associated with retrieval-augmented multi-step QA and chain-of-thought-guided retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored IRCoT, which interleaves retrieval and chain-of-thought reasoning for knowledge-intensive QA.
- Helped evaluate the method on HotpotQA, 2WikiMultihopQA, MuSiQue, and IIRC.
