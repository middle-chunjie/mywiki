## Description

Jiawei Sun is a researcher affiliated with the Shanghai Research Institute for Intelligent Autonomous Systems at Tongji University. Sun co-authored the 2024 survey on RAG for LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the survey covering retrieval, generation, augmentation, and evaluation in RAG.
- Helped synthesize adaptive and iterative retrieval patterns.
- Contributed to the survey's treatment of long-context tradeoffs and future research directions.
