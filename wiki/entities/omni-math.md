## Description

Omni-MATH is an Olympiad-level mathematical reasoning benchmark for large language models, covering a broad range of competition mathematics problems. It is one of the four problem sources used in [[processbench]].

## Key Contributions

- Provides the hardest subset in [[processbench]]: 51.8% of solutions with correct final answers still contain process errors, the highest rate across all four subsets.
- Used alongside [[gsm8k]], [[math-dataset]], and [[olympiadbench]] to create a difficulty gradient in ProcessBench.
