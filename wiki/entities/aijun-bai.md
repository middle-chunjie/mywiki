## Description

Aijun Bai is a Google DeepMind researcher and a co-author of [[unknown-nd-inference]]. The paper places Bai on the team that studies how long-context LLMs should allocate retrieval, demonstrations, and iteration budget in RAG.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the introduction of DRAG and IterDRAG for long-context RAG.
- Helps analyze how different inference parameters affect performance under fixed compute budgets.
- Supports the benchmark evaluation showing gains from optimized test-time allocation.
