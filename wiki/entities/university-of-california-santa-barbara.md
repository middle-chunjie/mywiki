## Description

University of California Santa Barbara is the primary institutional affiliation for Xinyi Wang, Wanrong Zhu, Michael Saxon, and William Yang Wang in [[wang-2024-large-2301-11916]]. It is the academic base from which the paper's latent-variable demonstration-selection study was conducted.

## Key Contributions

- Hosts most of the paper's author team.
- Anchors the research environment for the paper's theory-plus-experiments workflow on in-context learning.
