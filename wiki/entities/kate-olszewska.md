## Description

Kate Olszewska is a Google DeepMind researcher and co-author of [[kazemi-2025-bigbench-2502-19187]], which introduces [[bbeh]] as a new benchmark for broad LLM reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored a benchmark that raises difficulty while preserving BBH's task diversity.
- Helped document how current models still struggle with broad reasoning despite major progress elsewhere.
