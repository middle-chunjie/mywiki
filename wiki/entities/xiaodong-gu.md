## Description

Xiaodong Gu is a co-author and corresponding author of [[gu-2018-deep]] and is affiliated with [[shanghai-jiao-tong-university]]. The paper ties Gu to the proposal of CDCS and to the broader line of neural code-search research cited throughout the work.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CDCS method that combines pre-training, MAML, and target-language fine-tuning.
- Helped position the work against earlier deep code-search models and transfer-learning baselines.
