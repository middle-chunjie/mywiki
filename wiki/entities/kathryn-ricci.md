## Description

Kathryn Ricci is a researcher affiliated with [[johns-hopkins-university]] and a co-author of [[weller-2025-rank-2502-18418]]. In this paper she contributes to building and analyzing reasoning-based reranking models for information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the Rank1 training pipeline distilled from DeepSeek R1 reasoning traces.
- Contributes to the empirical evaluation across BRIGHT, NevIR, and mFollowIR.
