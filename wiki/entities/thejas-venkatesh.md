## Description

Thejas Venkatesh is a researcher affiliated with [[samaya-ai]] and a co-author of [[yen-2025-lost-2510-18939]]. In this paper he is part of the team introducing Slim for long-horizon agentic search.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the Slim framework that separates search and browse actions.
- Contributes to the empirical evaluation of long-horizon search on BrowseComp and HLE.
