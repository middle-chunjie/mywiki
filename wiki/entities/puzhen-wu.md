## Description

Puzhen Wu is affiliated with University College Dublin and co-authored [[huang-2024-effilearner-2405-15189]]. He is one of the researchers behind the paper's profiler-driven code-refinement framework.

## Key Contributions

- Helped introduce EFFI-LEARNER as a model-agnostic efficiency optimization method.
- Participated in the cross-benchmark evaluation on EffiBench, HumanEval, and MBPP.
