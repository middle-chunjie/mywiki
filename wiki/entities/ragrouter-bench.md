## Description

RAGRouter-Bench is the dataset and benchmark introduced in [[wang-2026-ragrouterbench-2602-00296]] for studying adaptive routing across multiple RAG paradigms.

## Key Contributions

- Standardizes five paradigms under a shared abstraction for cross-paradigm comparison.
- Combines four corpora, three query types, and both effectiveness and efficiency evaluation.
- Introduces corpus-level structural and semantic metrics as routing signals.
