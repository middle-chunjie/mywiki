## Description

Pythia 70M is the smallest generator highlighted in [[almeida-2024-exploring]] as an efficiency-oriented baseline for zero-shot synthetic question generation. With random sampling it offers the best speed-quality trade-off among the paper's showcased downstream settings.

## Key Contributions

- Represents the low-cost end of the paper's generator spectrum.
- Produces a synthetic dataset that still improves over BM25 across all downstream collections.
