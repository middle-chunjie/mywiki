## Description

Harkirat Singh Behl is a researcher at Microsoft Research and a co-author of *Textbooks Are All You Need*. In this paper he is one of the authors behind the phi-1 training and evaluation pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the phi-1 work connecting curated data mixtures to strong code benchmark performance.
