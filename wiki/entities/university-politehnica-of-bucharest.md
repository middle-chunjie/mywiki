## Description

The University Politehnica of Bucharest (UPB) is Romania's leading technical university, comprising the Faculty of Automatic Control and Computers (ACS), which hosts NLP and AI research groups working on text processing, educational technology, and complex word identification.

## Key Contributions

- Research group led by Mihai Dascalu at the ACS faculty conducts NLP research including automated text simplification, CWI, and cross-lingual transfer learning.
- Supported by Romanian National Authority for Scientific Research and Innovation (UEFISCDI) under the ATES project (TE 70 PN-III-P1-1.1-TE-2019-2209).
