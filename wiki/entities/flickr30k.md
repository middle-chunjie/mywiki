## Description

Flickr30K is one of the established text-to-image retrieval benchmarks used in [[levy-2023-chatting-2305-20062]] for zero-shot comparison against CLIP and BLIP.

## Key Contributions

- Provides an external benchmark for testing whether dialog also helps off-the-shelf retrieval models.
- Helps show that concatenated Q&A text improves retrieval even for models not trained on dialogs.
