## Description

Nikhita Vedula is an author of [[senel-2024-generative-2406-05255]] affiliated with Amazon.com, Inc., Seattle. In this work she is part of the author team studying question suggestion as a generative recommendation problem.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the question-generation formulation used to evaluate generative recommendation.
- Contributed to the reported experiments on user personas, CTR, and recommendation quality.
