## Description

Sedigheh Eslami is a researcher affiliated with Jina AI GmbH in Berlin and a co-author of [[g-nther-2025-jinaembeddingsv-2506-18902]]. The paper studies multimodal embedding quality and reports cross-modal alignment gains over CLIP-style systems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the model and benchmark presentation for `jina-embeddings-v4`.
- Helped analyze modality-gap reduction and cross-modal alignment behavior.
