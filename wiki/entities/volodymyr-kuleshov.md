## Description

Volodymyr Kuleshov is a Cornell University computer scientist and co-author of [[morris-2023-text-2310-06816]]. In this paper he is part of the team studying how easily dense text embeddings can be inverted into original text.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the Vec2Text formulation that treats embedding inversion as controlled generation with embedding feedback.
- Helped validate inversion performance across GTR-base, OpenAI embeddings, BEIR corpora, and MIMIC clinical notes.
