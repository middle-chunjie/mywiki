## Description

Hansi Zeng is a researcher affiliated with [[university-of-massachusetts-amherst]] who is marked in [[unknown-nd-inference]] as having done the work while at [[google-deepmind]]. Zeng co-authors the paper's iterative long-context RAG study and its analysis of multi-hop QA.

## Key Contributions

- Contributes to the design of IterDRAG for decomposing complex questions into sub-queries.
- Helps evaluate retrieval and reasoning behavior on multi-hop QA benchmarks.
- Co-authors the analysis of inference scaling laws for RAG.
