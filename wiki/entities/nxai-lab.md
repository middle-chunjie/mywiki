## Description

NXAI Lab is one of the Linz-based institutional affiliations on the xLSTM paper. It is part of the research environment that produced the xLSTM architecture and its public code release.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as an affiliation base for several xLSTM authors.
- Contributed the institutional setting behind the paper and its released implementation.
