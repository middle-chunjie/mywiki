## Description

BERTopic is the topic-modeling framework used in [[li-2024-your-2410-10814]] to inspect the semantic themes induced by clustering over routing-weight and hidden-state embeddings. In this paper it supports the qualitative comparison between the two representation families.

## Key Contributions

- Helps reveal that RW and HS clusters emphasize different topic words and thematic structure.
- Provides qualitative evidence for the paper's claim that routing-weight embeddings capture complementary semantics.
