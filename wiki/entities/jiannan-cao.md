## Description

Jiannan Cao is a co-author of [[liu-2024-raisf-2403-06840]] and is listed with [[massachusetts-institute-of-technology]] in the paper's author block. The paper connects this author to the design of the RA-ISF framework and its benchmark analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored RA-ISF, including its recursive decomposition procedure with threshold `D_th = 3`.
- Contributed to experiments showing RA-ISF remains competitive when the three sub-models are reduced from Llama 2-7B to T5-780M.
