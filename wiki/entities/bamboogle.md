## Description

Bamboogle is the paper's manually written dataset of varied 2-hop questions intended to be hard for a popular search engine to answer directly but still answerable from constituent facts.

## Key Contributions

- Contributes `125` hand-authored compositional questions that are less template-bound than Compositional Celebrities.
- Highlights the advantage of self-ask over chain-of-thought on more varied inference questions.
