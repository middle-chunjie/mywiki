## Description

Shiji Song is a co-author of [[jiang-2022-crossmodal-2211-09623]] and was affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]] and BNRist. In this paper Shiji Song contributes to the multimodal retrieval research program around CLIP adaptation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Cross-Modal Adapter paper and its benchmark study.
- Helped establish the empirical comparison between shared adapters and vanilla adapters.
