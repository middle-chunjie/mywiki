## Description

Zhihao Zhang is a researcher affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]] and a co-author of this survey on efficient LLM serving. In this source he contributes to the review of serving algorithms and deployment systems for generative LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the survey's synthesis of speculative decoding, parallel computation, and memory-management approaches.
- Helps survey open-source serving stacks and their latency-versus-throughput priorities.
