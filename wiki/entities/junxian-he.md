## Description

Junxian He is a coauthor of RETOMATON and is listed with the Language Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon University in this paper. His role here is tied to efficient retrieval for neural language models.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored RETOMATON's automaton-based approximation of nearest-neighbor search.
- Helped compare RETOMATON against ADAPTRET and standard `kNN-LM`.
