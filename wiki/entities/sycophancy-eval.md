## Description

SycophancyEval is the evaluation benchmark used in [[weston-2023-system-2311-11829]] to test whether opinionated prompts sway model behavior in factual QA and longform generation.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the modified TriviaQA factual QA setting used to measure sycophancy under suggested or refuted answers.
- Supplies the longform argument prompts used to evaluate answer quality and objectivity under inserted opinions.
