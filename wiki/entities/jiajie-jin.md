## Description

Jiajie Jin is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and first author of [[jin-2024-bider-2402-12174]]. In this paper, Jin helps frame retrieval-to-generator mismatch as a key evidence refinement problem for RAG.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced BIDER for refining retrieved documents into compact key supporting evidence.
- Helped design the three-stage training pipeline spanning synthesis, seq2seq distillation, and preference alignment.
