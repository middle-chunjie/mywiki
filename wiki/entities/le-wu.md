## Description

Le Wu is the corresponding author of [[yang-2023-generative-2307-05100]] and is affiliated with [[hefei-university-of-technology]]. Wu is a recurring author in graph-based recommendation research cited throughout the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the proposal to replace manual graph augmentation with variationally sampled contrastive views.
- Helped design the cluster-aware twofold contrastive objective used in VGCL.
