## Description

Huaying Yuan is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and co-author of the LLM4IR survey, contributing to the systematic review of LLM-enhanced information retrieval systems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the comprehensive survey covering LLM applications to query rewriting, retrieval, reranking, and reading modules.
- Contributed to the taxonomy of LLM-based approaches across all IR pipeline stages.
