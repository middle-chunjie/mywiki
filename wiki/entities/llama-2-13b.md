Llama-2-13B is the base language model used throughout [[unknown-nd-making-2310-01558]] for prompted baselines and for QLoRA fine-tuning into retrieval-robust RALMs.

## Key Contributions

- Is fine-tuned into SA-NoRet, SA-Ret@1, and SA-RetRobust for the main training comparison.
