## Description

Qipeng Wang is a researcher at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China. He co-authored [[sun-2025-rearter-2501-07861]] on trustworthy process rewarding for retrieval-augmented reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored [[sun-2025-rearter-2501-07861]], which presents ReARTeR for multi-step retrieval-augmented reasoning.
- Part of the reported empirical comparison against RAG, RAG-CoT, and PRM-based planning baselines.
