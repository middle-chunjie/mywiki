## Description

Xingcheng Yao is a co-first author of SimCSE and is affiliated with Tsinghua University in this source, with the paper noting that the work was done during a remote visit to the Princeton NLP group. He contributes to the training design and empirical evaluation of the method.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the sentence-embedding training framework used in SimCSE.
- Helped evaluate supervision choices across NLI, QQP, Flickr30k, and ParaNMT.
- Contributed to the ablation studies on dropout, pooling, and hard negatives.
