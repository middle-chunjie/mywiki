## Description

Yulei Sui is a co-author of [[gui-2022-crosslanguage-2201-07420]] and is affiliated with the University of Technology Sydney. In this work Sui is part of the collaboration proposing XLIR for code matching across modalities and languages.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the IR-based formulation for cross-language binary-source matching.
- Contributed to the study of robustness across architectures, compilers, and optimization settings.
