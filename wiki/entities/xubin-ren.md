## Description

Xubin Ren is a researcher at [[university-of-hong-kong]] and the first author of [[ren-2024-representation]], which introduces RLMRec for LLM-enhanced recommendation representation learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed the RLMRec framework that aligns collaborative and LLM-derived semantic representations.
- Helped evaluate the method across Amazon-book, Yelp, and Steam with multiple graph-based recommender backbones.
