## Description

Claude 3.5 Sonnet is one of the black-box LLMs evaluated in [[wang-2024-astute-2410-07176]]. The paper uses it for both controlled conflict analysis and the main robustness experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the model for reporting the paper's `19.2%` conflict rate analysis.
- Achieves the strongest reported overall score in Table 1 with Astute RAG at `61.7`.
