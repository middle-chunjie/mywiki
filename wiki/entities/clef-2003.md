## Description

CLEF 2003 is the multilingual retrieval benchmark used in [[yang-2024-distillation-2405-00977]] with English queries over German, French, Spanish, and English documents. It is one of the paper's main MLIR evaluation collections.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the four-language CLEF testbed where MTD is compared against BM25, document translation, English distillation, and MTT.
- Shows best reported MTD effectiveness up to `0.699 nDCG@20`, `0.535 MAP`, and `0.922 Recall@1000`.
