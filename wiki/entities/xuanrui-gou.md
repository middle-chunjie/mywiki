## Description

Xuanrui Gou is a researcher affiliated with the School of Cyber Security, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, and a co-author of [[ma-2026-lightretriever-2505-12260]]. The work studies how to reduce online query computation without discarding document-side LLM modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the dense and sparse query-lightweight retrieval design in LightRetriever.
- Helped evaluate the method on multilingual retrieval benchmarks and speed tests.
