## Description

NarrativeQA is the long-document question answering dataset used in [[yen-2024-helmet-2410-02694]] to evaluate book- and script-scale comprehension. The paper pairs it with GPT-4o-based judging to better score long, open-ended answers.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of HELMET's main long-document QA datasets.
- Motivates the paper's reference-based model evaluation for long-form answers.
