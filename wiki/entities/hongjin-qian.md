## Description

Hongjin Qian is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and a co-author of [[mao-2023-large-2303-06573]]. In this work, he contributes to using prompted LLM outputs as robust representations of contextual search intent.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the LLM4CS method for conversational search intent understanding.
- Contributed to experimental comparisons against conversational query rewriting and dense retrieval baselines.
