## Description

Chengshu Li is the first author of [[li-2023-chain-2312-04474]] and is affiliated with [[stanford]]. In this paper, he is part of the team proposing Chain of Code for hybrid interpreter-and-LM reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed [[chain-of-code]] as a framework that interweaves Python execution with a [[language-model-emulator]].
- Co-evaluated the method on [[big-bench-hard]] and [[gsm8k]].
- Helped position the approach as a bridge between semantic reasoning and executable tool use.
