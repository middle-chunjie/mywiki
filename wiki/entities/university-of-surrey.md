## Description

The University of Surrey is the primary affiliation for Yucheng Li, Bo Dong, and Frank Guerin in [[li-2023-compressing]]. It is the institution from which most of the paper's author team introduces Selective Context.

## Key Contributions

- Hosts three of the four authors of [[li-2023-compressing]].
- Serves as the main institutional base for the paper's long-context compression study.
