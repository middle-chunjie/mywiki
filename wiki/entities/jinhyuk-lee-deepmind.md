## Description

Jinhyuk Lee is a researcher at [[google-deepmind]] and the lead author of [[lee-nd-rethinking]]. Disambiguated from [[jinhyuk-lee]] by affiliation: Google DeepMind.

## Key Contributions

- Led the paper introducing XTR for efficient multi-vector retrieval.
- Helped reposition token retrieval as the main training and inference target in late-interaction retrieval.
- Co-authored the efficiency analysis showing that gathering-free scoring can drastically reduce rescoring cost.
