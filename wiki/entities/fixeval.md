## Description

FixEval is a context-aware program-repair benchmark derived from competitive-programming submissions. In [[dinh-2023-large-2306-03438]], it serves as the basis for constructing buggy-FixEval with realistic buggy and accepted submission pairs.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies rejected and accepted user submissions that let the paper mine realistic [[potential-bug]]s.
- Enables evaluation beyond synthetic operator mutations by grounding [[benchmark]] instances in actual contest code.
