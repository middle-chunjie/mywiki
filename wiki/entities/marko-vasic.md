## Description

Marko Vasic is a co-author of [[vasic-2019-neural-1904-01720]], affiliated in this paper with [[google-brain]] and [[university-of-texas-at-austin]]. He works on neural program repair for variable-misuse bugs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the joint localization-and-repair pointer-network formulation for variable misuse.
- Helped evaluate the model on synthetic Python data and real developer-history snapshots.
