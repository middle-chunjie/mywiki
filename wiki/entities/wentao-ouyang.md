## Description

Wentao Ouyang is a researcher at [[alibaba-group]] and the lead author of [[ouyang-2023-contrastive]] on contrastive learning for post-click conversion modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed the CL4CVR framework for leveraging unlabeled interaction data in CVR prediction.
- Helped introduce embedding masking, false negative elimination, and supervised positive inclusion for feature-rich advertising data.
