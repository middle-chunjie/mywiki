## Description

Chengkai Huang is a researcher affiliated with The University of New South Wales on this paper. He is the first author of [[huang-2023-dual]] and works on sequential recommendation with relation-aware and contrastive modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed the HPM framework for hierarchical preference modeling in sequential recommendation.
- Helped design the dual-transformer, SCEL, and dual contrastive learning modules.
