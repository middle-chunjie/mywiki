## Description

Yunpu Ma is a co-author of [[yan-2026-memoryr-2508-19828]] affiliated with Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich and the Munich Center for Machine Learning. In this paper, he is listed as the corresponding author for the Memory-R1 study.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's two-agent RL framework for memory management and answer generation.
- Contributed to the empirical analysis of data efficiency, scaling, and zero-shot transfer.
