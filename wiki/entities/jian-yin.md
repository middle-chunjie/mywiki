## Description

Jian Yin is the corresponding author of [[guo-2023-longcoder-2306-14893]] and is affiliated with [[sun-yat-sen-university]] in the paper. The work attributes to this team the proposal of LongCoder and the release of the associated long-code benchmark and codebase.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the syntax-guided sparse attention design used in LongCoder.
- Served as corresponding author for the LongCoder and LCC release.
