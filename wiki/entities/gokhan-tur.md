## Description

Gokhan Tur is a researcher at [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and a co-author of [[qian-2025-smart-2502-11435]]. In this work he contributes to the broader study of when language agents should ask, search, or reason internally.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the SMART framework for balancing external tools with parametric knowledge.
- Contributed to the multi-domain evaluation of strategic tool use.
- Helped frame user-intention clarification as a first-class tool decision problem.
