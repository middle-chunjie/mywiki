## Description

Yongliang Ma is a researcher affiliated with Langboat Technology and a co-author of [[ma-2026-lightretriever-2505-12260]]. The paper evaluates lightweight query encoding for large-scale bilingual retrieval benchmarks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the LightRetriever framework for efficient LLM-based retrieval.
- Contributed to the benchmark evaluation across BeIR and CMTEB Retrieval.
