## Description

PIC-Bench is the benchmark introduced in [[he-2025-precise-2506-06589]] for evaluating claim-faithful long-form generation under full and partial Precise Information Control settings.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `8` long-form tasks spanning `6` full-PIC and `2` partial-PIC settings.
- Measures claim-level faithfulness through automatic claim extraction and claim verification rather than coarse whole-response judgments.
- Reveals that perfect PIC remains rare even for frontier LMs.
