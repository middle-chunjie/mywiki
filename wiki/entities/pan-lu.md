## Description

Pan Lu is a researcher affiliated with Stanford University and a co-senior author of AgentFlow. In this paper he helps define the agentic framework and the on-policy optimization strategy for its planner.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the AgentFlow system for effective planning and tool use.
- Co-developed Flow-GRPO and the experimental analysis showing planner-specific gains.
