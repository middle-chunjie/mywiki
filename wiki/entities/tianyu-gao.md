## Description

Tianyu Gao is the lead author of [[gao-2024-how-2410-02660]] and is affiliated with Princeton Language and Intelligence at [[princeton-university]]. In this paper, he helps formulate the long-context training recipe and evaluation methodology behind ProLong.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ablation-driven recipe for continued long-context training and SFT.
- Helped establish the paper's evaluation protocol centered on downstream long-context tasks after SFT.
- Contributed to the final ProLong model and its empirical comparison against open and closed long-context baselines.
