## Description

Hamed Zamani is a researcher at the University of Massachusetts Amherst working on conversational information seeking and information retrieval. In [[samarinas-2024-procis]], he co-authors the ProCIS benchmark and its proactive retrieval framing.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored ProCIS as a benchmark for proactive contextual suggestion in conversations.
- Helped formulate the proactive retrieval task and the associated benchmark methodology.
