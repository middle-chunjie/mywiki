## Description

Soujanya Poria is a researcher at Singapore University of Technology and Design and a co-author of [[hu-2023-llmadapters-2304-01933]] on parameter-efficient LLM adaptation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LLM-Adapters paper and its reasoning-focused benchmark study.
- Helped demonstrate that adapter-tuned smaller LLMs can outperform stronger baselines on some in-distribution tasks.
