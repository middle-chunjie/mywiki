## Description

Keisuke Sakaguchi is a researcher affiliated with Tohoku University and a co-author of REALTIME QA. In this paper he is part of the team that builds the weekly annotation and evaluation pipeline for time-sensitive QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper introducing the REALTIME QA platform and its real-time submission workflow.
- Helped establish the benchmark framing around current-world questions and dynamic evaluation.
