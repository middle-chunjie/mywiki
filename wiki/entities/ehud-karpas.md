## Description

Ehud Karpas is a researcher at [[ai21-labs]] and a co-author of [[ratner-2023-parallel-2212-10947]], which studies how to expose LLMs to more context during inference.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PCW approach for windowed long-context processing without finetuning.
- Helped evaluate the method on retrieval-based QA and information extraction.
