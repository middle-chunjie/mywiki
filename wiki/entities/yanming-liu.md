## Description

Yanming Liu is the first author of [[liu-2024-raisf-2403-06840]] and is affiliated with [[zhejiang-university]] in the paper's author block. The paper positions him as one of the core contributors to the RA-ISF retrieval-augmented reasoning framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored RA-ISF, which combines self-knowledge judgment, passage relevance filtering, and iterative question decomposition.
- Contributed to experiments showing GPT-3.5 average exact match of `55.0` across five open-domain QA benchmarks.
