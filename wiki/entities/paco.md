## Description

PACO is the parts-and-attributes dataset used in [[ray-nd-cola]] to test attribute-object retrieval on more realistic object categories. The paper uses PACO to measure how much multimodal adaptation helps on seen and unseen compositions in natural images.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies a real-image benchmark with `75` object categories and `55` attributes for `COLA`-style evaluation.
- Confirms that multimodal adaptation improves retrieval even when absolute performance remains modest.
