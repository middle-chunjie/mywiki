## Description

Yao Wan is a corresponding author of [[gui-2022-crosslanguage-2201-07420]] and is affiliated with Huazhong University of Science and Technology. In this paper Wan co-leads the XLIR work on LLVM-IR-based cross-language code matching.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the XLIR approach that aligns binary and source code through intermediate representations.
- Helped curate the benchmark and empirical analysis reported in the paper.
