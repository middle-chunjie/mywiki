## Description

Zhankui He is a co-author of [[wu-2024-coral-2403-06447]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-california-san-diego]]. In this source he is part of the team developing reinforcement-learning-based collaborative retrieval for recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the sequential retrieval-policy design used by CoRAL.
- Participated in the experimental validation on Amazon long-tail recommendation tasks.
