## Description

OctoTools is the agentic framework introduced in [[lu-2025-octotools-2502-11271]]. It combines standardized tool cards with a planner, executor, verifier, and summarizer to solve complex reasoning tasks without additional training.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces a modular planner-executor workflow for multi-step tool use.
- Standardizes heterogeneous external tools through tool cards that support extensible integration.
- Adds a lightweight task-specific toolset optimization procedure and reports strong gains across 16 benchmarks.
