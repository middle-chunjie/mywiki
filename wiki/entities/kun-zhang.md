## Description

Kun Zhang is a researcher at Nanjing University of Science and Technology and a co-author of [[sun-2024-consistency-2401-13598]] on consistency-guided knowledge retrieval and denoising for zero-shot extraction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the method that fuses synthetic and pseudo labels through cross-document consistency scoring.
- Contributed to the reported gains over ChatGPT and LLaMA2 baselines on document-level extraction benchmarks.
