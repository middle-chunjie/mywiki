## Description

Mariam El Mezouar is a researcher at the Royal Military College of Canada and a co-author of [[breton-2025-neobert-2502-19587]]. She is part of the collaboration that studies architecture, data, and optimization updates for encoder-only models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the empirical analysis of NeoBERT design choices on GLUE and MTEB.
- Helped evaluate the effect of long-context pretraining and efficiency-focused systems changes.
