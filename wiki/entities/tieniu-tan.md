## Description

Tieniu Tan is a leading researcher at the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS). He is a senior figure in computer vision and pattern recognition research in China.

## Key Contributions

- Senior author and principal investigator overseeing domain adaptation and generalization research at NLPR/CASIA.
- Co-authored the Environment Label Smoothing work for Domain Adversarial Training.
