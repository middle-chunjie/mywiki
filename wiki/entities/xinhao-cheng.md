## Description

Xinhao Cheng is a researcher affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]] and a co-author of this survey on efficient LLM serving. In this paper he contributes to the systems-oriented review of scheduling, kernels, and deployment trade-offs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the survey's treatment of request scheduling and framework-level implementation choices.
- Helps organize the paper's practical discussion of latency metrics such as TTFT and TPOT.
