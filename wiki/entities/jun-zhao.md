## Description

Jun Zhao is a co-author of [[weng-2023-large-2212-09561]] and was affiliated with the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the paper's argument that answer verification can be implemented with prompting alone, without an auxiliary trained verifier.
- Helped position self-verification as a general inference-time improvement for LLM reasoning.
