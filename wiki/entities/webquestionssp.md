## Description

WebQuestionsSP is a benchmark for question answering over [[freebase]] and the second dataset used in [[ding-2024-enhancing-2402-02175]]. The paper follows the split of Zhang et al. and reports `2,848` train, `250` validation, and `1,639` test questions.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a comparatively simpler benchmark where EPR remains competitive with the strongest IR baselines.
- Helps show that increasing the number of retrieved atomic patterns has smaller effects on simpler KGQA questions.
