## Description

Oleksandra Prudnikova is a co-author of the xLSTM paper. The paper presents her as part of the same Linz-based JKU / NXAI research team behind xLSTM.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the xLSTM paper's design and evaluation of extended LSTM cells.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison across language-modeling and long-context benchmarks.
