## Description

Hannaneh Hajishirzi is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and the Allen Institute for AI. She is the senior author on SELF-RAG and is known for work on knowledge-intensive NLP and retrieval-grounded reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the SELF-RAG project on adaptive retrieval and self-reflective generation.
- citation quality, and open-domain QA. Helped frame the method around grounded long-form generation
