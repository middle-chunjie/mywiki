## Description

Darren Edge is a Microsoft Research researcher and co-first author of [[edge-2024-local-2404-16130]]. Edge is part of the team that proposes [[graphrag]] for corpus-scale query-focused summarization.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the GraphRAG pipeline based on LLM-extracted graphs and hierarchical community summaries.
- Contributed to the paper's evaluation of global sensemaking over large corpora.
