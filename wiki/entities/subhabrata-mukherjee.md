## Description

Subhabrata Mukherjee is a researcher at [[microsoft-research]] and a co-first author of [[mukherjee-2023-orca-2306-02707]]. He proposed the Orca idea, led implementation and experiments, and designed much of the data collection, training, and evaluation setup.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed the Orca project and drove the overall research direction.
- Designed the data collection, training, and evaluation protocol.
- Led implementation, experiments, and major writing for the paper.
