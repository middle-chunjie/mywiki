## Description

Llama-3.1-70B-Instruct is a 70-billion-parameter instruction-tuned language model from Meta, part of the Llama 3.1 series. It is one of the critic models evaluated on [[processbench]].

## Key Contributions

- Achieves F1 = 52.7 average on [[processbench]] via majority voting, the best among non-reasoning-specialized open-source models at its time in the Llama family.
- Used as a solution generator for the OlympiadBench and Omni-MATH subsets of [[processbench]].
