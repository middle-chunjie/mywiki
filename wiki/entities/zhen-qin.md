## Description

Zhen Qin is a co-author of [[jin-2025-llm-2502-03699]] affiliated with [[google-deepmind]]. In this paper Qin contributes to the formulation and evaluation of LARPO as an IR-inspired alignment method.

## Key Contributions

- Helped connect ranking theory from IR with direct LLM preference optimization.
- Contributed to the listwise and contrastive variants of LARPO.
- Supported the benchmark study on AlpacaEval 2 and MixEval.
