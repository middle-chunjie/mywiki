## Description

Linyi Li is a corresponding author of [[li-2024-infibench-2404-07940]] and is affiliated with Simon Fraser University. The paper positions them as a lead contributor to the design and evaluation of the InfiBench benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the introduction of InfiBench for evaluating code-LLM question answering.
- Helped analyze large-scale evaluation results across more than `100` code LLMs.
