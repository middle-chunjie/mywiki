## Description

Arijit Ghosh Chowdhury is a researcher affiliated with the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and a co-author of [[samuel-2023-can-2309-12426]]. In this paper he helps study whether synthetic QA data from GPT-4 can improve low-resource reading comprehension.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the experiments comparing synthetic augmentation against original-data and T5 baseline setups.
- Helped analyze robustness differences across CovidQA, PolicyQA, and TechQA.
