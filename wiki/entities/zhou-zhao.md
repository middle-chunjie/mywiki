## Description

Zhou Zhao is a co-author of [[wan-2019-multimodal-1909-13516]] and is affiliated with Zhejiang University. In this paper Zhao is part of the collaboration studying attention-based multi-modal semantic code retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MMAN retrieval framework that fuses token, AST, and CFG representations.
- Contributed to the paper's qualitative analysis of interpretability through attention visualization.
