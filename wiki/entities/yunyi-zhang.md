## Description

Yunyi Zhang is a researcher at [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and a co-author of [[gao-2021-scaling-2101-06983]]. The paper credits Zhang as part of the team introducing gradient-cache training for memory-limited contrastive retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the derivation that separates representation-level and encoder-level gradients in contrastive training.
- Contributed to the retrieval experiments validating gradient cache on single-GPU hardware.
