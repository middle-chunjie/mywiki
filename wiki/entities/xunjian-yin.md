## Description

Xunjian Yin is a co-author of [[yin-2023-alcuna-2310-14820]] and is affiliated with [[peking-university]] in the paper. He contributes to the proposal of KnowGen and the construction of the ALCUNA benchmark for evaluating LLMs under new knowledge.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced KnowGen for generating artificial entities from structured biological knowledge.
- Helped build and analyze the ALCUNA benchmark across knowledge understanding, differentiation, and association settings.
