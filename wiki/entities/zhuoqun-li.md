## Description

Zhuoqun Li is the first author of [[li-2025-deepsolution-2502-20730]], affiliated with the Chinese Information Processing Laboratory, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the introduction of [[solutionbench]] for evaluating complex engineering solution design.
- Co-proposed [[solutionrag]], including its tree-based exploration and bi-point thinking mechanisms.
- Helped frame the task as a new RAG setting requiring reliable engineering plans rather than short answers.
