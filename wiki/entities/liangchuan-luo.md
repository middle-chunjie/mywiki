## Description

Liangchuan Luo is a researcher at [[nanjing-university]] and a co-author of [[ding-2024-enhancing-2402-02175]]. He is part of the team developing retrieval-based methods for complex knowledge-graph question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the EPR framework that retrieves atomic patterns and ranks candidate evidence patterns.
- Helped analyze efficiency and failure modes such as unseen relations and numerical evidence gaps.
