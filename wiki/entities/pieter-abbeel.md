## Description

Pieter Abbeel is a co-author of [[gudibande-2023-false-2305-15717]] and is affiliated with [[uc-berkeley]]. The paper includes him in the collaboration arguing that broad imitation is not a shortcut to frontier LLM capability.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's empirical critique of proprietary-model imitation.
- Helped connect the paper's training setup with its broader claims about model capability gaps.
- Contributed to the positioning of stronger base models as the more durable path for open-model progress.
