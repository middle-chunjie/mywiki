## Description

Yi Mao is a researcher at Microsoft Corporation, affiliated with its AI and Research division. He is a co-author of RepoCoder and has published on generation-augmented retrieval for open-domain QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored RepoCoder, contributing to the iterative retrieval-generation design.
- Prior work on generation-augmented retrieval (Mao et al., 2020) forms part of the intellectual lineage of RepoCoder's query-expansion approach.
