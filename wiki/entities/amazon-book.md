## Description

Amazon-book is a public recommendation dataset of Amazon book interactions and reviews used in [[ren-2024-representation]] to evaluate RLMRec under book recommendation scenarios.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the three benchmark datasets for comparing base recommenders against RLMRec-Con and RLMRec-Gen.
- Supports the paper's analyses of noisy interactions, reranking limitations, and profile generation examples.
