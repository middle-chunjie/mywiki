## Description

The University of Zurich (UZH) is Switzerland's largest university, located in Zurich, with strong research programs in computer science, software engineering, and empirical SE. It is the affiliation of Harald Gall in the ACCENT paper. Note: distinct from ETH Zurich (staging entity [[eth-zurich]]).

## Key Contributions

- Home institution for Harald Gall's contribution to the ACCENT adversarial robustness study on code comment generation.
