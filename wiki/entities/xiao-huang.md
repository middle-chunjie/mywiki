## Description

Xiao Huang is a faculty member (corresponding author) at [[hong-kong-polytechnic-university]] in the Department of Computing. His research focuses on knowledge graphs, graph neural networks, and their integration with language models.

## Key Contributions

- Corresponding author of KnowGPT; supervised the black-box knowledge injection framework design.
- Research group has produced multiple KG-enhanced QA models including HamQA and collaborative work on GreaseLM.
