## Description

Edouard Grave is a researcher affiliated in this paper with Meta AI Research. He co-authored the Contriever paper on unsupervised dense retrieval with contrastive learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the core Contriever method and its evaluation on BEIR and QA benchmarks.
- Contributed to the multilingual retrieval extension described as mContriever.
