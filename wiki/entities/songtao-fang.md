## Description

Songtao Fang is an author of [[fang-2021-guided]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China. He contributed to the proposed guided-attention approach for concept extraction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored GACEN, which integrates topic-aware and position-aware attention for concept extraction.
- Participated in the empirical validation on CSEN, KP-20K, and MTB.
