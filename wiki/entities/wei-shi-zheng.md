## Description

Wei-Shi Zheng is a researcher at Sun Yat-sen University and a co-author of [[gao-2024-sculpting-2311-01734]]. He is credited on the paper developing holistic contrastive language-image-3D pre-training for open-world 3D understanding.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the MixCon3D framework that fuses 2D and 3D evidence before text alignment.
- Participates in the training-recipe analysis covering batch size, scheduler, temperature, and EMA choices.
