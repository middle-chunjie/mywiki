## Description

Fei Wang is the lead author of [[wang-2024-astute-2410-07176]], affiliated with [[google-cloud-ai-research]] and [[university-of-southern-california]]. In this paper Wang helps frame imperfect retrieval and knowledge conflict as central robustness problems for RAG.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed Astute RAG as a training-free method for consolidating internal and external knowledge.
- Contributed to the controlled analysis connecting retrieval precision, knowledge conflict, and RAG failure modes.
