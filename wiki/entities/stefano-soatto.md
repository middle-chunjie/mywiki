## Description

Stefano Soatto is a researcher at [[aws-ai-labs]] and a co-author of [[ding-2024-fewer-2404-10830]]. The paper lists him on the team introducing Best-fit Packing for more reliable language modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the argument that unnecessary truncation weakens context-grounded learning.
- Contributed to the large-scale evaluation of packing on text and code pretraining.
