## Description

Junfeng Yang is a Columbia University researcher and co-author of [[ding-2024-semcoder-2406-01006]]. The paper attributes him to the effort to make code models reason about execution behavior and bug fixing more explicitly.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored SEMCODER's semantics-oriented training and evaluation setup.
- Helped position monologue reasoning as a static alternative to explicit execution tracing for debugging.
