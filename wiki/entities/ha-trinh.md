## Description

Ha Trinh is a Microsoft Research researcher and co-first author of [[edge-2024-local-2404-16130]]. Trinh is part of the team that introduces [[graphrag]] for global question answering over large text corpora.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the GraphRAG method built on knowledge-graph indexing and community summaries.
- Helped frame the task as corpus-scale query-focused summarization and sensemaking.
