## Description

Zhitao Li is a co-author of [[yang-2023-prca]] from [[ping-an-technology-shenzhen]]. The paper positions him on work about reward-driven context adaptation for retrieval QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRCA architecture and its empirical study on black-box LLM generators.
- Contributed to experiments pairing frozen retrievers with frozen generators.
