## Description

Aaran Arulraj is a researcher at the University of Waterloo and a co-author of MMLU-Pro. The paper places Arulraj on the effort to build a more challenging multi-task language understanding benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MMLU-Pro and its expert-reviewed benchmark design.
- Helped evaluate how additional distractors and harder questions affect LLM scoring.
