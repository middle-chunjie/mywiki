## Description

Zhaochun Ren is a researcher at Leiden University and a co-author of [[askari-2024-generative-2408-02152]], focused on efficient generative retrieval without fine-tuning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes the few-shot indexing pipeline for constructing a docid bank without model training.
- Contributes to the efficiency analysis comparing Few-Shot GR with DSI-QG and GenRET.
