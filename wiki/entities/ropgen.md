## Description

RoPGen is the robustness framework proposed in this paper for defending deep-learning-based source code authorship attribution against adversarial examples and coding-style manipulation.

## Key Contributions

- Combines style-based data augmentation and gradient augmentation in one training pipeline.
- Significantly lowers targeted and untargeted attack success rates across four datasets.
- Provides a general defense layer usable with both DL-CAIS and PbNN.
