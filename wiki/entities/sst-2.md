## Description

SST-2 is a binary sentiment classification dataset used in [[wang-2023-label]] to evaluate anchor behavior in [[in-context-learning]]. It is one of the four benchmark tasks used throughout the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a sentiment-classification testbed for measuring `S_wp` and `S_pq` across layers.
- Shows strong gains from anchor re-weighting, reaching `90.07` accuracy.
