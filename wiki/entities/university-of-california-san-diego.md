## Description

University of California, San Diego is the affiliation of Canwen Xu and Julian McAuley in [[guo-2023-longcoder-2306-14893]]. In this source it is one of the institutions behind the LongCoder collaboration on long-context code completion.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed authors to the LongCoder method and benchmark paper.
- Participated in the empirical study of long-range code completion.
