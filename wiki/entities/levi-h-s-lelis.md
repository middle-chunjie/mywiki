## Description

Levi H. S. Lelis is the [[universidade-federal-de-vicosa]]-affiliated co-author of [[orseau-2018-singleagent-1811-10928]]; the paper notes that this work was carried out while he was at the University of Alberta, Canada.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the policy-tree-search framework evaluated on procedural Sokoban.
- Helped connect reinforcement-learned policies with deterministic search guarantees.
