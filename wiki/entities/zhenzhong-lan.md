## Description

Zhenzhong Lan is a researcher at the School of Engineering, Westlake University. He is a co-author of SynCSE and serves as a supervisory advisor for Junlei Zhang.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored SynCSE, contributing to the supervised contrastive framework built on SimCSE with synthesized data.
