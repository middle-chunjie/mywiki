## Description

CiteSeer is a citation-network dataset used in [[gong-2022-magcl-2212-07035]] to evaluate graph representation quality via [[node-classification]].

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the standard citation benchmarks in Table 1 and Table 2 of the paper.
- Helps show that MA-GCL outperforms strong unsupervised baselines under both public and random splits.
