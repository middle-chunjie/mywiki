## Description

Jiashu Zhao is a researcher at [[wilfrid-laurier-university]] and co-author of [[ren-2023-disentangled]]. In this work, he is part of the collaboration studying disentangled self-supervision for recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the DCCF framework and its empirical evaluation on sparse recommendation datasets.
- Helped analyze intent prototypes, sparsity robustness, and training efficiency.
