## Description

nano-vectordb is the vector data management tool used in [[unknown-nd-lightrag]] for keyword-based matching over LightRAG's profiled entities and relations. In this paper it serves as the vector retrieval substrate beneath the graph-enhanced retrieval pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Stores and retrieves vectorized representations used for local and global keyword matching.
- Supports the efficient retrieval stage that LightRAG contrasts with GraphRAG's heavier community traversal.
