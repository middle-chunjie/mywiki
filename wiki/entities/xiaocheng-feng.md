## Description

Xiaocheng Feng is a co-author of [[feng-2023-retrievalgeneration-2310-05149]] listed under [[harbin-institute-of-technology]]. In this paper he contributes to the retrieval-generation collaboration design and its QA evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the iterative collaboration loop between retrieval and generation.
- Helps evaluate the approach on both single-hop and [[multi-hop-reasoning]] benchmarks.
