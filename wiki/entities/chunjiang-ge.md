## Description

Chunjiang Ge is a co-author of [[jiang-2022-crossmodal-2211-09623]] and was affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]] and BNRist. The paper lists Chunjiang Ge among the main contributors to the proposed multimodal adapter method.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Cross-Modal Adapter design for frozen-backbone CLIP adaptation.
- Supported experiments on large-scale text-video retrieval benchmarks.
