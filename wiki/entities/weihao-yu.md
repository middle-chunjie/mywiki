## Description

Weihao Yu is a paper author affiliated with the [[national-university-of-singapore]]. In this work, he co-proposes the MambaOut family and the argument that SSM is unnecessary for standard ImageNet classification.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the conceptual framing that Mamba is most appropriate for long-sequence autoregressive tasks.
- Co-develops the MambaOut architecture by removing SSM from Mamba-style blocks and stacking Gated CNN blocks.
- Reports the empirical comparison against visual Mamba baselines on ImageNet, COCO, and ADE20K.
