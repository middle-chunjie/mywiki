## Description

Zhiqiang Hu is a researcher at Singapore University of Technology and Design and a co-author of [[hu-2023-llmadapters-2304-01933]], which studies adapter-based PEFT design for open-access LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the LLM-Adapters framework integrating Prefix-Tuning, Series Adapter, Parallel Adapter, and LoRA into open-access LLMs.
- Helped report the placement and hyperparameter analysis across math and commonsense reasoning benchmarks.
