## Description

Douban-Book is a public recommendation benchmark used in [[yang-2023-generative-2307-05100]]. In the paper it contains `13,024` users, `22,347` items, and `792,062` interactions.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the three main datasets for evaluating VGCL.
- Shows the largest relative NDCG@20 gain over LightGCN among the reported datasets.
