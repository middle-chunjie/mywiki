## Description

Dani Lischinski is a co-author of [[levy-nd-chatting]] and is affiliated with [[hebrew-university-of-jerusalem]]. The paper credits him with the ChatIR study on conversational image retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the formulation of ChatIR as a dialog-guided image retrieval framework.
- Helps position the work against composed image retrieval, visual dialog, and text-to-image retrieval baselines.
