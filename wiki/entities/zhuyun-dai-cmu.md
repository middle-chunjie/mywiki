## Description

Zhuyun Dai is a researcher affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]] in [[gao-2021-coil-2104-07186]] and a co-author of the COIL architecture. Disambiguated from [[zhuyun-dai]] by affiliation: Carnegie Mellon University.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed COIL's contextualized token scoring and CLS-augmented retrieval formulation.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison against DeepCT, DocT5Query, dense retrieval, and ColBERT.
- Helped position COIL as an efficient first-stage retriever rather than only a reranking method.
