## Description

Martin Franz is a researcher at [[ibm-research-ai]] and a co-author of [[saad-falcon-2023-udapdr-2303-00807]]. In this work he contributes to the IBM side of the collaboration on synthetic-query-based reranker training and ColBERTv2 distillation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the UDAPDR retrieval adaptation framework built around synthetic query generation and multi-reranker teachers.
- Helps validate the method on zero-shot retrieval benchmarks spanning long-tail and general-domain tasks.
