## Description

Han Peng is the first author of [[peng-nd-rethinking]] and is affiliated with [[peking-university]]. The paper positions him as a contributor to tree-structured Transformer modeling for source-code representation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed the recursive 2D tree position description used to encode AST nodes.
- Co-designed the fusion of global absolute bias and local relative bias in Transformer attention for code tasks.
