## Description

Houda Aynaou is a researcher affiliated with the Georgia Institute of Technology and a co-author of [[samuel-2023-can-2309-12426]]. Her contribution in this source is part of the study of LLM-driven data augmentation for extractive reading comprehension.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the empirical evaluation of one-shot, two-shot, and filtered synthetic augmentation.
- Contributed to the analysis of when GPT-4-generated training data helps low-resource QA.
