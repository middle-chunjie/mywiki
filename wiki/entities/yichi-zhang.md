## Description

Yichi Zhang is a PhD student at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJUKG lab, supervised by Huajun Chen). His research focuses on knowledge graph-enhanced LLMs and preference alignment for domain-specific applications.

## Key Contributions

- First author of KnowPAT, which introduces knowledgeable preference alignment for domain-specific LLM QA.
- Co-authored work on using LLMs for knowledge graph completion.
