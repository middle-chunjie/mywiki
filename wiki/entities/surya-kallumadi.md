## Description

Surya Kallumadi is a coauthor of [[salemi-2024-optimization]] and is affiliated with [[lowes-companies]]. In this paper, he contributes to the study of retrieval-based personalization for frozen LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthors the retrieval optimization framework evaluated across the seven LaMP tasks.
- Helps analyze when retriever selection is preferable to a single fixed retrieval strategy.
