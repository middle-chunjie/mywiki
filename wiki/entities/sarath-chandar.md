## Description

Sarath Chandar is a researcher affiliated with Chandar Research Lab, Mila, Polytechnique Montreal, and the Canada CIFAR AI Chairs program, and a senior author of [[breton-2025-neobert-2502-19587]]. He is responsible for the broader research direction behind NeoBERT as an open next-generation encoder.

## Key Contributions

- data strategy, and large-scale pretraining. Co-led the design of NeoBERT's architecture
- Framed the paper's argument that encoder backbones need modernized pretraining rather than only more elaborate fine-tuning.
