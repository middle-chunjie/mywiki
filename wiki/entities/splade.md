## Description

SPLADE is the neural sparse retrieval baseline evaluated in [[samarinas-2024-procis]] for both reactive and proactive contextual suggestion. It represents the sparse-neural point in the benchmark's retriever spectrum.

## Key Contributions

- Contributes candidate pools for test-set annotation.
- Serves as a stronger benchmark than BM25 but weaker than ColBERT and LMGR on the reported results.
