## Description

Kate Saenko is a researcher affiliated with Boston University and Meta AI in [[ray-nd-cola]] and a co-author of the paper. The work ties her to the benchmark's design and to the study of lightweight multimodal adaptation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the `COLA` benchmark and its empirical evaluation protocol.
- Helped establish that lightweight multimodal adaptation can outperform stronger-looking unimodal tuning baselines.
