## Description

S1 is the main length-control baseline compared against in the paper. It enforces budgets through decoding-time interventions rather than by training a model to reason under explicit length constraints.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the budget-forcing comparison point for evaluating [[l1]].
- Illustrates the limitations of truncation and special-token continuation strategies for chain-of-thought control.
