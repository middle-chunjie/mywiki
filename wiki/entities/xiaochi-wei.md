## Description

Xiaochi Wei is a researcher at [[baidu]] and co-author of [[qu-2024-completenessoriented-2405-16089]] on collaborative tool retrieval for LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the empirical evaluation showing COLT improves retrieval completeness across ToolLens and ToolBench.
- Helped position tool collaboration, rather than only semantic similarity, as a retrieval target.
