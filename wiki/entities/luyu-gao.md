## Description

Luyu Gao is a researcher at [[carnegie-mellon-university]] and the lead author of [[gao-2021-scaling-2101-06983]]. In this paper Gao develops the gradient-cache training procedure for large-batch contrastive learning under memory constraints.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the gradient-cache method that reproduces exact large-batch contrastive updates with sub-batch replay.
- Implemented and evaluated the method inside [[dpr]] training on [[natural-questions]].
