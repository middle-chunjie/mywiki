## Description

Xueying Du is a researcher at Fudan University and a co-author of [[du-2023-classeval-2308-01861]], which introduces the ClassEval benchmark for class-level code generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the manual construction of the `100`-task ClassEval benchmark.
- Contributed to the empirical evaluation of `11` LLMs under three generation strategies.
