Yue Fan is a co-author of the TokenFormer paper and is affiliated with Max Planck Institute for Informatics in this work.

- Co-authored the token-parameter attention design used throughout TokenFormer.
- Helped develop the progressive-scaling study that reuses smaller TokenFormer checkpoints.
