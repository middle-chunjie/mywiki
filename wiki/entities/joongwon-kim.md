## Description

Joongwon Kim is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and the Allen Institute for AI. In this paper, he is the first author and helps frame task transfer as a source-task selection problem for multi-task NLP.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed TASKWEB, the benchmark of pairwise transfer experiments across `22` NLP tasks.
- Co-proposed TASKSHOP for selecting helpful source tasks for unseen targets.
