## Description

Ensheng Shi is a co-author of [[shi-2023-cocosoda-2204-03293]] and was affiliated with [[xian-jiaotong-university]] in this paper. The paper credits Shi as part of the team proposing CoCoSoDa for semantic code retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CoCoSoDa framework combining soft data augmentation with momentum-based multimodal contrastive learning.
- Contributed to the six-language evaluation on [[codesearchnet]].
