## Description

CosQA is the text-to-code search benchmark used in [[unknown-nd-code-2309-09980]] to evaluate transfer from execution-aware code pre-training to natural-language retrieval. The paper reports mean reciprocal rank on this dataset.

## Key Contributions

- Measures whether FuzzPretrain improves text-to-code retrieval in an unseen domain.
- Highlights that execution-aware pre-training helps even when downstream queries are natural language rather than code.
