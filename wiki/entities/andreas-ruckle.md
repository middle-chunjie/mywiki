## Description

Andreas Rücklé is a coauthor of the BEIR paper and is affiliated with [[technical-university-of-darmstadt]] through UKP-TUDA in this source. The author note indicates that his contributions were made prior to joining Amazon.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the BEIR benchmark design and the evaluation of ten retrieval systems under a unified protocol.
- Supported the paper's analysis of zero-shot generalization across heterogeneous retrieval tasks.
