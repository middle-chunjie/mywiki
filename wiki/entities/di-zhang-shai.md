## Description

Di Zhang is a researcher affiliated with Fudan University and the Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (SHAI Lab), working on LLM reasoning and mathematical problem-solving. Disambiguated from [[di-zhang]] by affiliation: Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory / Fudan University.

## Key Contributions

- Lead author of MCTSr, integrating Monte Carlo Tree Search with LLM self-refinement for Olympiad-level mathematical reasoning.
- Demonstrated that a small open-source model (LLaMA-3 8B) can approach GPT-4-level math performance via iterative tree search without fine-tuning.
