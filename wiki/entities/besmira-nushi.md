## Description

Besmira Nushi is a researcher at Microsoft Research and the corresponding author of the KITAB paper. In this work she co-leads the study of constraint satisfaction as an evaluation problem for LLM-driven information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored KITAB and its dynamic benchmark-construction workflow.
- Helped frame constraint satisfaction as a practical reliability problem for conversational search systems.
