## Description

James Mayfield is a researcher affiliated with [[johns-hopkins-university]] and a co-author of [[yang-2024-distillation-2405-00977]]. In this paper he contributes to the formulation and evaluation of distillation-based multilingual information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the proposal of [[multilingual-translate-distill]] for MLIR.
- Helps validate MTD on CLEF and NeuCLIR collections against both sparse and dense baselines.
