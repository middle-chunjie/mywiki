## Description

BrowseComp is the long-horizon web-search benchmark used in [[yen-2025-lost-2510-18939]] to study exhaustive information seeking. Its questions target hard-to-find information and were validated to require more than `10` minutes of open-web search.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the paper's main benchmark for long search trajectories and outcome analysis.
- Supports the paper's fine-grained failure taxonomy, including hallucination and unfocused-search analysis.
