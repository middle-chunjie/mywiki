## Description

Matej Balog is the lead author of DeepCoder and is affiliated in this paper with the Department of Engineering at the University of Cambridge. He co-developed the paper's neural-guided approach to inductive program synthesis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored [[deepcoder]] and the broader LIPS framing used in the paper.
- synthetic data-generation pipeline, and evaluation setup. Helped define the DSL
