## Description

Zihan Liu is the lead author of [[liu-2024-chatqa-2401-10225]]. Based on repeated co-author overlap with established [[nvidia]] researchers in this author group, this page treats him as part of the same NVIDIA research team.

## Key Contributions

- Led the ChatQA paper that proposes two-stage instruction tuning for conversational QA and retrieval-augmented generation.
- Co-developed the paper's multi-turn retriever fine-tuning and unanswerable-data construction recipe.
