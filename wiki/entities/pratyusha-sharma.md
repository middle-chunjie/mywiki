## Description

Pratyusha Sharma is the first author of the LASER paper and is affiliated with MIT in the author list. The acknowledgements note that this work was done while she was an intern at Microsoft Research, New York City.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LASER intervention for post-training layer-selective rank reduction.
- Helped analyze how LASER improves access to infrequent facts and robustness to paraphrases.
