## Description

Qi Sun is a researcher at Nanjing University of Science and Technology and the first author of [[sun-2024-consistency-2401-13598]] on zero-shot document-level relation triplet extraction with LLM-generated synthetic data.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the GenRDK framework for generating and denoising synthetic document-level relation triplet data.
- Helped formulate the ZeroDocRTE setting and evaluate it on [[docred]] and [[re-docred]].
