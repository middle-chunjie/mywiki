## Description

Rong Jin is a researcher at Machine Intelligence Technology, Alibaba Group (noted as "now affiliated with Twitter" at publication time). He is known for contributions to machine learning theory including label smoothing analysis and domain adaptation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the theoretical analysis connecting label smoothing to adversarial training stability.
- Senior author on the Environment Label Smoothing paper.
