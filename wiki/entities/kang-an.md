## Description

Kang An is a first author of StepSearch and is affiliated with [[sensetime]] and Shenzhen University in this paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the design of StepSearch's step-wise reward mechanism based on information gain and redundancy penalties.
- Contributed to the benchmark construction and empirical validation reported in the paper.
