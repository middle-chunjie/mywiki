## Description

PRM800K is the step-level human feedback dataset released in [[lightman-2023-lets-2305-20050]] for training process-supervised reward models on mathematical reasoning traces.

## Key Contributions

- Provides roughly `800k` filtered step-level labels over `75k` solutions and `12k` problems.
- Supplies the supervision used to train the paper's large-scale [[reward-model]] for [[process-supervision]].
- Supports later ablations about [[active-learning]] and data efficiency.
