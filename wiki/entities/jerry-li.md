## Description

Jerry Li is a researcher at Microsoft Research and a co-author of the KITAB paper. In this work he is part of the team evaluating LLM reliability on constrained book-retrieval queries.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the KITAB benchmark paper.
- Contributed to the empirical analysis of GPT-4 and GPT-3.5 on constrained retrieval.
