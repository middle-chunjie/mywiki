## Description

Miguel Freire is a coauthor of LumberChunker affiliated with NeuralShift AI. In this paper he participates in the design and evaluation of LLM-driven segmentation for narrative retrieval tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the LumberChunker approach to [[document-segmentation]].
- Contributes to experimental comparisons across chunking baselines for [[dense-retrieval]].
- Helps connect chunk quality to downstream [[retrieval-augmented-generation]] accuracy.
