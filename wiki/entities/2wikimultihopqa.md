## Description

2WikiMultihopQA is a multi-hop question answering benchmark used in [[su-2025-parametric-2501-15915]] to evaluate how well Parametric RAG supports compositional reasoning over retrieved knowledge.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the paper's main evaluation datasets, with results broken down by Compare, Bridge, Inference, and Compose subsets.
- Shows consistent total-F1 gains for Parametric RAG and even larger gains when combined with in-context RAG.
