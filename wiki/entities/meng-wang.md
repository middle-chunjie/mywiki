## Description

Meng Wang is a researcher affiliated with the College of Design and Innovation at Tongji University. Wang is a co-author of the 2024 survey on retrieval-augmented generation for large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the survey's review of RAG methods and ecosystems.
- Helped summarize benchmark and tool coverage, including evaluation-specific frameworks for RAG.
- Contributed to the discussion of engineering issues such as retrieval efficiency and deployment.
