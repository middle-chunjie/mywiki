## Description

Georg Wölflein is a researcher affiliated in this paper with EKFZ for Digital Health, TU Dresden, the University of St Andrews, and Synagen AI. He is the first author introducing [[toolmaker]] for autonomous repository-to-tool conversion.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduces [[toolmaker]] as a framework for turning papers with code into LLM-compatible tools.
