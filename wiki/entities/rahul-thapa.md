## Description

Rahul Thapa is a researcher affiliated with Stanford University and a co-author of OctoTools. The paper credits the team with developing a general agentic framework for multi-step reasoning with external tools.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored OctoTools and its multi-step planner-executor workflow.
- Helped validate the framework on diverse reasoning benchmarks spanning text and vision tasks.
