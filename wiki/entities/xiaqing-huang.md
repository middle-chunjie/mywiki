## Description

Xiaqing Huang is an author of [[fang-2021-guided]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China. She is listed among the contributors to the paper's guided-attention concept extraction framework.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to experiments demonstrating gains under limited labeled data.
