## Description

Mohammad Aliannejadi is a researcher at the University of Amsterdam and a co-author of [[askari-2024-generative-2408-02152]], which studies prompting-only indexing for generative retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the Few-Shot GR method and its one-to-many docid mapping design.
- Helps evaluate the method against training-based GR baselines.
