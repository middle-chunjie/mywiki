## Description

Max Ku is a researcher at the University of Waterloo and a co-author of MMLU-Pro. The paper associates Ku with building a more discriminative benchmark for large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MMLU-Pro and its expert-reviewed question construction pipeline.
- Helped analyze model ranking gaps, prompt robustness, and CoT gains on the new benchmark.
