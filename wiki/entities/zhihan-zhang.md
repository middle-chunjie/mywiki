## Description

Zhihan Zhang is a researcher at the University of Notre Dame (email: zzhang23@nd.edu) and co-author of LLM-URL. Distinct from [[zhihao-zhang]] (CMU), who shares a similar romanized name but is a different person at a different institution.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored LLM-URL, contributing to the experimental design and evaluation on ODQA benchmarks.
- Also co-authored related Notre Dame work on contrast consistency in open-domain QA (TACL 2023) and multi-task learning survey (EACL 2023).
