## Description

Yu Gu is a researcher at The Ohio State University and a co-author of [[guti-rrez-2024-hipporag-2405-14831]].

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to HippoRAG's retrieval formulation that connects query entities to a corpus-scale open knowledge graph.
- Helped evaluate the system across MuSiQue, 2WikiMultiHopQA, and HotpotQA with both single-step and IRCoT-enhanced settings.
