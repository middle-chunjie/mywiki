## Description

HotpotQA is the multi-hop question answering dataset used in [[almeida-2024-exploring]] as one of the five IR evaluation collections. It is included to test synthetic question generation and reranking on a large collection with complex information needs.

## Key Contributions

- Acts as a downstream benchmark where both highlighted synthetic-data pipelines beat BM25.
- Helps test whether the method generalizes beyond straightforward single-hop retrieval settings.
