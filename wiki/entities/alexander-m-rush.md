## Description

Alexander M. Rush is a Cornell University researcher and co-author of [[morris-2024-contextual-2410-02525]]. In this paper he contributes to a retrieval-oriented contextual embedding framework that adapts document representations to target-corpus context.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CDE architecture that conditions retrieval embeddings on sampled corpus documents.
- Co-designed the adversarial clustering and false-negative filtering setup for harder contrastive retrieval training.
