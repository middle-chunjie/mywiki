## Description

Xiaojie Jin is a ByteDance-affiliated author and the corresponding author of [[jin-2024-mvadapter-2301-07868]]. The paper lists Jin as one of the researchers introducing MV-Adapter for parameter-efficient video-text retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the proposal of parameter-efficient video-text retrieval (PE-VTR).
- Contributed to the design of MV-Adapter, including temporal adaptation and cross modality tying.
