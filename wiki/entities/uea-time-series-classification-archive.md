## Description

The UEA Time Series Classification Archive is the multivariate classification benchmark suite used in [[liu-2023-timesurl]]. The paper reports average accuracy and average rank across `30` UEA datasets to evaluate instance-level representation quality.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the paper's headline classification benchmark where TimesURL reaches average accuracy `0.752`.
- Supports the ablation study showing how frequency mixing, Universums, and reconstruction each affect performance.
