## Description

Aaron van den Oord is a researcher at [[deepmind]] and the lead author of [[oord-2019-representation-1807-03748]], where he co-introduces Contrastive Predictive Coding as a general self-supervised representation-learning framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CPC method that combines latent future prediction with a contrastive objective.
- Helped demonstrate the same representation-learning recipe across speech, vision, language, and reinforcement learning.
