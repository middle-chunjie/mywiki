## Description

Ludwig Schmidt is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and the Allen Institute for AI. In this paper, he co-authors the study of how compositional reasoning scales in large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Helped analyze scaling behavior of the compositionality gap across GPT-3-family models.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison of prompting strategies and benchmark results.
