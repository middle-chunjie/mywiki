## Description

Zhangyang Wang is an author of [[wang-2023-graph-2304-02806]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-texas-at-austin]] in this paper. He co-authors the proposal of GMoE as an explicit diversity model for large-scale graphs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the central GMoE architecture and its load-balancing formulation.
- Contributes to the paper's empirical validation across multiple OGB tasks and pretraining settings.
