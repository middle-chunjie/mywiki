## Description

Jianzhe Lin is a Microsoft-affiliated researcher and co-author of [[lin-2023-batchprompt-2309-00384]], which proposes BatchPrompt, BPE, and SEAS for efficient batched LLM inference.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the formulation of batch permutation and vote aggregation for batched prompting.
- Helped evaluate the method on BoolQ, QQP, RTE, and a GSM8K side experiment.
