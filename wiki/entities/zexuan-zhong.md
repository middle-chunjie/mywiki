## Description

Zexuan Zhong is a PhD student at Princeton University (Princeton NLP group) and lead author of the TRIME paper on memory-augmented language model training. Supported by a JP Morgan PhD fellowship.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed and led the TRIME training framework for memory-augmented language models.
- Designed the in-batch contrastive training objective and the three data batching strategies (local, long-term, BM25-external).
- First author on "Training Language Models with Memory Augmentation" (arXiv:2205.12674).
