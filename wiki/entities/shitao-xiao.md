Shitao Xiao is a researcher at the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence. He is an author of [[li-2024-making-2409-15700]] and works on LLM-based embedding and retrieval systems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the `bge-en-icl` study on query-side in-context learning for embeddings.
- Contributed to the empirical study of representation methods and benchmark performance.
