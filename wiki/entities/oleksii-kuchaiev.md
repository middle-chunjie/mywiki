## Description

Oleksii Kuchaiev is a co-author of [[wang-2023-shall]] affiliated with [[nvidia]]. He appears in the systems-oriented part of the RETRO study covering scalable indexing, retrieval, and large-model training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the implementation of chunk-level retrieval over `5.3B` datastore chunks using Faiss-based ANN search.
- Contributed to the paper's matched GPT-versus-RETRO pretraining comparison and compute-overhead analysis.
