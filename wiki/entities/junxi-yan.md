## Description

Junxi Yan is a co-author of [[su-2025-parametric-2501-15915]] affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]]. In this paper, Junxi Yan is part of the team proposing Parametric RAG for parametric knowledge injection in LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Parametric RAG framework that updates model parameters from retrieved documents.
- Contributed to the evaluation on multi-hop and factual QA benchmarks.
