## Description

Zixuan Zhou is a first co-author of the Skeleton-of-Thought paper and is listed with affiliations to [[tsinghua-university]] and [[infinigence-ai]]. The paper places Zhou among the researchers introducing the SoT approach.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the design of the skeleton-stage and point-expanding-stage prompting scheme.
- Contributed to the paper's cross-model evaluation of latency and answer quality.
