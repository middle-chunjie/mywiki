## Description

Parrot-40K is the multi-turn instruction-tuning dataset collected in [[sun-2024-parrot-2310-07301]]. It contains conversations generated by letting Parrot-Ask interact with `gpt-3.5-turbo` across diverse topics.

## Key Contributions

- Contains `40K` dialogues with `8.71` average turns and `3551` average tokens.
- Improves over Baize, UltraChat, and ShareGPT on topic diversity, contextual reference count, and human-likeness.
- Supplies the supervision used to train Parrot-Chat.
