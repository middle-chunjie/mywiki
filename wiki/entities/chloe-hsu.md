## Description

Chloe Hsu is a UC Berkeley researcher and co-author of [[sun-2024-learn-2310-13807]], which frames test-time training as a learnable inner-loop adaptation mechanism.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's MTTT method and its interpretation of context windows as datasets for inner-loop learning.
- Helped evaluate MTTT variants on both patch-based and raw-pixel ImageNet settings.
