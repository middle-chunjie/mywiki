## Description

Yun Xiong is a researcher affiliated with the Shanghai Key Laboratory of Data Science, School of Computer Science, Fudan University. In this paper, Xiong is a co-author of a survey covering RAG architectures, optimization methods, and evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the 2024 survey that categorizes RAG research into three major paradigms.
- Helped summarize retrieval-side techniques such as query optimization, indexing, and embedding selection.
- Contributed to the survey's assessment of RAG benchmarks, tools, and downstream tasks.
