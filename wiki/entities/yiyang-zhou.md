## Description

Yiyang Zhou is a co-author of [[xia-2026-skillrl-2602-08234]] and is affiliated with [[unc-chapel-hill]]. In this paper, he is part of the collaboration on the skill-based RL pipeline and its multi-benchmark evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the retrieval design that combines always-on general skills with task-specific skills.
- Helps validate transfer to search-augmented QA benchmarks beyond embodied and web tasks.
