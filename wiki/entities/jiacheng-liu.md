## Description

Jiacheng Liu is a researcher at the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Washington, and the lead author of the infini-gram paper. In this work he helps introduce an unbounded `n`-gram language model and its suffix-array retrieval engine.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the `∞`-gram formulation that selects the longest observed suffix in a trillion-token corpus.
- Helped build and evaluate the infini-gram engine for low-latency arbitrary-`n` counting and next-token queries.
- Co-authored the empirical analysis showing large perplexity gains when `∞`-gram is interpolated with neural LMs.
