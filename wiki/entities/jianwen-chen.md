## Description

Jianwen Chen is a co-first author of [[xia-2026-skillrl-2602-08234]] and is listed with [[unc-chapel-hill]]. In this paper, he contributes to the SkillRL system for skill distillation, retrieval, and RL-based agent improvement.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the structured SkillBank design with general and task-specific skills.
- Helps demonstrate the empirical gains of skill abstraction over raw memory baselines.
