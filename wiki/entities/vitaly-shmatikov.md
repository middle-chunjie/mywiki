## Description

Vitaly Shmatikov is a Cornell University computer scientist and co-author of [[morris-2023-text-2310-06816]]. His contribution in this paper centers on the privacy implications of reconstructing text from embeddings.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the threat model showing that black-box access to an embedder can suffice for text recovery.
- Helped connect the empirical inversion results to privacy risks in vector databases and clinical-note storage.
