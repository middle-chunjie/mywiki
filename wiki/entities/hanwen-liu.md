## Description

Hanwen Liu is a researcher at Beihang University (BUAA), contributing to research on retrieval-augmented generation systems. Co-author of the Mix-of-Granularity paper alongside Zijie Zhong et al.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the MoG and MoGG frameworks for adaptive retrieval granularity in RAG.
- Contributed to experimental evaluation on MIRAGE benchmark datasets.
