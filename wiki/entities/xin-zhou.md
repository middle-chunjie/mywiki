## Description

Xin Zhou is a researcher affiliated with the Alibaba-NTU Singapore Joint Research Institute at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, and a co-author of [[li-2023-rethinking-2310-08069]]. The paper places Xin Zhou on work about weighted contrastive objectives for code retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the empirical study showing Soft-InfoNCE improves several strong code-search backbones.
- Contributes to the analysis of negative-pair weighting and false-negative handling in retrieval training.
