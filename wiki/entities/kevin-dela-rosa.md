## Description

Kevin Dela Rosa is an author of [[wu-2023-forb-2309-16249]] at [[snap-inc]] and a contributor to the paper's flat-object retrieval benchmark and evaluation design.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the release of the [[forb]] benchmark.
- Contributed to the evaluation design around `mAP` and [[thresholded-mean-average-precision]].
- Helped compare representative retrieval systems on diverse flat-object categories.
