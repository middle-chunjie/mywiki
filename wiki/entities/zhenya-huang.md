## Description

Zhenya Huang is the corresponding author of [[fang-2021-guided]] and is affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China. The paper places her among the core contributors to the model design and evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored GACEN for concept extraction with guided attention over topic and clue-word signals.
- Helped establish the paper's evaluation across three concept-extraction datasets.
