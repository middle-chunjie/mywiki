## Description

GraphMAE is the self-supervised masked graph autoencoder method used as the pretraining technique in [[wang-2023-graph-2304-02806]]. In this paper it serves as the auxiliary pretraining baseline paired with GIN and GMoE-GIN.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the masked reconstruction pretraining objective integrated with GMoE.
- Supports the paper's comparison between pretrained and non-pretrained graph models.
