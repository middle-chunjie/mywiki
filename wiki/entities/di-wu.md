## Description

Di Wu is a researcher affiliated with [[ucla]] in this paper and the first-listed author of [[wu-2024-repoformer-2403-10059]]. The work notes that it was completed during an internship at [[aws-ai-labs]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored REPOFORMER, a self-selective retrieval framework for repository-level code completion.
- Contributed to the self-supervised training recipe that labels retrieval decisions from performance deltas between retrieval-free and retrieval-augmented completion.
- Helped evaluate selective retrieval across RepoEval, CrossCodeEval, and CrossCodeLongEval.
