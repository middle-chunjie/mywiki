## Description

Sean MacAvaney is an information retrieval researcher at the University of Glasgow and co-author of [[macavaney-2024-reproducibility]]. In this paper he studies how PLAID behaves under reproduction, broader baselines, and deeper parameter analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the reproduction, parameter sweep, and baseline study for PLAID on MS MARCO Dev and TREC DL 2019.
- Adds exhaustive ColBERTv2 comparisons and cluster-level analysis to explain why lexical-first pipelines remain competitive.
