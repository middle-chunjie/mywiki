## Description

Rami Ben-Ari is a co-author of [[levy-nd-chatting]] and is affiliated with [[originai]]. The paper places him on the team building a chat-based retrieval system for image search.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the ChatIR framework and its evaluation against text-to-image and RL-based baselines.
- Contributes to studying how dialog history can improve retrieval over a large image corpus.
