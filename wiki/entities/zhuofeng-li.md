## Description

Zhuofeng Li is a researcher affiliated with Stanford University and Texas A&M University, and a co-first author of AgentFlow. In this paper he helps introduce the trainable agentic framework and its in-the-flow optimization method.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the AgentFlow system for multi-turn planning, tool execution, verification, and answer generation.
- Co-developed Flow-GRPO for on-policy planner optimization under sparse trajectory-level rewards.
