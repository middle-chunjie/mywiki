## Description

Jiongnan Liu is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and co-author of this paper on robust conversational retrieval. The work uses LLM-generated augmentations to improve context encoding.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the augmentation and evaluation pipeline behind ConvAug.
- Helped demonstrate improvements across normal and zero-shot retrieval settings.
