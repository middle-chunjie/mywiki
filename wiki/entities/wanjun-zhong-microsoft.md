## Description

Disambiguated from [[wanjun-zhong]] by affiliation: Microsoft. Wanjun Zhong is a researcher at Microsoft and a co-author of AGIEval. In this paper, Zhong is the lead author responsible for designing the human-centric benchmark, running experiments, and writing the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Lead author of AGIEval, introducing the first bilingual human-centric benchmark for foundation model evaluation sourced from official standardised exams.
- Designed evaluation settings (zero-shot, few-shot, CoT) and ran experiments on GPT-4, ChatGPT, and Text-Davinci-003.
- Co-authored earlier LSAT reasoning dataset work (referenced as Zhong et al., 2022).
