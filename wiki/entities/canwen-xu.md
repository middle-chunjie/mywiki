## Description

Canwen Xu is a co-author of [[guo-2023-longcoder-2306-14893]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-california-san-diego]] in the paper. The source places Canwen Xu on the team proposing LongCoder for efficient long-range code completion.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LongCoder architecture and its empirical evaluation on LCC, CodeXGLUE, and RepoBench XF-R.
- Contributed to the study of long-context code completion beyond fixed-window baselines.
