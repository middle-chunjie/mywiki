## Description

Karim Elmaaroufi is a UC Berkeley co-author of this paper on SPEAC for very low-resource programming languages. He contributes to the paper's combination of LLM prompting with compiler and repair techniques.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the intermediate-language and repair pipeline for UCLID5.
- Participates in the empirical comparison against prompting, self-repair, and fine-tuning baselines.
