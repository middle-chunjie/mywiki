## Description

Jan Ludziejewski is a researcher affiliated with the University of Warsaw and IDEAS NCBR, and a co-author of [[krajewski-2024-scaling-2402-07871]]. He focused on the scaling-law design and optimization aspects of the project.

## Key Contributions

- Designed and implemented the paper's MoE scaling-law formulation.
- Optimized and tuned the fine-grained MoE implementation.
