## Description

GPT-3.5-Turbo is the smallest context-window model evaluated in [[li-2024-retrieval-2407-16833]], limited to `16k` tokens.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the weakest long-context baseline among the three evaluated LLMs.
- Shows cases where RAG can beat LC on ultra-long inputs because the model's context window is too small for full-context prompting.
