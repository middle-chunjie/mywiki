## Description

Gabriele Oliaro is a researcher affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]] and a co-author of this survey on efficient LLM serving. In this paper he contributes to the synthesis of decoding and systems optimization literature for generative LLM inference.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the survey's two-axis taxonomy of algorithmic innovation and system optimization.
- Helps contextualize framework-level trade-offs between latency, throughput, and implementation complexity.
