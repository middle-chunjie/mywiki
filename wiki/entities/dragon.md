## Description

Dragon is the alternative dense retriever used in [[li-2024-retrieval-2407-16833]] to test whether the paper's conclusions generalize beyond Contriever.

## Key Contributions

- Confirms that the main LC-versus-RAG findings persist with a different retriever.
- With Gemini-1.5-Pro, yields average scores of `49.70` for LC, `38.09` for RAG, and `46.81` for Self-Route.
