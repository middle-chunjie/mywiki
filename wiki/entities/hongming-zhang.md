## Description

Hongming Zhang is a co-author of the REVELA paper and is affiliated with Tencent AI Lab in this source. He is part of the paper's effort to train retrievers directly from raw text without query-document labels.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored REVELA's self-supervised retriever-learning framework.
- Contributed to the paper's claims about cross-domain robustness and scalability.
