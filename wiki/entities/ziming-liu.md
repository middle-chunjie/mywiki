## Description

Ziming Liu is the first author of [[liu-2024-kan-2404-19756]] and is affiliated in the paper with Massachusetts Institute of Technology and the NSF Institute for Artificial Intelligence and Fundamental Interactions. In this source he is associated with the proposal and framing of KANs as interpretable alternatives to MLPs.

## Key Contributions

- First author of the paper that introduces KANs as spline-parameterized edge-activation networks.
- Co-authored the paper's theoretical, empirical, and scientific-discovery case for KANs.
