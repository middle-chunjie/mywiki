## Description

Wen Zhang is a researcher at the School of Software Technology, Zhejiang University, Ningbo. He works on knowledge graph embedding, LLM applications, and e-commerce KG systems (ZJUKG lab).

## Key Contributions

- Senior co-author of KnowPAT, contributing expertise in KG-based applications.
- Co-authored works on billion-scale e-commerce KG models and KG embedding for e-commerce.
