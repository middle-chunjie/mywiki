## Description

Zhiqi Shen is a researcher at [[nanyang-technological-university]] and a co-author of [[li-2024-rewriting-2401-04514]]. In this paper, he contributes to the proposal of ReCo and the formulation of CSSim for LLM-augmented code retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the ReCo framework that rewrites corpus code to better match query-generated exemplar style.
- Helps introduce CSSim as a style-sensitive metric for analyzing code-search gains.
- Participates in the empirical evaluation across sparse, zero-shot dense, and fine-tuned dense retrieval settings.
