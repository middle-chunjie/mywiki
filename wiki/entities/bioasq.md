## Description

BioASQ is the biomedical retrieval and question answering dataset used in [[almeida-2024-exploring]] for both quality validation and downstream reranking evaluation. The paper uses the official 2022 version with a 33 million document PubMed collection.

## Key Contributions

- Provides one of the five benchmark collections used to assess synthetic question generation.
- Shows strong downstream gains over BM25, reaching `0.436` and `0.438` nDCG@10 for the paper's two highlighted synthetic-data settings.
