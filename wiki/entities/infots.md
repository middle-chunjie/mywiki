## Description

InfoTS is a time-series contrastive learning baseline used in [[liu-2023-timesurl]] for comparison on classification and imputation. The paper positions it as competitive but limited by augmentation choices that may introduce inappropriate inductive biases.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the strongest self-supervised classification baseline that TimesURL surpasses on UEA and UCR averages.
- Acts as a comparison point for the claim that augmentation design strongly affects universal transfer.
