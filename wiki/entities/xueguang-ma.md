## Description

Xueguang Ma is a researcher at [[university-of-waterloo]] and the lead author of [[ma-2023-finetuning-2310-08319]]. He studies large-model retrieval and reranking systems for information retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduces `RepLLaMA`, which fine-tunes LLaMA-2 as a dense retriever for passage and document retrieval.
- Co-introduces `RankLLaMA`, a pointwise reranker trained on retriever-generated hard negatives.
- Helps demonstrate strong zero-shot transfer from MS MARCO supervision to BEIR evaluation.
