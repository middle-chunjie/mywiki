## Description

Yoon Kim is a Massachusetts Institute of Technology researcher and a co-author of DR Tulu. The paper places him on the team that develops the RLER training recipe and analyzes its effects on long-form deep-research quality.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the DR Tulu method paper.
- Helped frame the reinforcement-learning and long-form generation analyses reported in the work.
