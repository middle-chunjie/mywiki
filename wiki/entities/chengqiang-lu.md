## Description

Chengqiang Lu is an author of [[hao-2020-asgn]] at the University of Science and Technology of China. The paper places him on the team designing the ASGN teacher-student framework and its molecular graph encoder.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ASGN method for label-efficient molecular property prediction.
- Contributed to the message-passing and semi-supervised representation-learning design used by the model.
