## Description

Yijun Tian is a co-author of [[he-2024-gretriever-2402-07630]] and is affiliated with the University of Notre Dame in the paper. In this work, Yijun Tian is part of the collaboration on graph QA over real-world textual graphs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the GraphQA benchmark spanning commonsense, scene, and knowledge-graph tasks.
- Contributed to the empirical evaluation of G-Retriever against prompt tuning and LoRA baselines.
