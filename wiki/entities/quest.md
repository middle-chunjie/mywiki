## Description

QUEST is the Wikipedia-category-derived list benchmark used in [[dhuliawala-2023-chainofverification-2309-11495]] for harder entity-set generation.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies `55` test questions, each with `8` answers, after filtering out examples requiring logical operations.
- Helps show that factored CoVe improves precision from `0.12` to `0.22` on harder list generation.
