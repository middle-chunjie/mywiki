## Description

Hsiang-Fu Yu is a co-author of FLOATER and is affiliated with Amazon Inc. in this paper. The work studies continuous positional dynamics for Transformer models across translation and language understanding tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the FLOATER positional encoding method based on continuous dynamics.
- Contributed to the empirical evaluation spanning WMT14 translation, GLUE, RACE, and SQuAD.
