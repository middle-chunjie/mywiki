## Description

Llama-3-8B-Instruct is the instruction-tuned large language model used in [[askari-2024-generative-2408-02152]] for both few-shot indexing and retrieval-time docid generation.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the shared backbone for indexing and retrieval in Few-Shot GR.
- Delivers `70.1` Recall@1 and `87.6` Recall@10 on NQ320K when paired with the proposed pipeline.
