## Description

Sherry Tongshuang Wu is a co-author of the REVELA paper and is listed with Carnegie Mellon University in this source. She is among the authors studying retrieval under reasoning-intensive settings in the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the REVELA framework for self-supervised dense retrieval.
- Contributed to the paper's evaluation on BRIGHT and broader cross-domain retrieval tasks.
