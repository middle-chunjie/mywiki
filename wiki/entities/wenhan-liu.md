## Description

Wenhan Liu is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and co-author of the LLM4IR survey on integrating large language models with information retrieval pipelines.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the comprehensive LLM4IR survey covering query rewriting, retrieval, reranking, and reading modules.
- Contributed to the coverage of LLM-based reranking methods and future research directions.
