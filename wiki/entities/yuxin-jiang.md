## Description

Yuxin Jiang is the first author of [[jiang-2022-improved-2203-06875]] and is affiliated with [[hong-kong-university-of-science-and-technology-guangzhou]] and [[hong-kong-university-of-science-and-technology]] in this paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed PromCSE as a prompt-based sentence embedding framework for robust STS under domain shift.
- Helped formulate the connection between NT-Xent contrastive learning and energy-based learning.
- Reported strong results on standard STS and CxC-STS benchmarks.
