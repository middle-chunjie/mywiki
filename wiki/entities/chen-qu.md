## Description

Chen Qu is a researcher affiliated with the University of Massachusetts Amherst and a co-author of [[mo-2024-historyaware-2401-16659]]. In this paper, he contributes to the design and evaluation of history-aware conversational dense retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed HAConvDR for denoised conversational retrieval with historical supervision.
- Helped validate the method on TopiOCQA and QReCC against strong conversational retrieval baselines.
