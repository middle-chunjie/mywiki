## Description

The Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) is a major research institute in Beijing, China. It hosts the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), one of China's leading centers for computer vision, pattern recognition, and machine learning research.

## Key Contributions

- Home institution for the ELS authors Yi-Fan Zhang, Jian Liang, Zhang Zhang, Liang Wang, and Tieniu Tan.
- Conducts research in domain adaptation, domain generalization, person re-identification, and related areas.
