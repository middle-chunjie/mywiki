## Description

Michel Galley is listed as a co-author of [[wu-2025-collabllm-2502-00640]]. In this paper, he is part of the Microsoft Research-Stanford collaboration developing CollabLLM for multiturn human-LLM interaction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CollabLLM training framework based on collaborative simulation and Multiturn-aware Rewards.
- Contributed to the empirical study spanning document editing, coding assistance, and math collaboration.
