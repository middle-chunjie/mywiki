## Description

Newman Cheng is an author of [[edge-2024-local-2404-16130]] affiliated with Microsoft Strategic Missions and Technologies. He contributes to the GraphRAG work on graph-based retrieval and summarization.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the GraphRAG pipeline and its evaluation on two million-token-scale corpora.
- Contributed to the study of community-summary-based answering versus vector RAG and source-text summarization.
