## Description

GPT-4V is the vision-capable large multimodal model examined in this paper, studied through qualitative probes over prompting, perception, reasoning, and agent-style interaction.

## Key Contributions

- Supports text-only, single-image, and interleaved image-text inputs in the paper's evaluations.
- Demonstrates the paper's central capabilities in visual referring prompting, document reasoning, temporal understanding, and multimodal tool use.
- Serves as the base model for the report's discussions of plugins, multimodal chains, self-reflection, and retrieval augmentation.
