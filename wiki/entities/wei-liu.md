## Description

Wei Liu is a co-author of WildToolBench and is affiliated with King's College London according to the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the WildToolBench benchmark for realistic multi-turn LLM tool-use evaluation.
- Contributed to the benchmark's focus on hidden intention and instruction transition in dialogue.
