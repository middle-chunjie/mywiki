## Description

Dongfang Du is a co-author of [[liu-2018-patent]] from [[university-of-science-and-technology-of-china]]. In this paper he is part of the team developing content-aware tensor factorization for litigation-risk ranking.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CTF framework and its patent-content encoding design.
- Contributed to the experimental comparison against TF, SVM, LR, and Generality baselines.
