## Description

Bole Yi is a researcher at RALI, Universite de Montreal and a co-author of ConvSDG. In this paper, he contributes to LLM-based session generation and the empirical evaluation of conversational retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the ConvSDG framework for dialogue-level and query-level session generation.
- Helped validate generated training data on CAsT and TopiOCQA benchmarks.
