## Description

Jian Guo is a co-author and corresponding author of [[sun-2024-thinkongraph-2307-07697]], affiliated with IDEA Research and the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). In the paper, he helps lead the Think-on-Graph project on LLM reasoning over knowledge graphs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ToG framework and its experimental evaluation over nine datasets.
- Helped frame the method's claims around deep reasoning, traceability, and correctability.
