## Description

Llama 3 8B is one of the open-source generator baselines evaluated in RAGChecker. The paper uses it to compare smaller open-source generation behavior against GPT-4, Llama 3 70B, and Mixtral-8x7B.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of four generator backbones in the 8-system RAG evaluation matrix.
- Underperforms Llama 3 70B on overall quality and most generator diagnostics in the reported averages.
