## Description

Ilgee Hong is a researcher at Georgia Institute of Technology and a co-author of [[xu-2026-alternating-2602-01511]]. The paper connects Hong to research on interpretable rubric-based reward modeling for LLM post-training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Rubric-ARM and its rubric-conditioned judging setup.
- Contributed to the empirical study showing gains in downstream DPO, IterDPO, and GRPO training.
