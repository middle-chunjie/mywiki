## Description

Vinay Samuel is a researcher affiliated with Carnegie Mellon University and the first-listed author of [[samuel-2023-can-2309-12426]]. In this paper he co-authors a study on GPT-4-based synthetic augmentation for low-resource extractive QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's two-stage GPT-4 pipeline for synthetic context and QA generation.
- Helped evaluate the method across CovidQA, PolicyQA, and TechQA.
