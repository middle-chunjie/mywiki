## Description

Jiawei Wang is a co-author of [[wang-2022-codemvp-2205-02029]] and is affiliated with Monash University in the paper. The work studies multi-view contrastive pretraining for source-code representation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CODE-MVP framework that integrates textual, structural, execution, and transformed views of code.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison against GraphCodeBERT, CodeT5, and SynCoBERT.
