## Description

SIGIR 2022 is the 45th International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval, held July 11–15, 2022 in Madrid, Spain. It is the flagship venue for information retrieval research.

## Key Contributions

- Published GAST (Zhang et al., 2022) on cross-domain sentiment classification with graph adaptive semantic transfer.
- A top-tier venue for IR, NLP, and web mining research.
