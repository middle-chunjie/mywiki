## Description

Nicolas Astorga is a co-author of LLAMBO and is affiliated in this paper with DAMTP, University of Cambridge. The author list marks Astorga as an equal-contribution contributor to the study.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LLAMBO framework for integrating LLMs into core Bayesian optimization components.
- Contributed to the benchmark-based investigation of warmstarting, surrogate modeling, and candidate generation.
