## Description

Moshe Wasserblat is a coauthor of the SETFIT paper and is affiliated with [[intel-labs]]. In this source he is part of the team arguing that compact sentence-transformer models can replace prompt-heavy few-shot systems in practice.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the SETFIT method and its efficiency analysis against T-Few.
- Helped support the paper's focus on practical deployment with smaller models.
