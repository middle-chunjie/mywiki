## Description

Tong Chen is a co-author of the REVELA paper and is listed with the University of Washington affiliation in this source. He contributes to the paper's self-supervised dense retrieval formulation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the REVELA framework and its retrieval-via-language-modeling setup.
- Contributed to the evaluation on code and general retrieval benchmarks.
