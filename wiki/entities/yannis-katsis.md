## Description

Yannis Katsis is a researcher at [[ibm-research]] and a co-author of [[katsis-2025-mtrag-2501-03468]]. He is one of the equal-contribution authors on the mtRAG benchmark paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed mtRAG as a human-generated benchmark for multi-turn [[retrieval-augmented-generation]] evaluation.
- Helped frame the benchmark around later-turn retrieval, answerability, and passage-diversity challenges.
