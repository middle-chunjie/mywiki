## Description

Tianqi Liu is a researcher at [[google-research]] and a co-author of [[qin-2024-large-2306-17563]]. The paper includes Liu on the team that studies pairwise LLM judgments for passage reranking.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the empirical study comparing PRP against prior pointwise and listwise LLM rankers.
- Helped validate PRP on open FLAN models under zero-shot settings.
