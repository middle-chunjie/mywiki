## Description

Sumit Agarwal is a researcher at the Language Technologies Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, and a co-author of [[zhou-2023-codebertscore-2302-05527]]. The `-cmu` suffix disambiguates from other researchers with the same name.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed CodeBERTScore and contributed to its empirical evaluation across programming languages.
- Part of the CMU NeuLab team that released five language-specific CodeBERT models to the Hugging Face Hub.
