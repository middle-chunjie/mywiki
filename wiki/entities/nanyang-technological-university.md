## Description

Nanyang Technological University is the Singapore institution hosting the School of Computer Science and Engineering authors of [[li-2023-rethinking-2310-08069]]. It is the main academic affiliation behind the paper's work on weighted contrastive learning for code retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the institutional base for the paper's first, third, and senior authors.
- Hosts the research environment in which Soft-InfoNCE is developed and evaluated.
