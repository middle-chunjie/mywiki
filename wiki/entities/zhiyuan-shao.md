## Description

Zhiyuan Shao is a co-author of [[gui-2022-crosslanguage-2201-07420]] and is affiliated with Huazhong University of Science and Technology. The paper credits Shao as part of the collaboration behind XLIR and its LLVM-IR-based matching pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the XLIR architecture and evaluation design.
- Helped analyze how intermediate representations reduce cross-language matching difficulty.
