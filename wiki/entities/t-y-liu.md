## Description

T. Y. Liu is a researcher at Moonshot AI and a co-author of Kimi Linear. The paper credits this author with contributing to the hybrid linear-attention architecture and its large-scale evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Kimi Linear, including the KDA-based hybrid attention design and its matched-scale evaluation against MLA and GDN-H.
