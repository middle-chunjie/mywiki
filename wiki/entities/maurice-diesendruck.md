## Description

Maurice Diesendruck is a Microsoft-affiliated researcher and co-author of [[lin-2023-batchprompt-2309-00384]], contributing to its batched prompting and self-ensemble design.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the BPE voting scheme that permutes batch order across repeated LLM calls.
- Contributed to the empirical analysis of accuracy-efficiency trade-offs under different batch sizes and vote counts.
