## Description

Tengyu Ma is a co-author of [[xie-2022-explanation-2111-02080]] and was affiliated with [[stanford-university]] at the time of publication. In this paper, he contributes to the theoretical and empirical study of how pretraining structure can induce in-context learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the asymptotic analysis linking posterior concentration to optimal in-context prediction.
- Helps connect distribution mismatch, example length, and distinguishability in the theoretical results.
- Contributes to the GINC experiments that compare Transformer and LSTM behavior.
