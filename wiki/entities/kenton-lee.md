## Description

Kenton Lee is a researcher affiliated with [[google-research]] and an equal-contribution co-author of [[guu-2020-realm-2002-08909]]. The paper presents Lee as part of the team that introduced REALM for retrieval-augmented pre-training and open-domain QA.

## Key Contributions

- Equal-contribution co-author of REALM.
- Helped introduce unsupervised retriever training through masked language modeling.
- Reported strong open-domain QA gains over ORQA and T5 baselines.
