## Description

Muru Zhang is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington. In this paper, he co-authors the evaluation of compositional reasoning and structured prompting for multi-hop question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Helped build and analyze the Compositional Celebrities benchmark.
- Co-evaluated self-ask and search-augmented prompting on multi-hop QA datasets.
