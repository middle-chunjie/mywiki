## Description

PY117K is the deduplicated benchmark Python dataset used by [[izadi-2022-codefill-2202-06689]] for fine-tuning and evaluation. It contains `117K` files, `29M` lines of code, `766K` unique tokens, and `103` unique token types.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the downstream training split for the token-level and statement-level completion tasks.
- Provides the held-out benchmark on which CodeFill reports its main accuracy, MRR, METEOR, and ROUGE-L results.
