## Description

Chen Luo is a researcher at Amazon who co-authors PAG, a planning-ahead constrained beam search framework for generative retrieval presented at SIGIR 2024.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the PAG framework combining set-based and sequential document identifiers for generative retrieval.
- Co-authored the three-stage training pipeline that jointly optimizes sparse (SPLADE-style) and dense (residual-quantization-based) document representations.
