## Description

Sepp Hochreiter is a co-author of the xLSTM paper and is explicitly listed with Johannes Kepler University Linz, NXAI Lab, and NXAI GmbH. He is also the historical originator of core LSTM ideas cited throughout the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored xLSTM as a modern extension of the LSTM family for large-scale language modeling.
- Connects the paper's new recurrent cells back to the original LSTM line of research.
