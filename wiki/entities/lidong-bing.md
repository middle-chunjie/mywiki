## Description

Lidong Bing is a researcher at DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group and a co-author of [[hu-2023-llmadapters-2304-01933]], which studies adapter design choices for open-access LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the framework that unifies Prefix-Tuning, Series Adapter, Parallel Adapter, and LoRA in one implementation.
- Helped report empirical results on both arithmetic and commonsense reasoning tasks.
