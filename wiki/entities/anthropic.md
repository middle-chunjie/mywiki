## Description

Anthropic is the research organization behind the model family studied in [[kadavath-2022-language-2207-05221]]. All authors of the paper are affiliated with Anthropic, and the study analyzes both pure language models and RLHF policies derived from them.

## Key Contributions

- Produced the `800M` to `52B` model family used to study calibration, `P(True)`, and `P(IK)`.
- Demonstrated that relevant context can shift self-knowledge estimates substantially, including the paper's `18% -> 78%` TriviaQA example.
