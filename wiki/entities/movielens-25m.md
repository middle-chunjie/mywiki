## Description

MovieLens-25M is a large-scale recommendation benchmark included in [[yang-2023-generative-2307-05100]]. The paper converts rating `5` to positive feedback and reports `92,901` users, `8,826` items, and `2,605,952` interactions.

## Key Contributions

- Tests whether VGCL still improves over strong baselines on a denser and larger dataset.
- Confirms gains over LightGCN and SimGCL even when the absolute margin is smaller than on Douban-Book.
