## Description

Kai-Wei Chang is a UCLA researcher and co-author of [[ahmad-2021-unified]]. In this work, he is part of the group that introduces PLBART as a unified pre-trained model for program understanding and generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PLBART architecture and large-scale pre-training recipe.
- GraphCodeBERT, and other baselines across multiple tasks. Helped position the model against CodeBERT
