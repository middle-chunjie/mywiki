## Description

Alan Zhu is a researcher at [[carnegie-mellon-university]] School of Computer Science and co-author of RaLMSpec, a speculative retrieval framework for accelerating iterative RAG serving.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designs the speculative retrieval and batched verification mechanisms in RaLMSpec.
- Co-authors empirical evaluation across GPT-2, OPT, and LLaMA-2 models on four QA datasets.
