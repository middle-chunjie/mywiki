## Description

Chris Samarinas is a researcher at the University of Massachusetts Amherst and the first author of [[samarinas-2024-procis]]. In this paper, he co-develops the ProCIS benchmark for proactive retrieval in conversations.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ProCIS benchmark, including the dataset formulation and evaluation setup.
- Contributed to the LMGR baseline and the empirical comparison between reactive and proactive retrieval.
