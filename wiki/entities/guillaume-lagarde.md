## Description

Guillaume Lagarde is a researcher affiliated in this paper with CNRS, LaBRI, and Université de Bordeaux. He is a co-author of [[fijalkow-2022-scaling]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the theoretical and algorithmic treatment of probability-guided search in program synthesis.
- Helped introduce Heap Search and SQRT Sampling in [[fijalkow-2022-scaling]].
