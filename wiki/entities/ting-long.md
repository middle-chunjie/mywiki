## Description

Ting Long is a researcher at [[shanghai-jiao-tong-university]] and a co-first author of [[long-2022-multiview]]. The paper credits Long with introducing the multi-view graph formulation for semantic program representation in algorithm detection.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed MVG with `DFG`, `CFG`, `RWG`, and `CG` views.
- Helped position algorithm detection as a benchmark for semantic [[program-representation]].
- Contributed to experiments on [[poj-104]] and [[alg-109]].
