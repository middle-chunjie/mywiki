## Description

Heinz Koeppl is the final author of the REVELA paper and is affiliated with the Technical University of Darmstadt in this source. He is part of the team proposing dense retriever learning via language modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the REVELA method and its efficiency-oriented comparison against REPLUG.
- Contributed to the paper's scaling and generalization analysis.
