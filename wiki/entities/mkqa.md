## Description

MKQA is a multilingual question answering benchmark used in [[wang-2025-jinarerankerv-2509-25085]] for cross-lingual retrieval evaluation. The paper reports Recall@10 on MKQA to assess whether the reranker transfers beyond English retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the cross-lingual Recall@10 result where jina-reranker-v3 reaches `67.92`.
- Complements BEIR and MIRACL by focusing on multilingual question answering retrieval.
