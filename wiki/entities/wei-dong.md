## Description

Wei Dong is a co-author of [[wang-2022-bridging-2112-02268]] affiliated with [[national-university-of-defense-technology]]. In this work he contributes to the study of data augmentation and robust fine-tuning for code tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the empirical study showing that lightweight downstream adaptation can outperform stronger code-pretrained baselines.
- Helped evaluate the method on POJ-104, BigCloneBench, and CodeSearchNet.
