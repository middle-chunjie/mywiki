## Description

Yongkang Wu is a researcher at [[huawei-poisson-lab]] and co-author of [[li-2024-retrollm-2412-11919]]. The paper associates Wu with the system design and evaluation of retrieval within generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the RetroLLM framework for generating fine-grained evidence under corpus constraints.
- Contributed to experiments spanning in-domain and out-of-domain open-domain QA benchmarks.
