## Description

Qwen1.5 72B is one of the main open-source baselines in the paper's bilingual evaluation. It is used to contextualize DeepSeek-V2's tradeoffs against another strong Chinese-and-English dense model.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a bilingual dense baseline for English, Chinese, code, and math comparisons.
- Highlights that DeepSeek-V2 is stronger on most English, code, and math benchmarks while remaining competitive on Chinese tasks.
