## Description

Chandan K. Reddy is a researcher in the Department of Computer Science at [[virginia-tech]] and co-author of [[jha-2023-codeattack-2206-00052]]. In this paper he contributes to studying robustness vulnerabilities in sequence-to-sequence programming-language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed [[codeattack]] for adversarial testing of code generation models.
- Helped frame the transferability evaluation across tasks and programming languages.
