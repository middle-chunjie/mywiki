## Description

Jiale Cheng is a researcher affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]] and [[zhipu-ai]], and a co-first author of [[cheng-2024-blackbox-2311-04155]]. He is listed with the CoAI Group in the paper that introduces BPO.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the BPO framework for training-free LLM alignment through prompt rewriting.
- Contributed to the construction of the `14k` prompt-optimization training pairs used to train the optimizer.
