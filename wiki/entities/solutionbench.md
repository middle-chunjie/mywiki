## Description

SolutionBench is the benchmark introduced in [[li-2025-deepsolution-2502-20730]] for evaluating complex engineering solution design across eight engineering domains.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `950` datapoints and `6,137` knowledge entries grounded in real engineering reports.
- Separates analytical and technical evaluation through two LLM-judged scores.
- Supplies domain-specific knowledge bases for testing RAG systems on realistic constrained design tasks.
