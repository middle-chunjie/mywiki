## Description

Dheeru Dua is the first author of [[dua-2023-adapt]], affiliated with [[university-of-california-irvine]]; the paper notes that this work was done while he was an intern at [[google-research]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the paper's shift taxonomy for end-to-end open-domain QA.
- Co-authors the zero-shot and few-shot intervention analysis for cross-domain adaptation.
