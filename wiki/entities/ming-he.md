## Description

Ming He is an author of [[fang-2021-guided]] affiliated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University and Didi Chuxing. He is part of the collaboration that introduced GACEN for concept extraction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the guided-attention architecture combining topic and clue-word supervision.
- Contributed to the experimental study showing gains over BERT-CRF and Joint-layer RNN.
