## Description

Yunyao Li is a researcher affiliated with Adobe, with the paper noting that the reported work was done during a position at Apple. She is a co-author of [[reddy-2024-agrame-2405-15028]] focused on fine-grained ranking and attribution.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the AGRaME method for ranking at sentence and proposition granularity.
- Helps connect fine-grained ranking to attribution-oriented applications such as citation assignment.
- Contributes to the paper's experimental evaluation on proposition retrieval and long-form QA citation metrics.
