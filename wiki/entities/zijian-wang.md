## Description

Zijian Wang is a researcher at [[aws-ai-labs]] and a co-author of [[ding-2024-fewer-2404-10830]]. He appears on the paper introducing Best-fit Packing for more context-faithful language-model pretraining.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the packing-based data pipeline that preserves document integrity during pretraining.
- Contributed to the empirical analysis linking fewer truncations to lower hallucination.
