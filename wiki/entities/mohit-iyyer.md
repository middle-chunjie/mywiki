## Description

Mohit Iyyer is a researcher at [[university-of-massachusetts-amherst]] and a co-author of BOOOOKSCORE, which studies long-context LLM summarization and coherence evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed BOOOOKSCORE as a sentence-level, reference-free metric for summary coherence.
- Co-authored the systematic evaluation across GPT-4, Claude 2, Mixtral, GPT-3.5-Turbo, and LLaMA 2.
