## Description

Robert Osazuwa Ness is a Microsoft Research author of [[edge-2024-local-2404-16130]]. Ness is part of the team that proposes GraphRAG for large-corpus question answering and summarization.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the GraphRAG method built from LLM-derived graph indexing and community summaries.
- Contributed to the paper's analysis of token efficiency and answer quality.
