## Description

Wentao Ding is a researcher at [[nanjing-university]] and the first author of [[ding-2024-enhancing-2402-02175]]. He works on complex KGQA and retrieval-oriented reasoning over knowledge graphs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed Evidence Pattern Retrieval for improving [[subgraph-extraction]] in IR-style KGQA.
- Helped design the atomic-pattern retrieval and evidence-pattern ranking pipeline evaluated on CWQ and WebQSP.
