## Description

Robin Jia is a University of Southern California researcher and co-author of [[rodriguez-2021-evaluation]], known for work on question answering benchmarks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the SQuAD 2.0 case study used to validate DAD leaderboards.
- Helps connect leaderboard analysis with question answering dataset quality and annotation issues.
