## Description

ChatGLM2-6B is an updated bilingual ChatGLM baseline evaluated in [[chen-2023-benchmarking-2309-01431]] and used repeatedly for the paper's qualitative error analyses.

## Key Contributions

- Its English noise-robustness accuracy drops from `91.33%` at noise ratio `0` to `57.33%` at `0.8`, illustrating retrieval brittleness under distractors.
- The paper uses ChatGLM2-6B outputs to exemplify noise-robustness, negative-rejection, and information-integration error patterns.
