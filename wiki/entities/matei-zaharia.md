## Description

Matei Zaharia is a researcher at [[stanford-university]] and a co-author of the DSP paper. In this work, he helps connect strong retrieval infrastructure to explicit LM-guided reasoning programs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors DSP and its evaluation with CoLBERTv2-backed retrieval for knowledge-intensive question answering.
- Helps demonstrate that frozen retrievers and frozen language models can be composed through explicit textual control flow.
