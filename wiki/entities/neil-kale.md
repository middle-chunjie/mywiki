## Description

Neil Kale is the lead author of [[kale-2026-reliable-2508-19461]] and is affiliated in the paper with both [[scale-ai]] and [[carnegie-mellon-university]]. He works on monitoring and red teaming of autonomous LLM agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's monitor red teaming workflow and the hybrid monitoring design.
- Helped introduce the CUA-SHADE-Arena benchmark for computer-use agent monitoring.
