## Description

Huanhuan Yuan is a researcher affiliated with Soochow University and a co-author of [[qin-2023-metaoptimized]]. The paper associates Huanhuan Yuan with the development and evaluation of MCLRec.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MCLRec framework for sequential recommendation under sparse and noisy data.
- Helped validate the benefits of learned augmenters and meta-optimized training.
