## Description

Zheng Chen is a researcher at [[shandong-university]] and a co-author of [[sun-2023-tokenize-2304-04171]], which proposes GENRET for semantic docid learning in generative retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the GENRET framework that jointly learns document tokenization, retrieval, and reconstruction.
- Contributes to experiments on NQ320K, MS MARCO, and BEIR for learned docid retrieval.
