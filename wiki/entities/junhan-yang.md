## Description

Junhan Yang is the first author of [[yang-2023-longtriever]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-science-and-technology-of-china]]. The paper presents Yang as part of the team proposing Longtriever for long-document dense retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Longtriever, a hierarchical encoder for dense retrieval over long documents.
- Contributed to the LMAE pretraining objective for retrieval-oriented document encoding.
