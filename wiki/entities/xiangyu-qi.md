## Description

Xiangyu Qi is a Princeton University researcher and co-lead author of SORRY-Bench. In this paper, he helps frame systematic evaluation of LLM safety refusal across categories, prompt mutations, and judge designs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the benchmark design spanning taxonomy curation, balanced data collection, and large-scale model evaluation.
- Contributed to the paper's analysis of cross-model safety refusal differences.
