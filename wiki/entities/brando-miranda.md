## Description

Brando Miranda is a Stanford University researcher and co-author of [[schaeffer-2023-emergent-2304-15004]], which critiques abrupt-emergence claims in large language models through metric and statistics analyses.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the mathematical framing that links smooth scaling behavior to apparent emergence under nonlinear or discontinuous metrics.
- Contributed to the empirical analyses spanning arithmetic benchmarks, BIG-Bench, and vision-based induced-emergence demonstrations.
