## Description

Xinyan Yu is a researcher affiliated with the University of Southern California and a co-author of REALTIME QA. In this work she contributes to the benchmark's dynamic QA framing and evaluation effort.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the REALTIME QA paper and its analysis of open-book versus closed-book baselines.
- Helped frame the benchmark around present-time information needs instead of fixed-answer QA.
