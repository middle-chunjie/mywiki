## Description

Yiming Yang is a coauthor of [[jiang-2023-active-2305-06983]] and is affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]] in this paper. He contributes to the formulation and benchmarking of active retrieval for long-form knowledge-intensive generation.

## Key Contributions

- Helped position FLARE as a generic inference-time method rather than a task-specific prompt-engineering trick.
- Contributed to experiments showing forward-looking retrieval outperforms previous-context retrieval.
- Coanalyzed task-level differences in gains across 2WikiMultihopQA, StrategyQA, ASQA, and WikiAsp.
