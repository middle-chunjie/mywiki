## Description

mBART is the multilingual denoising sequence-to-sequence pretrained model highlighted in [[ranathunga-2021-neural-2106-15115]] as a strong low-resource translation initializer.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as evidence that multilingual pretraining can substantially improve low-resource translation.
- Bridges multilingual modeling, transfer learning, and semi-supervised NMT through parameter initialization and fine-tuning.
