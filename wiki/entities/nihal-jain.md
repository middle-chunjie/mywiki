Nihal Jain is a researcher affiliated with [[aws-ai-labs]] and a co-author of [[ding-nd-crosscodeeval]]. In this source he contributes to a multilingual benchmark for repository-level code completion and retrieval-aware evaluation.

- Helped evaluate code LMs and retrieval methods under cross-file completion settings.
