## Description

Danqi Chen is a co-author of the C-STS paper and is affiliated with Princeton University in this source.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the formulation of C-STS as a conditioned alternative to standard STS.
- Contributed to the benchmark's model-evaluation setup across encoder and LLM families.
- Helped motivate condition-aware evaluation for sentence representation learning.
