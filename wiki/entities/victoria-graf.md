## Description

Victoria Graf is a co-author of the C-STS paper and is affiliated with Princeton University in this source.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the conditional similarity benchmark and its annotation pipeline.
- Contributed to the collection and verification of condition-specific similarity labels.
- Helped position C-STS as a finer-grained alternative to conventional STS benchmarks.
