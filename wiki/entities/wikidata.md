## Description

Wikidata is the knowledge base used to automatically generate the paper's easier list-question benchmark in [[dhuliawala-2023-chainofverification-2309-11495]].

## Key Contributions

- Supplies `56` test questions of the form "Who are some [Profession]s who were born in [City]?"
- Provides large gold answer sets, typically around `600` entities per query, for measuring precision and hallucinated entities.
