## Description

Xupeng Miao is a researcher affiliated with [[purdue-university]] and the lead author of this survey on efficient LLM serving. In this source he helps synthesize algorithmic and systems approaches to low-latency, high-throughput generative inference.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the survey taxonomy spanning decoding algorithms, compression, parallelism, memory management, scheduling, and kernel optimization.
- Surveys representative LLM serving frameworks and benchmark gaps from an MLSys perspective.
