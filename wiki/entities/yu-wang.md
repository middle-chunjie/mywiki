## Description

Yu Wang is a co-author of the Skeleton-of-Thought paper and part of the [[tsinghua-university]] author team listed on the manuscript. The paper credits Wang as part of the collaboration proposing SoT and its routed extension.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the SoT and SoT-R framework for reducing answer-generation latency.
- Contributed to the study of routing and evaluation for structured parallel generation.
