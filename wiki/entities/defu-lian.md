## Description

Defu Lian is a researcher affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China and the State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence. He is listed as a corresponding author on the Retro* paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Retro* and supervised the work as a corresponding author.
- Contributed to the paper's reasoning-intensive retrieval agenda and experimental study.
