## Description

Wikipedia is the document corpus used in [[samarinas-2024-procis]] for proactive and reactive contextual suggestion. The benchmark maps Reddit-linked articles into a cleaned Wikipedia dump of over 5.3 million English pages.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the retrieval corpus for all benchmarked methods.
- Provides the title-description pairs used by LMGR grounding and dense candidate retrieval.
