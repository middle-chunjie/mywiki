## Description

InfoSeek is the paper's open data-synthesis framework and dataset for deep-research question answering. It constructs research trees from webpages and Wikipedia, then filters the resulting tasks for difficulty and verifiability.

## Key Contributions

- Produces more than `52K` HCSP-style deep-research QA samples with explicit construction traces.
- Supplies the training data used to supervise and optimize InfoSeeker.
- Open-sources both the data-construction framework and the resulting dataset.
