## Description

Julian Dolby is a researcher at IBM Research, T.J. Watson Research Center and a co-author of the BLANCA benchmark for understanding text about code.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed BLANCA's task suite over forum posts and documentation.
- Co-authored the evaluation of multiple sentence encoders and code-domain models.
