## Description

APPS is the programming benchmark used in [[jiang-2024-training-2405-18649]] both as a source of training problems and as part of the reinforcement-learning data construction.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies `5,000` training problems for collecting wrong programs, explanations, and verified refinements.
- Contributes `16,372` wrong solutions and `6,419` correct refinements in the paper's verified data pipeline.
