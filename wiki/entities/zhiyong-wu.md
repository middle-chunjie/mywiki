## Description

Zhiyong Wu is the lead author of [[wu-2023-selfadaptive]] and is affiliated in this paper with [[shanghai-artificial-intelligence-laboratory]]. The paper credits him with leading the project and writing the manuscript.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduces self-adaptive in-context learning as an instance-level search problem over demonstration selection and ordering.
- Contributes to the select-then-rank framework and the MDL-based ranking analysis.
- Leads the project and manuscript according to the paper's acknowledgement.
