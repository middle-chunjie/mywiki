## Description

MS MARCO is the open-domain retrieval benchmark used in [[almeida-2024-exploring]] as one of the downstream evaluation collections and as a reference training source for external baselines. The paper reports `4,102` question-document pairs and a collection of more than `8M` documents.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the five downstream evaluation datasets for synthetic-data-trained rerankers.
- Provides the corpus context for the `castorini/monot5-base-msmarco-10k` checkpoint cited in the paper.
