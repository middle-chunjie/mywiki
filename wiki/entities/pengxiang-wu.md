## Description

Pengxiang Wu is a researcher at [[snap-inc]] and the first author of [[wu-2023-forb-2309-16249]], which introduces the FORB benchmark for flat-object retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Led the introduction of the [[forb]] benchmark for evaluating flat-object [[image-retrieval]].
- Co-authored the proposal of `t-mAP` for margin-sensitive evaluation of [[image-embedding]] quality.
- Helped establish the empirical comparison across bottom-up, top-down, and top-only retrieval baselines.
