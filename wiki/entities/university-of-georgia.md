## Description

The University of Georgia (UGA) is a public research university in Athens, Georgia, USA. In Img2Loc, the UGA team (Zhongliang Zhou, Jielu Zhang, Ni Lao, Lan Mu, Gengchen Mai) led the development of the geolocation system.

## Key Contributions

- Hosted the primary research group behind the Img2Loc system.
- Faculty PIs Lan Mu and Gengchen Mai serve as corresponding authors and specialize in geospatial machine learning.
