## Description

Seungju Han is a researcher affiliated with Stanford University and a co-author of AgentFlow. In this paper he contributes to the trainable agentic system and its benchmark evaluation across diverse reasoning tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the AgentFlow framework and its multi-benchmark evaluation.
- Helped demonstrate that a tuned 7B agentic system can outperform stronger monolithic baselines.
