## Description

Bernt Schiele is a co-author of the TokenFormer paper and is affiliated with Max Planck Institute for Informatics in this work.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the proposal to scale Transformer-like models by adding parameter tokens.
- Contributed to the paper's evaluation of compute savings from progressive scaling.
