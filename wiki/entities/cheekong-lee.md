## Description

Cheekong Lee is an author of [[hao-2020-asgn]] affiliated with Tencent America. The paper credits him as part of the team introducing ASGN for label-efficient molecular property prediction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ASGN framework and its active-learning formulation.
- Helped validate the method on QM9 and OPV against supervised, Mean-Teacher, and InfoGraph baselines.
