## Description

Bingzhe Wu is a corresponding author of [[ma-2023-fairnessguided-2303-13217]] and is affiliated with AI Lab, Tencent.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the work on fairness-guided prompt search for large language models.
- Helped develop the greedy search variant that jointly optimizes demonstration choice and order.
- Oversaw evaluation against random, diversity-based, and similarity-based prompting strategies.
