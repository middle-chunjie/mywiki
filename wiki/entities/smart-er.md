## Description

SMART-ER is the multi-domain training dataset introduced in [[qian-2025-smart-2502-11435]]. It contains compositional reasoning chains annotated with when parametric knowledge suffices and when tools are required.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `3K+` examples across Math, Time, and Intention for strategic tool-use training.
- Attaches step-level justifications that make the reasoning-tool boundary explicit.
- Supplies the supervision used to train SMARTAgent.
