## Description

Frank Guerin is a researcher at the [[university-of-surrey]] and co-author of [[li-2023-compressing]]. He appears on the paper introducing Selective Context for compacting prompts and long input histories before LLM inference.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the empirical analysis of quality-efficiency trade-offs under different compression ratios.
- Helped demonstrate that phrase-level selective pruning is more reliable than token- or sentence-level filtering.
