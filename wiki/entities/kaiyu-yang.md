## Description

Kaiyu Yang is the lead author of [[yang-nd-leandojo]] and is affiliated with Caltech in this paper. He contributes to the LeanDojo toolkit, benchmark construction, and the ReProver retrieval-augmented prover.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduces LeanDojo as an open toolkit for extracting proof data and interacting with Lean programmatically.
- Co-develops ReProver and the benchmark design centered on the `novel_premises` generalization split.
