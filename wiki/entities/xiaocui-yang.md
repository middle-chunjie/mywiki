## Description

Xiaocui Yang is a researcher at Northeastern University, China, and a co-author of [[sun-2024-consistency-2401-13598]] on synthetic-data generation for zero-shot document-level relation triplet extraction.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the paper's LLM-based synthetic data generation setup for unseen relation types.
- Co-authored the evaluation of GenRDK on zero-shot document-level relation and triplet extraction benchmarks.
