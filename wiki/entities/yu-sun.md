## Description

Yu Sun is a Stanford University researcher, project lead, and first author of [[sun-2024-learn-2310-13807]], which introduces Meta Test-Time Training (MTTT) as a learned form of test-time adaptation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the formulation of MTTT as a nested inner-loop and outer-loop learning problem.
- Helped establish the paper's connections between TTT, linear attention, and self-attention.
