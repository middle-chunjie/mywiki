## Description

LLaMA-2-7B is the language model used in the paper's retrieval-augmented QA evaluation under fixed token budgets of retrieved context.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the downstream reader for comparing passage, sentence, and proposition prompts at `100` and `500` retrieved tokens.
- Shows higher average QA accuracy when the prompt is built from proposition retrieval rather than passage retrieval.
