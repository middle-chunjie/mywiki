## Description

Researcher at Google Research at the time of the Transformer paper; equal-contribution co-author of [[vaswani-2017-attention-1706-03762]]. Also co-author of the earlier decomposable attention work for NLI (reference [27] in the Transformer paper).

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the Transformer architecture.
- Prior work on decomposable attention is cited as one of the few precedents for attention-only models.
