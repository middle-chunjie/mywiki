## Description

TimesURL is the self-supervised time-series representation learning framework introduced in [[liu-2023-timesurl]]. It combines frequency-temporal augmentation, dual Universum hard negatives, and masked time reconstruction on top of a TCN encoder.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces FTAug for temporally safer positive-pair construction.
- Uses temporal-wise and instance-wise Universums as hard negatives in contrastive training.
- Achieves strong aggregate results across classification, forecasting, imputation, anomaly detection, and transfer learning benchmarks.
