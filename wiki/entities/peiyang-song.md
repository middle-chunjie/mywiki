## Description

Peiyang Song is a co-author of [[yang-nd-leandojo]] and is listed with UC Santa Barbara as the affiliation in this paper. The work credits the author team jointly with the benchmark, tooling, and ReProver system.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the open benchmark containing traced Lean theorems, tactics, and premise metadata.
- Participates in the release of an accessible baseline for retrieval-augmented theorem proving.
