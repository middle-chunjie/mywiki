## Description

Yan Liu is an author of [[liu-nd-uncovering]] and is affiliated in this paper with Microsoft Research and The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored a NeurIPS 2023 study uncovering social bias in [[code-generation]] models.
- Helped introduce prompt-based evaluation, a code-bias dataset, and bias metrics for demographic unfairness analysis.
