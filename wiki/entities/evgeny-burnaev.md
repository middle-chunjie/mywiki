## Description

Evgeny Burnaev is a researcher affiliated with Applied AI and Learnable Intelligence Lab in Moscow. He is a co-author of [[sorokin-2026-qrag-2511-07328]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Q-RAG method for value-based multi-step retrieval over long contexts.
- Helped position the work as a compute-efficient alternative to LLM fine-tuning for retrieval reasoning.
