## Description

Dan Friedman is the first author of [[friedman-2023-vendi-2210-02410]] and is affiliated with [[princeton-university]] in the paper. He co-introduces the Vendi Score as a general diversity metric for machine learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the formal definition and properties of [[vendi-score]].
- Co-authored the cross-domain empirical evaluation spanning molecules, images, text, and datasets.
