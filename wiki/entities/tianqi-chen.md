## Description

Tianqi Chen is a researcher affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]] and a co-author of this survey on efficient LLM serving. In this paper he contributes to the synthesis of compiler, runtime, and systems perspectives on LLM inference efficiency.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the survey's review of serving frameworks, compilation approaches, and hardware-software co-design considerations.
- Helps position efficient LLM serving as an MLSys problem spanning kernels, runtimes, and deployment infrastructure.
