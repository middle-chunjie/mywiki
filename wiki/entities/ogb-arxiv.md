## Description

OGB-arxiv is a citation-graph benchmark used in [[tang-2024-graphgpt-2310-13023]] for both supervised evaluation and transfer experiments. It contains computer science arXiv papers and their citation links.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the primary supervised benchmark on which GraphGPT reports up to `0.7511` accuracy and `0.5600` macro-F1.
- Provides the source domain for zero-shot transfer to PubMed and Cora in the paper's main experiments.
