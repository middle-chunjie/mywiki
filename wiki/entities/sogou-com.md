## Description

Sogou.com is the Chinese commercial search engine whose query log provides the training and evaluation data used in [[xiong-2017-endtoend-1706-06613]].

## Key Contributions

- Supplies roughly `35` million search sessions, including queries, clicked documents, and dwell-time signals.
- Anchors the paper's evaluation of neural ad-hoc ranking under DCTR, TACM, and raw-click settings.
