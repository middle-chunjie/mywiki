## Description

Microsoft Research is the institutional affiliation listed for all authors of the Phi-4 technical report. In this paper it is the research organization responsible for the model's training, evaluation, and safety analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Developed phi-4 and its data-centric training recipe.
- Designed pivotal-token DPO, long-context midtraining, and the associated synthetic-data pipelines.
- Conducted internal benchmarking, responsible-AI evaluation, and red-teaming for the model.
