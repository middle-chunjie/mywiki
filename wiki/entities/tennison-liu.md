## Description

Tennison Liu is a co-author of LLAMBO and is affiliated in this paper with DAMTP, University of Cambridge. The paper marks Liu as an equal-contribution author on the proposed LLM-augmented Bayesian optimization framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed [[llambo]] as a modular method for using LLMs in warmstarting, surrogate modeling, and candidate sampling.
- Helped design the end-to-end empirical evaluation across Bayesmark, HPOBench, and private or synthetic tasks.
