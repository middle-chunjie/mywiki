## Description

WebSailor is the family of post-trained web-agent models introduced in [[li-2025-websailor-2507-02592]] for difficult information-seeking tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Combines SailorFog-QA synthesis, reasoning reconstruction, RFT cold start, and DUPO reinforcement learning.
- Sets the strongest open-source results reported in the paper on BrowseComp-en and BrowseComp-zh.
- Scales across Qwen-2.5 backbones from `3B` to `72B`.
