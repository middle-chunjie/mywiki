## Description

Yi-Fan Zhang is a researcher at the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS). His work focuses on domain generalization, domain adaptation, and learning domain-invariant representations.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed Environment Label Smoothing (ELS) for stabilizing Domain Adversarial Training.
- Contributed theoretical analysis connecting ELS to JS/KL divergence minimization and non-asymptotic convergence.
- Led experiments across 13+ benchmarks spanning image, NLP, genomics, and graph tasks.
