## Description

INESC-ID is the research institution listed as the affiliation for most LumberChunker authors, including Andre V. Duarte, Joao Marques, Miguel Graca, and Arlindo Oliveira. In this source it is the main institutional base for the paper's LLM-based segmentation work.

## Key Contributions

- Hosts the primary research team behind LumberChunker.
- Serves as the affiliation context for the paper's work on [[document-segmentation]] and [[passage-retrieval]].
- Anchors the development of the [[gutenqa]] evaluation benchmark.
