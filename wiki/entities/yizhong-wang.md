## Description

Yizhong Wang is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and the Allen Institute for AI. He co-authored SELF-RAG, a method for grounded generation through retrieval and self-critique.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the training-data construction and evaluation pipeline used for SELF-RAG.
- Helped position the method across instruction-following and knowledge-intensive benchmarks.
