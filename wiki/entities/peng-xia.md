## Description

Peng Xia is a co-first author of [[xia-2026-skillrl-2602-08234]] and is affiliated with [[unc-chapel-hill]] in the paper. He is part of the team proposing SkillRL and its recursive skill evolution pipeline for LLM agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the SkillRL framework that distills trajectories into reusable skills instead of storing raw memory.
- Helps formulate the recursive skill evolution loop and the evaluation across ALFWorld, WebShop, and search-augmented QA tasks.
