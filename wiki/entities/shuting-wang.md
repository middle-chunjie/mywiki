## Description

Shuting Wang is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and co-author of the LLM4IR survey on applying large language models to information retrieval systems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LLM4IR survey providing a unified taxonomy of LLM-enhanced IR methods.
- Contributed to the analysis of retrieval-augmented generation and reader module sections.
