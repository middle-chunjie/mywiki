## Description

Zhuo Chen is a researcher at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJUKG lab). His research spans knowledge graphs, multimodal entity alignment, and KG-enhanced language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of KnowPAT, contributing to the preference alignment framework for domain-specific QA.
- Co-authored MEAformer (multi-modal entity alignment) and work on KG-enhanced visual QA.
