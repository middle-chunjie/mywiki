## Description

Guenevere (Qian) Chen is a co-author of RoPGen and is affiliated with the University of Texas at San Antonio in this paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the robust training framework that combines style-based data augmentation with gradient augmentation.
- Contributed to the empirical evaluation across four code-authorship datasets.
