## Description

Junlei Zhang is a researcher affiliated with Zhejiang University and the School of Engineering at Westlake University (joint affiliation). He is the first author of SynCSE, with work conducted during a visit to HKUST.

## Key Contributions

- First author of SynCSE; designed the data synthesis pipeline using ChatGPT for contrastive sentence embedding training.
- Introduced the prompt and exemplar pool mechanism for diversity control in LLM-generated training data.
