## Description

Jai Gupta is a Google Research co-author of [[pradeep-2023-how]]. In this paper he contributes to the study of how generative retrieval behaves when scaled from small corpora to the full MS MARCO passage collection.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the ablation of identifier schemes and synthetic-query training.
- Contributes to the full-corpus evaluation on MSMarcoFULL.
