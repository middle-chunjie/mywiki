## Description

Dinghao Wu is a researcher at Pennsylvania State University and a co-author of [[li-2022-alphaprog]]. The paper positions him as part of the team that built and analyzed ALPHAPROG for compiler fuzzing.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored ALPHAPROG and its reward-driven approach to generating valid and diverse compiler tests.
- Contributed to the deployment on BFC and the reporting of two confirmed compiler bugs.
