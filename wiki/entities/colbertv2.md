## Description

CoLBERTv2 is the late-interaction neural reranking model evaluated in [[anand-2024-understanding-2408-17103]] on the DL-MIA benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Achieves the strongest reported performance in the paper with `nDCG@10 = 0.261` and `α-nDCG@10 = 0.532` when intents are used as queries.
