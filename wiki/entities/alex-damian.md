## Description

Alex Damian is a co-author of [[malladi-2024-finetuning-2305-17333]] and is affiliated with [[princeton-university]]. His role in this source is tied to the optimizer design and evaluation of memory-efficient forward-only fine-tuning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MeZO formulation that replays perturbations from random seeds to avoid storing full perturbation vectors.
- Helped demonstrate that MeZO remains competitive with backpropagation while using much less memory.
- Contributed to the theoretical framing around why MeZO can avoid catastrophic dimension-dependent slowdown.
