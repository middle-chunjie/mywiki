## Description

Saizhuo Wang is a co-author of [[sun-2024-thinkongraph-2307-07697]] affiliated with the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology and IDEA Research. The paper credits him with work on the ToG framework for graph-guided LLM reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the beam-search-based KG reasoning framework proposed in the paper.
- Helped evaluate how different backbone LLMs affect ToG performance.
