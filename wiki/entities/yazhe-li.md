## Description

Yazhe Li is a researcher at [[deepmind]] and a co-author of [[oord-2019-representation-1807-03748]], contributing to the paper's multimodal study of Contrastive Predictive Coding.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CPC paper that formalizes InfoNCE-based future prediction in latent space.
- Helped validate CPC across speech, image, language, and RL benchmarks.
