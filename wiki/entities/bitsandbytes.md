## Description

bitsandbytes is the software library released alongside this work to provide CUDA kernels and low-bit training support for QLoRA-style 4-bit fine-tuning.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the implementation substrate for the paper's 4-bit training and optimizer support.
- Makes QLoRA-style quantized fine-tuning accessible in open-source tooling.
