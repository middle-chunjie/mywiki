## Description

MingHao Qin is a researcher affiliated with the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence and the Chinese Academy of Sciences. He is a co-first author of [[li-2024-making-2409-15700]] and contributes to the paper's ICL-oriented embedding training design and evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the `bge-en-icl` embedding model and its few-shot training recipe.
- Helped analyze attention, pooling, and prompt ablations for LLM-based embeddings.
