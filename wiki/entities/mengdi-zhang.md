## Description

Mengdi Zhang is a co-author of [[zhang-2022-incorporating]] affiliated with Meituan. She is a contributor to the DR-BERT model for aspect-based sentiment analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the DR-BERT framework incorporating Dynamic Re-weighting Adapters into BERT for aspect-aware sentiment understanding.
- Contributed to the experimental evaluation on three standard ABSA benchmarks.
