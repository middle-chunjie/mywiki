## Description

DeepSeek-AI is the organization behind DeepSeek-V2 and the group author listed on the paper. In this work it provides the model design, the internal HAI-LLM systems stack, and the large-scale pretraining and alignment pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces the DeepSeek-V2 architecture built around MLA and DeepSeekMoE.
- Pretrains the model on an `8.1T` token bilingual corpus and releases both base and chat variants.
- Reports an integrated systems-and-model co-design story spanning training efficiency, long-context extension, and post-training alignment.
