## Description

Weiwen Xu is a co-author of [[li-2024-can-2410-01428]] affiliated with DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group and Hupan Lab. Xu contributes to the paper's framework for critic-guided planning with retrieval augmentation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the CR-Planner framework and its evaluation setup.
- Helped study how critic-guided routing affects retrieval and reasoning quality.
