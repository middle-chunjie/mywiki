## Description

RealNews is a news-domain corpus used in [[ram-2023-incontext-2302-00083]] to evaluate token-level perplexity for In-Context RALM.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the five language modeling corpora used to test whether retrieval augmentation generalizes beyond Wikipedia-style text.
- Shows large gains from BM25 retrieval and reranking, complementing the WikiText-103 results.
