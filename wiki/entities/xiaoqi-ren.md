## Description

Xiaoqi Ren is a researcher on the [[google-research]] Gecko project and a co-author of [[lee-2024-gecko-2403-20327]]. The paper credits Ren as co-lead of Gecko and the main implementer of both the English and multilingual Gecko models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-led the Gecko model effort.
- Implemented the main Gecko training pipeline.
- Built the multilingual Gecko variant.
