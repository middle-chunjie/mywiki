## Description

Jiatong Yu is a co-author of [[gao-2023-enabling-2305-14627]] and is affiliated with Princeton University in this source. The paper associates him with the benchmark construction and empirical study of citation-supported answer generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the benchmark curation across ASQA, QAMPARI, and ELI5.
- Helped evaluate the interaction between retrieval coverage, correctness, and citation support.
- Contributed to evidence-synthesis experiments with long-context prompting and reranking.
