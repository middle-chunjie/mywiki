## Description

QM9 is a benchmark dataset of about 134k equilibrium molecules with 13 DFT-computed properties, used in [[lu-2019-molecular]] as the main evaluation benchmark. It serves as the paper's primary testbed for comparing MGCN against both classical and neural baselines.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the 13-property benchmark on which MGCN reports the best MAE on 11 tasks.
- Supports the paper's limited-data, transferability, and interaction-depth analyses.
