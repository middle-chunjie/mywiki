## Description

Omar Khattab is a researcher at [[stanford-university]] and a co-author of the DSP paper. In this work, he helps frame retrieval-augmented NLP as explicit LM-RM programs rather than fixed retrieve-then-read pipelines.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the DEMONSTRATE-SEARCH-PREDICT framework for composing frozen language and retrieval models.
- Contributes the multi-hop and retrieval-centric design choices evaluated with CoLBERTv2 and GPT-3.5.
