## Description

Mengjie Deng is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and co-author of [[dong-2024-progressive-2412-14835]] on retrieval-enhanced multimodal reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the AR-MCTS framework that integrates hybrid-modal retrieval with Monte Carlo Tree Search.
- Contributed to the design and analysis of process-level supervision for multimodal reasoning.
