## Description

Bahare Fatemi is a Google Research researcher and co-author of [[kazemi-2025-bigbench-2502-19187]], which introduces [[bbeh]] as a harder successor to [[bbh]] for broad LLM reasoning evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the benchmark construction strategy that preserves BBH-style task diversity while increasing difficulty.
- Helped evaluate modern LLMs on broad reasoning skills beyond math and coding.
