## Description

Minsang Kim is a researcher affiliated with [[korea-university]] and [[sk-telecom]] who co-authored [[kim-2025-syntriever-2502-03824]] on LLM-assisted retriever training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes the two-stage Syntriever framework for retriever distillation and alignment from black-box LLM outputs.
- Contributes the use of synthetic query/passage generation with hallucination relabeling for dense retrieval training.
