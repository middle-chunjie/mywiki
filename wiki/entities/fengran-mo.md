## Description

Fengran Mo is a researcher affiliated with Universite de Montreal and a co-author of [[mao-2023-large-2303-06573]]. In this paper, she helps study how prompted LLMs can improve contextual intent understanding for conversational retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the LLM4CS prompting framework for conversational search.
- Contributed to the analysis of prompting variants, aggregation strategies, and benchmark evaluation on CAsT.
