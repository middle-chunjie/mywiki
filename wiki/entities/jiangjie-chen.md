## Description

Jiangjie Chen is a researcher affiliated with [[fudan-university]] and a co-author of [[yuan-2024-evoagent-2406-14228]]. In this work, he is part of the team building and evaluating automatic multi-agent generation for LLM-based agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the EvoAgent method for evolving agent settings into stronger collaborative systems.
- Helps validate the method on both benchmark-style tasks and open-world environments.
