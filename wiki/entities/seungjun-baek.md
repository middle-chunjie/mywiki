## Description

Seungjun Baek is a researcher at [[korea-university]] and the corresponding author of [[kim-2025-syntriever-2502-03824]], which studies synthetic-data training for dense retrievers.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designs Syntriever's distillation stage based on synthetic positives, hard negatives, and query decomposition.
- Co-introduces the alignment stage based on LLM preference feedback and partial Plackett-Luce ranking.
