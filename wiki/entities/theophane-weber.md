## Description

Theophane Weber is a [[deepmind]] researcher and co-author of [[orseau-2018-singleagent-1811-10928]], which evaluates policy-guided search on Sokoban using a learned neural policy.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the experimental validation of LevinTS and LubyTS on `1,000` generated Sokoban levels.
- Helped integrate learned policy guidance with systematic search.
