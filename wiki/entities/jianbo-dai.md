## Description

Jianbo Dai is affiliated with the University of Edinburgh and is a co-lead author of [[huang-2024-effilearner-2405-15189]]. He is part of the collaboration that studies efficiency-oriented refinement for LLM-generated programs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the EFFI-LEARNER self-optimization pipeline.
- Helped evaluate profiler-guided refinement across open-source and closed-source code LLMs.
