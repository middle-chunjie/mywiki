## Description

Yihe Dong is a co-author of [[gao-2025-metadata-2501-01956]] from Princeton Language and Intelligence at [[princeton-university]]. The paper places Yihe Dong on the team studying how metadata cues improve pre-training efficiency and controllability.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MeCo method and its evaluation on DCLM, RefinedWeb, and C4.
- Helped study conditional inference with both real and fabricated URLs.
- Contributed to the ablation analysis on cooldown length and metadata granularity.
