## Description

André V. Duarte is the first author of LumberChunker and is affiliated with INESC-ID. In this paper he contributes to the LLM-based document segmentation method and its retrieval evaluation on long-form narrative text.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes [[dynamic-chunking]] through the LumberChunker method.
- Co-develops the GutenQA evaluation setup for [[passage-retrieval]].
- Helps frame chunking as a core design choice for [[retrieval-augmented-generation]] systems.
