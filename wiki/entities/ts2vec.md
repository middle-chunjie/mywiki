## Description

TS2Vec is a prior self-supervised time-series representation learning baseline repeatedly compared against in [[liu-2023-timesurl]]. The paper treats it as a strong reference point but argues it undercaptures instance-level information for universal transfer.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as a major baseline for classification, imputation, forecasting, and anomaly detection comparisons.
- Motivates TimesURL's addition of reconstruction and harder negatives by exposing gaps in instance-level semantics.
