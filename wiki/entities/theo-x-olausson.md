## Description

Theo X. Olausson is the lead author of [[unknown-nd-selfrepair-2306-09896]] and is affiliated with [[mit-csail]] in the paper. The paper also notes that part of the work was completed while he was at [[microsoft-research]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-formalizes self-repair as a repair tree over program generation, execution, feedback, and repair.
- Helps show that feedback quality is a core bottleneck in budget-aware code-generation repair.
