## Description

Shiyu Zhao is a researcher at Stanford University and a co-first author of [[wu-2024-stark-2404-13207]]. The paper lists Shiyu Zhao among the main contributors behind the STaRK benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the STaRK benchmark paper on semi-structured retrieval.
- Contributed to the benchmark's construction, analysis, and evaluation as part of the author team.
