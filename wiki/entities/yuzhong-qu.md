## Description

Yuzhong Qu is a researcher at [[nanjing-university]] and the senior author of [[ding-2024-enhancing-2402-02175]]. He has worked on knowledge-base question answering and related retrieval or semantic-parsing methods.

- Oversaw the evaluation of EPR against strong IR and semantic-parsing baselines.
