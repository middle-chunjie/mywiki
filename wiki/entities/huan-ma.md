## Description

Huan Ma is the first author of [[ma-2023-fairnessguided-2303-13217]] and is affiliated with AI Lab, Tencent and Tianjin University. The paper notes that the project was conducted during an internship at Tencent.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed fairness-guided prompt search for few-shot in-context learning.
- Helped formulate predictive bias as an entropy-based surrogate for prompt quality.
- Contributed to the empirical evaluation across BLOOM and LLaMA models.
