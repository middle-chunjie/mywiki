## Description

Jinmei Luo is a researcher at [[alibaba-group]] and a co-author of [[ouyang-2023-contrastive]] on representation learning for conversion prediction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CL4CVR method for using unlabeled click data in post-click CVR estimation.
- Helped validate the effects of embedding masking and supervised positive inclusion in ablation studies.
