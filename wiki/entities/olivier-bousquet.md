## Description

Olivier Bousquet is a research scientist at Google Brain / Google Research and a co-author of the least-to-most prompting paper. He is known for foundational contributions to statistical learning theory, including stability-based generalization bounds.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored least-to-most prompting, demonstrating that decomposition-based prompting solves SCAN compositional generalization with `99.7%` accuracy.
- Known for stability and generalization bounds in statistical learning theory.
