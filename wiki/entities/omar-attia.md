## Description

Omar Attia is a researcher at [[apple]] and a co-author of [[reddy-2024-agrame-2405-15028]]. In this work he contributes to the AGRaME formulation and its application to citation addition.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the any-granularity ranking formulation built on top of ColBERT-style scoring.
- Contributes to the fine-grained attribution and PropCite application in retrieval-augmented generation.
- Participates in the empirical validation across QA and proposition retrieval benchmarks.
