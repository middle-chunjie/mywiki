## Description

Researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and a co-author of [[kusupati-2024-matryoshka-2205-13147]]. He worked on the formulation and evaluation of Matryoshka Representation Learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MRL paper, including experiments on adaptive classification and adaptive retrieval.
