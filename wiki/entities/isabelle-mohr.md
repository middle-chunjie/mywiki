## Description

Isabelle Mohr is a researcher affiliated with Jina AI GmbH in Berlin and a co-author of [[g-nther-2025-jinaembeddingsv-2506-18902]]. The paper presents a unified multimodal embedding model with dense and late-interaction retrieval modes.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the design and evaluation of `jina-embeddings-v4`.
- Helped report multilingual, multimodal, and code-retrieval results across MTEB, ViDoRe, and Jina-VDR.
