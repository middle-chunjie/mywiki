## Description

Oren Pereg is a coauthor of the SETFIT paper and is affiliated with [[intel-labs]]. In this source he is part of the author group proposing efficient prompt-free few-shot text classification.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the SETFIT framework and its experimental evaluation.
- Helped support the paper's deployment-oriented comparison to prompt-based baselines.
