## Description

Hai Jin is a corresponding author of [[gui-2022-crosslanguage-2201-07420]] and is affiliated with Huazhong University of Science and Technology. In this paper Jin co-leads the proposal and validation of XLIR for cross-language binary-source matching.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the formulation of the new cross-language binary-source code matching problem.
- Oversaw the empirical study showing improvements over BinPro, B2SFinder, and LICCA-style baselines.
