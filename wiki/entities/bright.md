## Description

BRIGHT is the main benchmark used in the Retro* paper to evaluate reasoning-intensive document retrieval. It covers 12 datasets spanning StackExchange, coding, and theorem-based tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the primary evaluation benchmark for all main Retro* results.
- Provides task-specific relevance definitions that match the paper's rubric-based scoring setup.
- Exposes the gap between standard retrievers and reasoning-enhanced rerankers on hard retrieval problems.
