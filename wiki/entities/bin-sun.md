## Description

Bin Sun is a co-author of [[weng-2023-large-2212-09561]] and was affiliated with the College of Electrical and Information Engineering, Hunan University.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the evaluation of self-verification on arithmetic, commonsense, and logical reasoning tasks.
- Helped validate that candidate reranking can improve chain-of-thought reasoning without training a separate verifier.
