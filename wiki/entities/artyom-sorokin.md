## Description

Artyom Sorokin is a researcher affiliated with Applied AI and Learnable Intelligence Lab in Moscow. He is a co-author of [[sorokin-2026-qrag-2511-07328]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Q-RAG, which trains a retrieval embedder with value-based reinforcement learning for multi-step retrieval.
- Helped validate the method on long-context retrieval and open-domain QA benchmarks.
