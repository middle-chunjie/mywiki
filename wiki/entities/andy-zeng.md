## Description

Andy Zeng is a co-author of [[li-2023-chain-2312-04474]] and is affiliated with [[google-deepmind]]. In this paper he is part of the collaboration extending code-based reasoning toward embodied and tool-using settings.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the introduction of [[chain-of-code]].
- Helped evaluate the method across benchmark reasoning tasks and robotics demonstrations.
- Contributed to the argument that code structure can mediate both computation and action interfaces.
