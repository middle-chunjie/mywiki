## Description

Guolin Ke is a researcher at [[microsoft-research]] and the lead author of [[ke-2021-rethinking-2006-15595]]. In this paper, he studies how positional information should be parameterized in BERT-style pre-training and helps introduce TUPE.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed [[untied-positional-encoding]] for BERT-style language pre-training.
- Helped analyze the noisy mixed correlations created by standard [[absolute-positional-encoding]].
