## Description

Ruomeng Ding is the first author of [[ding-2024-everything-2311-04254]], affiliated with [[microsoft]] and [[georgia-institute-of-technology]]. The paper footnote notes that the work was completed during her internship at Microsoft Research Asia.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed XoT as an MCTS-LLM collaborative framework for thought generation.
- Helped formulate thought search as a state-action process guided by lightweight policy/value networks.
