## Description

Codeforces is the competitive-programming platform used in [[gong-2024-evaluation-2403-04814]] as the source of problem statements, tests, and accepted solutions for the algorithmic-block and control-flow splits of SAFIM.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies executable programming tasks that support unit-test-based evaluation.
- Provides recent competitive-programming solutions that are filtered for correctness, length, and duplication.
