## Description

Ranjita Naik is a researcher at Microsoft Research and a co-author of the KITAB paper. She is part of the team studying failure modes of LLMs on constrained retrieval tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored KITAB and its empirical evaluation.
- Helped analyze factual errors, irrelevance, and incompleteness in model outputs.
