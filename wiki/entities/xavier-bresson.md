## Description

Xavier Bresson is a co-author of [[he-2024-gretriever-2402-07630]] and is affiliated with National University of Singapore in the paper. He is one of the authors shaping the graph-encoder side of the G-Retriever design.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the use of a GAT-based graph encoder and projection head inside G-Retriever.
- Contributed to the analysis of graph encoder choices, including GCN, GAT, and GraphTransformer variants.
