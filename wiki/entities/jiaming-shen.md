## Description

Jiaming Shen is a researcher at [[google-research]] and a co-author of [[qin-2024-large-2306-17563]]. In this work Shen contributes to the design and assessment of pairwise prompting for zero-shot reranking.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRP framework and its efficient aggregation variants.
- Supported the evaluation of zero-shot ranking quality on TREC-DL benchmarks.
