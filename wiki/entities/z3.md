## Description

Z3 is the SMT solver used in [[tang-2024-code-2405-17503]] to check whether synthesized loop invariants satisfy precondition, preservation, and postcondition requirements. The paper sets the Z3 timeout to `2` minutes for these experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the executable verifier for the loop-invariant synthesis benchmark.
- Produces the failure feedback that the LLM can use during refinement.
