## Description

PolicyQA is an extractive question answering dataset over United States immigration and travel policy documents and is one of the benchmarks used in [[samuel-2023-can-2309-12426]]. In this paper it shows modest but consistent benefit from synthetic data augmentation.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the largest dataset in the paper, with `12,102` question-answer pairs.
- Its best reported setup is unfiltered one-shot augmentation at `32.18` EM / `59.61` F1, above the original `30.56` EM / `58.15` F1.
