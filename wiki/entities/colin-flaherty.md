## Description

Colin Flaherty is an Augment Computing-affiliated author on [[yu-2023-megabyte-2305-07185]], with the paper noting that the work was performed while at Meta AI. He co-authors the study introducing MEGABYTE for long raw-byte sequences.

## Key Contributions

- Helped develop the paper's multimodal evaluation of the MEGABYTE architecture.
- Contributed to the framing of efficient long-context byte modeling without tokenization.
