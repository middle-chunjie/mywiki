## Description

Dianping is a public recommendation benchmark used in [[yang-2023-generative-2307-05100]]. The paper reports `59,426` users, `10,224` items, and `934,334` interactions for this dataset.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a second large-scale benchmark for comparing VGCL with graph and VAE recommendation baselines.
- Is used for ablation and user-group analysis of learned variance and cluster-aware contrastive learning.
