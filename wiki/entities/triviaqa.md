## Description

TriviaQA is one of the four question answering benchmarks used in [[feng-2023-retrievalgeneration-2310-05149]]. In this paper it functions as a single-hop QA dataset for evaluating iterative retrieval-generation methods.

## Key Contributions

- Shows the strongest single-hop result for ITRG (refine), which reaches `80.6` EM in the `5`-shot setting.
- Helps compare iterative synergy against vanilla LM, retrieve-then-read, and generate-then-read baselines.
