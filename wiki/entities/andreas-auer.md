Andreas Auer is a co-author of the xLSTM paper. The author list explicitly associates him with Johannes Kepler University Linz and NXAI Lab.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the xLSTM study on scaling modernized LSTMs for language modeling.
- Helped benchmark xLSTM against matched baselines on SlimPajama and downstream tasks.
