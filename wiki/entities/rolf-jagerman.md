## Description

Rolf Jagerman is a researcher at [[google-research]] and a co-author of [[qin-2024-large-2306-17563]]. In this paper he contributes to the formulation and evaluation of pairwise prompting for zero-shot document reranking.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRP method and its empirical comparison against pointwise and listwise prompting baselines.
- Helped evaluate zero-shot reranking on TREC-DL2019 and TREC-DL2020.
