## Description

Aixin Sun is the senior author of [[ma-2023-large-2303-08559]] and a researcher at [[nanyang-technological-university]]. He is the corresponding institutional lead behind the paper's study of adaptive LLM reranking for IE.

## Key Contributions

- Oversees the paper's unified evaluation of LLMs and SLMs on few-shot IE.
- Co-proposes the adaptive [[filter-then-rerank]] design that reranks only hard samples.
