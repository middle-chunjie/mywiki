## Description

Haochen Li is a researcher at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, and the first author of [[li-2023-rethinking-2310-08069]]. He leads the paper's proposal of [[soft-infonce]] for graded negative handling in code retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces Soft-InfoNCE as a weighted reformulation of InfoNCE for [[code-search]] fine-tuning.
- Develops and compares BM25, SimCSE, and trained-model strategies for estimating negative-pair relevance.
