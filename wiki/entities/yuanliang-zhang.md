## Description

Yuanliang Zhang is a researcher at the National University of Defense Technology and a co-author of this paper. His work here is part of an empirical analysis of code data as a factor in LLM reasoning performance.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the controlled experiments on PanGu and CodePanGu training stages.
- Contributed to the multi-benchmark evaluation of reasoning and code-generation performance.
