## Description

SVHN is the digit classification benchmark used in [[sener-2018-active-1708-00489]] alongside CIFAR-10 and CIFAR-100.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies a third evaluation domain for testing CNN active learning.
- Helps show that the proposed geometric selection strategy generalizes beyond a single image dataset.
