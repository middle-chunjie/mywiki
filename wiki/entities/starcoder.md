## Description

StarCoder is the open-source code large language model evaluated in [[chen-2023-teaching-2304-05128]], reported at `15.5B` parameters.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the paper's open-source model baseline for self-debugging experiments.
- Improves from `70.0` to `76.6` on TransCoder and from `47.2` to `53.2` on MBPP under richer feedback.
