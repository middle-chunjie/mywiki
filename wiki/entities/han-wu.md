## Description

Han Wu is a co-author of [[liu-2018-patent]] affiliated with [[university-of-science-and-technology-of-china]]. The paper places him on the team that designed and evaluated the CTF framework for patent litigation prediction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CTF framework that combines NCNN with three-way tensor factorization.
- Contributed to the empirical study over USPTO and Patexia litigation data.
