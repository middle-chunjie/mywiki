## Description

Prateek Mittal is a Princeton University researcher and senior co-author of SORRY-Bench. The paper positions his author team around systematic LLM safety evaluation, judge design, and large-scale benchmarking.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the overall benchmark design connecting taxonomy construction, judge evaluation, and model comparison.
- Helped frame SORRY-Bench as a systematic instrument for tracking safety refusal behavior.
