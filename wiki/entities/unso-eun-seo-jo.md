## Description

Unso Eun Seo Jo is a coauthor of the SETFIT paper and is affiliated with [[hugging-face]]. In this source she is part of the author team that studies prompt-free few-shot classification and multilingual transfer.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the SETFIT framework and its multilingual evaluation.
- Contributed to the paper's empirical comparison against prompt-based baselines.
