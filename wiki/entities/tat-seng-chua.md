## Description

Tat-Seng Chua is a researcher affiliated with [[national-university-of-singapore]] through the Sea-NExT Joint Lab, and a co-author of [[xu-2024-searchinthechain-2304-14732]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored SearChain, a retrieval-augmented framework that verifies and completes intermediate reasoning steps for knowledge-intensive tasks.
- Contributed to the paper's evaluation across multi-hop QA, slot filling, fact checking, and long-form QA.
