## Description

Wenya Wang is a faculty member at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, and senior/corresponding author of MemSkill. Her research interests include NLP, knowledge representation, and LLM agents.

## Key Contributions

- Senior author of MemSkill, providing research leadership for the learnable and evolvable memory skill framework.
- Holds the NTU Start-Up Grant (#023284-00001) and co-holds the MOE AcRF Tier 1 Seed Grant (RS37/24) that supported this work.
