## Description

Hantian Ding is a researcher at [[aws-ai-labs]] and the first author of [[ding-2024-fewer-2404-10830]]. In this paper he is part of the team proposing Best-fit Packing for LLM pretraining.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Best-fit Packing method for reducing unnecessary truncation during pretraining.
- Helped frame document truncation as a source of closed-domain hallucination and weaker context use.
