## Description

Tsinghua University is the primary academic affiliation for all eight authors of [[cheng-2024-blackbox-2311-04155]], through the CoAI Group and the Knowledge Engineering Group. It is the institutional home of the paper's BPO research effort.

## Key Contributions

- Hosted the research groups that developed and evaluated BPO.
- Provided the academic setting for the paper's prompt-optimization and alignment experiments.
