## Description

Ping An Technology (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. is the primary institutional affiliation for six authors of [[yang-2023-prca]]. In this source it is the organizational base for building PRCA, a pluggable reward-driven contextual adapter for retrieval QA.

## Key Contributions

- Hosts most of the PRCA author team.
- Anchors the paper's work on black-box LLM integration and context distillation.
