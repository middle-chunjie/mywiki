## Description

Cihang Xie is a researcher at UC Santa Cruz and a co-author of [[gao-2024-sculpting-2311-01734]]. In this work, he is part of the team studying cross-modal pre-training for open-world 3D representation learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the multi-view and joint-alignment design of MixCon3D.
- Helps demonstrate that the method transfers beyond recognition to retrieval and captioning settings.
