## Description

Yucheng Li is a researcher at the [[university-of-surrey]] and first author of [[li-2023-compressing]]. The paper studies how to prune redundant context before LLM inference using self-information.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed [[selective-context]] as an input-side compression method for long-context LLM inference.
- Helped evaluate the method across arXiv, BBC News, and ShareGPT data with summarization, QA, reconstruction, and conversation tasks.
