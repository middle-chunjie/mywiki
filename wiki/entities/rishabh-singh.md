## Description

Rishabh Singh is a [[google-brain]] researcher and co-author of [[vasic-2019-neural-1904-01720]]. In this paper he works on learning-based program repair that localizes and fixes variable-misuse bugs directly from source-code examples.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the joint formulation combining classification, localization, and repair in one pointer model.
- Helped position the method against prior enumerative neural repair and graph-based VARMISUSE systems.
