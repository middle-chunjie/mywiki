## Description

Mark Niklas Müller is a co-author of the SWT-Bench paper with affiliations to ETH Zurich and LogicStar.ai. In this source he contributes to the benchmark design and empirical study of code-agent-based test generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the SWT-Bench benchmark and its issue-reproduction evaluation protocol.
- Participated in the comparison of direct prompting baselines, LIBRO, and code agents.
