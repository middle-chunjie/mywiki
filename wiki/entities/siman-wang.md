## Description

Siman Wang is an author of [[wu-2023-forb-2309-16249]] affiliated with [[snap-inc]], contributing to the construction and evaluation of the FORB retrieval benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the [[forb]] benchmark and its retrieval protocol.
- Contributed to the empirical study of OOD-style [[image-retrieval]] performance across diverse baselines.
- Helped analyze difficulty labels, distractors, and score-margin behavior in the benchmark.
