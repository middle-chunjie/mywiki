## Description

Law-MT is the law-domain English-German dataset used for the paper's domain-adaptation and fine-tuning experiments. In this source its English side is used to build a `19M`-token datastore and automaton.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the target-domain corpus for evaluating RETOMATON beyond in-domain language modeling.
- Highlights that automaton-augmented retrieval is especially strong when target-domain text is repetitive.
