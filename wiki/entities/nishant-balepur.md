## Description

Nishant Balepur is a co-author of AstaBench affiliated with the University of Maryland; the paper notes that this work was performed while on the AI2 Asta Team.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored AstaBench as part of the benchmark's scientific-research evaluation effort.
- Contributed to a large empirical study of `57` agents across `22` agent classes.
