## Description

Qian Liu is a coauthor of [[jiang-2023-active-2305-06983]] and is affiliated with Sea AI Lab in this paper. He contributes to the study of when retrieval should be triggered during long-form generation.

## Key Contributions

- Helped define active retrieval as a decision about when and what to retrieve during generation.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison against single-time retrieval and passive multi-time baselines.
- Participated in analyzing the trade-off between retrieval frequency, factuality, and generation overhead.
