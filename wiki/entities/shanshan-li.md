## Description

Shanshan Li is a researcher at the National University of Defense Technology and the corresponding author of this paper. She co-leads the analysis of when code data most effectively improves LLM reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored and supervised the study of code data across pre-training and instruction-tuning stages.
- Helped formulate the paper's conclusions about general reasoning gains, task-specific code gains, and dynamic mixing.
