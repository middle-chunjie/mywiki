## Description

Bowen Fang is the first author of ToolWeaver and is affiliated with NLPR/CASIA, the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Zhongguancun Academy.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the ToolWeaver framework for scalable generative tool use.
- Contributes to the collaborative-aware tokenization and large-scale ToolBench evaluation.
