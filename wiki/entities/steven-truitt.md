## Description

Steven Truitt is an author of [[edge-2024-local-2404-16130]] affiliated with Microsoft Strategic Missions and Technologies. He is part of the team that studies GraphRAG as an alternative to vector RAG for global corpus questions.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's comparison among GraphRAG, source-text summarization, and vector RAG.
- Helped evaluate the efficiency gains from community-level summaries.
