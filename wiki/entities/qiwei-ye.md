## Description

Qiwei Ye is a researcher at the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence (BAAI) and co-author of the Activation Beacon paper. His work focuses on efficient LLM inference and long-context processing.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Activation Beacon method for plug-in context length extension of LLMs via activation condensing.
- Contributed to the design and evaluation of the beacon token mechanism and streaming processing approach.
