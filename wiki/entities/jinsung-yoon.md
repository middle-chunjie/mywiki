Jinsung Yoon is a co-author of [[jin-2025-llm-2502-03699]] affiliated with [[google-cloud-ai-research]]. In this source Yoon is part of the team that connects direct alignment with IR-style retriever optimization.

## Key Contributions

- Helped develop and evaluate LARPO's ranking-based preference objectives.
- Participated in the paper's empirical analysis of hard negatives and candidate lists.
