## Description

DeepCS is one of the two neural code search models evaluated in [[sun-2022-importance-2202-06649]]. The paper treats it as a representative Siamese retrieval architecture.

## Key Contributions

- Demonstrates large gains from filtered training data, including average improvements of `21.3%` `A@1` and `19.2%` `MRR`.
- Reaches `MRR` of `0.512`, `0.644`, and `0.271` on CSN-v, CB, and NCSED after filtering.
