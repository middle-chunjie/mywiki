## Description

Zora Tung is a researcher affiliated with [[google-research]] and a co-author of [[guu-2020-realm-2002-08909]]. The paper credits Tung as part of the team behind REALM's end-to-end retrieval-augmented pre-training framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored REALM.
- Contributed to a system that trains dense retrieval without supervised retrieval labels.
- Helped evaluate the model on open-domain QA benchmarks.
