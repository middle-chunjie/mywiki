## Description

BERTOverflow is a BERT-based model trained on StackOverflow text and used in this paper as an in-domain baseline for code-related language understanding.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the two main starting points for BLANCA fine-tuning.
- Delivers the strongest results on several tasks after single-task or multi-task tuning.
- Shows that domain-adjacent pretraining plus BLANCA supervision improves code-text embeddings.
