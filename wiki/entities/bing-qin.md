## Description

Bing Qin is a co-author of [[feng-2023-retrievalgeneration-2310-05149]] affiliated in the paper with [[harbin-institute-of-technology]]. The paper credits him on an LLM-based method for combining internal and retrieved knowledge in QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the ITRG framework that integrates [[parametric-knowledge]] with [[non-parametric-knowledge]].
- Helps present the experimental gains over retrieve-then-read and generate-then-read baselines.
