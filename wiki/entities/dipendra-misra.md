## Description

Dipendra Misra is a co-author of the LASER paper and is affiliated with Microsoft Research, New York City. The paper marks him as an equal advisor and places him on the Microsoft Research side of the collaboration.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LASER intervention and the analysis of selective low-rank pruning in Transformers.
- Helped extend the evaluation beyond factual QA to multiple NLU datasets and a decision-Transformer setting.
