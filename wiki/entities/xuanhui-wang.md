## Description

Xuanhui Wang is a researcher at [[google-research]] and a co-author of [[qin-2024-large-2306-17563]]. The paper places Wang on the author team behind the pairwise prompting approach for text ranking.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRP formulation for zero-shot ranking with LLMs.
- Helped position the method against both supervised rerankers and GPT-based listwise systems.
