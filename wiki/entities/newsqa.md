## Description

NewsQA is a reading comprehension and question answering dataset used in [[gou-2023-diversify]] to evaluate diverse question generation.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the three main evaluation datasets for RAST.
- Shows the strongest diversity gains of RAST relative to reported baselines in the paper.
