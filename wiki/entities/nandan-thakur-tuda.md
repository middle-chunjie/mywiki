## Description

Nandan Thakur is the lead author of the BEIR benchmark paper and is affiliated with [[technical-university-of-darmstadt]] through UKP-TUDA in this source. Disambiguated from [[nandan-thakur]] by affiliation: Technische Universität Darmstadt / UKP-TUDA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced BEIR as a zero-shot benchmark spanning `18` datasets and `9` retrieval tasks.
- Helped analyze architectural trade-offs between lexical, sparse, dense, late-interaction, and re-ranking retrieval systems.
