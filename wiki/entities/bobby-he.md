## Description

Bobby He is a paper author and researcher in the Department of Computer Science at ETH Zurich. In [[he-2023-simplifying-2311-01906]], he co-develops simplified Transformer blocks motivated by signal propagation theory and efficiency analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the study introducing SAS and SAS-P as simpler alternatives to standard Pre-LN Transformer blocks.
- Connects signal-propagation arguments with practical design choices such as shaped attention and identity value/projection matrices.
