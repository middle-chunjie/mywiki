## Description

PROTEINS is a graph classification dataset from TUDataset that the paper Multi-Scale Subgraph Contrastive Learning uses in its semantic-similarity analysis and benchmark experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as one of the datasets used to show that larger sampled subgraphs have higher semantic similarity.
- MSSGCL reaches `75.49 +- 0.70` in the unsupervised setting and `75.76 +- 0.52` with `10%` labels in the semi-supervised setting.
