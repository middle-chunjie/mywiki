## Description

Johannes Kepler University Linz is the academic affiliation named on the xLSTM paper through the ELLIS Unit and LIT AI Lab. It is the university base for much of the author team.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the university affiliation for the main xLSTM author group.
- Anchors the paper's research program on modern recurrent language modeling.
