## Description

TempQuestions is the temporal reasoning benchmark used in [[he-2022-rethinking-2301-00303]]. The paper focuses on its implicit temporal subset to test whether RR can ground latent time constraints with external knowledge from Wikidata.

## Key Contributions

- Contributes `169` evaluation examples after filtering to single-answer implicit temporal questions.
- Serves as the testbed for the paper's entity-linking and temporal-relation verbalization pipeline over Wikidata.
