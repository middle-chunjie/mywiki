## Description

UniXcoder is the pretrained Transformer backbone used to initialize both the dual encoder and discriminators in [[li-2022-softlabeled-2210-09597]]. It serves as the main baseline and initialization source for SCodeR.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the starting parameters for SCodeR pre-training.
- Acts as the strongest baseline in the paper's comparison tables.
