## Description

Joseph Boen is a researcher affiliated with Stanford University and a co-author of OctoTools. In this source, he appears as part of the collaboration behind the framework's tool integration and empirical analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored OctoTools as a modular, training-free framework for complex reasoning.
- Contributed to experiments comparing OctoTools with prompting baselines and other agent frameworks.
