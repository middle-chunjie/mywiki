## Description

Mathlib is the large corpus of human-written Lean proofs used by [[unknown-nd-leanstar]] as the source of supervised proof data and retrospective rationale annotations. In the paper it is the main formal mathematics resource from which thought-augmented examples are synthesized.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the successful proof trajectories used to build the direct tactic-prediction baseline.
- Supplies the `52,438` thought-annotated proof steps used to train Lean-CoT.
- Grounds the paper's claim that informal thoughts can be recovered from real formal proof corpora.
