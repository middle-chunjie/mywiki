## Description

Ni Lao is a researcher at the University of Georgia and co-author of the Img2Loc paper on multimodal image geolocation, with expertise in geospatial machine learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Img2Loc and prior geospatial work including Sphere2Vec and CSP self-supervised spatial pre-training.
