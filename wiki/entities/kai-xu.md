## Description

Kai Xu is a ByteDance-affiliated author of [[jin-2024-mvadapter-2301-07868]]. Xu is listed among the researchers building MV-Adapter on top of a frozen CLIP backbone.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the frozen-backbone adapter approach used for PE-VTR.
- Participated in experiments on CLIP-based video-text retrieval benchmarks.
