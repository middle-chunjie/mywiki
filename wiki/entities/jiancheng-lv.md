## Description

Jiancheng Lv is a professor at the College of Computer Science, Sichuan University, and a co-author of AR2 (Adversarial Retriever-Ranker for Dense Text Retrieval).

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the AR2 framework for adversarial joint training of dense retrieval and re-ranking.
