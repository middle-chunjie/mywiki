## Description

Vladimir Mikulik is a researcher at [[deepmind]] and a co-author of [[menick-2022-teaching-2203-11147]]. He is one of the main designers of the paper's inline-evidence approach and reward-learning pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed the Inline Evidence approach with [[nat-mcaleese]].
- Led reward-model training, human evaluation tooling, and data quality monitoring contributions.
- Helped build the RL environment and the constrained sampling implementation.
