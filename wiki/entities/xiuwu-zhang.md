## Description

Xiuwu Zhang is a researcher at [[alibaba-group]] and a co-author of [[ouyang-2023-contrastive]] on contrastive CVR prediction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the empirical evaluation showing CL4CVR outperforming supervised and masking baselines.
- Helped frame embedding masking as a better augmentation strategy than field-level masking for feature-rich samples.
