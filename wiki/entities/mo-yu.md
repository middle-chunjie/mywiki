## Description

Mo Yu is a co-author of [[xu-2024-unsupervised-2402-18150]] affiliated with Pattern Recognition Center, WeChat AI under [[tencent]]. In this paper he contributes to the proposal and evaluation of InFO-RAG for robust retrieval-augmented generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the unsupervised InFO-RAG training framework for LLM-based RAG.
- Helped position the method across zero-shot evaluation, in-context learning, and robustness analyses.
