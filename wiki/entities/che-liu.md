## Description

Che Liu is a Kuaishou researcher and co-first author of [[sun-2024-parrot-2310-07301]], where he helps develop the Parrot data-generation pipeline for multi-turn chat alignment.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the design of Parrot-Ask and Parrot-Chat.
- Helped construct the Parrot-40K multi-turn instruction-tuning corpus.
- Contributed to benchmark evaluation on Alpaca-Eval, MT-Bench, and MT-Bench++.
