## Description

Nishant Yadav is a researcher at the University of Massachusetts Amherst and the first author of [[unknown-nd-adaptive-2405-03651]]. In this paper, he works on scalable `k`-NN search for cross-encoder retrieval models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Axn adaptive retrieval method for test-time cross-encoder search.
- Co-developed the sparse matrix factorization approach for offline item indexing.
