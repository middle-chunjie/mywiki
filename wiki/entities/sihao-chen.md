## Description

Sihao Chen is a co-author of the REVELA paper and is listed with Microsoft as his affiliation in this source. He is part of the author team behind the paper's dense retrieval and language-modeling integration.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the in-batch-attention architecture used by REVELA.
- E5-PT, and supervised baselines. Contributed to the benchmark comparisons against REPLUG
