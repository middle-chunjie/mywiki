## Description

Chengrun Yang is a Google DeepMind researcher and co-author of [[yang-2024-large-2309-03409]], which introduces OPRO for using large language models as optimizers.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed [[opro]] as a meta-prompting framework for iterative optimization without gradients.
- Helped validate the method on linear regression, TSP, GSM8K, and BBH.
