## Description

CARLCS is one of the two neural code search models evaluated in [[sun-2022-importance-2202-06649]]. The paper characterizes it as an interaction-based retrieval network.

## Key Contributions

- Shows strong sensitivity to training-data quality, with average gains of `63.3%` `A@1` and `38.6%` `MRR` after filtering.
- Highlights the utility of both rule-based and semantic cleaning in interaction-based code retrieval.
