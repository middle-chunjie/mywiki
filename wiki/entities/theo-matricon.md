## Description

Théo Matricon is a researcher affiliated in this paper with CNRS, LaBRI, and Université de Bordeaux. He is a co-author of [[fijalkow-2022-scaling]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the analysis of loss-optimal enumeration and sampling for program distributions.
- Contributed to the experimental study of Heap Search and parallel grammar splitting in [[fijalkow-2022-scaling]].
