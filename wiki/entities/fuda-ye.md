## Description

Fuda Ye is the first author of [[ye-2024-rag-2406-13249]] and is affiliated with [[south-china-normal-university]]. He works on retrieval-aware methods for improving retrieval-augmented generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes R2AG and the R2-Former bridge between retrievers and LLMs.
- Helps formulate retrieval features (`r`, `\gamma`, `\zeta`) and the joint QDM-plus-LM training setup.
