## Description

Kun Huang is a researcher at Nanjing University of Science and Technology and a co-author of [[sun-2024-consistency-2401-13598]], which studies consistency-guided denoising for zero-shot document-level relation extraction.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the GenRDK pipeline that combines chain-of-retrieval prompting with cross-document knowledge denoising.
- Co-authored experiments showing gains on [[docred]] and [[re-docred]] for both ZeroDocRE and ZeroDocRTE.
