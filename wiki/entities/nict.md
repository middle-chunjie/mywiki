## Description

NICT is the institutional affiliation listed for Raj Dabre in this survey paper, located in Kyoto, Japan. In the context of this source it is one of the research organizations contributing to multilingual neural machine translation research.

## Key Contributions

- Hosted author expertise contributing to the survey's synthesis of MNMT methods and datasets.
- Represents the research ecosystem around multilingual and low-resource machine translation.
