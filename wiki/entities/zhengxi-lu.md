## Description

Zhengxi Lu is the lead author of [[lu-2026-skill-2604-02268]] and is affiliated with both [[zhejiang-university]] and [[meituan]] in this paper. He works on skill-based reinforcement learning for LLM agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed Skill0 as a training objective for internalizing agent skills into model parameters.
- Contributed to the design of in-context reinforcement learning with rendered visual context and dynamic skill withdrawal.
