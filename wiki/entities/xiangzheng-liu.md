## Description

Xiangzheng Liu is a researcher at [[alibaba-group]] and a co-author of [[ouyang-2023-contrastive]], focusing on robust training for sparse advertising labels.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the duplicate-aware false negative elimination component in CL4CVR.
- Contributed to the comparative analysis against feature-masking and regularization baselines.
