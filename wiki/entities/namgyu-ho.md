## Description

Namgyu Ho is a researcher affiliated with [[kaist-ai]] who worked on [[ho-2024-block-2406-02657]] during an internship at [[lg-ai-research]]. He is a co-first author of the paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced Block Transformer, a global-to-local architecture for faster autoregressive language-model inference.
- Helped validate the throughput-quality trade-off of block-level global modeling plus token-level local decoding.
