## Description

Kai Wang is a researcher at the University of Toronto and a co-author of MMLU-Pro. The paper places Wang on the collaboration developing a harder benchmark for LLM reasoning and knowledge evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MMLU-Pro and its evaluation of more than `50` open and closed models.
- Helped support the benchmark's focus on harder distractors and stronger discrimination between models.
