## Description

Xianzhi Wang is a researcher at the [[university-of-technology-sydney]] and a co-author of [[huang-2023-dual]]. In this work he contributes to relation-aware sequential recommendation with transformer-based preference modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the integration of explicit product relations into the SCEL module.
- Contributed to the design and evaluation of HPM on Amazon recommendation benchmarks.
