## Description

Zirui Guo is a co-author of [[unknown-nd-lightrag]] affiliated with both [[beijing-university-of-posts-and-telecommunications]] and [[university-of-hong-kong]]. The paper credits him with the graph-based RAG design and experimental study behind LightRAG.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LightRAG framework that combines graph-based indexing with dual-level retrieval.
- Helped evaluate LightRAG on the UltraDomain benchmark against chunk-based and graph-based RAG baselines.
