## Description

Web of Science is the second benchmark dataset used in [[liu-2023-enhancing]] for hierarchical text classification. In this paper it contains paper abstracts with `141` total categories over `2` hierarchical levels.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a shallower hierarchy benchmark complementary to BGC for evaluating K-HTC.
- Confirms that the method improves both Macro-F1 and Micro-F1 over strong HTC baselines on scientific abstracts.
