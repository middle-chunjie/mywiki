## Description

Lawrence McAfee is a co-author of [[unknown-nd-instructretroinstruction-2310-07713]]. In this paper he appears with the recurring [[nvidia]] author group working on retrieval-augmented large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Retro-fitting recipe that unfreezes the pretrained decoder instead of freezing it during retrieval-augmented continued pretraining.
- Co-authored the evaluation showing that the decoder-only InstructRetro `43B` remains close to the full encoder-equipped `48B` model.
