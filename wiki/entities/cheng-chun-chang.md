## Description

Cheng-Chun Chang is a co-author of [[wu-2024-coral-2403-06447]] and is affiliated with [[columbia-university]]. In this source he is part of the collaboration that studies collaborative evidence retrieval for LLM-based recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CoRAL method for long-tail recommendation with collaborative prompting.
- Contributed to the empirical study across multiple Amazon recommendation datasets.
