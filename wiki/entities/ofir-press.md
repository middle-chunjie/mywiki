## Description

Ofir Press is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and MosaicML. In this paper, he is the lead author introducing the compositionality gap analysis and the self-ask prompting method.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced the compositionality gap as a measurement framework for LLM compositional reasoning.
- Co-developed self-ask and its search-engine-augmented variant.
