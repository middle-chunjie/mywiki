## Description

Rui Dong is a researcher at [[alibaba-group]] and a co-author of [[ouyang-2023-contrastive]], which studies contrastive representation learning for CVR prediction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the CL4CVR training framework on industrial advertising data.
- Contributed to the design and evaluation of EM, FNE, and SPI for sparse-conversion settings.
