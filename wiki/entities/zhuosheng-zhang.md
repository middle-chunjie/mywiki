## Description

Zhuosheng Zhang is a researcher affiliated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University, working on large language model reasoning and prompting techniques. He is a co-first author (with Aston Zhang) of the Auto-CoT paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed Auto-CoT, the diversity-based automatic chain-of-thought prompting method that eliminates the need for manually designed demonstrations.
- Analyzed the misleading-by-similarity failure mode of similarity-based demonstration retrieval.
