## Description

Jianke Zhang is a co-author of [[jiang-2022-crossmodal-2211-09623]] and was affiliated with [[beijing-institute-of-technology]] at the time of publication. He contributes to the paper's multimodal retrieval study from the Beijing Institute of Technology side.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Cross-Modal Adapter study on parameter-efficient text-video retrieval.
- Contributed to the empirical evaluation against prompt-learning and full fine-tuning baselines.
