## Description

Chengjin Xu is a co-author of [[sun-2024-thinkongraph-2307-07697]] from IDEA Research. In this paper, he contributes to the introduction of Think-on-Graph as a training-free LLM-KG reasoning framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ToG framework for iterative KG exploration and reasoning.
- Contributed to the empirical comparison against prompting and fine-tuning baselines.
