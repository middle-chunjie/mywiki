## Description

Daria Kryvosheieva is the lead author of [[kryvosheieva-2025-efficient-2508-21290]] and is affiliated in the paper with the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and Jina AI. The paper notes that the work was done during an internship at Jina AI.

## Key Contributions

- Led the presentation of compact code embedding models adapted from Qwen2.5-Coder backbones.
- Co-authored the training recipe combining task prefixes, contrastive learning, and Matryoshka truncation for code retrieval.
