## Description

Mixtral 8x7B is the sparse Mixture-of-Experts backbone used to instantiate the larger [[gritlm]] variant in [[muennighoff-2024-generative-2402-09906]].

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the initialization model for GritLM 8x7B.
- Provides the higher-capacity backbone that lifts the paper's generative average to `65.7`.
