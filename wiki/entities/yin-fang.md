## Description

Yin Fang is a researcher at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJUKG lab). She works on knowledge-enhanced LLMs and biomolecular instruction tuning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of KnowPAT, contributing to the knowledge graph integration and preference set construction.
- Co-authored Mol-Instructions, a large-scale biomolecular instruction dataset for LLMs.
