## Description

Zeqiu Wu is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and a co-author of SELF-RAG. In this paper, he contributes to the design and evaluation of retrieval-aware self-reflective generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the SELF-RAG framework and its reflection-token formulation.
- fact verification, and long-form generation tasks. Helped evaluate the model on open-domain QA
