## Description

Kangxiang Jia is a researcher affiliated with the Shanghai Key Laboratory of Data Science, School of Computer Science, Fudan University. Jia is listed as a co-author of the 2024 survey on retrieval-augmented generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored a survey that formalizes Naive, Advanced, and Modular RAG.
- Helped summarize generation-stage techniques such as reranking and context compression.
- Contributed to the paper's overview of evaluation metrics and benchmark ecosystems.
