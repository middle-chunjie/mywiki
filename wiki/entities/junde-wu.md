## Description

Junde Wu is the first author of [[wu-2025-agentic-2502-04644]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-oxford]] and [[national-university-of-singapore]]. In this paper he co-develops the Agentic Reasoning framework for tool-augmented LLM reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the overall Agentic Reasoning framework.
- Contributes to the design of the Mind-Map, web-search, and coding-agent toolbox studied in the paper.
