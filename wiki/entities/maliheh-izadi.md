## Description

Maliheh Izadi is a researcher at [[delft-university-of-technology]] and the lead author of [[izadi-2022-codefill-2202-06689]]. In this paper, she contributes to the design and evaluation of CodeFill for Python autocompletion.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CodeFill architecture that jointly learns token values, token types, and statement completion.
- Helped frame more realistic autocompletion evaluation through cardinal-point and statement-level tasks.
