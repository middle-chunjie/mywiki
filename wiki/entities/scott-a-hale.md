## Description

Scott A. Hale is a researcher affiliated with the University of Oxford and Meedan and a co-author of the PRISM dataset paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRISM dataset paper.
- Helped shape the dataset's emphasis on multicultural participation and downstream societal welfare.
- Contributed to case studies connecting feedback-population design to aggregate alignment outcomes.
