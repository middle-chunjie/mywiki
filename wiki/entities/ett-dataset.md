## Description

ETT is the electricity transformer temperature time-series benchmark family used in [[liu-2023-timesurl]] for imputation and forecasting. The paper specifically reports results on ETTh1, ETTh2, and ETTm1.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the main benchmark family for short- and long-term forecasting evaluation.
- Provides the imputation setting with masking ratios from `12.5%` to `50%`.
