## Description

Zhouyang Jia is a co-author of [[wang-2022-bridging-2112-02268]] from [[national-university-of-defense-technology]]. His contribution in this paper centers on adapting pretrained encoders to robust code understanding with transformed training data.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the augmentation and curriculum framework evaluated on multiple source-code-understanding tasks.
- Helped establish the empirical comparison between RoBERTa, CodeBERT, and GraphCodeBERT under the proposed recipe.
