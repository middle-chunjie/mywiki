## Description

Ameet Deshpande is a co-first author of the C-STS paper and is affiliated with Princeton University and the Allen Institute for AI in this source.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced the C-STS task and the C-STS-2023 benchmark.
- Contributed to the dataset construction pipeline built from image-caption retrieval and crowd annotation.
- Helped evaluate encoder and LLM baselines for conditional similarity.
