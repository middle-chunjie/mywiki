## Description

Hao Liu is a co-author of [[gudibande-2023-false-2305-15717]] and is affiliated with [[uc-berkeley]]. In this paper, he helps investigate whether public imitation data can substitute for stronger proprietary training pipelines.

- Helped analyze broad-imitation failure modes on factual QA and coding benchmarks.
