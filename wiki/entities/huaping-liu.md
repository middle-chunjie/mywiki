## Description

Huaping Liu is a researcher at BNRist and the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, and the corresponding author of [[wang-2021-understanding-2012-09740]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's gradient-based analysis of contrastive loss and its temperature-controlled hardness sensitivity.
- Contributed to the empirical study of uniformity, tolerance, and downstream linear evaluation across four image datasets.
