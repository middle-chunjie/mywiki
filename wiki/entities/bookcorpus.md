## Description

BookCorpus is the unlabeled text corpus used in [[oord-2019-representation-1807-03748]] to train sentence-level CPC representations for downstream NLP transfer tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the source data for CPC's natural-language pretraining setup.
- Enables evaluation on MR, CR, Subj, MPQA, and TREC after unsupervised sentence representation learning.
