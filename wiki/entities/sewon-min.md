## Description

Sewon Min is a researcher affiliated with the University of Washington and Meta AI. In this paper, she is the first author studying what demonstrations contribute to in-context learning.

## Key Contributions

- Led the paper's empirical analysis of random-label demonstrations across multiple large language models.
- Helped frame the claim that prompt gains often come from label space, input distribution, and format rather than ground-truth pairings.
