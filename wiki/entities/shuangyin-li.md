## Description

Shuangyin Li is the corresponding author of [[ye-2024-rag-2406-13249]] and is affiliated with [[south-china-normal-university]]. The paper positions him as a lead contributor to retrieval-augmented generation under frozen-model constraints.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the R2AG framework for bridging retrievers and generators.
- Contributes to the design of retrieval-aware prompting and the low-resource training setup.
