## Description

Dorsa Sadigh is a co-author of [[li-2023-chain-2312-04474]] and is affiliated with both [[stanford]] and [[google-deepmind]]. In this work she is part of the team linking language-model reasoning with embodied execution contexts.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored [[chain-of-code]] as a hybrid reasoning method.
- Helped motivate the value of combining semantic reasoning with executable APIs.
- Contributed to the paper's robotics application examples.
