## Description

Yiheng Shu is a researcher at The Ohio State University and a co-author of [[guti-rrez-2024-hipporag-2405-14831]].

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the design and evaluation of HippoRAG's KG-based long-term memory and retrieval pipeline.
- Co-authored experiments showing that single-step graph retrieval can outperform stronger passage-only retrievers on integration-heavy benchmarks.
