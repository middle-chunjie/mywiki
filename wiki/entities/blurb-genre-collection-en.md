## Description

BlurbGenreCollection-EN is one of the two hierarchical text classification benchmarks used in [[liu-2023-enhancing]]. It contains book advertising descriptions with `146` total categories across `4` hierarchical levels and is the harder of the two evaluated datasets.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the main deep-hierarchy benchmark for testing whether knowledge helps on fine-grained classification.
- Shows the strongest gains from K-HTC's hierarchy-aware label attention and contrastive components.
