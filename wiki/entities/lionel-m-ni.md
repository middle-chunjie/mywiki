## Description

Lionel M. Ni is a co-author of [[sun-2024-thinkongraph-2307-07697]] affiliated with the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). The paper lists him among the researchers behind the Think-on-Graph framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper proposing tight LLM-KG coupling via explicit graph-path reasoning.
- Contributed to the framing of ToG as a responsible and explainable reasoning approach.
