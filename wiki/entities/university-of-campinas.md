## Description

The University of Campinas (UNICAMP) is a public research university in Campinas, Brazil, home to Rodrigo Nogueira's research group focused on information retrieval and NLP.

## Key Contributions

- Institutional affiliation of Rodrigo Nogueira, co-author of the UIA personalized dense retrieval framework.
