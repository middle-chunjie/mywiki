## Description

EleutherAI is the open research collective behind the Pythia suite and the GPT-NeoX training stack used in the paper. It serves as the primary institutional home for most of the paper's authors.

## Key Contributions

- Released the Pythia model suite and checkpoints.
- Developed GPT-NeoX, the training library used for the experiments.
- Enabled a public, reproducible research release rather than a closed model report.
