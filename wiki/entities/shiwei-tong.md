## Description

Shiwei Tong is an author of [[fang-2021-guided]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China. The paper credits him as part of the team behind the guided concept-extraction model.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored GACEN and its integration of structured document information into sequence labeling.
- Supported the evaluation on concept-extraction benchmarks with manually curated clue words.
