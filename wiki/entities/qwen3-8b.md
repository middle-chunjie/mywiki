## Description

Qwen3-8B is the base language model checkpoint from which DR Tulu is initialized. The paper compares plain `Qwen3-8B + Our Search` against the SFT and RL variants of DR Tulu.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the initialization point for DR Tulu training.
- Provides the paper's main naive-search baseline for measuring gains from SFT and RLER.
