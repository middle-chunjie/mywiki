## Description

2WikiMultiHopQA is one of the multi-hop question answering benchmarks used in [[feng-2023-retrievalgeneration-2310-05149]]. The paper uses it to test whether iterative retrieval-generation can recover multi-step reasoning paths better than standard baselines.

## Key Contributions

- Shows a `5`-shot EM of `38.6` for ITRG (refresh), compared with `31.8` for vanilla LM.
- Provides evidence that iterative collaboration improves both reasoning quality and answer recall over successive iterations.
