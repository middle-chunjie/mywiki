## Description

Yan Fang is the first author of [[fang-2024-scaling-2403-18684]] and is affiliated with the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University and Quan Cheng Laboratory. The paper places Yan Fang at the center of the empirical study of scaling laws for dense retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's formulation of contrastive perplexity for dense-retrieval scaling analysis.
- Helped carry out model-size, data-size, and annotation-quality experiments on MS MARCO and T2Ranking.
