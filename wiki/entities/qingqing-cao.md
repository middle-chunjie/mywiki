## Description

Qingqing Cao is a researcher at the University of Washington and the lead author of BTR. In this paper, she works on efficient retrieval-augmented readers through binary token caching and compression.

## Key Contributions

- Led the design of BTR for compact passage-state caching in retrieval-augmented language models.
- Co-developed calibrated binarization and token-compression techniques for faster Atlas-style readers.
