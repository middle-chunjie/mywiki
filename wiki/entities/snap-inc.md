## Description

Snap Inc. is the industry affiliation listed for all authors of [[wu-2023-forb-2309-16249]]. In this paper it is the organization behind the release and evaluation of the FORB flat-object retrieval benchmark.

## Key Contributions

- Sponsored the creation of the [[forb]] benchmark and its supporting code release.
- Provided the institutional setting for the paper's large-scale evaluation of [[image-retrieval]] methods.
