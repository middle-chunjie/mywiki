## Description

BIG-Bench Hard is the primary reasoning benchmark used in [[li-2023-chain-2312-04474]], comprising `23` difficult tasks spanning semantic, numeric, and symbolic reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the main evaluation suite for comparing direct prompting, Chain-of-Thought, and [[chain-of-code]].
- Provides human baselines and heterogeneous task families that support both single-task and cross-task prompting analyses.
- Makes it possible for the paper to separate algorithmic gains from more language-heavy reasoning behavior.
