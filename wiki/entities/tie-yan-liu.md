## Description

Tie-Yan Liu is a researcher at [[microsoft-research]] and a co-author of [[ke-2021-rethinking-2006-15595]]. In this paper, he is part of the team proposing TUPE as a better positional encoding strategy for language pre-training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed [[untied-positional-encoding]] and its relative-position variant for [[bert]]-style encoders.
- Helped frame the paper around inductive bias and pre-training efficiency on [[glue]].
