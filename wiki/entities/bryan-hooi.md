## Description

Bryan Hooi is a co-author of [[he-2024-gretriever-2402-07630]] and is affiliated with National University of Singapore in the paper. In this source he is part of the team studying graph retrieval, hallucination, and large-scale graph QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the end-to-end G-Retriever system and its benchmark evaluation.
- Helped demonstrate the method's gains in retrieval efficiency and hallucination reduction.
