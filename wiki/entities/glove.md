## Description

GloVe is the pretrained word-embedding initialization used in [[friedman-2023-transformer-2306-01128]] for the named-entity-recognition and text-classification experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `300`-dimensional initial word representations for both Transformer Programs and standard Transformer baselines on the real-world NLP tasks.
- Helps the paper evaluate whether an interpretable-by-design architecture can remain competitive when initialized with standard lexical features.
