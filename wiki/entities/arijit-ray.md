## Description

Arijit Ray is a vision-language researcher affiliated with Boston University and Meta AI in [[ray-nd-cola]], where he is the first author. The paper places him on the benchmark design and adaptation study for compositional text-to-image retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the `COLA` benchmark for attribute-object compositional retrieval.
- Helped design and evaluate multimodal adaptation strategies on CLIP and FLAVA.
