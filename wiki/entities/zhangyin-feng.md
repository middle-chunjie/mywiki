## Description

Zhangyin Feng is a co-author of [[feng-2023-retrievalgeneration-2310-05149]] affiliated in the paper with [[harbin-institute-of-technology]]. He contributes to the iterative retrieval-generation framework for knowledge-intensive question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the ITRG framework that alternates [[generation-augmented-retrieval]] and [[retrieval-augmented-generation]].
- Helps position the method for [[open-domain-question-answering]] and multi-step reasoning tasks.
