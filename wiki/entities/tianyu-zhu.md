## Description

Tianyu Zhu is a researcher affiliated with the University of Montreal and Beihang University, and a co-author of [[mo-2024-historyaware-2401-16659]]. The paper notes that this work was done while he was at the University of Montreal.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the formulation of PRJ-based context denoising for conversational retrieval.
- Helped analyze how historical supervision improves performance under topic shifts.
