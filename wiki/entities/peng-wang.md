## Description

Peng Wang is a ByteDance-affiliated author of [[jin-2024-mvadapter-2301-07868]]. The paper lists Wang among the researchers adapting CLIP from image-text pretraining to video-text retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the multimodal retrieval adaptation framework.
- Co-authored the benchmark evaluation showing near-parity or gains over full fine-tuning.
