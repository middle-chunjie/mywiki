## Description

The UCR Time Series Archive is the large classification benchmark collection used in [[liu-2023-timesurl]]. The paper evaluates TimesURL on `128` UCR datasets to measure how well the learned representations transfer across many time-series domains.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the large-scale classification setting where TimesURL reaches average accuracy `0.845`.
- Supplies the source and target domains used in the paper's transfer-learning study.
