## Description

Song Han is a senior co-author affiliated with the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and NVIDIA. In this paper he helps position StreamingLLM as a systems-friendly method for efficient, persistent LLM inference.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the efficient rolling-KV design that works with standard dense-attention kernels.
- Helped connect the attention-sink analysis to practical decode-time latency and memory measurements.
