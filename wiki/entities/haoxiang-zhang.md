## Description

Haoxiang Zhang is a researcher affiliated with Stanford University and UC San Diego, and a co-first author of AgentFlow. The paper presents him as part of the core team behind the framework and its planner-training recipe.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the AgentFlow architecture for decomposed planning and tool use.
- Co-developed the empirical study showing large gains from in-the-flow planner optimization.
