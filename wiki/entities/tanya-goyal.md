## Description

Tanya Goyal is a researcher at [[princeton-university]] and a co-author of the BOOOOKSCORE study on coherence errors in book-length LLM summaries.

## Key Contributions

- Helped define the fine-grained coherence annotation protocol and error taxonomy used in the paper.
- Co-authored the analysis comparing hierarchical merging and incremental updating for long-form summarization.
