## Description

Anima Anandkumar is a co-author of [[wang-2023-shall]] affiliated with [[nvidia]]. She appears on the paper's large-scale study of whether retrieval should be built into autoregressive LM pretraining.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the comprehensive empirical study across perplexity, generation quality, factuality, toxicity, and open-domain QA.
- Contributed to the argument that retrieval-augmented pretraining is promising despite additional compute and datastore complexity.
