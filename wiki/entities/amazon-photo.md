## Description

Amazon-Photo is a co-purchase graph benchmark used in [[gong-2022-magcl-2212-07035]] for downstream [[node-classification]] evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Provides one of the strongest absolute-accuracy results in the paper, where MA-GCL reaches `93.80 +/- 0.1`.
- Helps demonstrate that the proposed model augmentations are effective outside citation networks.
