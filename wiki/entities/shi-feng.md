## Description

Shi Feng is a co-author of [[zhao-2021-calibrate-2102-09690]], affiliated with the University of Maryland. His research focuses on robustness and evaluation of natural language processing systems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the analysis of few-shot in-context learning instability due to prompt sensitivity.
- Contributed to experiments on contextual calibration across text classification and information extraction tasks.
