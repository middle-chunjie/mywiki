## Description

Rafael Rafailov is a Stanford University researcher and first author of [[rafailov-2023-direct-2305-18290]], where he derives the core Direct Preference Optimization objective.

## Key Contributions

- Derived the DPO objective and the main theoretical reparameterization connecting rewards and policies.
- Contributed to PPO and reward-learning baselines used in the paper.
