## Description

Yi Dai is a researcher affiliated with the Shanghai Research Institute for Intelligent Autonomous Systems at Tongji University. Dai is a co-author of the survey on retrieval-augmented generation for large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the survey's taxonomy of RAG methods.
- Helped synthesize retrieval design issues such as chunking, metadata, and structured indexing.
- Contributed to the paper's comparison between RAG, fine-tuning, and prompt engineering.
