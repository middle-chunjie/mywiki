## Description

Jiashuo Sun is a co-author of [[sun-2024-thinkongraph-2307-07697]] affiliated with Xiamen University and IDEA Research. The paper credits him as part of the team proposing Think-on-Graph for LLM-guided reasoning over knowledge graphs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Think-on-Graph, which uses beam search to explore and prune KG reasoning paths with an LLM.
- Helped validate the framework across KBQA, open-domain QA, slot filling, and fact-checking benchmarks.
