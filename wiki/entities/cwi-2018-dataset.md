## Description

The CWI 2018 Shared Task dataset is a multilingual benchmark for complex word identification, containing binary and continuous complexity annotations for target words in English (News, WikiNews, Wikipedia), German, Spanish, and French — introduced at the 13th Workshop on Innovative Use of NLP for Building Educational Applications (BEA 2018).

## Key Contributions

- Provides 27,299 / 3,328 English training/validation entries across three proficiency-level sub-genres; 6,151 German, with no French training/validation data.
- Serves as the primary cross-lingual CWI benchmark; evaluation metric is MAE (competition) with Pearson reported as secondary.
- Zero-shot French generalization (no French training data) is a notable challenge in this benchmark.
