## Description

Carl Yang is a researcher at Emory University and a co-author of [[xu-2026-alternating-2602-01511]]. In this paper, Yang helps study how rubric generation and judgment can be jointly optimized for LLM alignment.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Rubric-ARM framework and its alternating optimization design.
- Helped analyze transfer from reward-model improvements to offline and online policy post-training.
