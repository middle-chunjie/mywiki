## Description

Benjamin Van Durme is a researcher affiliated with Johns Hopkins University and a co-author of [[weller-2024-followir-2403-15246]]. In this work he is part of the team connecting retrieval evaluation with instruction-following behavior.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the benchmark and analysis showing that many retrieval systems ignore long instructions.
- Contributes to the training-resource design used to build FollowIR-7B.
