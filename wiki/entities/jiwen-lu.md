## Description

Jiwen Lu is a co-author of [[jiang-2022-crossmodal-2211-09623]] and was affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]] and BNRist. He is one of the senior contributors on the paper's CLIP adaptation work for video retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the multimodal adapter design and evaluation protocol.
- Contributed to the study of efficient transfer from pretrained CLIP to video retrieval benchmarks.
