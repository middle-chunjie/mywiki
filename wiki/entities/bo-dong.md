## Description

Bo Dong is a researcher at the [[university-of-surrey]] and co-author of [[li-2023-compressing]]. He is listed on the paper as part of the team introducing Selective Context for efficient LLM inference.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Selective Context method for pruning low-information lexical units before generation.
- Contributed to the empirical study of compression ratios and lexical-unit granularity.
