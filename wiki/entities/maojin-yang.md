## Description

Maojin Yang is a co-author of [[feng-2023-retrievalgeneration-2310-05149]] from [[harbin-institute-of-technology]]. In this paper he contributes to the iterative QA framework and its empirical study on four benchmarks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the iterative synergy design used to improve [[open-domain-question-answering]].
- Helps validate the method on Natural Questions, TriviaQA, 2WikiMultiHopQA, and HotpotQA.
