## Description

Haofen Wang is a researcher affiliated with both the Shanghai Research Institute for Intelligent Autonomous Systems and the College of Design and Innovation at Tongji University. Wang is a co-author of the 2024 survey on retrieval-augmented generation for large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored a comprehensive survey of RAG paradigms, components, and evaluation.
- Helped synthesize how RAG interfaces with fine-tuning, robustness, and long-context modeling.
- Contributed to the paper's outlook on production-ready and multimodal RAG systems.
