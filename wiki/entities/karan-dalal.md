## Description

Karan Dalal is a UC Berkeley researcher and co-author of [[sun-2024-learn-2310-13807]], which studies learned test-time adaptation through Meta Test-Time Training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's replacement of attention layers with TTT layers inside transformer-style architectures.
- Contributed to the ImageNet evaluations that compare MTTT against efficient attention baselines.
