## Description

BEIR is a heterogeneous information retrieval benchmark spanning 18 datasets across 9 domains, and it serves as the main evaluation suite in [[dai-2022-promptagator-2209-11755]]. The paper extends it from zero-shot retrieval to a few-shot setting with `2-8` task-specific exemplars.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the 11 public evaluation datasets used for the paper's main nDCG@10 comparison.
- Motivates the paper's focus on query-distribution and search-intent heterogeneity across retrieval tasks.
- Serves as the basis for the proposed few-shot retrieval evaluation protocol.
