## Description

Guanfeng Liu is a researcher affiliated with Macquarie University and a co-author of [[qin-2023-metaoptimized]]. In this paper, Guanfeng Liu contributes to a contrastive sequential recommendation method that improves robustness under sparse and noisy interactions.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MCLRec and its evaluation on public recommendation benchmarks.
- Helped analyze robustness to reduced batch size, noisy interactions, and shorter user histories.
