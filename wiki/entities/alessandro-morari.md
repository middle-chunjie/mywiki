## Description

Alessandro Morari is an IBM Research co-author of [[ding-2022-dissimilarity-2110-03868]]. The paper places him on the team building a contrastive pretraining approach for code semantics.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the DISCO pretraining recipe based on semantic positives and bug-injected negatives.
- Helped validate the method on vulnerability detection and clone detection datasets.
