## Description

Xu Tan is a researcher affiliated with [[microsoft-research-asia]] and a co-author of [[yuan-2024-evoagent-2406-14228]]. The paper credits him as part of the team designing EvoAgent and evaluating it across multiple agent tasks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the mutation, crossover, selection, and result-update pipeline used by EvoAgent.
- Contributes to experiments showing gains over prompt-only and handcrafted multi-agent baselines.
