## Description

Taolin Zhang is a co-author of [[guo-2024-refir-2410-05601]] and is affiliated with Tsinghua University in the paper. He appears on the work introducing retrieval-augmented restoration for diffusion-based LRMs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ReFIR method and its experiments on faithful high-fidelity restoration.
