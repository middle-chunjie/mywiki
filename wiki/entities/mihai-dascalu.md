## Description

Mihai Dascalu is a faculty member at the University Politehnica of Bucharest (Faculty of Automatic Control and Computers), leading research on automated text evaluation, simplification, and NLP for educational applications.

## Key Contributions

- PI or senior co-author on multiple CWI and text simplification projects; leads the ATES (Automated Text Evaluation and Simplification) research program funded by UEFISCDI, Romania.
- Co-developed domain-adaptation methods for lexical complexity prediction using adversarial training and auxiliary tasks.
