## Description

Kaiyu Huang is a researcher affiliated with Beijing Jiaotong University and a co-author of [[mo-2024-historyaware-2401-16659]]. In this paper, he contributes to conversational retrieval experiments and analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the empirical study showing stronger HAConvDR gains on TopiOCQA than on QReCC.
- Helped analyze the role of historical supervision in reducing retrieval errors from irrelevant prior turns.
