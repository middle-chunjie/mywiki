## Description

Xuefei Ning is a first co-author of the Skeleton-of-Thought paper and is affiliated with [[tsinghua-university]]. The paper lists Ning as one of the researchers proposing the SoT latency-reduction framework.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the proposal of Skeleton-of-Thought for parallel answer generation.
- Contributed to the empirical study of latency and answer quality across multiple LLMs.
