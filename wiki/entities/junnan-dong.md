## Description

Junnan Dong is a researcher at [[hong-kong-polytechnic-university]], co-first-author (equal contribution) of KnowGPT with Qinggang Zhang. His work spans knowledge graph reasoning, active learning for KG error detection, and multi-hop question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the KnowGPT framework; also first author of HamQA (hierarchy-aware multi-hop QA over KGs) and lead on active ensemble learning for KG error detection (WSDM 2023).
- Contributed the MAB-based prompt construction strategy that adapts to per-question context.
