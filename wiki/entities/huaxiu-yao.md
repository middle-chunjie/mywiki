## Description

Huaxiu Yao is the corresponding author of [[xia-2026-skillrl-2602-08234]] and is affiliated with [[unc-chapel-hill]] in the paper. She is part of the leadership team behind the SkillRL framework for evolving LLM agents through reusable skills.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the full SkillRL framework spanning skill distillation, cold-start SFT, and recursive RL-time skill evolution.
- Helps position the work as a structured alternative to raw-memory methods for agent learning from experience.
