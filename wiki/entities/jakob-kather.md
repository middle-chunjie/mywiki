## Description

Jakob N. Kather is the senior author of this paper, affiliated with EKFZ for Digital Health, TU Dresden, NCT Heidelberg University Hospital, and University Hospital Dresden. He co-leads the introduction of [[toolmaker]] for scientific software integration by LLM agents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the paper's main framework and benchmark for autonomous scientific tool creation.
- Positions the work toward future agent-based scientific workflows in medicine and life sciences.
