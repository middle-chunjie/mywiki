## Description

Vicuna-7B-v1.3 is an open chat-oriented LLM baseline evaluated in [[chen-2023-benchmarking-2309-01431]] on bilingual RGB testbeds.

## Key Contributions

- Achieves the highest English information-integration accuracy at noise ratio `0`, reaching `60%`.
- Still degrades sharply under noise, falling to `43%` on English information integration at noise ratio `0.4`.
