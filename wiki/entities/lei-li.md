## Description

Lei Li is a coauthor of LumberChunker affiliated with Carnegie Mellon University. In this paper he contributes to the LLM-based chunking method and the retrieval-oriented evaluation of narrative documents.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors LumberChunker as a [[dynamic-chunking]] method for retrieval.
- Contributes to the benchmarked analysis of [[retrieval-granularity]] choices.
- Helps evaluate the method inside a downstream [[retrieval-augmented-generation]] pipeline.
