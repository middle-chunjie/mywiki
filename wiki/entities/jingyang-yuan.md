## Description

Jingyang Yuan is the lead author of [[yuan-2025-native-2502-11089]] and is affiliated with DeepSeek-AI and Peking University. The paper credits him with the overall NSA sparse-attention design and its long-context evaluation program.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors NSA, a hardware-aligned and natively trainable sparse attention architecture for long-context LLMs.
- Contributes to the paper's algorithm design spanning compression, block selection, and sliding-window branches.
