## Description

Yongqi Zhang is a co-author of [[ye-2024-rag-2406-13249]] and is affiliated with [[hong-kong-university-of-science-and-technology-guangzhou]]. He contributes retrieval and ranking expertise to the paper's list-wise retrieval-feature design.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the list-wise retrieval feature formulation used by R2AG.
- Supports evaluation across multi-hop QA and long-context QA benchmarks.
