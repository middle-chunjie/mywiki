## Description

Minmin Hou is a co-author of [[yen-2024-helmet-2410-02694]] and is affiliated with [[intel]]. In this paper she contributes to the design and evaluation of a realistic long-context benchmark for large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored HELMET's application-centric benchmark design across seven long-context task categories.
- Helped support the paper's large-scale empirical evaluation over `59` long-context models.
