## Description

Shangyu Wu is a researcher affiliated with City University of Hong Kong and the lead author of [[unknown-nd-improving-2401-02993]]. In this paper, he is associated with the proposal and evaluation of the ReFusion retrieval-fusion framework for NLU.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ReFusion method for computation-efficient retrieval representation fusion.
- Helped validate the method across 15 non-knowledge-intensive NLU benchmarks.
