## Description

Zhicheng Dou is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and senior co-author of the Tool-Light paper. His role in this work is tied to tool-augmented reasoning, retrieval-oriented evaluation, and the broader multi-tool reasoning agenda.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Tool-Light framework for efficient and accurate tool-integrated reasoning.
- Contributed to the benchmark design spanning both knowledge-intensive QA and mathematical reasoning tasks.
