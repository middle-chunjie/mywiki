## Description

Dan Roth is a coauthor of [[he-2022-rethinking-2301-00303]] and is affiliated with the University of Pennsylvania in this paper. Disambiguated from [[dan-roth]] by affiliation: University of Pennsylvania.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the RR framework for improving LLM explanation faithfulness with external knowledge.
- Contributed to the formulation of faithfulness-based reranking over sampled reasoning paths.
- Helped position RR as a no-fine-tuning alternative to trained retrieval-augmented systems.
