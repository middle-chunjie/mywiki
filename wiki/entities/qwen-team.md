## Description

The Qwen Team is a research group at Alibaba Group responsible for developing and releasing the Qwen series of large language models, including Qwen2.5, Qwen2.5-Math, and associated reward and evaluation models.

## Key Contributions

- Released the Qwen2 and Qwen2.5 family of language models spanning 0.5B to 72B parameters.
- Developed Qwen2.5-Math-Instruct models and the Qwen2.5-Math-PRM-7B/72B process reward models.
- Open-sourced multiple models including Qwen2.5-Math-PRM-7B and Qwen2.5-Math-PRM-72B (state-of-the-art open-source PRMs as of early 2025).
