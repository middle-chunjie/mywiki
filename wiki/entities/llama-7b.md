## Description

LLaMA-7B is the base model that [[li-2023-camel-2303-17760]] fine-tunes on CAMEL-generated datasets to study knowledge emergence and code-generation gains.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the initialization point for the CAMEL-7B models evaluated in the paper.
- Demonstrates measurable gains after training on synthetic cooperative data across AI Society, Code, Math, and Science.
- Provides the baseline for HumanEval and HumanEval+ comparisons against CAMEL-7B.
