## Description

Yuyang Ye is a co-author of [[liu-2018-patent]] from [[university-of-science-and-technology-of-china]]. In this paper he is part of the group proposing hybrid content-collaborative modeling for patent litigation risk.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the integration of patent text, metadata, and lawsuit-history tensor signals in CTF.
- Helped validate the method under leave-one-out and percentage-wise evaluation settings.
