## Description

SQuAD is a reading comprehension and question answering benchmark used in [[gou-2023-diversify]] through two train/validation/test splits for evaluating diverse question generation.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the SQuAD split 1 and split 2 benchmarks used for the paper's main automatic evaluations.
- Supports the paper's ablation analyses on the diversity-consistency trade-off.
