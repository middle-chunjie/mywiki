## Description

Matan Levy is a co-author of [[levy-nd-chatting]] and is affiliated with [[hebrew-university-of-jerusalem]]. In this paper he contributes to the design and evaluation of ChatIR for multi-round image retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops ChatIR as a two-stage pipeline combining question generation with dialog-conditioned image retrieval.
- Helps adapt [[visdial]] into a retrieval benchmark and compare LLM questioners against human and RL baselines.
