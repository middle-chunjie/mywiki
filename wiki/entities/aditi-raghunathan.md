## Description

Aditi Raghunathan is a co-author of [[springer-2024-repetition-2402-15449]] affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]]. In this work she contributes to the argument that decoder-only LMs can recover whole-sequence embedding signals through prompting rather than bidirectional architectural changes.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the echo embeddings framework and its evaluation on MTEB.
- Helped establish the compute-matched comparison against classical and bidirectional baselines.
