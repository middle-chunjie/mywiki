## Description

Zhengbao Jiang is the first author of [[jiang-2023-active-2305-06983]] and is affiliated with [[carnegie-mellon-university]] in this paper. He helps formulate active retrieval-augmented generation for long-form factual generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed the active retrieval-augmented generation framework that interleaves retrieval and generation.
- Co-developed FLARE, which predicts the next sentence, triggers retrieval from low-confidence tokens, and regenerates with retrieved evidence.
- Co-evaluated the method across 2WikiMultihopQA, StrategyQA, ASQA, and WikiAsp.
