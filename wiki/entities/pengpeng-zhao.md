## Description

Pengpeng Zhao is a researcher affiliated with Soochow University and a co-author of [[qin-2023-metaoptimized]]. In this work, Pengpeng Zhao contributes to the design of contrastive and meta-optimization components for sequential recommendation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored a model that couples data augmentation and learnable model augmentation.
- Participated in the benchmark evaluation against CL4SRec, DuoRec, SRMA, and other baselines.
