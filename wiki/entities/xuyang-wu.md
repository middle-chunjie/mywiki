## Description

Xuyang Wu is a researcher at Santa Clara University and a coauthor of [[peng-2024-soft-2307-08303]], contributing to the study of soft-prompt-based retrieval augmentation.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthors the empirical study showing that tuned soft prompts outperform hard-prompt augmentation for dense retrieval.
- Contributes to experiments over open-source LLMs and downstream retrievers.
