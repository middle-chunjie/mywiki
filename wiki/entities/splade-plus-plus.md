## Description

SPLADE++ is the sparse late-expansion retriever used in [[lin-2023-how-2302-07452]] as the final teacher in progressive label augmentation and as a strong zero-shot retrieval baseline.

## Key Contributions

- Provides the most advanced teacher in the paper's final supervision trajectory.
- Serves as a high-performing reference point that DRAGON+ matches on BEIR-13 while retaining dense-retrieval efficiency at inference time.
