## Description

PaintSkills is a skill-based text-to-image dataset and benchmark reused in [[cho-2023-visual-2305-15328]] for both VPGen training and VPEval analysis. It is especially important for prompts where spatial layout matters.

## Key Contributions

- Contributes text-layout supervision to the `F30+C+P` training mix for VPGen.
- Inspires the paper's skill taxonomy of object, count, spatial, scale, and text-rendering evaluation.
- Highlights VPGen's layout-control gains on prompts where spatial structure is central.
