## Description

CANARD is a human-written conversational query reformulation dataset used in [[mao-2022-convtrans]] to train CNL-T5 and to instantiate baseline conversational retrievers. The paper reports `5,644` sessions and `40,527` turns for CANARD.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies supervision for conversational query rewriting in the CNL-T5 stage.
- Provides the human-authored training sessions used by CQE and ConvDR-CANARD baselines.
