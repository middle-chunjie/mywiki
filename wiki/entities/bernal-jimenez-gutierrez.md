## Description

Bernal Jiménez Gutiérrez is a researcher at The Ohio State University and the first author of [[guti-rrez-2024-hipporag-2405-14831]], which introduces HippoRAG.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed HippoRAG, a graph-based retrieval framework that uses OpenIE, dense retrieval, and Personalized PageRank for single-step multi-hop retrieval.
- Helped demonstrate strong retrieval and QA gains on MuSiQue and 2WikiMultiHopQA together with substantial cost and latency reductions relative to IRCoT.
