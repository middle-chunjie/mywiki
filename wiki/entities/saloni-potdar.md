## Description

Saloni Potdar is a researcher at [[apple]] and a co-author of [[reddy-2024-agrame-2405-15028]]. In this paper she contributes to the ranking framework and its attribution-oriented evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the AGRaME training and scoring framework for any-granularity ranking.
- Contributes to experiments on open-domain QA and proposition-level retrieval.
- Helps evaluate PropCite as a post-hoc citation addition method for RAG outputs.
