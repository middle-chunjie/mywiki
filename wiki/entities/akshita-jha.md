## Description

Akshita Jha is a researcher in the Department of Computer Science at [[virginia-tech]] and the first author of [[jha-2023-codeattack-2206-00052]]. She studies robustness and adversarial evaluation for programming-language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed [[codeattack]] as a black-box attack for pre-trained code models.
- Led the empirical evaluation across code translation, repair, and summarization tasks.
