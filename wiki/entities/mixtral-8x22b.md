## Description

Mixtral 8x22B is the main open-source MoE comparison model in the paper. It provides the closest architectural baseline for judging whether DeepSeek-V2's MLA plus DeepSeekMoE stack offers a better quality-efficiency frontier.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the primary external MoE baseline in both base-model and chat-model comparisons.
- Helps show that DeepSeek-V2 remains competitive on English, code, and math while being much stronger on Chinese benchmarks.
- Anchors the claim that DeepSeek-V2 is the strongest open-source MoE language model reported in the paper.
