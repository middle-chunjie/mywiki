## Description

ULIP is an earlier image-text-3D contrastive learning method referenced in [[gao-2024-sculpting-2311-01734]]. The paper treats ULIP as part of the baseline lineage that first aligned 3D encoders to frozen CLIP image and text spaces.

## Key Contributions

- Establishes the basic point-image and point-text contrastive training setup that MixCon3D builds upon.
- Provides one of the prior training recipes that the paper revisits and improves.
