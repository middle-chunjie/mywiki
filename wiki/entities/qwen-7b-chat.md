## Description

Qwen-7B-Chat is a bilingual open LLM baseline evaluated in [[chen-2023-benchmarking-2309-01431]] and is one of the strongest open models reported in RGB.

## Key Contributions

- Records the best exact negative-rejection scores in the benchmark: `31.00%` in English and `8.67%` in Chinese.
- Achieves the highest Chinese information-integration accuracy, reaching `67%` at noise ratio `0` and `55%` at `0.4`.
