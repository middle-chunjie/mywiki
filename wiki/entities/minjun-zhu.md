## Description

Minjun Zhu is a co-first author of [[weng-2023-large-2212-09561]] and was affiliated with the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the paper's self-verification pipeline for reranking candidate reasoning answers without a separate trained verifier.
- Contributed to the experimental study across multiple reasoning datasets and model variants.
