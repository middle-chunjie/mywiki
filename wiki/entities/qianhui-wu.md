## Description

Qianhui Wu is a Microsoft Corporation researcher and co-author of [[jiang-2023-llmlingua-2310-05736]], a paper on prompt compression for black-box large language models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LLMLingua method, including its budget-control and iterative compression design.
- Helped validate prompt compression on GSM8K, BBH, ShareGPT, and Arxiv-March23.
