## Description

Alexander L. Gaunt is a co-author of DeepCoder affiliated in this paper with Microsoft Research, with an additional note of affiliation with the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems. He contributed to the neural and synthesis components of the system.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored [[deepcoder]] and its Learning Inductive Program Synthesis formulation.
- Contributed to the model design and empirical comparison against symbolic baselines.
