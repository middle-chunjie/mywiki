## Description

Kehang Wang is an author of [[liu-2023-enhancing]] affiliated with the University of Science and Technology of China and the State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence. He contributed to the K-HTC model and its evaluation on hierarchical classification benchmarks.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the Knowledge-aware Text Encoder and the overall K-HTC pipeline.
- Contributed to the experiments showing gains over prior HTC baselines on BGC and WOS.
