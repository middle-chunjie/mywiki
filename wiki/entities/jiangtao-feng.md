## Description

Jiangtao Feng is a co-author of [[yu-2026-memagent-2507-02259]], listed on the title page under the ByteDance Seed and AIR, Tsinghua University collaboration. The paper credits this author on the design and evaluation of MemAgent for ultra-long-context question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the MemAgent workflow that streams documents chunk by chunk while maintaining a fixed-length memory.
- Contributes to the RL-based training and evaluation showing extrapolation from `32K` training documents to `3.5M`-token QA.
