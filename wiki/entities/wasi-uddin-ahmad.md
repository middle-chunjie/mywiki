## Description

Wasi Uddin Ahmad is a researcher affiliated with the University of California, Los Angeles and a co-author of [[ahmad-2021-unified]]. In this paper, he helps introduce PLBART for unified program and language understanding and generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PLBART paper and its denoising pre-training setup over code and developer text.
- generation, translation, repair, and classification tasks. Contributed to empirical evaluation across summarization
