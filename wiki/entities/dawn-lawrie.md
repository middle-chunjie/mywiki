## Description

Dawn Lawrie is a researcher affiliated with Johns Hopkins University and a co-author of [[weller-2024-followir-2403-15246]]. The paper places her in a collaboration focused on TREC-based evaluation and instruction-aware retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the reuse of TREC narratives as realistic retrieval instructions.
- Helps establish the paper's benchmark-oriented evaluation framework for instruction-following IR.
