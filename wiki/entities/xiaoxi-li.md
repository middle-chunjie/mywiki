## Description

Xiaoxi Li is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and first author of [[li-2024-retrollm-2412-11919]]. In this paper, Li helps design a unified retrieval-and-generation framework that retrieves evidence during generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced RetroLLM as a single autoregressive framework for clue generation, evidence generation, and answer generation.
- Helped formulate hierarchical [[fm-index]] constraints and the evidence-generation pipeline for open-domain QA.
