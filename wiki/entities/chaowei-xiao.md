## Description

Chaowei Xiao is a co-author of [[wang-2023-shall]] affiliated with [[nvidia]] and [[university-of-wisconsin-madison]]. In this paper he contributes to the analysis of retrieval-augmented autoregressive models for factuality, toxicity, and QA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the evaluation showing RETRO reduces repetition and improves several knowledge-intensive tasks relative to GPT.
- Contributed to the RETRO++ QA variant that moves the top retrieved passage into the decoder context.
