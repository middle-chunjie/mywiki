## Description

Bowen Chen is a researcher affiliated with Stanford University and a co-author of OctoTools. In this paper, he is part of the team building the framework's modular tool interface and broad benchmark evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the OctoTools framework for training-free tool-augmented reasoning.
- Contributed to the planner-executor design and the evaluation across 16 reasoning benchmarks.
