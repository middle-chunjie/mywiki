## Description

SWE-Gym Lite is the lightweight subset of SWE-Gym defined in [[pan-2024-training-2412-21139]]. It contains easier, more self-contained tasks for faster experimentation with SWE agents.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a `230`-task subset aligned with the SWE-Bench Lite filtering philosophy.
- Filters out multi-file and poorly specified tasks to lower evaluation cost.
