## Description

Wei Xiong is a co-author of [[jin-2025-llm-2502-03699]] affiliated with [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]]. In this paper Xiong helps develop the iterative preference-optimization perspective grounded in retriever training.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the link between iterative retriever optimization and iterative LLM alignment.
- Helped frame hard-negative mining and candidate-list construction as alignment design choices.
- Contributed to the benchmark experiments and ablation studies.
