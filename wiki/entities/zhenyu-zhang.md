## Description

Zhenyu Zhang is a PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin and lead author of H2O. His research focuses on efficient LLM inference, model compression, and sparse computation.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed the H2O KV-cache eviction framework based on the heavy-hitter observation.
- Formalized the eviction problem as dynamic submodular maximization with theoretical guarantees.
- Led the system implementation and throughput benchmarking against DeepSpeed, Accelerate, and FlexGen.
