## Description

Armen Aghajanyan is a Meta AI researcher and co-author of [[yu-2023-megabyte-2305-07185]]. He is part of the author team proposing MEGABYTE as an efficient Transformer for million-byte autoregressive modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the MEGABYTE architecture and its efficiency analysis for long sequences.
- Helped evaluate the model on text, image, and audio byte streams.
