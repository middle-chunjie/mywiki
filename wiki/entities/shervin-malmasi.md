## Description

Shervin Malmasi is an author of [[senel-2024-generative-2406-05255]] affiliated with Amazon.com, Inc., Seattle. He is part of the team presenting an LLM-based Explore-Exploit optimizer for generative recommender systems.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper introducing training-free CTR-guided optimization for generated question pools.
- Contributed to the analysis of offline simulation results and deployment considerations.
