## Description

Zhixu Li is a senior co-author of [[wang-2024-improving-2401-04361]], appearing with the Soochow University author team and shaping the paper's knowledge-graph-centered dialogue modeling direction.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the EnCo framework and its KGD formulation.
- Contributed to the knowledge encoder and context-knowledge fusion design.
- Supported the benchmark evaluation on three public KGD datasets.
