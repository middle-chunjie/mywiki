## Description

Sebastian Riedel is a researcher affiliated in this paper with Meta AI Research and University College London. He co-authored the paper introducing Contriever.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the contrastive dense retrieval framework used in Contriever.
- Contributed to the zero-shot and few-shot retrieval evaluation in the paper.
