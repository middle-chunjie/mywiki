## Description

Gemini Pro is a Google DeepMind model family variant evaluated in [[allamanis-2024-unsupervised-2402-08699]] for both synthesis and editing RTC experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Achieves `34.8% RTC_pass` on HumanEval and `11.1%` on ARCADE in the paper.
- Reaches average cross-domain forward lift `21.5%` and EDITINGRTC exact-match RTC `12.9%`.
