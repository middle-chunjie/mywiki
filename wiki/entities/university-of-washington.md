## Description

The University of Washington is a public research university and the primary academic affiliation for most SELF-RAG authors in this paper. It is the institutional base for the work's retrieval-grounded LLM research.

## Key Contributions

- Hosted the main academic team behind SELF-RAG.
- Provided the research setting connecting instruction-tuning, retrieval, and grounded generation.
