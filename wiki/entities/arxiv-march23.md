## Description

Arxiv-March23 is the summarization dataset used in [[jiang-2023-llmlingua-2310-05736]] to evaluate whether compressed prompts preserve semantic coverage in long-context summarization.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies the summarization setting where LLMLingua achieves BERTScore F1 `90.33` at `345` tokens (`4x`) and `89.03` at `176` tokens (`9x`).
- Shows that the proposed compressor improves BLEU and ROUGE over sentence selection and Selective-Context under identical token constraints.
