## Description

Dayiheng Liu is a senior researcher at the Qwen Team, Alibaba Group (corresponding author of [[zhang-2025-lessons-2501-07301]]), with broad contributions to the Qwen series of language models.

## Key Contributions

- Corresponding author on the PRM lessons paper, overseeing the empirical study and model releases.
- Co-author on multiple Qwen and Qwen2.5-Math technical reports (Yang et al. 2024).
