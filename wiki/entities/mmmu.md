## Description

MMMU is a multimodal reasoning benchmark covering image-based problems across multiple academic disciplines; in [[unknown-nd-geminia-2312-11805]] it is used as a headline measure of college-level multimodal reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Evaluates image-grounded reasoning across six broad disciplines.
- Shows Gemini Ultra reaching `62.4%` with `Maj@32` and `59.4%` with `pass@1`, above the cited GPT-4V baseline `56.8%`.
