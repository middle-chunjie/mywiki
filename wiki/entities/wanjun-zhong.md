## Description

Wanjun Zhong is a researcher affiliated with ByteDance Seed and a co-author of ReTool. In this paper, Zhong is associated with a framework for learning strategic code-interpreter use through reinforcement learning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored ReTool, which combines cold-start tool traces with outcome-driven PPO training.
- Helped characterize emergent behaviors such as earlier tool invocation and interpreter-guided self-correction.
