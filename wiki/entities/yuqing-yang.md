## Description

Yuqing Yang is a Microsoft Corporation researcher and co-author of [[jiang-2023-llmlingua-2310-05736]], a prompt-compression paper for closed LLM APIs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LLMLingua method for coarse-to-fine prompt compression.
- Helped analyze compression behavior across reasoning, dialogue, and summarization tasks.
