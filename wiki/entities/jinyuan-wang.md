## Description

Jinyuan Wang is the first author of [[wang-2023-selfprompted-2310-13552]] and is affiliated with [[shanghai-jiao-tong-university]]. The paper associates Wang with the design of the SP-CoT framework and the ODMR data-generation pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposed SP-CoT for automated chain-of-thought construction in open-domain multi-hop reasoning.
- Helped build the self-generation and composition pipeline for `2-4` hop ODMR datasets.
- Co-ran the evaluation across ChatGPT, InstructGPT, and multiple `13B` instruction-tuned LLMs.
