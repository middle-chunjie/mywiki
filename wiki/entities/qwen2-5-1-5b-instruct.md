## Description

Qwen2.5-1.5B-Instruct is the smaller backbone model used in SLEA-RL's scaling study. The paper reports that step-level experience augmentation yields especially large WebShop gains for this weaker base model.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the backbone for the paper's smaller-scale ALFWorld and WebShop experiments.
- Shows that SLEA-RL reaches `87.5%` ALFWorld success and `75.4%` WebShop success with a `1.5B`-parameter model.
