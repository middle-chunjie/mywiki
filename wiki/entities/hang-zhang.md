## Description

Hang Zhang is a researcher affiliated with the College of Computer Science, Sichuan University. He was a co-first author of AR2 (Adversarial Retriever-Ranker), conducted during an internship at Microsoft Research Asia.

## Key Contributions

- Co-first author of AR2, proposing the adversarial retriever-ranker framework for dense text retrieval.
- Developed the minimax training objective and distillation regularization in AR2.
