## Description

Graham Neubig is a coauthor of RETOMATON and is listed with the Language Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon University in this paper. He is part of the team proposing automaton-augmented retrieval for language modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored RETOMATON and the accompanying open-source implementation.
- Helped evaluate how retrieval structure affects perplexity and search efficiency across domains.
