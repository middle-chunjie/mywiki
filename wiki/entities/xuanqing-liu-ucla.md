## Description

Xuanqing Liu is a co-author of FLOATER and is affiliated with UCLA in this paper. Disambiguated from [[xuanqing-liu]] by affiliation: UCLA.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored FLOATER, a continuous dynamical position encoder for Transformer models.
- Helped design the compatibility and warm-start strategy that lets the method initialize from vanilla Transformer or RoBERTa checkpoints.
