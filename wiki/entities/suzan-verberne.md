## Description

Suzan Verberne is a researcher at Leiden University and a co-author of [[askari-2024-generative-2408-02152]], where she studies efficient prompting-based generative retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the Few-Shot GR framework and its prompting-only indexing formulation.
- Contributes to the paper's analysis of dynamic corpora and future evaluation directions.
