## Description

MoatlessTools is the specialized workflow scaffold evaluated in [[pan-2024-training-2412-21139]]. It constrains the action space of a software engineering agent through pre-defined stages instead of free-form long-horizon interaction.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the paper's specialized-workflow baseline alongside OpenHands.
- Enables the paper's online self-improvement experiments on SWE-Gym Lite.
- Achieves stronger zero-shot Lite performance than OpenHands for the tested open-weight models.
