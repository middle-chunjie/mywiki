## Description

João Marques is a coauthor of LumberChunker affiliated with INESC-ID. In this source he helps develop the narrative-document chunking method and the associated benchmark construction pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authors the LLM-guided [[document-segmentation]] method.
- Contributes to the creation of [[gutenqa]] as a retrieval [[benchmark]].
- Supports the evaluation of chunking choices for [[passage-retrieval]].
