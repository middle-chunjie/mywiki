## Description

Sang Michael Xie is the lead author of [[xie-2022-explanation-2111-02080]] and was affiliated with [[stanford-university]] at the time of the paper. In this work, he helps frame in-context learning as latent-concept inference under a probabilistic pretraining model.

## Key Contributions

- Co-develops the theoretical view of in-context learning as implicit Bayesian inference.
- Helps introduce the mixture-of-HMM analysis and its distinguishability condition.
- Co-creates the GINC synthetic dataset used to test the paper's predictions empirically.
