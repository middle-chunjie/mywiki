## Description

Haitao Li is the first author of [[li-2023-constructing-2304-11943]] and is affiliated with the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University and Zhongguancun Laboratory. In this paper, he leads the proposal of JTR for efficient dense retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the JTR framework for joint optimization of tree-based indexing and query encoding.
- Contributed to the design of tree-based negative sampling and overlapped clustering.
