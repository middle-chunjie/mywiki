## Description

CollabLLM is the framework and resulting model family introduced in [[wu-2025-collabllm-2502-00640]] for training LLMs to optimize long-horizon human collaboration rather than only next-turn helpfulness.

## Key Contributions

- Introduces Multiturn-aware Reward as a response-level proxy for conversation-level outcomes.
- Uses collaborative simulation and user simulators to estimate future interaction consequences.
- Delivers gains on multiturn benchmarks and a real-user study in document writing.
