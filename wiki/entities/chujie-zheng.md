## Description

Chujie Zheng is a researcher at the Qwen Team, Alibaba Group, co-author of [[zhang-2025-lessons-2501-07301]] and lead author of the PROCESSBENCH benchmark (arXiv:2412.06559) for evaluating step-level reasoning error identification.

## Key Contributions

- Created PROCESSBENCH, the step-level evaluation benchmark used as a central evaluation tool in this paper.
- Co-developed the consensus filtering approach for PRM training data quality.
- Contributed to the analysis of BoN-PRM misalignment.
