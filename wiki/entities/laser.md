## Description

LASER is the multilingual sentence-embedding toolkit referenced in [[ranathunga-2021-neural-2106-15115]] for large-scale bitext mining across many languages.

## Key Contributions

- Provides multilingual sentence embeddings used to retrieve parallel sentences from comparable corpora.
- Anchors the survey's discussion of parallel-data mining for low-resource NMT.
