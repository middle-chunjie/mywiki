## Description

Gemma2 27B is the open-weight language model evaluated in [[luo-2024-improve-2406-06592]] as both a reasoning policy and a base checkpoint for reward modeling.

## Key Contributions

- Serves as the open-model testbed for OmegaPRM, improving from `42.3%` to `58.2%` on MATH500.
- On GSM8K, OmegaPRM-weighted decoding improves Gemma2 27B from `74.0%` to `92.2%`.
