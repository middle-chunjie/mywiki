## Description

Jiewen Hou is a researcher affiliated with the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and a co-author of [[mao-2023-large-2303-06573]]. His role in this paper is tied to evaluating LLM-based search-intent interpretation for conversational passage retrieval.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the LLM4CS framework that combines prompted rewrites, hypothetical responses, and vector aggregation.
- Helped validate the method on multi-turn CAsT benchmarks for conversational search.
