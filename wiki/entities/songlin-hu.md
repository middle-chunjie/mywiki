## Description

Songlin Hu is a researcher affiliated with the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. He is a co-author of [[ma-2026-lightretriever-2505-12260]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the LightRetriever architecture for efficient LLM-based text retrieval.
- Helped position the method as a balance between online efficiency and retrieval effectiveness.
