## Description

Max Bartolo is a researcher affiliated with UCL and Cohere and a co-author of the PRISM dataset paper.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PRISM dataset paper.
- Helped study model-level preference variation under different conversational contexts.
- Contributed to analyses comparing PRISM rankings with other LLM leaderboards.
