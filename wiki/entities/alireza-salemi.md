## Description

Alireza Salemi is the first author of [[salemi-2024-optimization]] and is affiliated with [[university-of-massachusetts-amherst]]. His work in this paper centers on retrieval optimization and retriever selection for LLM personalization.

## Key Contributions

- Co-proposes `ROPG-RL` and `ROPG-KD` for training personalized dense retrievers from downstream LLM feedback.
- Co-develops `RSPG-Pre` and `RSPG-Post` for per-input retriever selection on the LaMP benchmark.
