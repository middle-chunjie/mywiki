## Description

Panupong Pasupat is a researcher affiliated with [[google-research]] and a co-author of [[guu-2020-realm-2002-08909]]. In this paper, Pasupat is part of the group that applies retrieval-augmented pre-training to open-domain question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored REALM.
- Helped connect retrieval-augmented pre-training to open-domain QA evaluation.
- Contributed to the paper's evidence-grounded alternative to purely parametric factual recall.
