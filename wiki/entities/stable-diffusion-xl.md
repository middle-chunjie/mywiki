## Description

Stable Diffusion XL is an open text-to-image diffusion model included in the paper's cross-model mitigation evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Provides one of the paper's main open-model baselines for copyright-risk measurement.
- Shows a large `DETECT` reduction under combined prompt rewriting and negative prompting.
