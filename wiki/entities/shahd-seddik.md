## Description

Shahd Seddik is a researcher at the University of British Columbia and the first author of [[unknown-nd-contextaugmented-2601-20810]]. In this paper, she co-proposes Programming Knowledge Graphs for retrieval-augmented code generation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the code-centric and text-centric PKG formulation.
- Co-authored the experimental study on HumanEval and MBPP.
