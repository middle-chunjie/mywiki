## Description

LibriSpeech is the speech dataset used in [[oord-2019-representation-1807-03748]] to evaluate CPC on phone and speaker classification with linear probes.

## Key Contributions

- Provides `100` hours of speech data for CPC pretraining in the audio experiments.
- Supports the paper's main speech results, including `64.6` phone classification accuracy and `97.4` speaker classification accuracy.
