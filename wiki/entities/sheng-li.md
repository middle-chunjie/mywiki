## Description

Sheng Li is a faculty researcher at the University of Virginia (corresponding author) and co-author of the Img2Loc paper on multimodal image geolocation, with expertise in machine learning and computer vision.

## Key Contributions

- Served as corresponding author and faculty advisor for the Img2Loc project at the University of Virginia.
- Co-leads the UVA group contributing to Img2Loc alongside Zihan Guan and Mengxuan Hu.
