## Description

Marah I. Abdin is a researcher at Microsoft Research and the lead author of the KITAB benchmark paper. In this work she studies how LLMs handle constrained information-retrieval queries.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored KITAB and its dynamic data-collection workflow.
- Analyzed GPT-4 and GPT-3.5 failures on constrained retrieval tasks.
