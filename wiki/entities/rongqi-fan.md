## Description

Rongqi Fan is a researcher at the University of Waterloo and a co-author of MMLU-Pro. The paper places Fan on the team constructing a more robust benchmark for multi-discipline language understanding.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored MMLU-Pro and its data curation workflow across MMLU, STEM Website, TheoremQA, and SciBench.
- Helped support the benchmark's focus on harder reasoning and reduced prompt sensitivity.
