## Description

Karthik Narasimhan is a co-author of the C-STS paper and is affiliated with Princeton University in this source.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the proposal of C-STS and its benchmark framing.
- Contributed to the analysis showing that strong STS systems fail to transfer to conditioned similarity.
- Helped connect the benchmark to broader questions in language understanding evaluation.
