## Description

Jieke Shi is a researcher at [[singapore-management-university]] and a co-author of [[yang-2022-natural-2201-08698]]. In this paper Jieke Shi is part of the team studying naturalness-aware adversarial attacks against pre-trained code models.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the ALERT attack pipeline that ranks variable substitutions by contextual similarity before adversarial search.
- Contributed to the empirical study on attack success, naturalness, and adversarial fine-tuning.
