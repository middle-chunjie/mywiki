## Description

Luke Bates is a coauthor of the SETFIT paper and is affiliated with the UKP Lab at [[technical-university-of-darmstadt]]. In this source he is part of the collaboration that evaluates few-shot sentence-transformer adaptation.

## Key Contributions

- Coauthored the SETFIT experiments on English benchmarks and RAFT.
- Participated in the academic collaboration behind the prompt-free classification setup.
