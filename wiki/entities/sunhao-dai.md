## Description

Sunhao Dai is a researcher at [[renmin-university-of-china]] and co-author of [[qu-2024-completenessoriented-2405-16089]], which studies completeness-oriented tool retrieval for LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the design of COLT's dual-view graph collaborative learning framework.
- Co-developed the ToolLens dataset and the completeness-focused evaluation setup.
