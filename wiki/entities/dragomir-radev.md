## Description

Dragomir Radev is a researcher associated with Yale University and a co-author of REALTIME QA. The paper includes him among the senior contributors to the benchmark's design and analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the benchmark paper introducing REALTIME QA and its dynamic evaluation protocol.
- Contributed to connecting the benchmark to broader work on temporal QA, retrieval, and summarization over time.
