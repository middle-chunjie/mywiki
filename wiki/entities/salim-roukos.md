## Description

Salim Roukos is a researcher at [[ibm-research-ai]] and a co-author of [[rosenthal-2025-clapnq]]. He is part of the team introducing CLAPnq as a benchmark for grounded long-form QA and end-to-end RAG evaluation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the CLAPnq dataset and benchmark definition for grounded long-form QA.
- Contributed to the empirical study of retrieval, gold-passage generation, and noisy full-RAG performance.
