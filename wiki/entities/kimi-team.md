## Description

Kimi Team is the collective author group behind the Kimi Linear technical report, developed primarily at Moonshot AI with a small number of external collaborators. In this source it represents the organization-level effort that designed, trained, and released the hybrid KDA/MLA model family.

## Key Contributions

- Introduced Kimi Linear and Kimi Delta Attention as a hardware-aware hybrid linear-attention architecture.
- Reported matched-scale comparisons against MLA and GDN-H across pretraining, SFT, RL, and long-context evaluation.
- Released KDA kernels, vLLM integration, and model checkpoints to support follow-up research.
