## Description

Markus Spanring is a co-author of the xLSTM paper. The paper lists him within the JKU Linz / NXAI author team responsible for the architecture and experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the xLSTM architecture paper and its large-scale evaluation.
- Contributed to the study of recurrent alternatives for long-context language modeling.
