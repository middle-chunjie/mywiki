## Description

Researcher at Google Brain at the time of the Transformer paper; equal-contribution co-author of [[vaswani-2017-attention-1706-03762]]. Also co-author of Extended Neural GPU and Neural GPU works cited as related convolutional baselines.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designed the Transformer architecture.
- Prior work on Extended Neural GPU and Neural GPUs influenced the Transformer's "parallel computation of hidden states" motivation.
