## Description

Yi Gui is the first author of [[gui-2022-crosslanguage-2201-07420]] and is affiliated with Huazhong University of Science and Technology. The paper credits Gui with the XLIR study on cross-language binary-source code matching using LLVM-IR.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the formulation of cross-language binary-source code matching.
- Contributed to the design and evaluation of XLIR over binary-source and source-source matching tasks.
