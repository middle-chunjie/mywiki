## Description

Vicuna-13B-v1.5 is one of the open-source chat-oriented LLMs evaluated in [[su-2024-dragin-2403-10081]]. The paper uses it to test whether DRAGIN generalizes beyond the Llama2-Chat family.

## Key Contributions

- Shows DRAGIN reaching `0.266/0.3628` on 2WikiMultihopQA and `0.296/0.4185` on HotpotQA.
- Shows DRAGIN also leading on StrategyQA (`0.687` accuracy) and IIRC (`0.2379/0.2829` EM/F1) under the Vicuna setting.
