## Description

Sivakanth Gopi is a researcher at Microsoft Research and a co-author of *Textbooks Are All You Need*. He is listed on the paper introducing phi-1 and its emphasis on educational-value filtering plus synthetic data.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the phi-1 work on curated pretraining data and exercise-based finetuning.
