## Description

Kang Liu is a co-author of [[weng-2023-large-2212-09561]] and was affiliated with the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to the proposal that large language models can verify their own candidate conclusions through backward reasoning.
- Helped analyze the method across multiple datasets and different GPT-3 model variants.
