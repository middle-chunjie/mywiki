## Description

Meng Jiang is an associate professor at the University of Notre Dame (email: mjiang2@nd.edu), where he leads a research group on knowledge-enhanced NLP and information retrieval. He is the corresponding/senior author of LLM-URL and several related ODQA papers from Notre Dame.

## Key Contributions

- Senior author on LLM-URL, framing LLMs as built-in autoregressive search engines for document retrieval.
- Oversees the Notre Dame NLP group's work on knowledge-intensive generation, multi-hop QA, and knowledge graph enhanced retrieval.
