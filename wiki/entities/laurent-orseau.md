## Description

Laurent Orseau is a researcher at [[deepmind]] and a co-author of [[orseau-2018-singleagent-1811-10928]], where he helps formulate policy-guided tree search with explicit node-expansion guarantees.

## Key Contributions

- Co-introduced LevinTS and LubyTS as policy-guided search algorithms with theoretical guarantees.
- Analyzed policy mixing as a way to reduce failures caused by near-zero action probabilities.
