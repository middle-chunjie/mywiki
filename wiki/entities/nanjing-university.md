## Description

Nanjing University is the affiliation shared by all four authors of [[ding-2024-enhancing-2402-02175]], through the State Key Laboratory for Novel Software Technology. It is the institutional base of the paper's EPR-KGQA work.

## Key Contributions

- Hosted the research team that proposed Evidence Pattern Retrieval for complex KGQA.
- Provided the academic setting for experiments on CWQ, WebQSP, and Freebase-based retrieval.
