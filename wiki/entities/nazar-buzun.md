## Description

Nazar Buzun is a researcher affiliated with CILAB.AI in Moscow. He is a co-author of [[sorokin-2026-qrag-2511-07328]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored Q-RAG, a resource-efficient multi-step retrieval method trained in embedding space.
- Contributed to the paper's evaluation on ultra-long-context and multi-hop QA tasks.
