## Description

Chengquan Jiang is a researcher affiliated with ByteDance Seed and a co-author of ReTool. In this paper, Jiang is part of the group studying tool-aware RL training for mathematical reasoning.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the PPO-based rollout framework with interleaved code execution.
- Helped evaluate ReTool on AIME benchmarks and analyze code-use dynamics.
