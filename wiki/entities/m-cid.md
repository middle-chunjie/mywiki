## Description

M-CID (Multilingual Covid-19 Intent Dataset) is a small-scale cross-lingual intent detection dataset collected for Covid-19 information services, with 16 intents and 1,745 English utterances (train: 1,220, validation: 176, test: 349). Only the English subset is used in NID experiments.

## Key Contributions

- Provides a low-resource evaluation setting (16 intents, ~1.7K utterances) for new intent discovery, testing methods under data-scarce conditions.
- Used in MTP-CLNN experiments to demonstrate label efficiency of the proposed approach.
