## Description

Xinlei Chen is a Meta AI researcher, equal advising author, and co-author of [[sun-2024-learn-2310-13807]], which develops MTTT for learned test-time adaptation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the paper's neural-network inner-loop extension, `MTTT-MLP`, which outperforms linear-attention baselines on raw pixels.
- Helped guide the experimental comparison between MTTT, linear attention, and self-attention under long-sequence constraints.
