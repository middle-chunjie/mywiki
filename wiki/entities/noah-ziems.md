## Description

Noah Ziems is a researcher at the University of Notre Dame (email: nziems2@nd.edu) and lead author of LLM-URL. His work focuses on leveraging large language models for information retrieval and open-domain question answering.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed LLM-URL, demonstrating that GPT-3 can act as a training-free autoregressive search engine by generating Wikipedia URLs as document identifiers.
- Showed that nearly 90% of documents reached via LLM-generated URLs contain correct answers on WebQ under few-shot prompting.
