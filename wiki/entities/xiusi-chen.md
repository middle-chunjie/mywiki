## Description

Xiusi Chen is a researcher at [[university-of-illinois-urbana-champaign]] and a co-author of [[qian-2025-smart-2502-11435]]. In this paper she contributes to the design and evaluation of calibrated reasoning-tool switching.

## Key Contributions

- Co-developed the SMART data construction pipeline and step-level justifications.
- Co-authored the analysis of tool overuse and near-optimal tool-use behavior.
- Helped validate the method on OOD reasoning and knowledge benchmarks.
