## Description

Ziyi Xia is a researcher affiliated with [[baai]] and the first-listed author of [[xia-2025-open-2509-00375]]. In this work, Xia contributes to the formulation of deep-research QA as HCSPs and to the InfoSeek data-synthesis pipeline.

## Key Contributions

- Co-authored the InfoSeek framework for synthesizing deep-research questions from webpages and Wikipedia.
- Contributed to the InfoSeeker training and evaluation pipeline built on the synthesized dataset.
