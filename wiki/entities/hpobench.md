## Description

HPOBench is a collection of reproducible hyperparameter optimization benchmarks. In this paper it is used to evaluate LLAMBO beyond the Bayesmark setting on tabular tasks with precomputed results.

## Key Contributions

- Supplies `24` additional HPO tasks for evaluating LLAMBO under a different benchmark suite.
- Provides tabular benchmarks for XGBoost, RandomForest, and MLP with discretized search spaces.
